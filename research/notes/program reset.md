## Program reset

Key reasons for program reset:

 - discovery that Claude AI did not
   - read and digested actual meaning of verses
   - group context was based on fringe boundary analysis rather than actual verse context
   - terms and working of different terms are misused to support findings, not based on core analysis
   - registry word analysis and completeness was not properly done

the entire pipeline is flawed

 - the registry has duplicates
 - the validation that is is complete is not properly done
 - terms identified, but some is ignored, and the inclusion of others are not validated
 - verses identified, but ignored
 - verses context in correct
 - dimensions based on wrong premises
 - analytics are completely skewed toward hallusinations around boundaries and cross term correlations, rather than the real meaning of words.

Key tasks

    - registry to be duplicated
    - words with same fundamental meaning to be consolidated
    - missing words to be discovered
    - meaning of terms, and therefore words to be discribed much earlier
    - terms within context of verses to be extracted and consolidated
    - boundary considerations to be identified but investigated differently
    - collections and categorisations derived much later. meaning of collections and categorisation is incidental, not pivotal.

new pipe

    - role of registry is purely to surface terms and verses. once terms and verses are identified, then role of registry diminish completely
    - subject of analysis is terms, supported for meaning by verses.
