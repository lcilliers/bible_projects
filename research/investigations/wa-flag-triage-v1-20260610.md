## Net

- **Genuine inner-being with no home = the SEATS & FACULTIES (heart/soul/spirit/mind/flesh/conscience)** —
  the one real debate. Everything else resolves: a few **clear moves** (life → M25), some **framework** terms
  to debate (justice/covenant → M26/M44 or reference), and a large **reference/noise** remainder that behaves
  like T2 (embedded where used, never analysed standalone).
- So FLAG mostly **dissolves**: → M25 (life), → debate (seats; justice/covenant), → reference (the rest).
- **Recommended decision needed:** the **seats home-or-reference** question. My lean: the seats are the
  *constitution* (the where) and likely deserve a **home of their own** — they are not qualifiers but the
  structure the whole study describes (inner-life *structure* is one of the 3 orders of output) — whereas the
  framework and object terms are reference. But this is yours to settle.
