## CC assessment (2026-06-09)

**The triage mechanism works** — sensible split, interpretable signals, verifiable samples. **`ya.re` is a
worst-case term** (multi-shade fear↔reverence; **98% shade-ambiguous**), so it's the right stress test.

**The decisive number:** raw mechanical (morph only, no context heuristic) **ACCEPTs just 2%** — morph
resolves the *stem* but not the fear/reverence *shade*, so for a hard multi-shade term almost everything needs
the read *unless* context heuristics help. Context heuristics push ACCEPT to 72%. **The realistic answer lies
between, and the gap IS the calibration the researcher sets** (how far to trust the heuristics) — exactly the
judgement model.

**Signal reliability (from the samples):**
- **threat-object → dread: reliable** (1Ch 10:4 sword, 1Sa 28:5 army → dread; samples correct).
- **god-object → reverence: UNreliable** — (a) enemies also fear God *as a threat* (1Sa 4:7 "Philistines were
  afraid, a god has come" = dread, mis-tagged reverence); (b) God addressed as "you" is missed (1Ki 8:40
  "that they may fear *you*" = reverence, but god_obj didn't fire). Needs pronoun-aware detection + "god
  present ≠ reverence."
- **negation → comfort: mostly right** on imperatives ("do not fear" = dread displaced), but conflates with
  **indictment** ("did not fear God" = reverence lacking). Needs comfort-vs-indictment split.
- **both god + threat → RESEARCHER: good** — catches the genuinely-nuanced **holy-dread** ("David was afraid
  of God", 2Sa 6:9) that is neither plain terror nor plain reverence. Right to escalate these.

**Verdict:** the signals are the right *kind*; **threat-lean and both→escalate are sound**; **god-lean and
negation need refinement**. With refined signals a hard term like `ya.re` would likely land **~40–50%
escalate** — and `ya.re` is the **ceiling**: single-sense nouns (e.g. `e.mah`=terror, the batch-1 terms) are
clean ACCEPTs, so the **corpus-wide API/researcher load will be far lower**. The mechanism + the
researcher-calibrated heuristic-trust is the right design; refine the god/negation signals before scaling.
