### What Claude AI receives (the sampled export):

1. The term metadata (strongs, gloss, meaning text, flags)
2. The parsed senses from wa_meaning_sense
3. **2 representative verses per distinct usage pattern**
4. The count of how many verses each pattern represents

This gives Claude AI the **full semantic range** of the term — every distinct
way it is used in Scripture — without the redundancy of reading many verses
that all express the same meaning.

### What is NOT lost:

- Every distinct meaning/usage of the term is represented
- The statistical weight (verse count per pattern) is preserved
- Edge cases and unusual usages are captured in the 'other' patterns
- The full meaning text and parsed senses provide structural context
- Cross-testament range is preserved (early + late sample)

### What IS lost:

- Exhaustive verse listing (but counts are preserved)
- Ability to detect rare within-pattern variations
- Sequential/narrative reading across consecutive verses
- Verses that sit between two patterns (genuinely ambiguous usage)
