## 1. The two axes

- **Angle 1 — co-occurrence = *contextual* proximity** (do the clusters appear in the same verses?).
- **Angle 5 — keyword Jaccard = *semantic* kinship** (do the clusters *mean* similar things?).

Crossing them classifies every link:

| | high semantic | low semantic |
|---|---|---|
| **high co-occurrence** | **SAME-ish** — neighbours in meaning *and* context | **RELATIONAL** — appear together, mean different things |
| **low co-occurrence** | **KIN** — similar meaning, rarely co-present | (weak/independent) |

## 2. The decisive finding — most strong links are RELATIONAL, not SAME

The strongest contextual links are **not** semantic overlaps:

| Link | co-occur | kw-Jaccard | reading |
|---|---|---|---|
| Life ↔ Relational | **211** | 0.03 | the single strongest link is almost zero semantic — *life is lived in relationship* |
| Sin ↔ Repentance | 109 | 0.03 | a **narrative** sequence, not synonymy |
| Repentance ↔ Salvation | 99 | 0.05 | same — a storyline (sin→repentance→salvation) |
| Sin ↔ Righteousness | 104 | 0.04 | moral opposition co-present, not kinship |
| Fear ↔ Strength | 92 | 0.03 | two distinct faculties **operating together** in a verse |

→ **The cross-cluster fabric is mostly faculties *co-operating*, not clusters that should merge.** This is the
heart of the inner-life dynamics: how distinct faculties act together in a verse.

## 3. The three classes → three synergy treatments

1. **SAME-ish** (high both) — *candidate sub-group merges / shared characteristics.* e.g. Wisdom↔Strength
   (126, 0.07) · Sin↔Wickedness (65, 0.07) · Wisdom↔Righteousness (72, 0.08) · Grief↔Weakness (57, 0.16) ·
   Love↔Strength (90, 0.08). These genuinely overlap — synergy = consolidate / share characteristics.
2. **RELATIONAL** (high co-occur, low semantic) — *the verse-level relationships between distinct faculties.*
   e.g. Life↔Relational · Sin↔Repentance↔Salvation↔Righteousness · Fear↔Strength · Joy↔Praise. Synergy here
   is **not merging** — it is **mapping how the faculties operate together** (findings about inner-life
   dynamics). This is the bulk of the fabric.
3. **KIN** (high semantic, low co-occur) — *parallel facets / boundary distinctions.* e.g.
   Repentance↔Transformation (0.23, only 22) · Hope↔Trust · Truth↔Faith · Speech↔Prophecy · Pride↔Folly.
   They mean similar things but rarely co-occur — synergy = careful **boundary** work (are they one facet
   seen twice, or genuinely distinct?).

**So "the synergy phase" is not one operation — it is three**, keyed by link class. That is the reshaping the
researcher predicted, now evidenced.

## 4. Other correlated observations

- **Strength is a connective backbone, not a tight partner.** It co-occurs with almost everything but always
  at a *low %-of-Strength* (3–4%) and modest Jaccard — it is the large pervasive cluster threading the corpus,
  not a member of any one synergy group.
- **Shared forms are rare but sharp (37 forms span ≥2 clusters).** They mark genuine homonym/root straddles:
  **`ha.lal`** → Pride / Folly / Praise (the praise↔boast homonym), **`a.man`** → Truth / Trust (one
  faithfulness root), `tsar` → Grief / Strength / Relational. These are lexical seams, not the main driver of
  co-occurrence (most links have 0 shared forms — the fabric is verse-context, not shared vocabulary).
- **Qualifier layer (angle 2) is contaminated.** The top "qualifiers" per cluster are function-word T2 terms
  (`al, am, a.ni, lo, gam, hu`) that co-occur incidentally. The genuine qualifiers (`ophthalmos`→Repentance,
  `splanchnon`→Humility, `she.men`/oil→Praise) are a buried minority. **The T2 set still needs a
  particle/pronoun strip before the qualifier map is trustworthy.**

## 5. Caveats (honest)

- **Keyword-Jaccard is depressed in absolute terms** (0.02–0.23) because the keyword source is not yet
  normalised (full medium-def prose inflates the sets). The **ranking** is meaningful; the absolute number
  and the SAME-ish/RELATIONAL threshold are **directional**, not final. The P1 source-clean would sharpen
  every figure here.
- Co-occurrence counts a verse once per cluster-pair; it does not yet weight by how *central* each cluster is
  to the verse (that is the S3/shade depth, a later angle).

## 6. What this sets up

The synergy phase should be planned **by link class**, not cluster-by-cluster: consolidate the SAME-ish,
map the RELATIONAL dynamics, resolve the KIN boundaries. Recommended next: (a) the **qualifier particle-strip**
so angle 2 becomes usable, and (b) a **synergy-group proposal** built from the SAME-ish + KIN clusters, with
the RELATIONAL links as the cross-group dynamics map. Both read-only, for researcher review before any
synthesis writes.
