## Four distillation moves (only 3вЂ“4 need AI)
1. **Mechanical tier profile** вЂ” aggregate the option-list findings per term в†’ the quantified what-it-is
   (above). Feeds tiers T0вЂ“T3 directly. No AI.
2. **Emergent grouping** вЂ” cluster verses by **finding-signature** (origin Г— typology Г— sense Г— effect-type).
   The sub-groups *emerge* (above); refinable by adding signature dimensions. **Replaces manual VCGs /
   sub-groups** вЂ” grouping is a result, not a precondition.
3. **Behaviour distillation (AI)** вЂ” within each emergent group, cluster the free-text `immediate_response` /
   `produces_effect` / `relational_implication` into **named recurring behaviours** (threat-fear в†’ hide В·
   flee В· tremble В· paralysis; reverence в†’ moral-restraint В· obedience В· worship). **These named recurring
   behaviours ARE the "characteristic behaviours."** Feeds T1.5/T1.6.
4. **Relationship + differential (AI)** вЂ” the typed relationships across co-occurring terms in the verse
   (`A[type] вЂ”rel[effect]в†’ B[type]`) and the **differential of impact** across relationships (the ontology
   prize). Feeds T4/T5/T6.

## What this resolves
- **VCGs / sub-grouping** в†’ obsolete as *pre-imposed* structures; grouping **emerges** from finding-signatures.
- **Tier lens** в†’ holds and extends: verse `.1` findings feed the tier roll-up; the `.2/.3` "reveal/pattern"
  questions are *computed* (mechanical for T0вЂ“T3, AI for the effect/relationship tiers). The catalogue refit's
  L1/L2-vs-SYNTH split is exactly this boundary.
- **The roll-up UNIT (still open)** в†’ now **empirically testable**: does ya.re's *reverence* group behave like
  the other M01 fear-terms or like worship/reverence terms elsewhere? Distilling across clusters will *show*
  whether the cluster holds or dissolves into cross-cluster behaviour-groups вЂ” answered by data, not assertion.
