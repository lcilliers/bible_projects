## What reading the actual verses surfaced

1. **The English/gloss "corruption" actively MISLED.** The existing VCG-05 bundled **physical/cosmic decay**
   (1Cor 15 perishable, 2Cor 4:16 wasting away, Luk 12:33 moth, Rom 8:21 creation) with **moral depravity**
   (depraved mind, corrupt old self) — because they share the word "corrupt(ion)". Only reading the verses
   separates them. **The DB did *not* pre-split this** (G5356 covers both) — so unlike M10-D's avon, there
   was no lexical crutch, and the lexical grouping got it *wrong*. The read-actual-verses method got it right.
2. **The true inner core (H-1) was split** across VCG-01 (Proverbs) and VCG-05 (NT) — the perverse heart and
   the depraved mind belong together; the lexical split tore them apart.
3. **Two scope blocks fall out** (consistent with the external-pole frame, `feedback_external_pole_not_inner_state`):
   - **H-3b overthrow-to-ruin** = the **external pole** (imposed consequence), like punishment.
   - **H-4 physical/cosmic decay** = a **physical pole** (perishing of flesh/creation), not an inner state —
     a *third* axis alongside inner-core and external-pole.

## Verdict — the method holds, and is *more* valuable without the lexical crutch

Where M10-D's avon suffix had pre-separated the senses, **M10-H had none — and the gloss positively
misled** (the existing VCGs are lexically driven and conflate physical decay with moral inversion). Reading
the actual verses **separated senses the words had fused**, reunited the scattered inner core, and surfaced
two non-inner scope blocks (external-pole ruin; physical-pole decay). This is the stronger confirmation:
**the method's value is highest exactly where the early lexical/keyword signal is least reliable.**

**Emerging scope taxonomy (now 3 poles):** inner-being **core** (disposition/state) · **external pole**
(imposed consequence — punishment, overthrow, judgment) · **physical pole** (bodily/cosmic decay, perishing).
The external and physical poles are cross-referenced/handled as such, not housed as inner states.
