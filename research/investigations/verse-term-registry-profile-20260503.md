## Quick read

- **Q1** measures verse-level cross-registry overlap — high counts in N≥2 indicate verses that touch multiple inner-being characteristics simultaneously (e.g. complex psalms or prophet passages).
- **Q2** measures intra-verse term richness — verses with multiple Strong's may still belong to a single registry; this is independent of Q1.
- **Q3** measures programme-wide vocabulary sharing — the count of Strong's that have one OWNER home and ≥1 XREF copies elsewhere.
