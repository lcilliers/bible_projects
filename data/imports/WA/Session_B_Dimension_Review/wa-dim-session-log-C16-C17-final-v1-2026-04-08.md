## Cross-Cluster Pattern: C16 → C17

The shift from C16 to C17 reveals a structural pairing in the programme's inner-being architecture:

| | C16 — Sacred Identity | C17 — Covenant Relational |
|---|---|---|
| Primary dimension | Moral Character (35%) | Relational Disposition (42%) |
| Inner-being emphasis | What the person *is* before God | How the person *faces* God and others |
| GOD-dominant groups | 16 (12%) | 72 (25%) |
| Transformation pattern | Spirit-impartation, consecration | Atonement, forgiveness, reconciliation |

**Observation:** The person who *is* holy and set apart (C16) is also the person who *loves, forgives, and seeks peace* (C17). These are not competing descriptions but complementary dimensions of the same inner being. Session D synthesis should examine how character (C16) and relational orientation (C17) constitute each other in Scripture's inner-being anthropology.
