### Key analytical finding — Sin & Vice / Moral/Conscience boundary

The central analytical work of Phase C was systematically applying the distinction between:
- **Sin & Vice**: inner *condition* of moral failure (wickedness, deceit, corruption, perverseness as states — what the person *is* or *has*)
- **Moral/Conscience**: inner *evaluation* or *act* of moral assessment (verdict, condemnation, moral choice, moral turning — what is *judged* or *done*)

28 corrections were made; the majority are Moral/Conscience → Sin & Vice, where automated assignments correctly identified moral content but mislabelled conditions as evaluations.
