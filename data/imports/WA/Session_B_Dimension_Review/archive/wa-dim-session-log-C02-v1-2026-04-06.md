## 7. Emerging Cluster-Level Patterns (Preliminary — Phase C Will Develop These)

Three patterns are visible across the Phase B work so far, worth noting for Phase C orientation:

**Pattern 1 — The divine-human knowing relationship.** Multiple groups across counsel, knowledge, and insight describe either God knowing the human person or humans knowing God. The inner-being engagement is genuine in both directions, but the automated dimension vocabulary does not consistently distinguish the divine-knowing pole (Theological/Divine-Human) from the human-cognitive pole (Cognitive/Mind). Phase C will need to apply this distinction carefully.

**Pattern 2 — Cognitive-affective-relational integration.** Several groups in discernment and knowledge integrate love, knowledge, and moral discrimination in a single inner-being faculty (816-001, 817-001, 963-005). This is a cluster-level finding — C02 does not present pure cognition but cognition in relationship. This will be a significant Session B pointer.

**Pattern 3 — Cultivated vs. gifted vs. constitutive capacity.** Three different modes of cognitive endowment appear: knowledge/discernment as divine gift (952-003, 962-001), as trainable capacity (818-001), and as constitutive of humanness itself (962-002). These distinctions matter for Session B analysis of what Scripture understands the cognitive inner life to be.
