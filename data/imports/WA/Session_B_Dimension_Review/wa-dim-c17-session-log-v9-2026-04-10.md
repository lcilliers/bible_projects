## Key Analytical Observations

**Relational Disposition dominates (33/66)** — confirming that the love vocabulary is fundamentally about directed inner orientation toward another, whether divine or human. This is the single largest concentration of Relational Disposition groups in any registry processed so far.

**Moral Character is the second largest category (17/66)** — a more significant finding. The love vocabulary includes substantial content that describes love not as a felt orientation but as a stable inner character quality: love as the defining virtue (agape in 562-002), love-of-good, freedom-from-money-love, love of brothers as character disposition. The fil- compound terms in particular tend toward Moral Character when they name character traits of the person (filagathos, filarguros, filoneikia, filoneikos, filoproteuo).

**Major label correction pattern (7 corrections):** Seven Relational Disposition groups corrected to Moral Character. The automated system did not distinguish between love-as-orientation-toward-another (Relational Disposition) and love-as-character-quality-of-the-person (Moral Character). The distinction required close reading of whether the group describes the orientation (toward) or the quality (of).

**Divine-Human Correspondence appears only once (555-001):** The Father's agapetos love for the Son is the only genuine correspondence in Reg 103 — where the divine love relationship is constitutive and pattern-setting for all derivative love relationships. This is analytically significant: most groups that the automated system labelled Divine-Human Correspondence were actually Relational Disposition, GOD — divine love expressed toward humanity without a mirroring structure.

**The ye.di.dut / ra.cha.mim / dod pattern:** Three groups (535-001, 544-001, 551-001) use vocabulary that attributes visceral inner-being states to God — beloved of my soul, stirring of inner parts, my heart yearns. This raises the Session B question: does the love vocabulary of Reg 103 contain a theological claim about divine inner-being experience?
