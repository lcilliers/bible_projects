## 4. Programme-Level Finding B — Theological/Divine-Human Dimension Redefinition

**Status:** Researcher confirmed. Correction pass to be applied before Phase C.

### The drift identified

During Phase B, it became clear that the Theological/Divine-Human dimension label was drifting toward a catch-all for any group where God appears in the verse — including groups where the inner-being engagement is a human faculty directed toward God, or a human faculty that God perceives. This conflation obscures the actual structure of the inner life.

### The corrected definitions

**Theological/Divine-Human** — inner-being engagements where the *constitutive structure* of the phenomenon is the divine-human relationship itself. The soul's inner state, capacity, or experience is defined by its position within that relationship — being formed, being known, being called, being elected, being confronted with divine otherness. The phenomenon would not exist in its present form apart from the relationship as its constitutive frame.

*Clearest positive examples in C02:*
- 752-002 (boulē) — human inner life caught up in divine election and vocation
- 1073-002 (ma.cha.sha.vah) — human confrontation with the limits of comprehension before God's infinite purposes
- 936-002 (ye.tser) — creaturely constitution known and addressed by God as creature

**Spiritual/God-ward** — the soul's own faculties oriented *toward* God. The inner act originates in the soul and moves toward God as its object or direction. Seeking, fearing, trusting, worshipping, remembering, petitioning. The faculty belongs to the soul; God is its direction, not its constitutive source.

*Examples needing reassignment from Theological/Divine-Human to Spiritual/God-ward:*
- 932-002 (bin) — prayerful seeking of understanding from God — human seeking faculty
- 1180-001 (za.khar) — commanded human act of remembering God — human memory directed Godward

**The key distinction:**
- *Being constituted within* the divine-human relationship → Theological/Divine-Human
- *Directing oneself toward* God → Spiritual/God-ward

### The consolidating correction pass

Before Phase C begins, a systematic pass will be made over all C02 groups currently labelled Theological/Divine-Human (whether automated or C01-anchored) to apply the corrected definition. This pass will:

1. Identify all groups in C02 with `dimension = 'Theological/Divine-Human'`
2. Apply the test: is the inner-being phenomenon *constitutively defined* by the divine-human relationship, or is it a human faculty *directed toward* God?
3. Reassign to Spiritual/God-ward where the latter applies
4. Confirm or maintain Theological/Divine-Human where the former applies
5. Flag any C01-anchored groups that require reassignment — these require researcher confirmation per DR-8
