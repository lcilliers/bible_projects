## 9. Observations for Researcher Decision

**Three decisions are needed before further word studies are produced:**

1. **Header block:** What is the standard format for lines 2–6? The instruction does not specify this. A decision is needed and should be written into the instruction.

2. **Section 1/2 word count:** The 230–270 word spec has never been met in practice. The actual word counts across all documents range from ~290 to ~711. Either the spec should be revised (e.g. to 300–450 words), or enforcement is needed. Leaving the spec at 230–270 when no document achieves it creates a recurring problem.

3. **Section 3 structure:** Three approaches exist. The instruction specifies prose interleaved. Compassion (the largest registry) took a term-by-term approach; mercy used thematic sub-sections. Are either of those permitted alternatives for large registries, or should all documents use the interleaved prose approach?

**Existing documents that need correction before Session B proceeds on them:**
- Fellowship (062): missing Section 2 heading; header block non-standard; word count issue
- Love (103): duplicate Section 3 heading
- Compassion (023): non-standard section headings throughout
