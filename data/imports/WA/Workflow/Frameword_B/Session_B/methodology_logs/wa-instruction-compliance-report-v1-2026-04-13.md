## Part 4 — What Is Missing or Should Be Added to the Instructions

This section records gaps where a required outcome is implied by the programme architecture but no instruction specifies how to produce it.

**4.1 No instruction governs `mti_term_flags` write-back discipline.**  
Session B v4.7 states that `mti_term_flags` is the authoritative mechanism for GOD_AS_SUBJECT and somatic flags, and that `wa_term_inventory.god_as_subject` and `somatic_link` should not be updated. However, the instruction contains no step, no hard gate, and no mandatory output requiring that `mti_term_flags` records are actually written. The instruction says what not to do (don't update the redundant field) but does not mandate a step that ensures the authoritative field receives the data.
