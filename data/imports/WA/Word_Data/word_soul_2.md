### soul registry 182

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

**soul** refers to the inner animating self of a person — the seat of life, appetite, emotion, and personal identity. In Hebrew, *ne.phesh* (H5315) is the primary term, covering a wide semantic range from physical life and breath to personal desire, the self as subject, and even the dead. The related term *me.eh* (H4578, belly/bowels) connects inner emotional life to its bodily location. In Greek, *psuchē* (G5590) carries equivalent range across life, self, and personhood. The breath-word *ne.sha.mah* (H5397) marks the God-given animating breath that constitutes living existence.

**Conceptual Search Terms:** soul, life, self, person, appetite, desire, dead soul, living being, breath of life, neck, inner self
