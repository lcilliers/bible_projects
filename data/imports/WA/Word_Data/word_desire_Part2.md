### desire registry 043 --- Part 2: Greek

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

**Greek**
- **epithumia** (ἐπιθυμία) — desire, longing, craving — can be positive or negative (Luke 22:15, Gal 5:16)
- **orexis** (ὄρεξις) — appetite, desire (Rom 1:27)

**Conceptual Search Terms:** `appetite`, `craving`, `longing`, `lust`

**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Search epithumia carefully — it carries both positive desire (Luke 22:15) and sinful craving (Gal 5:16).
