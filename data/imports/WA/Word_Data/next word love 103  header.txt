### love

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`  

**Hebrew**

  - **ahav** (אָהַב) — to love — the broadest Hebrew love term (Deut 6:5, Song of Songs)
  - **chesed** (חֶסֶד) — steadfast love, lovingkindness, covenant loyalty (Ps 136)

**Greek**

  - **agape** (ἀγάπη) — unconditional, self-giving love (1 Cor 13, John 3:16, Rom 5:8)
  - **phileo** (φιλέω) — friendship love, affection (John 11:36, John 21:15-17)
  - **eros** (ἔρως) — romantic/passionate love — not in NT but present in LXX and cultural background

**Conceptual Search Terms:** `lovingkindness`, `affection`, `charity`, `steadfast love`, `covenant love`

**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Search agape and chesed as the two primary terms. 1 Cor 13 for agape's full definition. Ps 136 for chesed's covenantal range. Search ahav in OT for the broadest love vocabulary including Song of Songs.
