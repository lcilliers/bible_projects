**Greek**

- **thumos** (θυμός) — passionate wrath, outburst (Eph 4:31, Rev 15:1, Gal 5:20)

**Conceptual Search Terms:** `fury`, `rage`, `burning anger`, `hot displeasure`, `indignation`

**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Eph 4:31 lists both orge and thumos — critical to distinguish: orge is settled, deep wrath; thumos is passionate outburst. Both describe inner states. Note: anger is in your list; wrath/orge represents a deeper, more sustained form.
