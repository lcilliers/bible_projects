### zeal

> **Source list:** `Missing Inner Being Words`  
> **Category:** Affective/Emotional  

**Hebrew**

  - **qinah** (קִנְאָה) — zeal, jealousy, ardour (Ps 69:9, Num 25:11, Isa 9:7)

**Greek**

  - **zelos** (ζῆλος) — zeal, ardour (John 2:17, Rom 10:2, 2 Cor 9:2)
  - **prothumia** (προθυμία) — eagerness, willingness, zeal (2 Cor 8:11, Acts 17:11)

**Conceptual Search Terms:** `ardour`, `fervour`, `eagerness`, `enthusiasm`, `burning`

**STEP Bible Suggestion:** John 2:17 — 'zeal for your house has consumed me' — quotes Ps 69:9. Rom 10:2 — 'zeal for God, but not according to knowledge.' Zeal can be Spirit-directed or misdirected — important inner being distinction.
