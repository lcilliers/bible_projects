### fear registry 061

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

**Hebrew**
  - **yare** (יָרֵא) — to fear, be afraid, stand in awe — covers both terror and reverence (Prov 1:7, Gen 3:10)
  - **pachad** (פַּחַד) — dread, terror, sudden fear (Job 3:25)
**Greek**
  - **phobos** (φόβος) — fear, terror, reverence — covers both (Rom 8:15, 1 John 4:18, Acts 9:31)
  - **deilia** (δειλία) — cowardice, timidity, enslaving fear (2 Tim 1:7)
  - **eulabeia** (εὐλάβεια) — godly fear, reverence (Heb 5:7, 12:28)
**Conceptual Search Terms:** `reverence`, `awe`, `dread`, `terror`, `cowardice`
**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Search yare in OT — it carries both terror and reverent awe. Distinguish phobos (general fear/reverence) from deilia (enslaving fear) and eulabeia (godly reverence) in NT. 1 John 4:18 on fear and love is a key analytical text.
