### Set-aside rationale

### Groups

**7542-002 — "eschatological transformation of the person's constitution"**
- 1Cor 15:51 — we shall all be changed (resurrection transformation of the whole person)
- 1Cor 15:52 — elaboration of same eschatological event
- **Anchor: 1Cor 15:51** — declaratory statement; 15:52 is elaboration of same event

### Session B note
allassō functions as a property term across two distinct characteristics: volitional relational reorientation (7542-001) and eschatological constitutional transformation (7542-002). Session B should note that allassō does not name a single inner-being state but the *mechanism* of change operating across different characteristics. Relevant to evidential status assignment.
