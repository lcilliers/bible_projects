- **H5068/H5069 (willing)**: The willing/freewill disposition — the heart spontaneously moved — is one of the clearest inner-being terms in the Hebrew corpus for Registry 112.
- **H5588 (divided) and G1374 (double-minded)**: Two terms naming inner division of allegiance — a significant pattern across both testaments.
