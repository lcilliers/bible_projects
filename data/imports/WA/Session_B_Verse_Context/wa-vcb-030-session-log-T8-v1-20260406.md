## 3. Group and anchor detail per term

### Term 1 — H3513H ka.ved (to honor: heavy) | 4 groups

| Group | Code | Description | Anchor(s) |
|---|---|---|---|
| A | 1864-001 | Volitional or divinely imposed hardening of the heart — capacity for responsive obedience becomes weighted/resistant | Exo 8:15 (143656), Exo 10:1 (143654) |
| B | 1864-002 | Moral gravity of sin/iniquity as a weight borne by person or community | Psa 38:4 (143675) |
| C | 1864-003 | Dulling/weighting of the inner faculty of spiritual hearing and understanding | Isa 6:10 (143665) |
| D | 1864-004 | Inner weight of suffering, anguish, or divine discipline experienced by the person | Psa 32:4 (143674) |

**Note:** This term produced the richest inner-being profile in the "heavy" semantic range. The heart-hardening group (A) is particularly significant — it shows *kbd* as a descriptor of the will's resistance to God, both as a human act (Exo 8:15, 8:32, 9:34) and a divine action (Exo 10:1, 9:7; 1Sa 6:6 negative example). Group C (Isa 6:10) stands alone as the only instance where *kbd* applies to the faculty of spiritual hearing/understanding rather than the will.
