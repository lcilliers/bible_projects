All 54 verses relevant. The eucharist word group is semantically cohesive; every occurrence engages the inner being directly. Rom 1:21 selected as co-anchor for G2168 — the failure of thanksgiving as the root of inner corruption makes the inner-being necessity of gratitude most explicit. 1Th 5:18 as the normative command. Phili 4:6 anchors G2169 — thanksgiving as the inner posture accompanying all petition.

### R070 — greed
G0724 (harpagē): all 3 relevant; greed named as inner condition of the cup/person (Mat 23:25). G0726 (harpazō): split into two groups — (1) threat-snatching against the inner being (Mat 13:19 heart anchor); (2) divine catching-away (1Th 4:17 anchor). Set asides: Joh 6:15, Act 8:39, Act 23:10 (physical/geographical/procedural). G4123 (pleonektēs): all 4 relevant; Eph 5:5 anchor — greed as idolatry.

### R071 — grief

- H0056: 13 set aside (land/earth/vine/mountain mourning in Isaiah, Jeremiah, Hosea, Joel, Amos)
- H2483: 13 set aside (physical illness without inner-being engagement, Deuteronomy through Chronicles)
- H6937: 9 set aside (cosmic darkening: skies, sun, moon, stars)
- H4843: 3 set aside (physical attack, taste, Exo 23:21 span anomaly)
- H3510: 2 set aside (Gen 34:25 physical soreness, 2Ki 3:19 agricultural destruction)
- H6001A: 2 set aside (literal labourer, practical appetite observation)
- H0057: 1 set aside (Lam 1:4 personified roads)

Notable groupings:
- H2470I: split — grievous wound/weakness vs. absence of grief as moral failure (Amo 6:6 anchor for group 2)
- H4843: split — bitter grief vs. bitter fury (Dan 8:7, 11:11 as enraged inner state)
- H6040: split — God seeing affliction and responding vs. person's inner experience of affliction
- H6937 anchor: Job 30:28 "darkened but not by the sun" — inner mourning-darkness explicitly distinguished from physical darkness; most distinctive

No researcher decisions required across all three registries.
