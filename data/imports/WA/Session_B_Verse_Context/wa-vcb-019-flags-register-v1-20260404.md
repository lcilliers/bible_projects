### DF-001 | H5704 (ad) — till | mti_id=1236 | Registry 173 (will)
**Verse count:** 260
**Finding / Issue:** After full individual inspection of all 260 verses, every verse fails the relevance filter. *Ad* is a Hebrew preposition/adverb meaning "until / as far as / to the extent of." **The §3.5 test:** Remove *ad* mentally from each verse — does the inner-being content change meaningfully? In no verse does the inner-being content change. The inner-being states in surrounding verses (anger, longing, faithfulness, grief) are carried by other terms; *ad* provides only the syntactic link between clauses.

**Representative verses inspected:**

vid=48334 Gen 27:44: *"and stay with him a while, until your brother's fury turns away"* — the fury (inner-being) belongs to another term; *ad* is the temporal connector.

vid=48450 Job 27:5: *"till I die I will not put away my integrity from me"* — integrity is the inner-being term; *ad* marks the temporal limit.

vid=48367 Hab 1:2: *"O Lord, how long shall I cry for help, and you will not hear?"* — *ad* in the "how long" construction; the anguish is in the cry, not in the particle.

vid=48403 Isa 42:4: *"He will not grow faint or be discouraged till he has established justice in the earth"* — perseverance and discouragement are in other terms; *ad* connects them to an endpoint.

vid=48373 Hos 5:15: *"until they acknowledge their guilt and seek my face"* — guilt and seeking are inner-being; *ad* is the conditional threshold marker.

**Option A (CONFIRM):** All 260 verses confirmed all-verses-fail. No verse_context records inserted. Term proceeds with Rule R4 not applicable.
