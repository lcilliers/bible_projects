## Classification Decisions and Researcher Inputs

### G0019 — single group warranted
All 4 verses name goodness as Spirit-produced or divinely completed inner disposition. No grouping ambiguity. Anchor: Gal 5:22 (vr_id=24449).

### G5544 — two groups
Two distinguishable aspects: (1) kindness as God's inner disposition; (2) kindness as Spirit-produced quality of the believer. Anchors: Rom 11:22 (vr_id=24750) for group 1; Gal 5:22 (vr_id=24749) for group 2.

### H2896A — researcher decision at grouping stage

**Decision sought and received:** The large cluster of Proverbs and wisdom "better-than" comparative sayings (where the superior thing is an inner or relational quality) was identified as a structurally distinct use of ṭôḇ. The question was whether to distribute these by sub-type or treat them as a single "comparative wisdom good" group.

**Researcher decision (2026-04-01):** Single group — "comparative wisdom good" — is appropriate. All better-than sayings where inner or relational qualities are the superior value are grouped together under 884-004.

### H2896A — six groups established

|---|---|---|

| 884-002 | Human moral character and conduct as good | Mic 6:8 |

### H2896A — set-aside rationale
Material quality (land, animals, food, gold), physical appearance, procedural social approval ("if it pleases the king"), purely strategic assessments, and life-stage descriptors ("good old age") were set aside consistently across all books. Esther block (17 verses) set aside almost entirely as courtly procedural formulae. Genesis creation block (7 verses) set aside as divine assessment of material/cosmic creation, not inner-being.
