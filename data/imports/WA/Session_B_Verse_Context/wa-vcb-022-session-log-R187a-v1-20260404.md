The 4-group count was assessed before adding further groups; consolidation was considered and partially enacted (empowerment verses moved from Group 3 to Group 1).

**H2423 che.va (beast):** Two materially distinct patterns: Nebuchadnezzar's human-to-beast cognitive/spiritual debasement (Dan 4–5), and the apocalyptic kingdom-beasts of Dan 7. The distinction is significant — one is about the individual inner being under divine discipline, the other about the collective inner character of God-defying powers.
