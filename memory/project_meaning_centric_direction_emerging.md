**Emerging design direction, 2026-06-05 (researcher reflection — not yet settled, evidence pending).**

The realisation: a disciplined verse-meaning + keyword pass may **encapsulate most of the sub-group/VCG
grouping work**. Three observations:
1. **Grouping is largely encapsulated by interpretation + keywording** — sub-groups (faculty axes) and VCGs
   (similar-meaning clusters) *derive* from the keyword pool, which was built to be exactly that signal
   ([[feedback_phase2_passa_emits_keywords]]).
2. **Open questions (from the Seven-Principles augmentation) are a key driver** — textual / lexical-contested
   / reference-fulfilment / unclear-needs-corpus questions surface per verse and drive the process; they are
   the surfaced contested readings (foundations §c) and the verse-meaning-corroboration worklist
   ([[project_next_action_audit_surface_verses]]).
3. **Unclear verse → clarified by other verses, at the meaning stage** — analogy of Scripture (Principle 6) +
   the term's meaning corpus; resolve during Pass A, not downstream.

**Why it's attractive:** reads the verse **once, richly** instead of three times through the cluster lens —
directly attacks the bias-compounding [[feedback_two_governing_principles]] was created for; treats grouping
as a **derived view** (scaffolding-not-reality, [[feedback_no_forced_structure_audit_surfaces_analysis_compensates]]);
and is a candidate answer to the foundations' central unit-of-analysis question (§c-Q7) — emergent grouping
is less of a false partition than imposed grouping.

**Candidate Pass A additions:** emit `open_questions` (typed); cross-verse clarification step; formalise
sub-groups/VCGs as generated-then-confirmed, not authored fresh. **Risks:** Pass A cost/complexity rise;
storage for open-questions + clarification links; how much grouping truly derives vs needs judgment.

**Status / next:** NOT a redesign decision. Validate on a ~15–20-verse M01 slice by hand (does grouping fall
out of the keywords? how often do open-questions fire? cost?). Evidence before architecture. The **span
pairing rule itself ([[feedback_span_pairing_and_reciprocal_findings]]) IS settled and fundamental**; this
broader meaning-centric shape is the open exploration it triggered. Design note:
`research/investigations/pass-a-meaning-centric-direction-20260605.md`.
