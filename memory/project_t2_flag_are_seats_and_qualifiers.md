**Surfaced 2026-06-09** when the researcher noticed meaning paragraphs in the **T2** and **FLAG** buckets in
the M15 gate report and asked to *see the content before any decision*. (CC had proposed a too-blunt
"supersede T2/FLAG" — researcher correctly stopped it; lesson reinforces [[feedback_integrity_and_intent_first]]
and [[feedback_no_forced_structure_audit_surfaces_analysis_compensates]]: examine actual content before
proposing disposition of a whole data category.)

**What the buckets actually hold (three kinds mixed):**
- **Constitutional SEATS** (mostly FLAG) — the *where* the inner life operates: **heart** (lev/le.vav/kardia),
  **soul** (ne.phesh), **flesh** (sarx). Core vocabulary; the verse-read gave them rich, correct meanings
  (heart "made faint", the "inner self from which decision issues"). These are the constitutional location
  layer ([[feedback_external_pole_not_inner_state]] — and the `constitutional_location` field is the verse-
  level capture of exactly this).
- **Qualifiers** (mostly T2) — the *how*: body/faculty the characteristic runs through (yad "hands failed" =
  courage/agency collapse; qe.rev "inward part" = seat; ra.ah/o.zen = perceptual gateway into the affect).
  These realise [[feedback_qualifier_routes_per_verse_occurrence]] and [[feedback_transitive_faculty_verb_is_qualifier]].
- **Framework terms** (FLAG) — covenant (be.rit), justice (mish.pat), name (shem): substantive but not
  inner-being characteristics.
- **Intensifiers** — me.od "exceedingly" (degree-qualifier only).
- **Pure grammatical NOISE** (T2) — et (accusative marker), gam/lo/hen/de: the only genuinely disposable set.

**Implication.** The buckets are not "non-characteristics to delete" — they are the **constitutional-seat
(where) and qualifier (how) layers** that the M-characteristics (the *what*) operate through; valuable, even
essential, to the eventual synthesis. The verse-read in-scope filter (`cluster_code IS NOT NULL`) only
over-reaches on the **pure grammatical particles**. Going-forward scope + disposition of the already-written
T2/FLAG paragraphs are PENDING the researcher's taxonomy decision — do not act.
