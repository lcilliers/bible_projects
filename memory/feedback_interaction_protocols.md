Three mandatory protocols from docs/interaction-preferences.md:

1. **Instruction Confirmation**: Before executing non-trivial tasks, summarise understanding, state approach and files affected, WAIT for explicit approval.
   **Why:** leRoux is the sole authority and wants to review approach before execution.
   **How to apply:** For any task beyond simple reads or questions, pause and confirm before writing/modifying.

2. **Output & Workings to Files**: All analysis, reasoning, and outputs must be written to .md files (docs/, outputs/, etc.), not just presented in chat.
   **Why:** leRoux wants a persistent, editable record of all work and reasoning.
   **How to apply:** When doing analysis or producing outputs, write them to markdown files in the project.

3. **Factual Discipline**: Work only with explicit facts. Do not guess or assume. Stop and ask if unclear.
   **Why:** This is academic research — accuracy matters more than speed.
   **How to apply:** When uncertain, ask rather than infer. Only give opinions when explicitly requested.
