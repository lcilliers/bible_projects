**Researcher (2026-06-09), on the L2 triage ([[feedback_l2_mechanical_api_triage]]).**

**Clarifications are reusable.** The triage doesn't just sort verses — it discovers **disambiguation rules
("clarifications")** that generalise: once a clarification is validated (e.g. *fear with a threat-object →
dread*), it applies **mechanically to other terms/verses**. Hard cases resolved by API/researcher get
distilled into clarifications that raise the mechanical ACCEPT rate over time — a growing **clarification
library** (the resolver's handled-variation catalogue; cf.
[[feedback_l1_l2_is_multi_angle_report_then_synthesise]]).

**The validity test = "state, don't induce" (researcher caution).** A clarification is legitimate **only if it
READS a signal genuinely present in the verse**; it becomes a **forced finding** the instant it **INDUCES**
the answer from a proxy.
- *valid:* `fear + threat-object → dread` — the threat is in the verse ("draw your sword… afraid").
- *invalid (induction):* `God-mentioned → reverence` — induced from mere mention; the verse may show dread
  (1Sa 4:7 "Philistines were afraid, a god has come" = terror). This is exactly why that signal was
  unreliable on `ya.re` — it was a **forced finding**.

**The floor rule (applies at every level):** **if the verse does not resolve a tier question, the finding
STATES it (unresolved / silent) — never induced.** So the triage outcomes are: **ACCEPT** (a valid
clarification read a present signal, or clean single-sense) · **ESCALATE** (read *harder* — API/researcher —
for what might genuinely be in the verse) · **STATE-SILENT** (if even the read finds the verse doesn't settle
it, the finding is e.g. *"fear, shade unstated"* — a valid honest finding). Connects to
[[feedback_coverage_flags_philosophy]] (silence is significant), [[feedback_no_forced_structure_audit_surfaces_analysis_compensates]],
and the catalogue's own `.3` "if silent, note explicitly." **Corrects the `ya.re` read:** the "72% with
heuristics" was partly forced (god-lean inductions); honest ACCEPT = clean + genuinely-signalled
(threat-dread, comfort-negation), the rest STATES unresolved or escalates.
