**Governing principle (researcher, 2026-06-04).** The AI compulsion to create structure — *it must
have a home, it must belong to only one, it must be completed, it must reconcile* — has caused
recurring errors **from the start of this research**. Resist it. The subject is **human inner life and
behaviour: unpredictable, not systematisable, infinitely variable.** The study must **compensate for
that**, not force the data into clean exclusive boxes or tick-box completion. Findings driven by
"force completion / make the gate clean" are mechanical and false to the subject.

**The "remediation" frame is largely invalid.** It is NOT a programme of mass reassignment / routing /
finding-a-home. The only genuinely *remedial* essentials are:
- **(a) No orphans.** No object (term, verse, finding, pointer) is excluded from *all* analysis.
  Everything stays in view (the FLAG holding cluster, the audit, etc. serve this).
- **(b) The audit surfaces EVERYTHING.** The audit's job is to highlight **all objects related to a
  cluster that are not yet properly analysed and synthesised into the findings** — comprehensively,
  including objects that also belong to other clusters. It is a completeness/visibility instrument,
  **not** a worklist to zero out.

**When the audit shows outstanding items → holistic re-evaluation, not reassignment.** The next step is
to **re-read the cluster as a whole** and consider the *impact of the incomplete items on the analysis
as a whole** — does the cluster's synthesis adequately account for them? This may yield new findings,
may recognise genuine multi-cluster belonging, and may legitimately leave things open. It is ANALYSIS
of the whole, never per-item disposition.

**Allow what the subject requires:** multi-belonging (same verse in many clusters), ambiguity,
unresolved/open questions. Do not force closure.

**My failure pattern to watch (2026-06-04):** building scripts and chasing A6/A7→0, treating items as
rows to relocate, proposing "homes" and exclusivity. When I feel the pull to impose structure or
"complete" a cluster, STOP — that pull is the error. Supersedes/limits the mechanical framing in
[[feedback_term_is_the_unit_of_movement]] and [[feedback_remediation_is_analysis_not_reassignment]]:
those move toward analysis, but this is the frame — audit surfaces, analysis (holistic, human-led)
responds, nothing is forced. Built on [[feedback_two_governing_principles]] and
[[feedback_analytical_phase_vs_editorial_phase]].
