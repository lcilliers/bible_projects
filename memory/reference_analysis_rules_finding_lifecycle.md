The analysis rules settled in `wa-study-foundations.md` §c (2026-06-04). Governs all analytic work:

- **The finding is the universal unit of record.** Every output of analysis — each of the 189
  question-answers, every cluster-level/general observation, and the outputs of pointer/flag/synergy
  work — is captured **as a finding**. The **DB findings table is the single authoritative record for
  the whole study; nothing exists outside it.** (Hard storage form of [[feedback_two_governing_principles]].)
- **8 tiers, 189 questions, per sub-group = the consistency mechanism.** Tiers T0–T7: Divine Image ·
  Definition · Constitutional Location · Inner Faculties · Relational Interfaces · Formative/Developmental ·
  Structural Relationships · Evidential/Methodological. Names now match the live `wa_obs_question_catalogue`
  (the 189-question extract). Sub-group bundles data; meaning still derives from the verses.
- **Completeness floor + silence-valid.** A sub-group is structurally complete when every finding states
  its evidence, all anchor verses are covered by a finding, and all 189 are answered — **a "the text is
  silent" answer counts.** Silence-valid is the release valve against completion-pressure; the audit must
  ACCEPT silence, never chase a fill. (Consistent with [[feedback_no_forced_structure_audit_surfaces_analysis_compensates]]
  and [[feedback_coverage_flags_philosophy]].)
- **All findings are drafts; robustness is earned, not asserted.** Anything from analytics enters as a
  finding (no draft label). The study revisits the findings corpus repeatedly to sift out the
  unsubstantiated/thin — **mostly interactive researcher↔AI reading/review/rewriting**, light
  filtering/extraction only. A finding earns standing by withstanding deeper questioning.
- **Cluster done only by non-mechanical sign-off.** The audit verifies rule-compliance (important), but
  completion comes only when the researcher signs off — a reading judgement, never a gate cleared.
- **Multi-belonging** = a finding in EACH cluster it genuinely implicates; wording may coincide in the
  exploratory stage but is expected to diverge as synergy work matures (not a false copy).
- **Characteristics follow the evidence; final form open.** Discovered not imposed; how they land is
  undecided, clarifying as most clusters reach thorough analysis.
- **Set-aside is reversible** soft-delete, re-examined later; stays part of the term's semantic record
  ([[feedback_setaside_verses_inform_word_meaning]]). Nothing physically removed.
- **Unit-of-analysis (Q7, unresolved):** the cluster is an accepted working compromise (big enough for
  synergy, small enough for traction), NOT a claim about the subject — contend with the "jack out of the box".
