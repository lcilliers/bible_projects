The study's **definition and governing principles are documented** (programme prose: the "purpose"
area and the nine methodological principles — `Workflow/Programme/programme_prose/wa-programme-prose-extract-*.md`).
Read these before reasoning about scope/method; do not re-derive from memory. (Researcher, 2026-06-04:
my questions kept revealing I had not digested principles "stated many times in the documentation.")

**Definition — the "Soul Word Analysis Programme."** Takes the registry of inner-being words (scope
defined against a working definition of an inner-being characteristic) and examines each: what the term
means across Hebrew/Greek originals, how it occurs across its verses, what contexts they share, what it
contributes to the account of the inner person. **Three orders of output:** (1) per-word records +
studies; (2) cross-word **syntheses** (relationships, recurrent patterns); (3) programme-wide
**bottom-up account of the human inner being as Scripture reveals it**. The object of study
**encompasses all three** — vocabulary AND inner life AND relationships; do not split it into "zones"
(that is the narrowing reflex).

**The nine methodological principles (verbatim sense):**
1. **Registry completeness against the inner-being definition** — scope is a word list as complete as
   evidence permits before any word is studied; completeness is an objective with accepted margin of
   error (never 100%); the register stays open to new concepts.
2. **Data collation separated from analysis** — full data collated before analysis; every word read
   against the same completed body.
3. **Words are NOT forced into preconceived categories** — dimensions/groups/findings emerge from the
   verse evidence; a category not grounded in verse evidence is not used. (Clusters & characteristics
   are **disposable scaffolding, not reality** — registry → C-class → M-clusters were all scaffolding.)
4. **Verses qualify by original-language occurrence, not interpretation** — lexical presence admits a
   verse, irrespective of meaning/contribution.
5. **Every finding is substantiated by data — NO guessing, no assumptions, no made-up results.**
   Synthesis without quoted evidence is guessing; own opinion without cited evidence is not allowed at
   all. Inconclusive/multiple readings are **surfaced, not hidden**.
6. **Analysis reads the data as a whole** — the standing catalogue of questions is applied in full to
   every word; no cherry-picking angles.
7. **The database is the analytical memory** — every answer persisted; on re-analysis the prior finding
   is set aside and the data read freshly.
8. **Biblical lens primary, scientific lens secondary** — Scripture first; a **light-touch** science
   pass follows, governed by the Science Framework / science-topics policy + a per-cluster
   **science-extract pre-reading** (`Workflow/Sciences/wa-m{NN}-…-scienceextract`). See [[feedback_phase9_science_extract_required]].
9. **Synthesis follows analysis — bottom-up, not top-down.**
