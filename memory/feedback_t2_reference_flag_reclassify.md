**Researcher governing decision (2026-06-10), after interrogating how T2 spans are handled in the verse-read.**
Resolves the disposition left open in [[project_t2_flag_are_seats_and_qualifiers]].

**T2 = a reference, never a subject of analysis.**
- T2 terms (qualifiers — the *how*: hand→courage, inward-part→seat, perception→gateway) are a **reference
  point used inside other clusters**, never analysed on their own.
- A T2 term is engaged **only where an M-cluster verse uses it**, and there its meaning is **embedded in the
  cluster term's analysis, not recorded against T2**. → **Do not write standalone T2 findings/paragraphs.**
  The qualifier's implication belongs inside the co-occurring characteristic's meaning.
- Evidenced bidirectional incorporation (Ezr 4:4): the characteristic (ba.hal) names "the weakening of their
  hands"; the qualifier (yad) names "in consequence of ba.hal (terror)." Mechanisms: co-occurrence array fed
  to the read · the `compound` field · cross-referencing prose. ([[feedback_span_pairing_and_reciprocal_findings]],
  [[feedback_qualifier_routes_per_verse_occurrence]].)

**Corroboration holds (verified).** Every T2/FLAG paragraph-verse so far (1063/1063) contains an M-cluster
term; **0** were processed without a corroborating inner-being term. We drive by M-clusters and must **never**
run T2 as a standalone driver (that would interpret a qualifier's purely-physical verses). The
**participation gate**: within a corroborated verse, persist meanings only for terms that actually participate
in the inner-being content; **skip bystander terms** (pure particles like `et`, repeated social terms like
`am`) — 264/1759 (15%) self-declare "no inner faculty engaged."

**FLAG is the opposite case.** FLAG is a holding pen. **If a FLAG term carries inner-being meaning it almost
certainly belongs in a CLUSTER — it is no longer FLAG.** Move it to its home; **where no home exists, open a
debate** on where it fits (e.g. the heart/soul/flesh constitutional *seats* may need a home of their own).
Distinct from T2 (which is a reference, not a homeless characteristic).

**Implementation (pending):** the verse-read CC-mode must (a) scope to M-clusters as drivers, (b) embed T2
qualifier implications in the cluster term's meaning rather than write T2 paragraphs, (c) apply the
participation gate, (d) the existing 945 standalone T2 paragraphs are now out-of-rule → retire (reversible)
when the CC-mode lands. FLAG terms → triage to cluster homes / debate. Spec: wa-verse-read-meaning-plan-v1.
