**Researcher direction 2026-06-05**, resolving the meaning-centric exploration after the 4-verse reality
test ([[feedback_term_corpus_anchors_meaning]]) showed cold per-verse meaning is biased 4/4.

**The diagnosis:** we were **driving the verse meaning too soon**. The unit of data is **not the verse on
its own** — it is **the application of the term within the context of the verse**. Deriving a per-verse
meaning early means every downstream stage then reads that **skewed early meaning** instead of the actual
verses, and the bias compounds (Pass A → sub-group → VCG → findings — read through the lens three times,
[[feedback_two_governing_principles]]).

**The restructure (cuts the early bias):**
- **Phase A = light touch.** Its ONLY job is to **identify similarities between verses** so they can be
  grouped. It does **not** commit a full interpretive meaning per verse. (Light/surface signal — atomic
  keywords / lexical / span+term features; the exact Phase-A signal is an open design point.)
- **VCG stage = the real reading.** A VCG is read by **reading the ACTUAL VERSES** in the group together —
  **not** a pre-derived meaning — comparing and clarifying each against the others **in context**. The
  understanding/meaning **emerges here, late and grounded**, from the actual verses read as a group.

**Why it works:** reading similar verses together is exactly what disambiguates valence and sense (the
term-corpus remedy, [[feedback_term_corpus_anchors_meaning]]); doing it at VCG — on actual verses, not a
skewed early meaning — removes the early-commitment bias and lets verses be evaluated in context.

**Implications / how to apply:**
- The deep **verse-analysis methodology** (`wa-verse-analysis-methodology.md`: span-focal Seven Principles,
  span influence-test, surroundings, clarify-by-corpus, open questions) is **VCG-stage work on grouped
  actual verses — not Phase A.** Phase A is lighter.
- Resolves the keyword worry: light/atomic keywords can be the **Phase-A similarity signal**; the rich
  interpretive meaning is derived at VCG from the actual verses (the two-role keyword split maps here).
- Refines [[project_meaning_centric_direction_emerging]]: the heavy analysis is the VCG read of actual
  verses; Phase A grouping is the light pre-pass (provisional, refined by the actual reading).
- Span handling ([[feedback_span_pairing_and_reciprocal_findings]]) — the influence test is a VCG-stage
  judgement (made while reading the actual verses), not an early per-verse call.
