**Dry-ran M10 Sin · M23 Strength · M25 Life · M44 Relational through the L2 writer (2026-06-09), faculty
un-asserted (4 structural tiers), to learn from more clusters.** Did **not** live-write — the dry-run served
as a review gate and caught a real gap.

**Learnings:**
1. **Escalation is rare and concentrated.** M10/M25/M44 = **100% mechanical ACCEPT** (0 escalation); M23
   Strength = 7%; only **verb stem-shade** terms (`ya.re`, a few M23) escalate. So the **corpus-wide
   API/researcher load is low** — confirms `ya.re` is near the ceiling. SB-load varies sharply (Sin 500,
   Life 403, Strength 196, Relational 120).
2. **Lexical findings surface CLUSTERING MISFITS.** `ta.cha.rah` glossed *"to burn, be kindled (of anger)"*
   sits in **M44 Relational** — an Anger term mis-clustered; the per-term lexical finding makes it visible.
   Honest findings expose bad clustering — a valuable byproduct for cluster cleanup.
3. **PROSE-EMBEDDED POLYSEMY is under-detected (the real gap).** `pneuma` was ACCEPTed as *"wind, breath…"*
   for **Mat 1:18** ("conceived of the Holy **Spirit**"). Its sense_text is one prose block —
   *"wind, breath … by extension: spirit … God the Holy Spirit"* — with **no numbered/stem markers**, so the
   triage (which catches verb stem-shades) can't see the polysemy and takes the head RANGE. **The "100%
   ACCEPT" is partly false-confidence**: broad-gloss polysemous terms get the lexical *range* ACCEPTed instead
   of the *verse-specific* sense, which is induction ([[feedback_clarifications_reusable_but_state_not_induce]]).

**The mechanical lexical pass is reliable for** single-sense terms + verb stem-shades; **it is NOT reliable
for prose-embedded polysemy** (pneuma-class). **Fix (next refinement): a broad-gloss-polysemy adequacy
signal** — escalate terms whose gloss spans multiple distinct senses / carries "by extension" / "figuratively"
/ multiple semantic domains → STATED_UNRESOLVED, not ACCEPT. Extends the triage signals
([[feedback_l2_mechanical_api_triage]]). Backup `bible_research_pre_l2_multi_20260609.db`; dry-run reports in
`research/investigations/wa-l2-write-{M10,M23,M25,M44}-dryrun-v1-20260609.md` (kept as evidence).
