## Cost reality

Full execution of all 14 catch-up essays is a substantial undertaking. The 3 chapter-synthesis cluster essays are tractable in one session; the 11 direct-from-findings essays — particularly M04, M06, M07, M10, M10b — each individually approach M11's authoring cost. Doing all 14 in a single session is not realistic.

**Pragmatic interpretation of "bring all publishing Session C up to date":**

- Execute the **3 chapter-synthesis clusters now** (M03, M01, M15) — proves the method on existing drafts, produces 3 immediately-publishable .docx outputs.
- Execute the **3 small direct-from-findings clusters now** (M39, M46, M02) — exercises the direct method on tractable evidence bases.
- **Pause** at that point to review the 6 outputs.
- **Schedule the remaining 8** (M20, M26, M10c, M08, M07, M10b, M06, M04, M10) across subsequent sessions.

This is the path that respects the cost guidance ([feedback_methodology_cycle_cost]) while delivering meaningful progress today.
