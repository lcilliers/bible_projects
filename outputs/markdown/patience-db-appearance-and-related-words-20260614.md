- **description**: Patience is the inner quality of continuing to wait and trust without being unravelled by delay or difficulty. The Hebrew vocabulary here includes silence, the stilling of inner turmoil in the face of what is hard; waiting in the sense of active, expectant trust; and the weariness that must be borne without giving up. The Greek adds the sense of holding back under pressure. Patience is not passive but active: it is the continuous choice to keep trusting while the situation remains unresolved. It is counted among the fruits of the Spirit and the character of God.
