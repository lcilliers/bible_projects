### anxiety
**Hebrew**
  - **daag** (דָּאַג) — to be anxious, to worry (1 Sam 9:5, Jer 17:8)
  - **deagah** (דְּאָגָה) — anxiety, worry, care (Jer 49:23, Ezek 4:16)
**Greek**
  - **merimna** (μέριμνα) — anxiety, worry, care (Matt 6:25-34, Phil 4:6, 1 Pet 5:7)
  - **merimnao** (μεριμνάω) — to be anxious, to worry (Luke 10:41, 1 Cor 7:32-34)
**Conceptual Search Terms:** `worry`, `care`, `concern`, `troubled`, `distracted`
**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Search merimna and merimnao in NT — Matt 6:25-34 is the foundational text. Phil 4:6 presents the antidote. The root meaning (divided mind) is significant. In OT search daag in Jeremiah and Samuel.
