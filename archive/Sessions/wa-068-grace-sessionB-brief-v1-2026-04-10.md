## 2. Meaning findings

**Primary semantic picture:** Grace is the disposition of a greater toward a lesser that gives freely and without merit in the recipient. The Hebrew *chen* and Greek *charis* together constitute a complete semantic system: the disposition (chen/charis), the act (charizō), the state of having been graced (charitoō — biblical coinage), the structural logic of uncaused giving (chinnam — for nothing), and the creature's appeal to the disposition (tachanum — supplication, from the same root as chen).

**Four semantic faces of charis** — identified as a structure for the first time in Stage 2:
1. Divine disposition toward humanity (the theological ground)
2. Relational standing created in the recipient (the sphere in which one stands)
3. Specific capacity gifted for function (grace as enabling endowment)
4. Gracious character quality expressed outward (grace as inner character)

These are not separate phenomena but grace working its way through the inner person from spirit to soul to expression.

**Root connection:** charis/chairo (grace/joy) share the χαρ- root. Grace and joy are not merely theologically related — they share a lexical source.

**Key tension:** charis faces 2 and 3 create the standing-vs-enabling question: is grace received once (as standing) or continuously (as enabling capacity)? The data shows both — 888-002 (standing) and 888-004 (enabling) are distinct groups with distinct verse corpora.

**Senses underrepresented in existing analysis:** chinnam as the structural logic of grace (for-nothing, without cause); the four-face architecture as a named structure; the acharistos (graceless/ungrateful) vocabulary as the inner failure mode; tachanum as grace-produced supplication (Zec 12:10 paradox).

## 3. Divine dimension summary

**Dominant pattern:** God gives grace first, unilaterally, and without condition. All five owner terms confirm GOD as the primary actor. Human grace (5470-002: forgiving; 889-002: interpersonal favour; 889-003: gracious character) is consistently named as derivative — patterned on and enabled by prior divine grace.

**Divine-human relationship:** Derivative-and-responsive throughout. The human characteristic is not independently generated. The one exception in structure is 5470-002 (human forgiving) — but even there, "as God in Christ forgave you" makes the human act explicitly downstream.

**Key divine dimension finding — simultaneity (Dan 9:23):** God's response to supplication preceded the completion of Daniel's plea — "at the beginning of your pleas a word went out." Seeking and being-found are simultaneous, not sequential. The creature's orientation toward God and God's movement toward the creature happen in the same moment.

**Eschatological dimension:** Eph 2:7 — "in the coming ages he might show the immeasurable riches of his grace." Grace has a forward-pointing structure built in: what has been received is real but partial; the fullness is promised. Grace as present standing is inherently future-oriented.

## 4. Somatic signature

**Primary somatic markers:**
- **Eye** (chen — ~20/67 verses): The eye is the systematic somatic encoding of grace in Hebrew. "Found favour in the eyes of..." — to be in grace is to be within the favourable gaze of the greater party. Being-seen constitutes being-in-grace.
- **Face** (chen and ta.cha.nun): Prostration before the one whose favour is received (Rut 2:10); face turned toward God in earnest appeal (Dan 9:3,17); face transformed by received favour (1Sa 1:18).
- **Inner parts / heart** (eusplanchnos, kardia): tenderheartedness as the somatic organ of grace-transmission (Eph 4:32); heart as the seat of grace-reception (Rom 6:17, 2Cor 8:16).
- **Full-body postures** (Dan 9:3): fasting + sackcloth + ashes + turned face — the whole body enacting the inner posture of supplication simultaneously.
- **Flesh** (Joh 1:14): The incarnation as the supreme somatic event — divine grace taking bodily form.
- **Mouth/lips** (Luk 4:22, Psa 45:2, Ecc 10:12): Grace expressed through gracious speech.

**Somatic pattern:** Grace originates at the spirit level (received from God), works through the heart (soul level), and is expressed through face, eye, hands, mouth, and bodily posture (body level). Body is consistently expression, not origin.

**Somatic concentration:** High in chen (31/67 = 46% of active verses) and ta.cha.nun (8/18 = 44%). Moderate in charis (11/88 = 12%).

## 5. Spirit-soul-body provisional classification

**Classification: Spirit-soul interface** — confirmed, unchanged from prior assignment.

**Reasoning:** Grace is received at the spirit level (given by God, directed to "your spirit" Gal 6:18, mediated by the Spirit 1Cor 2:12), constitutes the inner-being condition at the soul level (standing, identity, character), and is expressed outward through the body (eye, face, hands, mouth, full-body postures). The spirit-soul interface is precisely where grace operates — received from above, expressed through the whole inner person. The body enacts what the spirit has received and the soul has processed.

**Confidence: High** — confirmed by Pass 4 somatic scan, Pass 2 divine dimension analysis, and the gospel statement in Joh 1:14 (the Word became flesh, full of grace — the incarnation as the paradigm of spirit-level grace fully embodied).
