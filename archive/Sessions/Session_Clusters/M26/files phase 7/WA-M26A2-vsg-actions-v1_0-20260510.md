### ACTION-005

**Type:** New VSG definition — gap finding  
**Status:** Pending researcher confirmation  
**Identified:** 2026-05-10  

**Subject:** New VSG for H6666 *tse.da.qah* — M26-A2

**Current state:** VSG `911-003` holds these two verses in M26-A2 under a description that does not fit them. No existing M26-A2 VSG captures the absence of or distance from righteousness as an OT phenomenon.

**Verses:**

| vr_id | ref | term | verse text |
|------:|-----|------|-----------|
| 25179 | Isa 46:12 | H6666 tse.da.qah | *"Listen to me, you stubborn of heart, you who are far from righteousness."* |
| 25196 | Isa 59:9 | H6666 tse.da.qah | *"Therefore justice is far from us, and righteousness does not overtake us; we hope for light, and behold, darkness."* |

**Gap identified:** The 10 OT meaning groups in the human analysis file address the presence and expression of righteousness, but not its absence. These two verses show that the OT also knows humans can be *far from* righteousness — a phenomenon parallel to the NT-2 characteristic ("Righteousness is a state that no human being possesses naturally," Rom 3:10) but expressed differently: not as a universal categorical absence (NT) but as a condition of specific persons or communities who are distant from the state.

**Action required:**

1. Create a new VSG in M26-A2 for H6666 *tse.da.qah* with the following description:

> *Term names the condition of being far from or lacking righteousness — the human person or community described as distant from the righteous state, hoping for it but not possessing it. Stubbornness of heart is named as the inner cause of the distance (Isa 46:12); the absence is experienced as darkness and the failure of justice (Isa 59:9). An OT parallel to the NT declaration of universal absence (Rom 3:10), but expressed as a particular condition rather than a universal category.*

2. Reassign vr_id 25179 (Isa 46:12) and vr_id 25196 (Isa 59:9) from VSG `911-003` to this new VSG.

3. Remove VSG `911-003` from the M26-A2 VSG list — once these two verses are moved, it has no remaining verses in M26-A2. Its 46 verses in M26-A1 are correctly placed and remain there.

**Anchor verse:** Isa 46:12 (vr_id 25179) — stubbornness of heart named as the inner cause of distance from righteousness; most direct statement of the condition.
