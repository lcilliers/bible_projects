### flesh registry 185 --- Part 1: Hebrew

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

Flesh denotes the physical substance of living creatures — the meat, body tissue, and bodily envelope of human and animal life. The Hebrew term בָּשָׂר (ba.sar) is the dominant word, covering flesh as bodily substance, human nature in its creaturely frailty, and living beings collectively. Related terms extend the semantic field to body fat, specific body parts, and physical processes of emergence from the body. This part covers the Hebrew terms; Part 2 covers the Greek.
