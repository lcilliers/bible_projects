### envy registry 056

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

**Hebrew**
  - **qinah** (קִנְאָה) — envy, jealousy (Prov 14:30 — envy rots the bones)
  - **ayin raah** (עַיִן רָעָה) — evil eye — idiom for envy/stinginess (Prov 23:6)
**Greek**
  - **phthonos** (φθόνος) — envy — always negative in NT, unlike zelos (Matt 27:18, Gal 5:21, Phil 1:15)
  - **zelos** (ζῆλος) — jealousy/zeal — overlaps with envy in some contexts (Gal 5:20)
**Conceptual Search Terms:** `covetousness`, `resentment`, `begrudging`, `spite`, `ill will`
**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Search phthonos in NT — it is always negative and distinct from zelos. Prov 14:30 in OT is the key somatic text (envy rots the bones). Matt 27:18 reveals envy as the motive behind the crucifixion.
