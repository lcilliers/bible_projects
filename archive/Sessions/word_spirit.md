### spirit registry 184

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

Spirit encompasses the immaterial animating principle of human life and the wider realm of spiritual beings and forces. The Hebrew root רוּחַ (ru.ach) is the dominant term, covering breath, wind, and the personal spirit or disposition of a person, as well as the Spirit of God. The Greek πνεῦμα (pneuma) performs a parallel function in the NT. The word set also includes terms for ancestral shades, mediums, divining spirits, and the breath God breathes into humankind, positioning spirit at the intersection of life, consciousness, and the supernatural.

Hebrew terms: be.liy.ya.al, me.eh, ne.sha.mah, ov, it.ti, re.pha.im, ru.ach, na.phach, ne.phesh
Greek terms: puthon, fantasma, sumpsuchos, kardia, hagios, pneuma
