### wisdom registry 174 --- Part 2: Greek

> **Source list:** `High Confidence`

**Greek**

  - **sophia** (σοφία) — wisdom — divine and human (1 Cor 1:24, James 3:17, Col 2:3)
  - **phronesis** (φρόνησις) — practical wisdom, prudence (Luke 1:17, Eph 1:8)
  - **sunesis** (σύνεσις) — understanding, insight (Col 1:9, 2:2)

**Conceptual Search Terms:** `understanding`, `discernment`, `prudence`, `insight`, `knowledge`

**STEP Bible Suggestion:** Distinguish sophia (comprehensive wisdom) from phronesis (practical wisdom) in NT. James 3:13-17 distinguishes earthly wisdom from wisdom from above. Col 2:3 hides all wisdom in Christ.
