## M03-BOUNDARY-VCG-01 — Aggregating VCG for Boundary Terms

**Context description:** This VCG aggregates all 178 verses belonging to the 28 BOUNDARY terms held at Phase 3 pending researcher disposition at Phase 12. No characteristic-bearing VCG analysis is conducted at Phase 7 for these terms per the governing instruction. The terms in this VCG span several analytically distinct but unresolved registers: the pressure/affliction register that may align to M24-Weakness (thlibō, ponos, stenochōreō, sumpaschō, mu.a.qah, ma.tsoq); the torment/judgment register (basanizō G0928G/H, basanos, ma.a.tse.vah); the faintness/weakness-under-grief register (dav.va, cha.lah); the labour-pain or toil-suffering register with uncertain M03 alignment (cha.val, e.tsev, o.tsev, its.tsa.von); terms spanning M03 and another cluster (ma.rar — grief-bitterness and anger/provocation; si.ach — complaint-lament and meditation); terms with wide or mixed semantic corpora (a.mal, che.vel-M cord-register, ra.ah-harm); and terms with insufficient corpus for a firm verdict (ne.dud, tsoq, tsu.qah, qe.pha.dah, pid, tsir). The 8 verses with missing Phase 2 meanings (all in che.vel-M) are included in the verse count but carry no analytical meaning. Researcher decision at Phase 12 will determine whether each BOUNDARY term is assigned to a characteristic sub-group, transferred to another cluster, or retained at boundary.

**Anchor verse:** vc_id 28983 — Job 10:1 (H7879 si.ach)  
*Rationale: "Job's complaint pours out freely as vocal expression of inner anguish — spoken 'in the bitterness of my soul,' locating it in the soul and driven by loathing of life itself." This verse has the most direct and clearly inner-being grief content within the BOUNDARY corpus, making it the strongest anchor for the aggregating VCG. The soul-location and bitter-anguish register are unambiguous.*
