### The M27 problem (CRITICAL cross-register watch)

**M27 — "Evil, Wickedness and Abomination"** is a separate sibling cluster, **status `Not started`**, with the SAME triple-noun description as M10b. The two clusters are NOT duplicates — they sit on different ontological tracks:

- **M10b** owns the **moral-character of evil**: a wicked PERSON, an abominable CONDUCT, the disposition of a heart bent toward evil, evil-as-abstract-nature. Centred on: ra.sha, to.e.vah, ponēros, kakia, ponēria, blasfēmeō, etc.
- **M27** owns **idolatry + concrete evil-as-harm + ruin / desolation**: idols (eidōlon, a.tsav), torturer (basanistēs), ruin/desolation (sha.chat, sha.mem), refuse (pig.gul), kakopoieō (to do harm), ra/ra.ah (general distress/calamity), shiq.qets (the verb 'to detest'). The *concrete object / outward harm / ruinous outcome* side.

The boundary matters because:

- **to.e.vah and shiq.quts** abomination uses for *idolatry* could arguably live in M27 (idolatry-as-abomination as the concrete-object register) — but the convention in the v6 split places **moral-character abomination in M10b** (the inner judgment "this is detestable to YHWH") and **idol objects themselves in M27** (eidōlon, a.tsav).
- **ra.sha** (the wicked PERSON) is M10b. M27's parallel is ra/ra.ah (general evil/distress AS HARM).
- **ponēros** narratives about *evil spirits* (Phase 1 set_aside-ed several of these → demonic-label, not character) sit on the M27 boundary because demonic evil is closer to "concrete evil agent" than to "evil character of human heart". Phase 3 may surface M27 cross-register flags for ponēros's demonic verses retained as relevant.

For any verdict in this debate that flags drift toward "evil-as-concrete-thing / idol / ruin / demonic agent", the destination is **M27**, not a re-routing to M10.

## §6.3.2 — Verse-level relationship test for TRANSFERS (CRITICAL)

A TRANSFERS verdict requires more than "the term's meaning corpus aligns primarily with another cluster's characteristic." Corpus-primary-register reasoning alone is too aggressive.

**The test, verse by verse:**

1. Read every verse in the term's meaning corpus.
2. For each verse, ask: *does this verse evidence any relationship with or impact on M10b's characteristic (the CHARACTER of evil — wickedness, abomination, evil-as-nature)?* "Relationship" is broad: direct evidence, structural opposite (righteousness-by-contrast, holiness-by-contrast), instrument-of (e.g. wicked person who acts), response-to (revulsion-against), in-tension-with.

**Verdict rule:**

- If **any** verse evidences a relationship with M10b → **STAYS** with cross-register flag in the rationale.
- If **no** verse evidences any M10b relationship → **TRANSFERS-TO-{cluster}** confirmed (accidental placement).

The cross-register flag travels with the term into Phase 5/7 and informs Phase 9 T6 findings.

## Disallowed BOUNDARY reasons (per v2_8 §6.3.1)

BOUNDARY may **NOT** be assigned solely because:

- The term's meaning corpus is predominantly horizontal (human-to-human evil) rather than vertical (evil-against-God).
- The meanings describe sensory / material / circumstantial inner-being rather than overtly spiritual inner-being.
- The meanings include corrupt, illicit, or morally-negative content. **M10b is BY DEFINITION about negative character.** Do not let "too negative" be a BOUNDARY ground.
- The meanings describe an inner-being state you would prefer not to be in scope.

Inner being is the entire human inner life — no theological narrowing.

## Valid BOUNDARY reasons (cite one of these three)

1. **Cluster-membership undecided** — corpus is genuinely on the borderline between M10b and another cluster (most likely M10 sin-act, M10c defilement, M27 idolatry/concrete-evil, M03 sorrow, or M06 hate); transfer destination unclear; researcher decision needed.
2. **Homonymic / polysemic spread** — the term's meaning corpus covers two or more distinct registers and the term may need sense-split treatment.
3. **Supportive / qualifying register** — the term's meanings enhance or qualify M10b's characteristic without itself carrying it as a primary characteristic.
