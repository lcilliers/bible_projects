## §1 — Characteristic 5 (Iniquity as active inner scheming and evil generation)

**Short name:** Iniquity as active inner scheming and evil generation
**Characteristic_id:** 51
**Definition:**

> Evil as an ongoing active inner process — devised, cultivated, generated, and enacted from within. Iniquity is plotted on the bed at night, conceived in the heart, schemed by the mind, and born into harmful deeds. The evildoer is a person who works iniquity as a habitual pursuit, exploiting others, lurking with predatory intent. Also encompasses the evil-deeds register — deeds whose inner origin is wickedness, and whose quality marks the one who commits them.
