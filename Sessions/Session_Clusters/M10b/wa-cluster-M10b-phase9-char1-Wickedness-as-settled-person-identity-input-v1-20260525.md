## §1 — Characteristic 1 (Wickedness as settled person-identity)

**Short name:** Wickedness as settled person-identity
**Characteristic_id:** 47
**Definition:**

> The wicked person as an inner-being type — the individual whose core moral character is defined by wickedness. Will corrupted, God actively excluded from the mind (practical atheism), inner orientation persistently bent toward harm and injustice. Conscience suppressed or displaced by an inner voice of transgression. Wickedness is not merely a series of acts but a stable identity and inner condition; divine judgment falls specifically on this person-type because their inner character warrants it.
