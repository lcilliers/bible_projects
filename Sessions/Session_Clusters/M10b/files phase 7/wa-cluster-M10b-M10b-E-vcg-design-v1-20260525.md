## VCG definitions

### M10b-E-VCG-01 — Trouble and distress as the affliction of evil (M03/M27 cross-register)

**Context description:** This VCG holds the verses where a.ven names the trouble, affliction, or distress that falls on a victim or community — the harm of evil experienced from the perspective of those who suffer it, rather than the iniquity of the agent who generates it. The psalmist receives crushing trouble dropped by an enemy with sustained grudge (Psa 55:3), iniquity and trouble are structural features of the city experienced as pervasive affliction (Psa 55:10), the evildoers' bold power is experienced as oppressive force requiring an advocate (Psa 94:16), the prophet is made to see iniquity as destructive trouble through forced witness (Hab 1:3), and empty reliance on false spiritual guidance leaves the soul wandering and afflicted (Zec 10:2). The two ro.a flag-only minorities join here: inner sadness of heart visible on the face (Neh 2:2, M03 register) and sorrow working inwardly to purify (Ecc 7:3, M03 register). Cross-register: M03 (distress/sorrow suffered — dominant); M27 (calamity/ruin-as-harm). Anchor: Psa 55:3 (38188) — enemy dropping trouble upon the psalmist with anger and sustained grudge; the most direct victim-of-trouble statement.

**Members (7V):** 38188 (Psa 55:3), 38239 (Psa 55:10), 38248 (Psa 94:16), 38194 (Hab 1:3), 38251 (Zec 10:2), 38449 (Neh 2:2), 38424 (Ecc 7:3)

**Anchor:** 38188 (Psa 55:3)

### M10b-E-VCG-02 — Iniquity as deliberate inner generation: conceived, devised, schemed

**Context description:** This VCG holds the verses where iniquity is most clearly an active generative process of the inner life — devised on the bed, conceived in the heart, schemed by the mind, and enacted at dawn. The inner-being content is the planning faculty deliberately producing evil: Mic 2:1 (devised by night, enacted at dawn), Psa 36:4 (cultivated deliberately during rest, will refusing to reject), Pro 6:18 (heart actively devising wicked plans, feet eagerly executing), Job 15:35 and Psa 7:14 (trouble/evil conceived and birthed from within — inner being as womb of deceit), Isa 10:1 (iniquitous decrees conceived in the mind of lawmakers and institutionalised as oppression), Isa 29:20 (vigilant scheming toward harm as premeditated inner pursuit), Isa 32:6 (fool's heart wholly occupied with troubling scheming), Isa 59:4 (iniquity generated from within — conceived as mischief, born as iniquity), Isa 59:7 (thoughts of iniquity saturating the whole inner person), Jer 4:14 (wicked thoughts as inner cognitive tenant to be washed out), Eze 11:2 (devising iniquity as deliberate mental project), 1Sa 15:23 (wilful presumption as equivalent to idolatry — the inner trouble of a will elevating itself above the divine word), Isa 55:7 (troubling iniquity residing in thoughts — inner cognitive patterns to forsake). Anchor: Psa 36:4 (38235) — trouble plotted in the quiet of the bed; evil cultivated deliberately in the inner life; the most concentrated statement of deliberate inner scheming.

**Members (14V):** 38185 (1Sa 15:23), 38208 (Job 15:35), 38245 (Psa 7:14), 38235 (Psa 36:4), 38224 (Pro 6:18), 38191 (Isa 10:1), 38198 (Isa 29:20), 38181 (Isa 32:6), 38200 (Isa 55:7), 38192 (Isa 59:4), 38203 (Isa 59:7), 38205 (Jer 4:14), 38193 (Eze 11:2), 38217 (Mic 2:1)

### M10b-E-VCG-03 — Evildoers as a defined inner-character class

**Context description:** This VCG holds the verses where iniquity defines a recognisable *class* of persons — those whose habitual character and conduct mark them as evildoers, workers of iniquity, in contrast to the righteous. The inner-being content is the settled identity and characteristic behaviour of those whose inner orientation is constitutively toward harm: incompatible with God's presence (Psa 5:5, 6:8), lacking moral knowledge while exploiting others (Psa 14:4, 53:4), carrying inner evil while outwardly speaking peace (Psa 28:3), paired with bloodthirsty harm-workers (Psa 59:2), scheming and conspiring covertly (Psa 59:5, 64:2), apparently thriving but oriented toward destruction (Psa 92:7), enemies of God by their inner opposition (Psa 92:9), boasting arrogantly revealing their will (Psa 94:4), turning back their evil upon themselves (Psa 94:23), disqualified from the Lord's community (Psa 101:8), deviating to crooked ways (Psa 125:5), laying predatory traps (Psa 141:9). Anchor: Psa 28:3 (38232) — evildoers carrying inner evil while outwardly speaking peace; the radical inner-outer split is the sharpest diagnostic of the evildoer class.

### M10b-E-VCG-05 — Evil deeds as the outward expression of an inwardly-defiled source

**Context description:** This VCG holds the ro.a evil-deeds corpus — the verses where the focus is on *the moral quality of deeds* as the outward expression of an inner disposition corrupted at its root. The evil is the badness of the deed as moral output from a defiled inner source: evil of deeds flowing from forsaking God (Deu 28:20), evil of deeds as measurable moral quality deserving retribution (Psa 28:4), evil deeds requiring removal through inner turning (Isa 1:16), evil deeds springing from the uncircumcised heart (Jer 4:4), evil deeds provoking divine wrath (Jer 21:12), evil deeds of negligent leaders (Jer 23:2), evil way and evil deeds as patterned inner orientation expressed outwardly (Jer 23:22, 25:5, 26:3), accumulated evil deeds exhausting divine forbearance (Jer 44:22), evil of deeds rooted in a place of rebellion severing relationship (Hos 9:15). The inner-being content is the inner directional orientation that produces these deeds, and the possibility of inner turning that could reverse them. Cross-register: M10 (act-register — ro.a's evil-deeds sub-corpus carries this from Phase 3). Anchor: Jer 4:4 (38447) — evil deeds springing from an uncircumcised heart; the most direct statement of the inner-origin-of-evil-deeds principle.
