## VCG definitions

### M10b-B-VCG-01 — Cosmic evil: the evil one, the evil age (M27 cross-register)

**Context description:** This VCG holds the verses where "evil" names a cosmic, supra-personal power — the evil one as a personal spiritual adversary (1Jo 2:13, 14, 5:18, 5:19), the domain from which divine deliverance is needed (Mat 6:13), the agent targeting the heart to snatch away implanted truth (Mat 13:19), the paternity defining a community's identity (Mat 13:38), the malevolent power from whose domain disciples need protective custody (Joh 17:15), and the encompassing moral-spiritual environment of the evil age (Gal 1:4) and evil days (Eph 5:16, 6:13). The inner-being content is the person's relationship to this cosmic evil: vulnerability (heart unprotected leaves space for the evil one), protection (faith as shield, divine birth as barrier, God's faithfulness as guard), and captivity (the world's collective inner orientation under the evil one's dominion). Cross-register: M27 (evil as cosmic/supra-personal force — concentrated in this VCG). Anchor: 1Jo 5:19 (14433) — the whole world lying under the dominion of the evil one; humanity's collective inner orientation captive to an evil governing power apart from God.

**Members (13V):** 14415 (Mat 6:13), 14435 (Mat 13:19), 14436 (Mat 13:38), 14427 (Joh 17:15), 14466 (Gal 1:4), 14467 (Eph 5:16), 14468 (Eph 6:13), 14428 (Eph 6:16), 14434 (2Th 3:3), 14429 (1Jo 2:13), 14430 (1Jo 2:14), 14432 (1Jo 5:18), 14433 (1Jo 5:19)

**Anchor:** 14433 (1Jo 5:19)

### M10b-B-VCG-02 — Constitutional evil: human nature bent toward corruption as baseline condition

**Context description:** This VCG holds the verses where evil names the constitutional baseline condition of human inner nature — not the individual's specific sinful act but the structural inclination of the human person toward corruption. Humans are constitutionally evil yet still capable of good impulses toward their children, revealing the tension between constitutional evil and natural affective capacity (Mat 7:11, Luk 11:13). A diseased inner nature inevitably produces corrupted fruit; moral constitution is determinative (Mat 7:17, 7:18). Evil generations are characterised by their collective inner disposition of faithless sign-demanding rather than trust (Mat 12:39, 16:4, Luk 11:29). Mat 6:34 kakia (the trouble-of-each-day M03 flag-only minority) joins here as the verse most proximate to constitutional-burden-as-baseline-condition. Cross-register: M03 (Mat 6:34 kakia — circumstantial-trouble register, flag-only). Anchor: Mat 7:11 (14438) — humans constitutionally evil yet producing good acts toward children; the most analytically direct statement of constitutional evil as baseline condition.

**Members (8V):** 14438 (Mat 7:11), 14439 (Mat 7:17), 14440 (Mat 7:18), 14416 (Mat 12:39), 14441 (Mat 16:4), 14445 (Luk 11:13), 14446 (Luk 11:29), 14371 (Mat 6:34 kakia)

**Anchor:** 14438 (Mat 7:11)
