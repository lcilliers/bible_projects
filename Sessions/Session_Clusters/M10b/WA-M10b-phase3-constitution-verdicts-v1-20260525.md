**Primary cross-register:** M10 (unrighteous-act / injustice-as-deed track)

**Rationale:** adikia's corpus evidences the inner character of moral corruption as its primary register — the will's allegiance to wrongdoing over truth (Rom 2:8), the soul saturated with corrupt orientations (Rom 1:29), the willful interior suppression of known truth (Rom 1:18). 1Cor 13:6 shows unrighteousness as a possible object of inner attraction that love refuses, confirming its M10b character-of-evil home. The M10 cross-register flag names the act-and-legal-consequence dimension for Phase 5/7 awareness.

## Cross-term observations (for Phase 5 consideration)

**1. The abomination family (to.e.vah, shiq.quts, bdelugma, bdeluktos) — characteristic design note.**
These four terms share the "moral-judgment-of-what-is-detestable-to-God" semantic field but operate across different sub-registers: to.e.vah spans the full range (moral-character, ethical-behavior, cultic-ritual, idolatrous); shiq.quts is heavily idol-object-centred; bdelugma leans toward both moral-character and concrete-sacrilegious-event; bdeluktos is purely inner-character disconnect. Phase 5 may want to design a sub-group for the "abomination-as-moral-divine-judgment-on-character" characteristic and note the M10c/M27 overlap throughout.

**2. The wicked-person family (ra.sha, re.sha, mir.sha.at, kakia, ponēros, ponēria) — primary characteristic axis.**
These terms together constitute the largest and most coherent M10b evidence base — the wicked person and their settled inner orientation. ra.sha alone (179 verses) will require Phase 5 attention to distribution (§8.6 gate). The Greek terms (kakia, ponēros, ponēria) map to the same characteristic with NT distinctive emphases (the heart as treasury, constitutional bent toward corruption, progressive deterioration).

**3. Cross-cluster relationship pattern — M10b and M26 (Righteousness) as structural opposites.**
Multiple terms across the corpus define the wicked or evil through explicit contrast with the righteous (ra.sha vs tsaddiq throughout Job, Psalms, Proverbs; adikia vs dikaiosynē in Romans; ponēros vs agathos in the Sermon on the Mount). This is a T6 (Structural Relationships) finding candidate for Phase 9.

**4. The evil-one / cosmic-evil dimension in ponēros — significant.**
ponēros's cosmic-evil-agent sub-corpus (Matt 13:19, 13:38; 1 John; Eph 6:16; 2Th 3:3) is substantial enough that Phase 5 may want to distinguish the personal-evil-character characteristic from the cosmic-evil-agent register in sub-group design, with the M27 cross-register flag travelling with the latter.

**5. The polysemy of a.ven — VCG-level distinction needed.**
a.ven's trouble/calamity register (M03/M27) and its iniquity/evildoer register (M10b) will need explicit VCG-level separation at Phase 7. Phase 5 sub-group assignment should reflect which register dominates per verse.

**6. blasfēmeō — bridging M10b and M06.**
Blasphemy as the expression of a hardened-evil-will (M10b) and as contemptuous speech (M06) are both present and genuine. Phase 5 may want to design the characteristic as "evil expressed in wilful contempt toward God and authority" — naming both registers in the sub-group description.
