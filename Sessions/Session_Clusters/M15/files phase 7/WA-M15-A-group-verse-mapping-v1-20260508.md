## Sub-group description

M15-A names wisdom as a constituted holistic quality of the inner person — a settled orientation of the whole person that shapes perception, decision, and character. Wisdom in this sub-group is Spirit-given, covenantally oriented, and morally constitutive. It includes the structural opposite (the unwise person). The sub-group covers wisdom as character quality (cha.kham, sofia), wisdom as effective inner resource (tu.shiy.yah), wisdom as good sense (se.khel), wisdom as inner mindedness (froneō/fronimos), and the directional (inverted) uses where wisdom serves self or evil.
