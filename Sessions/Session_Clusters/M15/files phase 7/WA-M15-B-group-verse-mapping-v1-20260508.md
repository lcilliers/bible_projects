## Group B-1 (existing code 1360) — Understanding as constituted inner faculty of the wise person

**Status:** RETAINED AND REFINED
**Description from verses:** bin as the inner perceptive faculty in its constituted form — the quality of the wise, discerning person. The faculty is seated in the heart (1Ki 3:9, Psa 49:20), enables right judgment (1Ki 3:9,11), receives instruction (Pro 9:8), perceives situations rightly (2Sa 12:19, Job 6:30), and is the inner capacity from which wise conduct flows. It is morally conditioned — it enables covenant-keeping (Deu 4:6) and is blocked by the absence of the fear of the Lord.
**Anchor verse:** Pro 14:33 ("wisdom rests in the heart of a man of understanding") — bin as the inner quality in which wisdom finds its dwelling place; the understanding faculty is the container in which wisdom rests.
