## §2. VCG definitions

### M11-D-VCG-01 — Divine relenting in response to intercession

**Context:** God turns from announced wrath when a mediator pleads. Moses at Sinai (Exo 32:12 appeal / Exo 32:14 enacted relenting); Amos's prophetic intercession reversing announced judgment twice (Amo 7:3, Amo 7:6). Inner-being content is responsive divine will moved by mediating plea.

**Members:** vc=30737 (Exo 32:14), vc=30744 (Exo 32:12), vc=30765 (Amo 7:3), vc=30766 (Amo 7:6) — **4 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30737 (Exo 32:14) — canonical Mosaic-mediated relenting enacted.

---

### M11-D-VCG-02 — Divine relenting triggered by human turning

**Context:** The largest VCG in M11-D — divine compassionate relenting triggered by observed or hoped-for inner human change. Jonah's three references to Nineveh's turning (Jon 3:9 hoped, Jon 3:10 enacted, Jon 4:2 known character); the Jeremianic conditional-mercy theology (Jer 18:8, Jer 26:3/13/19, Jer 42:10); David's census (1Ch 21:15) and Araunah threshing-floor (2Sa 24:16) mid-execution relenting; Joel's character-grounded relenting (Joe 2:13–14). Inner-being content is divine disposition genuinely responsive to human moral movement.

**Members:** vc=30738 (Jon 3:10), vc=30749 (2Sa 24:16), vc=30750 (1Ch 21:15), vc=30756 (Jer 18:8), vc=30758 (Jer 26:3), vc=30759 (Jer 26:13), vc=30760 (Jer 26:19), vc=30761 (Jer 42:10), vc=30763 (Joe 2:13), vc=30764 (Joe 2:14), vc=30767 (Jon 3:9), vc=30768 (Jon 4:2) — **12 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30738 (Jon 3:10) — canonical relenting from observed turning.

---

### M11-D-VCG-03 — Divine refusal to relent / judgment fixed

**Context:** The negative pole — divine refusal to relent, will fixed, judgment irreversible. The Samuel "Glory of Israel does not relent" (1Sa 15:29) and Balaam parallel (Num 23:19) frame divine constancy; the Psalm 110:4 oath; Eze 24:14, Jer 4:28, Jer 15:6 (relenting exhausted), Isa 57:6, Zec 8:14 specify circumstances of withheld mercy.

**Members:** vc=30745 (Num 23:19), vc=30747 (1Sa 15:29), vc=30752 (Psa 110:4), vc=30753 (Isa 57:6), vc=30754 (Jer 4:28), vc=30755 (Jer 15:6), vc=30762 (Eze 24:14), vc=30769 (Zec 8:14) — **8 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30747 (1Sa 15:29) — canonical "Glory of Israel does not relent."

---

### M11-D-VCG-04 — Divine grief / sorrow over human failure

**Context:** na.cham used in its grief/regret sense — God's inner sorrow over creation (Gen 6:6/7) and over Saul's failure (1Sa 15:11, 15:35). M03 cross-register flag per Phase 3 — divine grief is M03 territory while the relenting movement is M11-D.

**Members:** vc=30742 (Gen 6:6), vc=30743 (Gen 6:7), vc=30746 (1Sa 15:11), vc=30748 (1Sa 15:35) — **4 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30742 (Gen 6:6) — canonical divine grief penetrating to the heart.

---

### M11-D-VCG-05 — Conditional relenting / covenant memory

**Context:** Two verses framing relenting as a covenant-conditioned response. Jer 18:10 (God relents from promised good when a nation does evil — the opposite-direction conditional from VCG-02); Psa 106:45 (covenant remembered drives relenting).

**Members:** vc=30751 (Psa 106:45), vc=30757 (Jer 18:10) — **2 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30751 (Psa 106:45) — relenting motivated by remembered covenant.

---

### M11-D-VCG-06 — Human contrition / self-reproach

**Context:** Human na.cham as inner contrition. Job's dust-and-ashes self-abasement (Job 42:6); Ephraim's post-turning shame and self-reproach (Jer 31:19). Inner-being content is whole-person collapse of self-regard producing grief over past sin.

**Members:** vc=30736 (Job 42:6), vc=30741 (Jer 31:19) — **2 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30736 (Job 42:6) — canonical embodied contrition.

---

### M11-D-VCG-07 — Negative human relenting (resolve-collapse / absence-of-turning)

**Context:** The negative human side of na.cham — either inappropriate relenting (resolve collapsing under fear, Exo 13:17 — M20 cross-register per Phase 3) or absence of needed turning (no one relents of evil, Jer 8:6 — conscience silent). Both name the failure-mode of the inner turning faculty.

**Members:** vc=30739 (Exo 13:17), vc=30740 (Jer 8:6) — **2 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=30740 (Jer 8:6) — canonical absence of inner turning.

---

### M11-D-VCG-08 — Inner heart-turning and memory-recall (shuv repentance)

**Context:** The shuv-recall corpus — inner heart-level turning enabled by covenant memory. Solomon's exile-prayer pair (1Ki 8:47 / 2Ch 6:37); Deuteronomic exile-recall (Deu 30:1, Deu 4:39 "lay it to your heart"); Isaiah's command to transgressors (Isa 46:8) and the idol-maker's failure to recall (Isa 44:19); Lam 3:21 where recall pivots grief into hope. Inner-being content is the cognitive-volitional turning that opens the way for divine forgiveness.

**Members:** vc=35024 (Lam 3:21), vc=35025 (Deu 4:39), vc=35026 (Deu 30:1), vc=35027 (1Ki 8:47), vc=35028 (2Ch 6:37), vc=35029 (Isa 44:19), vc=35030 (Isa 46:8) — **7 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=35027 (1Ki 8:47) — canonical exile heart-turning.

---

### M11-D-VCG-09 — Marginal: faithfulness-under-pressure (structural-opposite)

**Context:** ud's sole IB verse — Psa 119:61 names sustained loyalty to God's law under external pressure. Per Phase 3 §6.3.2 verse-level relationship test, the term sits in M11 by structural-opposite reading — sustained faithfulness is the obverse of the turning-from-abandonment that M11-D's repentance characterises. Marginal STAYS verdict; flagged for Phase D T6 awareness via observation seed.

**Members:** vc=40564 (Psa 119:61) — **1 verse**

**Anchor:** vc=40564 (Psa 119:61) — only member.

## §3. Cross-VCG observations (Phase D + Phase E seeds)

1. **VCG-01 + VCG-02 (responsive divine relenting) vs VCG-03 (fixed divine refusal)** — the central tension in M11-D. Divine compassion is genuinely responsive yet bounded; both poles are real. Phase D T6 will articulate.
2. **VCG-04 (divine grief)** carries the M03 cross-register flag — divine sorrow is M03 inner content; the relenting-movement is M11-D. The cluster's verse evidence shows the two intertwined in the same lexical occurrences (Gen 6:6, 1Sa 15:11/35).
3. **VCG-02 (relenting triggered by turning) ↔ VCG-08 (shuv inner turning)** — these are the human-turning + divine-turning pair within M11-D. The same inner movement traced from both sides.
4. **VCG-06 (human contrition) ↔ M11-B-VCG-03 (repentance unlocks pardon)** — cross-sub-group structural pair. Contrition (M11-D) is the inner state that opens the divine forgiveness (M11-B).
5. **VCG-07 (negative human relenting)** carries the M20 cross-register flag (Exo 13:17 anxiety/resolve-collapse) per Phase 3.
6. **VCG-09 (Psa 119:61, ud)** — marginal STAYS. Phase D T6 reading may confirm or reverse; the cluster_observation flags this as `target_phase=D`.
