**T0.1.1 Divine Nature Reflected**
**[CLUSTER]** E — Across the five characteristics, the cluster as a whole reveals God's character as **active redemptive initiator** operating through a multi-stage relational repair: God atones (M11-A as character — Psa 65:3 "God himself acts to atone" when human resources are exhausted); God forgives (M11-B as character — Psa 86:5 "you, Lord, are forgiving... ready to forgive"); God releases (M11-C as character — Mat 26:28 "for the forgiveness of sins"); God relents (M11-D as character — Joe 2:13 "gracious, merciful, slow to anger"); God reconciles (M11-E as character — 2Cor 5:18 "God reconciled us to himself through Christ"). The integrated character is a *redemptive disposition* that operates in five linked modes: covering, releasing, letting-go, relenting, and restoring. Pro 16:6 (M11-A character anchor) names the inner-character ground: hesed + emet — steadfast love and faithfulness — produce the atoning disposition; the same hesed grounds Joe 2:13's relenting and Psa 86:5's forgiving character.

---

**T0.1.2 Divine Nature Reflected**
**[CLUSTER]** E — Scripture explicitly attributes all five characteristics to God: atonement (Psa 65:3; Psa 78:38; Psa 79:9); forgiveness (Psa 86:5; Neh 9:17; Dan 9:9); release (Mat 26:28; Eph 1:7); relenting (Exo 32:14; Jon 3:10; Joe 2:13); reconciliation (2Cor 5:18; Col 1:20). **The integrated significance for the human person:** the cluster grounds an entire redemptive economy in God's character — the human person can stand within an active divine-character that addresses guilt (atonement), declares release (forgiveness), lets-go (release), responds to turning (relenting), and restores relationship (reconciliation). No part of the redemptive movement is human-derivable; all stem from God's revealed character. The image-bearing implication: humans bear this divine image by participating in the same five-fold redemptive disposition toward others.

---

**T0.1.3 Divine Nature Reflected**
**[CLUSTER]** S — Across the cluster, the corpus is generally not silent on God's possession of the five characteristics. The narrower silence at the cluster level: Scripture does not depict God as the recipient of any of these — God is never atoned-for, never needs forgiveness, never released, never repents-of-sin (na.cham as regret/grief, yes; not as moral repentance), never reconciled-to-humans-from-his-side-as-offender. The directional asymmetry runs across all five characteristics: God is initiator and gracious extender; the human is recipient and (where appropriate) imitative extender. This unified asymmetry is a structural cluster-level finding.

---

**T0.2.1 Created Purpose**
**[CLUSTER]** E — The cluster's integrated created-purpose: equipping the person to be brought from misalignment (sin/defilement/alienation) back to right standing with God and others through five linked mechanisms: mechanism-cover (M11-A), declared-release (M11-B), broader-let-go (M11-C), inner-turning (M11-D), relational-restoration (M11-E). Each characteristic addresses a distinct stage of the redemptive movement: M11-D opens by turning; M11-A operates the cover; M11-B/C declare the release; M11-E completes the relational restoration. The created purpose is the human's restoration to image-bearing standing with the moral-formational outcome (Col 1:22 holy/blameless/above-reproach) as the telic completion.

---

**T0.2.2 Created Purpose**
**[CLUSTER]** E — Across all five characteristics, the **operational form** is principally response-to-fallen-condition — every characteristic presupposes prior misalignment that requires repair (atonement presupposes guilt; forgiveness presupposes offense; release presupposes obligation/attachment; repentance presupposes wrong direction; reconciliation presupposes enmity). However, the **underlying character-ground** is pre-fall: Pro 16:6 hesed + emet are God's character-attributes that would exist apart from fall; Psa 86:5 forgiving-character is divine identity not fall-dependent; the inner-being-faculty of relational repair-capacity is part of original design. The cluster's integrated answer: operational form is fall-response; character ground is pre-fall divine character.

---

**T0.2.3 Created Purpose**
**[CLUSTER]** E — All five characteristics have eschatological orientation. Atonement → Dan 9:24 cosmic resolution + Christic Mat 27:50. Forgiveness → Jer 31:34 "remember sin no more" + Mar 3:28 universal scope. Release → Rev 7:17, 21:4 eschatological tear-wiping + Heb 10:18 once-for-all. Repentance → Joe 2:13–14 + the implied new-covenant comprehensive turning. Reconciliation → Col 1:20 cosmic scope. The **integrated cluster eschatological trajectory** is comprehensive redemptive-completion — sin's record erased, divine wrath finally laid down, captive grief comprehensively wiped, the turning-and-mercy arc completed, all-things reconciled to God in Christ. Each characteristic's eschatological dimension feeds into a unified cluster-final state.

**T0.3.1 Image-Bearer Expression**
**[CLUSTER]** E — Across the cluster, image-bearer expression operates through **the capacity to participate in the redemptive economy as both recipient and extender**. M11-A images God as mediator (priest / Christ); M11-B as forgiver; M11-C as releaser; M11-D as turning-disposition (capacity for moral redirection); M11-E as reconciler (ambassador). The five together compose the *complete image-bearing person* — one who has received the full redemptive arc and extends it in all five modes. Pro 16:6 anchors this: when hesed + emet operate as the person's character, the person enacts atoning-forgiving-releasing-turning-reconciling all together at the character-level. The image isn't divided among the five; it is integrated in the formed inner-being-character that operates in all modes appropriately.

---

**T0.3.2 Image-Bearer Expression**
**[CLUSTER]** E — Across all five characteristics, the pattern is **genuinely shared with structural asymmetry**, resolved Christologically. Humans participate in atoning (priestly mediation, inner-character), forgiving (Lord's Prayer reciprocity), releasing (welcoming permission, discipleship-leaving), turning (the human shuv-act), and reconciling (ambassadorial ministry). At every characteristic, the divine is source and the human is participant-by-imitation-and-delegation. The Christological collapse runs across the cluster: Christ is both divine and human, both originator and incarnate participant — Mat 27:50 (atoning); Joh 20:23 (delegated forgiveness); Mat 26:28 (release-for-forgiveness); Mat 4 (turning-as-baptism inaugurator); Col 1:20 (reconciler) — Christ is the cluster's image-bearing exemplar at every characteristic.

---

**T0.3.3 Image-Bearer Expression**
**[CLUSTER]** E — Diagnostic across all five: the operative-or-disabled state of the redemptive economy in a person reveals the divine image's condition. Presence: the person who has received the redemptive economy and extends it (forgives others, has turned, lives in reconciled standing, exercises mercy) shows the image active. Absence: the negative pole runs internally across M11-A/B/C — atonement denied (VCG-08), forgiveness foreclosed (M11-B-VCG-04), release foreclosed (Mar 3:29) — together name the *sealed-against-redemption* state where the divine image-bearing capacity has been systematically closed. The cluster's negative-pole structure is itself a unified inner-being condition: the will sealed against the redemptive economy at every characteristic.

**T0.4.1 Typological Significance**

The cluster's integrated typological structure: **OT institutional patterns → Christological-incarnational fulfillment → eschatological-cosmic completion**. Atonement: Levitical priestly-sacrifice → Christic-self-substitution → eschatological-cosmic resolution. Forgiveness: OT sa.lach → NT-Christic authority + reciprocal extension → new-covenant comprehensive forgiveness. Release: Jubilee-and-Levitical patterns → Christic covenant-blood → eschatological tear-wiping. Repentance: OT shuv → NT metanoia → eschatological-complete turning. Reconciliation: covenant-restoration imagery → Christic-cosmic reconciliation → cosmic completion. The cluster's typological arc is therefore unified — *the OT-Christic-Eschatological pattern* runs across every characteristic as a single redemptive-historical arc.
