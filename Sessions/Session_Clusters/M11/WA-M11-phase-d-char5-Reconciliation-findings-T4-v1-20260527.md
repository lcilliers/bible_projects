# M11 Phase D — Char 5 (Reconciliation) — T4 findings

**Characteristic:** M11-E — 24 prompts. Authored 2026-05-27.

---

**T4.1.1 Divine Interface — God to Human** **[CHAR-5]** E — Densely evidenced: 2Cor 5:18 God initiates; Col 1:20 through-the-cross all-things-reconciled-to-God; Eph 2:16 hostility-killed; Rom 5:10 enmity-overcome by Christ's death.

**T4.1.2** **[CHAR-5]** E — Basis: Christic-act (through the cross); covenantal-grace (Rom 5 the gospel context); unconditional initiative (while still enemies, Rom 5:10).

**T4.1.3** **[CHAR-5]** E — Reveals God's character as **initiator of relational restoration despite the alienated party's enmity**. The "while still enemies" pattern reveals divine love at maximum extension.

**T4.1.4** **[CHAR-5]** S — God-to-human direction is densely evidenced.

---

**T4.2.1 Divine Interface — Human to God** **[CHAR-5]** E — Human-to-God: 2Cor 5:20 "be reconciled to God" — the human-side responsive movement of accepting the offered reconciliation; the will-summoned to abandon enmity. The human-to-God direction is principally one of receiving and responsively-aligning rather than initiating.

**T4.2.2** **[CHAR-5]** E — Required inner posture: willingness to abandon enmity; openness to receive the offer; faith-receiving.

**T4.2.3** **[CHAR-5]** E — Direction reveals the relationship as one of responsive uptake — humans don't initiate reconciliation but respond to the divine offer.

**T4.2.4** **[CHAR-5]** S — Sufficient.

---

**T4.3.1 Human Interface — Giving** **[CHAR-5]** E — Human-to-human extension: 2Cor 5:18 the ministry-of-reconciliation entrusted to apostolic / ecclesial agents; 1Cor 7:11 the marital-reconciliation pattern. Mat 5:24 (cross-evidence M11-C) reconciliation-before-worship at the interpersonal level. The human-giving register is principally ambassadorial (extending the divine reconciliation) and interpersonal (marital, fraternal).

**T4.3.2** **[CHAR-5]** E — Inner conditions: integration of the divine reconciliation received (cannot extend what one hasn't received); willingness to be a minister of the message; relational humility for interpersonal-cases.

**T4.3.3** **[CHAR-5]** E — Receiving precedes giving — same pattern as throughout the cluster.

**T4.3.4** **[CHAR-5]** S — Sufficient.

---

**T4.4.1 Human Interface — Receiving** **[CHAR-5]** E — Receiving evidenced in: receiving the gospel-appeal (2Cor 5:20); receiving back the marital partner (1Cor 7:11); the cosmic-scope implies receiving by all-things (Col 1:20).

**T4.4.2** **[CHAR-5]** E — Enabled by openness; blocked by maintained-hostility / refusal of the offer.

**T4.4.3** **[CHAR-5]** S — Non-receiving state in M11-E is implicit rather than explicit.

**T4.4.4** **[CHAR-5]** S — Sufficient.

---

**T4.5.1 Human Interface — Boundaries** **[CHAR-5]** E — Boundary crossing is structurally significant in M11-E. Eph 2:16 explicitly addresses Jew-Gentile boundary (the hostility-killed crosses the religious-ethnic boundary). Col 1:20 cosmic-scope crosses every boundary. Rom 11:15 the Israel-rejection-paradox connects the boundary structurally to the mechanism of extension.

**T4.5.2** **[CHAR-5]** E — Crosses every boundary in the eschatological-cosmic register; operates within and beyond covenantal-boundaries in the present-age ministry.

**T4.5.3** **[CHAR-5]** E — Included: cosmically all-things (Col 1:20); ethnically the Jew-Gentile previously-divided (Eph 2:16); relationally the alienated-marital-partner (1Cor 7:11). Excluded: those who refuse the offer (implied at 2Cor 5:20 implore-language).

**T4.5.4** **[CHAR-5]** S — Sufficient.

**T4.6.1 Spiritual Beings Interface** **[CHAR-5]** E — Col 1:20 "things in heaven and things on earth" — the cosmic scope explicitly includes the heavenly realm. Reconciliation has a spiritual-beings interface at the cosmic-scope level. The specific shape of heavenly-reconciliation is not elaborated in the M11-E verse set.

**T4.6.2** **[CHAR-5]** S — Adversarial-distortion is not directly named in M11-E; the focus is on the reconciliation-achievement.

**T4.6.3** **[CHAR-5]** S — Angelic ministry of reconciliation is not directly evidenced.

**T4.6.4** **[CHAR-5]** S — Partial engagement at the cosmic-scope (Col 1:20 includes things-in-heaven).
