# M11 Phase D — Char 4 (Repentance and turning) — T5 findings

**Characteristic:** M11-D — 21 prompts. Authored 2026-05-27.

---

**T5.1.1 Nature of Transformation** **[CHAR-4]** E — Transforms both condition (mis-aligned → aligned with God's path) and orientation (the inner-being's directional posture shifts). The transformation is profoundly directional — a re-orientation rather than a substitutionary-cover (M11-A) or a standing-shift (M11-B).

**T5.1.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Reversible at OT level (people return-and-fall repeatedly through the prophetic period); progressively more durable through formation; ultimately irreversible at the eschatological-fullness pole (Jer 31:34 new-covenant turning-and-forgiveness paired).

**T5.1.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — Densely evidenced.

---

**T5.2.1 Sequence of Inner States** **[CHAR-4]** E — Sequence: misalignment-state → recall-of-covenant (memory) → recognition-of-misalignment (cognition) → contrite-affect (na.cham) → volitional-turn (shuv) → embodied-posture (humility) → restored-orientation. Lam 3:21 explicitly names the memory-act as the pivot generating hope.

**T5.2.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Sequence-fidelity matters as in M11-A/B/C — skipping recall produces ungrounded turning; skipping contrition produces surface-turning; skipping volitional-turn leaves at felt-regret-without-change.

**T5.2.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — Tempo varies.

---

**T5.3.1 Mechanism of Change** **[CHAR-4]** E — Mechanism: **memory-prompting-recognition-prompting-volitional-turn**. The mechanism is cognitive-affective-volitional cascade. Triggered by external prompt (prophetic call; covenant-memory; crisis) or by inner conviction (conscience-stirring).

**T5.3.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Mechanism distinguished by context (institutional-collective vs personal-experiential vs eschatological).

**T5.3.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — Ontology of how memory-prompts-turning is given, not derived.

---

**T5.4.1 Suffering and Affliction** **[CHAR-4]** E — Affliction frequently prompts turning: 1Ki 8:47 the exile context — affliction prompting heart-turning; 2Ch 7:14 the affliction of national crisis as context for humility-prayer-seeking-turning. Job 42:6's contrition follows Job's whole affliction-encounter with God. The pattern: affliction is often the precipitating context for turning.

**T5.4.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Suffering **reveals** capacity-or-incapacity to turn (Job's turning vs Pharaoh's hardening); **produces** contrition where turning is genuine; **tests** the durability of inner-orientation.

**T5.4.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — Sufficient.

---

**T5.5.1 Formation and Sanctification** **[CHAR-4]** E — Strongly formational. Repeated turning forms the turning-disposition (Luk 17:3–4 cross-evidence: repeated turning); the post-Job character is permanently shaped by the dust-and-ashes encounter; the post-exile Israel is shaped by the corporate-turning pattern. Pro 16:6 (cross-evidence) the formed hesed-emet character includes turning-capability.

**T5.5.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Foundational + ongoing role. Foundational: the initial-turn enables sanctification's onset; ongoing: continued micro-turnings sustain the sanctification-arc.

**T5.5.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — Sufficient.

---

**T5.6.1 Eschatological Trajectory** **[CHAR-4]** E — Eschatologically: Jer 31:34 (the new-covenant context implies the eschatological-fullness — turning and forgiveness together comprehensively complete); Joe 2:13–14 the corporate-eschatological turning; Dan 9 prophetic-intercession anticipating eschatological mercy.

**T5.6.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Present-turning anticipates the eschatological-comprehensive turning when all who will turn will have turned; the final state has all-turned (or sealed-against-turning, the negative pole's final form).

**T5.6.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — Sufficient.

---

**T5.7.1 Deposit Consequence** **[CHAR-4]** S — No bodily-DNA deposit; soul-level deposit: turned-character; memory-of-having-turned; humility-as-disposition.

**T5.7.2** **[CHAR-4]** E — Generational: positive — the covenant-community's turning-pattern is transmitted; negative pole — Deu 29:20 (cross-evidence) wilful self-deception's generational consequence parallels.

**T5.7.3** **[CHAR-4]** S — T2.8 stands.
