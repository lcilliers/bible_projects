## §2. VCG definitions

### M11-E-VCG-01 — Cosmic / theological reconciliation through Christ's death

**Context:** The four core christological reconciliation verses. Col 1:20 names cosmic restoration of right relationship between creation and Creator through the blood of the cross; Col 1:22 names the inner-moral purpose — to present the person holy, blameless, above reproach; Eph 2:16 names enmity destroyed through the cross as the mechanism; Rom 5:10 names enmity-overcome-while-still-enemies as the ground for ongoing salvation. Inner-being content is the structural enmity dissolved and the relational standing restored.

**Members:** vc=37232 (Rom 5:10), vc=62945 (Eph 2:16), vc=62946 (Col 1:20), vc=62947 (Col 1:22) — **4 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=62946 (Col 1:20) — canonical cosmic reconciliation.

---

### M11-E-VCG-02 — Ministry-of-reconciliation appeal (2 Cor 5 corpus)

**Context:** The 2 Cor 5:18–20 ministry-of-reconciliation passage — the theological heart of M11-E. God initiates reconciliation through Christ and entrusts the ministry (5:18); does not count trespasses against people (5:19); appeals through ambassadors "be reconciled to God" (5:20). Inner-being content is divine initiative meeting human responsive will.

**Members:** vc=37234 (2Cor 5:18), vc=37235 (2Cor 5:19), vc=37236 (2Cor 5:20) — **3 verses**

**Anchor:** vc=37234 (2Cor 5:18) — canonical "God reconciled us... and gave us the ministry of reconciliation."

## §3. Cross-VCG observations (Phase D + Phase E seeds)

1. **M11-E is the smallest sub-group (9 verses) but theologically pivotal** — the entire M11 cluster's mechanism-to-outcome arc terminates at reconciliation. The kip.per/sa.lach internal arc (M11-A-VCG-02 ↔ M11-B-VCG-05) and the repentance-turning movement (M11-D) converge on reconciliation as the relational outcome.
2. **VCG-01 + VCG-02 together name the M11-NT theological centre** — Christ's death effects cosmic reconciliation (VCG-01); the church carries the ministry of reconciliation (VCG-02). Phase D T6 will articulate the M11-A-VCG-10 (Christic atoning surrender) → M11-E-VCG-01 (reconciliation accomplished) arc.
3. **VCG-04 (Rom 11:15)** — the apobole-as-mechanism paradox is structurally unique in the cluster; it names loss/rejection (M10 register) as the mechanism by which reconciliation extends. Phase D T6 should articulate the rejection-reconciliation paradox.
