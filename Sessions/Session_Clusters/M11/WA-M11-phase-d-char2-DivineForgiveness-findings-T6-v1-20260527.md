**T6.2.1 — Sequential Relationships**
**[CHAR-2]** E — Sequential patterns: (i) **Atonement → Forgiveness** (M11-A → M11-B): the canonical Levitical sequence (Lev 4:20 et al.); the most-evidenced inter-characteristic sequence in the M11 cluster. (ii) **Repentance → Forgiveness** (M11-D → M11-B): Mar 4:12, Isa 55:7, 2Ch 7:14, Act 2:38. (iii) **Confession + Repentance → Forgiveness**: 1Jo 1:9, 2Ch 7:14 explicit. (iv) **Faith → Forgiveness**: Mat 9:2, Act 10:43, Jam 5:15. (v) **Reciprocal**: human-forgiving ↔ divine-forgiving (Mat 6:14–15). Forgiveness is consistently the *declared outcome* in these sequences.

---

**T6.2.2 — Sequential Relationships**
**[CHAR-2]** E — Relationships are principally **causal-and-constitutive**. (i) Atonement causally produces forgiveness (Lev 4:20 explicit; M11-A is the *inside* of M11-B in the Levitical pattern). (ii) Repentance causally enables forgiveness (Isa 55:7 "abundant pardon for the wicked who forsake ways"; Mar 4:12 "turning required"; the mechanism: turning opens the receiving-channel). (iii) Faith causally enables forgiveness (Mat 9:2; Act 10:43 "faith activates"). (iv) The reciprocal binding (Mat 6:14–15) is *both causal* (forgiving causes one's own forgiving-receiving) *and constitutive* (the forgiving-disposition is constitutive of the forgiven-identity). Developmental: the recurring pattern (1Jo 1:9 ongoing) is developmental over time.

**T6.3.1 — Causal and Constitutive Relationships**
**[CHAR-2]** E — Forgiveness produces: (i) **M11-E Reconciliation** (relational restoration follows the declared-release at the standing-level; Mat 5:24 reconciliation-before-worship implies released-standing as precondition). **T6.3.2 — Causal and Constitutive Relationships**
**[CHAR-2]** E — Forgiveness is produced by / occasioned by: (i) **M11-A Atonement** (Levitical mechanism produces declared-forgiveness); (ii) **M11-D Repentance** (turning enables the receiving); (iii) **M21 Prayer** (intercession occasions the divine response; temple-prayer corpus); (iv) **M05 Compassion** (Mat 18:27 the master's pity-driven release; Psa 78:38 divine compassion); (v) **M30 Obedience-of-faith** (Mat 9:2 faith-as-trust enabling reception). The forgiveness-producing chain runs through M11-A or M11-D (or both), often mediated by M21, grounded in M05.

**T6.3.3 — Causal and Constitutive Relationships**

Conversely, **M05 Love** constitutes part of forgiveness (Mat 18:27 compassion as inner-mechanism); **M11-A Atonement** constitutes the operative-mechanism that the declared-forgiveness recognizes (the Levitical pattern); **M11-D Repentance** constitutes the opening-condition.
