## §2.1 `M39-A` — Blessing and Grace

_The sovereign, initiated movement of the greater toward the lesser — God's blessing, grace, and favour as dispositioned gift; the inner-being characteristic in which one person (primarily God) constitutes favour, standing, and inner transformation in another who cannot earn or generate it.
