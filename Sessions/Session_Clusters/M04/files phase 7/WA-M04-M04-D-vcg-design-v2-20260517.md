## M04-D-VCG-01 — Shared Joy: Constitutive Co-Rejoicing in Community

**Context description:** This VCG covers the entire M04-D corpus — rejoicing that is constitutively shared, invited, and completed in community. The sunchairo verb (7 verses) defines the register: the shepherd calls friends and neighbours to share his joy at the found sheep (Luk 15:6); the woman invites others into her joy at the found coin (Luk 15:9); Elizabeth's mercy becomes a communal bond as neighbours and relatives share in it (Luk 1:58); the body's shared gladness when one member is honoured (1Cor 12:26); love co-rejoicing with truth and refusing wrongdoing (1Cor 13:6); Paul and the Philippians sharing joy even in the face of sacrifice (Phil 2:17/18 ×2). The Aramaic ched.vah (Ezr 6:16) anchors the OT: communal emotional response to the house of God's completion after exile. The two missing XREF pairs (Phil 2:17 and 2:18) are assigned here as sibling-OWNER pairs. The defining trait across all 10: joy that moves from one to many, constituting a bond.
