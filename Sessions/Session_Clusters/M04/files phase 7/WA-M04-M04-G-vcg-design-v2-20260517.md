## M04-G-VCG-01 — Law and Word Delight: Affective Pleasure in God's Commands and Instruction

**Context description:** This VCG names the concentrated register of inner pleasure taken specifically in God's word, statutes, testimonies, commandments, and wisdom — pleasure that is affective (felt, relational), not merely intellectual. The Psalm 119 series is the defining corpus: sha.a (delight in statutes paired with memory, Psa 119:16; delight intensified by love for the commandments, Psa 119:47; law-delight as structural opposite of spiritual numbness, Psa 119:70; God's consolations delighting the soul amid cares, Psa 94:19) and sha.a.shu.im (testimonies as personal delight and counsellors shaping decisions, Psa 119:24; law as delight sustaining life under divine compassion, Psa 119:77; law as delight that prevented perishing in affliction, Psa 119:92 — the most existentially acute; delight as counterweight to trouble and anguish, Psa 119:143; law-delight sustaining the person while waiting for deliverance, Psa 119:174). Also: the Sabbath called a delight (o.neg, Isa 58:13), the inner exultation in pain grounded in faithfulness to God's word (sa.lad, Job 6:10), and the most explicit NT statement: delight located precisely in the inner being, with God's law as its object, creating a split between inner person and flesh (sunēdomai, Rom 7:22).

## M04-G-VCG-02 — Delight in YHWH and Wisdom's Relational Delight

**Context description:** This VCG names the register of delight directed toward YHWH himself as the direct recipient (a.nog), and the closely related register of Wisdom's relational and divine cherishing (sha.a.shu.im in the Pro 8/Isa/Jer relational register) and sha.a's carefree shalom-delight. Delight in the Almighty as a future inner state of intimacy for the restored person (Job 22:26), the hypocrite's absence of true delight in the Almighty contrasting with the genuine inner orientation of persistent prayer (Job 27:10), delighting in the Lord as an inner orientation aligned with his giving (Psa 37:4), the meek's delight in abundant peace (Psa 37:11), the call to direct delight toward what truly satisfies rather than hollow substitutes (Isa 55:2), the soul's delight in the Lord as response to honouring the Sabbath (Isa 58:14), delighting in Jerusalem's abundant consolation (Isa 66:11). The sha.a.shu.im relational register: Wisdom as God's daily delight before creation (Pro 8:30), Wisdom's delight in the children of man (Pro 8:31), Israel as God's pleasant planting — his treasured affection (Isa 5:7), God's tender cherishing of Ephraim persisting through rebuke (Jer 31:20). The sha.a carefree-shalom verses: nursing child playing fearlessly over a cobra's hole picturing the peace-age (Isa 11:8), infant cradled in comfort picturing tender renewal (Isa 66:12). The a.nog dainty-register entry (Deu 28:56) — the most refined woman whose delicate orientation is destroyed by siege — is assigned here as the negative/absent delight edge of the register.
