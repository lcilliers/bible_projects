## Flag P7-001 — Est 8:16-17 (vc_id 23866, 23867): M04-D routed but deliverance-joy content may fit M04-A-VCG-02

**Verses:** vc_id 23866 (Est 8:16, sa.s.von) and vc_id 23867 (Est 8:17, sa.s.von)  
**Current routing:** M04-D (primary sub-group for sa.s.von)  
**VCG assignment:** M04-D-VCG-01 (Joy of the Redeemed)  
**Observation:** These two verses name the joy filling the Jewish community after the edict of deliverance from Haman's decree. The meaning content is event-specific rejoicing at rescue — closer to M04-A-VCG-02 (Joy at God's Saving Acts) than to the promised/eschatological restoration register of M04-D. However, the sa.s.von term is primary-D by the Phase 5 routing, and the deliverance-reversal of sorrow (Purim) has an M04-D dimension. Assigned to M04-D-VCG-01 as primary. **CC note:** If correction is warranted, these two verses could route to M04-A-VCG-02 without disrupting term-level routing.

## Flag P7-006 — Divine-inner-state candidates: observation on M04-A-VCG-03 boundary

**Subject:** M04-A-VCG-03 — Divine Joy directed toward his people  
**Observation per Phase 7 brief §3:** The brief calls for watching for "verses where God's joy is described as an independent divine inner life event" (analogous to M01 Deu 32:27, Job 25:2) that might warrant set-aside. Reading the M04-A-VCG-03 corpus:

- All God-as-subject delight/joy verses in M04 are characteristic-in-operation: Father's delight in the Son is directed toward a person and shapes the saving relationship (eudokeō passages); Jer 32:41 (God's joy in doing good to his people) is relationally directed; Zep 3:17 (God rejoices over his people) is relationally directed.
- No verses in M04 meet the strict set-aside criterion of an independent divine inner life event without relational direction toward persons. Even Psa 135:6 / 115:3 (God does all that he pleases — sovereign will) and Isa 46:10 (God's pleasure/purpose standing firm) are expressions of sovereign will rather than isolated divine inner-state events analogous to M01's Deu 32:27.

**Assessment:** No divine-inner-state set-asides warranted for M04. All divine joy/delight verses in the corpus are relational (directed toward the Son, toward his people, toward steadfast love and righteousness). They stay in M04-A-VCG-03 per programme-scope note.

## Flag P7-008 — M04-F: Isa 28:29 and 29:14 assigned to both VCG-01 and VCG-02

**Verses:** vc_id 45094 (Isa 28:29, pa.la) and vc_id 45095 (Isa 29:14, pa.la) + vc_id 45128 (Isa 29:14, pe.le)  
**Observation:** These verses are assigned to M04-F-VCG-01 in the above design but also belong to VCG-02 (Wonder as Incomprehensible). Isa 28:29 ("God's counsel is wondrous, his wisdom great") evidences wonder at divine incomprehensibility (VCG-02 register) more than at proclaimed historical wonders (VCG-01 register). Isa 29:14 ("I will again do wonderful things with this people") is a proclaimed wonder (VCG-01) but the verse's content about overturning human wisdom is VCG-02 adjacent. **Correction:** Isa 28:29 and 29:14 (pa.la + pe.le at 29:14) should be in VCG-02. Update JSON key_verses accordingly. The design document places Isa 28:29 in VCG-01 key_verses but notes it could be VCG-02 — recommend moving both to VCG-02.

**Proposed correction for CC:** Move vc_ids 45094, 45095, 45128 from M04-F-VCG-01 to M04-F-VCG-02.
