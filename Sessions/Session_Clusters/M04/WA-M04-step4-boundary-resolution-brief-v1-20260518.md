## Your task

For each of the **257 BOUNDARY vc_ids** in the structural input, propose exactly one disposition per v2_5 §18.2:

### Disposition vocabulary

**SET-ASIDE** — the verse evidences no inner-being state of any kind. Eligibility: per §4.5.1, only valid when the verse describes external events, ritual procedure, geographic information, or genealogy without invoking an interior state; OR when the term refers to something other than an inner state (literal object, place, idiom).

**ROUTE-TO-CLUSTER {target_cluster_code}** — the verse's primary inner-being content belongs to another cluster, AND the target cluster has an active term at the same `verse_record_id`. **Eligibility check (§18.2):** consult the co-occurrence list in the structural input for each verse. If no target-cluster term appears, ROUTE-TO-CLUSTER is **not eligible** — choose SET-ASIDE or PROMOTE-TO-SUBGROUP instead.

**PROMOTE-TO-SUBGROUP {target_subgroup_code}** — the verse carries M04 inner-being content. Assign to an existing M04 sub-group (M04-A through M04-P). Only propose a new sub-group if a verse genuinely does not fit any of the 16 existing sub-groups; given Step 3 just added 6 new sub-groups specifically for register families that didn't fit before, new proposals should be rare.

**Forbidden non-dispositions** (§11A): PARK, DEFER, HOLD, RESEARCHER-DECISION-LATER, WAIT, PENDING. Every vc_id must receive one of the three. If you genuinely cannot decide between two valid dispositions, write `RESEARCHER-DECISION` with both options + your reasoning — but this should be rare.

### §4.5.1 forbidden grounds for SET-ASIDE

The following are **NOT valid reasons** for SET-ASIDE and will be rejected:

- "Not God-directed" / "no clear vertical framing"
- "Lacks theological depth" / "no spiritual category named"
- "Inner state too mundane" / "too circumstantial"
- "Inner state too negative" / "too corrupt to be inner being"
- "Inner state is bodily / sensory rather than spiritual"
- Terse one-phrase reasons like "physical_only" or "no_inner_being"

The **only valid reasons** for SET-ASIDE:

- "No inner-being state evidenced — the verse describes [external events / ritual procedure / geographic information / genealogy] only." (with verse-specific evidence)
- "The term in this verse refers to [literal object / place / idiomatic phrase] that does not invoke an interior state." (with verse-specific evidence)
- "Out-of-scope by design — the term/verse belongs to a different programme axis." (rare)

**Every SET-ASIDE rationale must cite verse-specific evidence.** Generic reasons will be rejected.

### §8.4.1 BOUNDARY-is-not-a-parking-lot

You are dispositioning verses OUT of BOUNDARY — not putting more verses INTO BOUNDARY. Every disposition removes the verse from `M04-BOUNDARY`. If you find yourself wanting to "leave it in BOUNDARY for now", the correct response is `RESEARCHER-DECISION` with a clear reason. Otherwise pick one of the three.

## §1.1 in-scope register families — keep this in front of you

Inner being covers the **entire human inner life** — no theological filter. Verses in any of these registers are legitimately M04 content (most belong in M04-K through M04-P sub-groups):

- **Vertical relational** — toward God (joy in God, exultation in YHWH) → M04-A
- **Communal/Festive** — corporate celebration, worship feasts → M04-B
- **NT Joy in Christ** — gospel-community joy → M04-C
- **Shared Communal** — solidarity, co-celebration → M04-D
- **Promised/Eschatological** — restoration joy, suffering-paradox → M04-E
- **Cheerfulness under Adversity** → M04-F
- **Delight in God's Word/Wisdom** → M04-G
- **Volitional Delight** — sovereign will and directed desire → M04-H
- **Wonder** — awe at marvellous works → M04-I
- **Pleasantness/Relational** — agreeableness of persons, bonds → M04-J
- **Material/Sensory** — luxury, sensory enjoyment, aromatic sacrifice → M04-K
- **Evaluative Goodness** — moral or relational appraisal of "good", "surpassing" → M04-L
- **Pleasing as Obedience** — what is acceptable to God → M04-M
- **Horizontal Relational** — between persons, parental, neighbour, captivation → M04-N
- **Circumstantial Gladness** — good news, beneficial states → M04-O
- **Corrupt/Illicit Delight** — predatory, lustful, misdirected → M04-P

A verse describing parental delight in children is **inner-being content** → M04-N or M04-J. A verse describing a "good appearance" of young men is **inner-being-of-evaluative-faculty content** → M04-L. A verse about predatory exultation devouring the poor is **corrupt inner-being content** → M04-P. None of these are SET-ASIDE candidates.
