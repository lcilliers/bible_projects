## Special handling for M04

### 1. M04 is more cohesive than M03 — expect fewer sub-groups

M01 produced 7 substantive + BOUNDARY (8 total). M02 produced 6 substantive + BOUNDARY (7 total). M03 produced 7 substantive + BOUNDARY (8 total). M04 is semantically tighter than M03 — the joy/delight register is more cohesive than M03's grief vocabulary which split across weeping, mourning, anguish, groaning, pain, bitterness, sorrow.

Expect **4–6 substantive sub-groups + 1 BOUNDARY** = 5–7 total. Don't artificially split a coherent register; don't artificially merge distinct registers. Let the meanings group.

### 3. Cross-register patterns to watch for

**Joy expressed in YHWH** — many sa.mach / chairō / agalliao verses anchor joy specifically in YHWH or in his salvation/works (Psa 16:11; Phil 4:4; Hab 3:18; 1Sa 2:1; Isa 61:10; Hab 3:18). This vertical-joy register may form its own sub-group distinct from horizontal/communal joy.

**Communal rejoicing** — joy as collective festival/feast/communal worship (Lev 23:40; Deu 12:7,12,18; Deu 16:11,14; 1Ch 12:40; 29:9; 2Ch 30:21,23; Neh 12:43; Ezr 6:22; Psa 100:1-2). May form a sub-group.

**Divine delight / God's pleasure in his people** — cha.phets/eudokeō verses where God is the subject (Pro 11:20; Isa 62:4; Mat 3:17; Luk 12:32; 2Pe 1:17). Programme-scope reminder (§7 below) applies.

**Eschatological joy** — promised joy / joy of the kingdom (Isa 35:10; 51:11; 65:18-19; 66:10; Joh 16:22; Rev 19:7). May be distinct.

**Joy in suffering / paradoxical joy** — NT-distinctive register (Mat 5:12; Rom 5:3; Jam 1:2; 1Pe 1:6,8; 4:13; Phil 1:18; 2Cor 6:10). May warrant its own sub-group if the corpus supports it.

**Wonder at God** (pa.la/pe.le) — God's marvellous works as object of inner wonder; may stay as its own sub-group or fold into "joy in YHWH" depending on corpus reading.

### 6. Programme scope reminder — human inner being

M04's scope is **human inner being**. However, God is frequently described as rejoicing, delighting, taking pleasure (Zeph 3:17, Isa 62:5, Mat 3:17, Luk 12:32, Pro 11:20). These are **characteristic-in-operation** descriptions — joy/delight enacted by God, often toward his people — and they belong in M04 as M01/M02/M03 precedent established. Don't set them aside.

The narrow set-aside criterion (per Phase 7 review at later phases): verses where God's joy is described as an *independent divine inner life event* (analogous to M01 Deu 32:27, Job 25:2). These are rare in M04's corpus.
