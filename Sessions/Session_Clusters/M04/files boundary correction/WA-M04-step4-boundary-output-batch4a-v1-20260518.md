**Analytical framework for tov dispositions:**

Tov spans multiple inner-being registers requiring per-verse decisions:
- **M04-L (Evaluative Goodness):** tov as the inner-being faculty of moral or relational evaluation — appraisal that something is good, better than, or surpassing. Includes: God's moral character declared as good; human character described as good/moral; comparative evaluations ("better than").
- **M04-H (Volitional Delight):** tov in the "seems good to you" formula — marking the inner volitional approval/pleasure as the criterion for action. Also: "what seems good to God" as sovereign determination.
- **M04-O (Circumstantial Gladness):** tov naming a positive circumstantial or experiential state — "it will go well with you," "it was better for us then," beneficial states received by the inner person.
- **M04-K (Material/Sensory):** tov naming material wellbeing, sensory goodness, refreshment.
- **SET-ASIDE:** Only when tov describes a purely external object or location without any inner-being state evidenced.
