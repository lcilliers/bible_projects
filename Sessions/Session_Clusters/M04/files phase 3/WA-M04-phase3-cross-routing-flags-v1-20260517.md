## Flag 1 — G2237 hēdonē: transfer destination may need revision if M29 activates

**Term:** G2237 hēdonē (pleasure, mti_id=3090)  
**Phase 3 verdict:** TRANSFERS-TO-M28-Envy  
**Flag:** The transfer to M28-Envy is based on the nearest currently available cluster. However the 5-verse corpus evidences enslaving, self-seeking sensual pleasure as an internal driving force (Jam 4:1 — "pleasures at war within you") rather than envy or greed per se. If M29-Desire (Desire, Longing and Will) is activated in future, hēdonē belongs there rather than in M28. The current M28-Envy designation is a holding route; researcher should review when M29 is established.

**Specific verse note:** Jam 4:1 — "passions at war within you" — this verse locates hēdonē as a warring inner drive, which is the M29-Desire register. Jam 4:3 — wrongful prayer motivated by self-serving pleasure — same register. 2Pe 2:13 — depraved inner evaluation of reveling — M27-Evil adjacent in addition to M28 or M29. Luk 8:14 — pleasures choking the word — M35-Testing (temptation/trial) adjacent.

## Flag 3 — H1361 ga.vah: M04-proximate verses in a M08-bound term

**Term:** H1361 ga.vah (to exult, mti_id=100)  
**Phase 3 verdict:** TRANSFERS-TO-M08-Pride  
**Flag:** Two verses in the ga.vah corpus are the most M04-proximate instances and may be worth noting when M08 builds its Phase 5 sub-groups:

- **2Ch 17:6** — Jehoshaphat's heart being courageous/lifted up positively in the ways of the Lord — the term here describes an elevated inner resolve directed toward faithful obedience, not pride. This is the clearest counter-register in the corpus. It does not change the verdict (the corpus as a whole is M08) but notes a contextual use that differs from the dominant pride register.
- **Isa 52:13** — The servant's exaltation: he is lifted high and exalted. This is divine vindication rather than self-promoting pride. Again counter to the dominant corpus register.

These are not mis-routing flags at the verse level — both verses are correctly included in the ga.vah corpus. They are noted for M08 Phase 5 sub-group design so that M08's sub-group structure can handle the positive-elevation sense as a distinct register.

## Flag 4 — H2896A tov: sub-register map for Phase 12 researcher decision

**Term:** H2896A tov (pleasant, mti_id=884)  
**Phase 3 verdict:** BOUNDARY  
**Flag:** The tov corpus (230 verses) contains at least four analytically distinct sub-registers. This flag provides a working register map to support the researcher's Phase 12 decision:

| Sub-register | Representative verses | M04 alignment |
|---|---|---|
| Divine evaluative delight in creation | Gen 1:4, 1:10, 1:12, 1:18, 1:21, 1:25, 1:31 | M04-adjacent (God's approving inner response to creation) |
| Aesthetic pleasantness / inner delight | Psa 133:1 (brotherly unity pleasant), Psa 135:3 (praise pleasant), Pro 16:24 (honey-like gracious words to soul), Zec 8:19 (cheerful feasts), Pro 15:15 (cheerful heart continual feast) | STAYS in M04 if split |
| Inner wellbeing / soul-flourishing | Lam 3:25–27 (it is good for the soul to wait), Jer 6:16 (good way brings rest for the soul), 1Sa 16:16/23 (Saul's restored inner state — good/well) | Potentially STAYS (inner wellbeing) |
| Moral-quality goodness | Pro 2:20, 12:2, 14:14, 16:8, 19:1 etc.; SA evaluative judgments about moral conduct | TRANSFERS to future Goodness cluster |
| Volitional-deference formula | Gen 16:6, 20:15, 1Sa 3:18, 11:10, 14:36, 14:40, 2Sa 10:12, 24:3 etc. | TRANSFERS to M29-Desire or future Goodness cluster |
| Property/agricultural/material good | Deu 8:10 (good land), Num 10:29 (do good to you), Jos 21:45, 23:14–16 etc. | Not M04 |

**Researcher note:** If the decision is to BOUNDARY and hold tov for Phase 12 split, the register map above provides the analytical basis for dividing the 230-verse corpus. The verses listed under "STAYS in M04 if split" are the ones with genuine inner-delight content. The moral-quality and volitional-deference verses are the majority and should be considered for transfer to a future Goodness or Will cluster. No tov re-extraction is being proposed — this is a Phase 12 analytical flag only.

## Flag 5 — H5207 ni.cho.ach: priestly formula sub-register

**Term:** H5207 ni.cho.ach (soothing, mti_id=2584)  
**Phase 3 verdict:** BOUNDARY  
**Flag:** The 43-verse corpus is overwhelmingly the priestly "pleasing aroma" formula (Lev 1–26, Num 15, 28–29 primarily). These 40+ instances form a coherent semantic block — the sacrificial-reception register — distinct from the 3 Ezekiel instances (misdirected worship to idols) and the single Eze 20:41 instance (God receiving restored Israel as a pleasing aroma).

If ni.cho.ach is retained in M04 at Phase 12, its sub-group at Phase 5 would likely be "divine pleasure in properly offered worship" — a distinct register from the core joy/gladness vocabulary. If transferred to M21-Prayer, the entire priestly corpus transfers as a block. The researcher's Phase 12 decision should consider whether the programme's treatment of sacrificial vocabulary is to be housed in the emotion clusters (M04) or the worship-practice cluster (M21).

## Flag 6 — H5951 a.li.tsut: single-verse corpus note

**Term:** H5951 a.li.tsut (exultation, mti_id=1096)  
**Phase 3 verdict:** BOUNDARY

## Flag 7 — H8588 ta.a.nug and H6028 a.nog (dainty): luxury register may belong to M46-Abundance

The researcher may wish to route both terms to M46-Abundance at Phase 12 if the verdict is not M04-STAYS.
