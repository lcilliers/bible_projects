## §1 — Characteristic 1 (Exultation)

**Short name:** Exultation
**Characteristic_id:** 1
**Definition:**

> The soul's active, surging, triumphant inner state directed at what is glorious or transcendent — above all at God. The inner being rises in exultant response. Distinguished from Joy by intensity and directedness; the person who exults is overwhelmed upward toward their object. In Scripture this is almost always Godward: the soul exults in YHWH's salvation, in his holy name, in his mighty acts.

**Constituent sub-groups:** 1
**Total verses in scope:** ~83

---

## §2 — Sub-groups (capacity organisers — not the analytical unit)

### M04-A — Exultation in YHWH: Vertical Rejoicing Directed Toward God

**core_description:** OT register of inner exultation directed explicitly toward YHWH as the object and ground of joy. The soul exults in YHWH's salvation, the heart rejoices in God's holy name, God's own inner delight is directed toward his people. Core OT vertical-exultation vocabulary: gil_verb, gil, su.s, cha.dah, re.na.nah, te.ev.

**Role under Exultation:** The Godward qualifier — exultation directed toward YHWH as object and ground; the primary and sole register of this characteristic in M04.

**Verses in this sub-group:** 83

**VCGs within this sub-group:**

- `M04-A-VCG-01` (58 verses) — God-directed exultation: rejoicing toward YHWH as object and source. Includes God's own delight toward his people.
- `M04-A-VCG-02` (18 verses) — Inverted, absent, or corrupt joy: rejoicing by negation — misdirected toward evil/idols/death, absent as curse, or enemies gloating.
- `M04-A-VCG-03` (7 verses) — Relational and natural gladness: rejoicing in human bonds, parental delight, creation's personified response.
