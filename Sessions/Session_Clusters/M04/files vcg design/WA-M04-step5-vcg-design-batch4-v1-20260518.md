## M04-H — Volitional Delight: Inner Pleasure as Sovereign Will and Directed Desire (51 verses, existing sub-group)

Existing VCGs:
- `M04-H-VCG-01` (anchor Mat 12:18, 23 members): Divine pleasure in the Son and sovereign will — eudokeo baptism/transfiguration + cha.phets/che.phets as God doing what he pleases.
- `M04-H-VCG-02` (anchor Psa 51:6, 29 members): God's relational delight toward persons — divine pleasure as redemptive affection motivating rescue, exaltation, mercy.
- `M04-H-VCG-03` (anchor Psa 40:8, 64 members): Human volitional willing and personal desire — cha.phets/che.phets marking human inner willingness, longing, directed will.
- `M04-H-VCG-04` (anchor 2Th 2:12, 17 members): Negated, absent, and corrupt pleasure — delight withheld, misdirected toward evil, or condemned as morally culpable.

The 51 verses are overwhelmingly the "seems good to you / if it pleases the king / do as you please" tov formula — human volitional judgment or deference to another's inner will as the operative criterion of action. These assign to VCG-03. Exceptions noted per verse.
