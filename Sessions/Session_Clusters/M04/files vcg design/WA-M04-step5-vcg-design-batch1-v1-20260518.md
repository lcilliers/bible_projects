### VCG definitions for M04-N

**VCG: M04-N-VCG-01**  
Description: Horizontal relational pleasantness — the inner-being experience of delight or felt value in a human bond or person. The register includes parental tender attachment to children ("children of your delight," Mic 1:16, ta.a.nug — an attachment so deep that their exile is mourned by self-shaving), and the comparative valuing of a nearby neighbour over a distant brother in crisis (Pro 27:10 — the pleasantness of relational nearness as an experienced good). Both verses share the phenomenon of inner-being appreciation directed at persons in human relationship: a felt pleasantness in the proximity or existence of another person.  
Anchor: Mic 1:16  (vc_id=9661)  
Members: vc=19666, vc=9661

**VCG: M04-N-VCG-02**  
Description: Captivating marvel at a powerful person/entity — the inner state of being drawn in wonder toward a being whose extraordinary power or apparent return generates awe and following. Both instances are in Revelation: the whole earth marvels at the beast's healed mortal wound and follows it (Rev 13:3), and the unredeemed marvel at the beast's apparent return (Rev 17:8). This wonder-at-an-entity is in the horizontal register — directed at a creature, not at God's works — and is marked as spiritually dangerous captivation belonging to those outside the book of life. Distinguished from M04-I's wonder-at-God's-works by its object (a false power, not the true God) and its function (deceptive captivation, not genuine awe).  
Anchor: Rev 13:3  (vc_id=44868)  
Members: vc=44868, vc=44871

### VCG definitions for M04-P

**VCG: M04-P-VCG-01**  
Description: Misdirected sacrificial delight — the pleasing aroma (ni.cho.ach) of worship offered to idols and unauthorized shrines rather than to YHWH. Three Ezekiel passages (Eze 6:13, 16:19, 20:28) document Israel's illicit worship aroma offered on high hills, under trees, and from God's own gifts diverted to false gods. The inner-being phenomenon is the act of offering designed to please — an act that in righteous form satisfies and pleases God (Gen 8:21, Lev passim) — misdirected so that the pleasure-toward-God function is perverted into pleasure-toward-idols. This is corrupt delight in the cultic register.  
Anchor: Eze 20:28  (vc_id=32677)  
Members: vc=32638, vc=32676, vc=32677

**VCG: M04-P-VCG-02**  
Description: Lustful captivation — the inner craving and erotic attraction (che.med) driving Israel's unfaithfulness to YHWH through desire for foreign military power. Three Ezekiel 23 verses (vv. 6, 12, 23) describe Oholah and Oholibah's lust for Assyrian and Babylonian young men — their desirability (che.med) is the inner experience of being captivated and drawn by outward power and appearance, fuelling spiritual adultery and political betrayal. The delight is rooted in the inner state of desire/craving directed at persons whose appeal is military, aesthetic, and powerful — corrupt because misdirected away from faithfulness to God.  
Anchor: Eze 23:12  (vc_id=10669)  
Members: vc=10670, vc=10669, vc=10671

**VCG: M04-P-VCG-03**  
Description: Predatory exultation — the inner elation (a.li.tsut) of those who attack the vulnerable in secret, rejoicing as they devour the poor. Hab 3:14 is the sole instance: the warrior band's exultation is not triumphant joy after honest battle but the sinister pleasure of predators who glory in cruelty against the defenceless. The inner-being state is a disordered, vicious gladness — delight taken in the act of inflicting harm — making it a distinct register of corrupt pleasure from worship-misdirection (VCG-01) and lustful captivation (VCG-02).  
Anchor: Hab 3:14  (vc_id=35728)  
Members: vc=35728
