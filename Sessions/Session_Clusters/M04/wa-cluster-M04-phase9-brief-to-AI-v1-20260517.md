### 4. M04-H Volitional delight (cha.phets, 133 verses, 4 VCGs)

### 5. Programme scope reminder — divine joy is in scope

Divine joy (Zep 3:17, Isa 62:5, Mat 3:17, Eph 1:5, Pro 11:20) describes **the characteristic in operation** — joy/delight enacted by God toward his people — and **stays in scope** per M01/M02/M03 precedent. The narrow set-aside criterion: only verses describing God's joy as an *independent divine inner life event* analogous to M01 Deu 32:27, Job 25:2. M04 has very few such candidates.

### 2. Authoring discipline (parser-safe form — §12.4)

The Phase 11 loader parses these documents — do not deviate from format:

- **One scope marker per line.** `**[scope]**` at line start.
- **Every prompt carries ≥1 explicit scope marker.**
- **One block per (prompt × scope).**
- **Block separator.** A horizontal rule `---` on its own line marks end-of-prompt.

**Marker catalogue:**

| Marker | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `**[A]**` ... `**[J]**` | sub-group M04-A ... M04-J |
| `**[A, B, C]**` | finding shared across listed sub-groups |
| `**[B-VCG-02]**` | VCG-level scope (v2_4 §14.4) |
| `**[B-VCG-01/03/04]**` | multi-VCG scope (slash-separated) |
| `**[CLUSTER]**` or `**[CLUSTER — all sub-groups]**` | cluster-level synthesis |
| `**[BOUNDARY — H1234 translit]**` | per-term structural characterisation |
| `**[BOUNDARY]**` | cross-BOUNDARY observation |

**Inline outcome codes:**
- `E — text` → finding (or `cluster_synthesis` if scope is CLUSTER)
- `S — text` → silent
- `G — text` → gap

### 3. Self-contained requirement
