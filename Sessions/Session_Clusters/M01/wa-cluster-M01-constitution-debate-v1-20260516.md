### G0085 ademoneo — be distressed (mti_id=2)

**Verdict: BOUNDARY**

The three-verse meaning corpus (Mat 26:37, Mar 14:33, Phili 2:26) shows a profound inner distress — in Gethsemane Jesus experiences "deeply agitated inner state," "severe inner anguish," and Epaphroditus is distressed by relational concern for the Philippians. The distress is real and acute, but its characteristic is better described as anguished heaviness of soul under pressure than fear-terror-dread. There is no element of alarm, threat-perception, or reverential awe in any of the three verses. The corpus sits closer to M03 Grief or M24 Weakness than to M01, yet the term is also distinguishable from those clusters. It is marked BOUNDARY pending researcher review of whether it belongs in M01 at all or should transfer elsewhere.

**Analytical question:** Does ademoneo carry any fear-component, or is it a grief-anguish term that drifted into M01 from its registry? Compare against M03 Grief and M24 Weakness before deciding.
