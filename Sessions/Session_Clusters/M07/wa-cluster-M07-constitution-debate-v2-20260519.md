## Cross-term observations (updated from v1)

### 1. The only confirmed accidental placements: H2616B and H2617B

The checed terms are the only terms in M07's cluster where verse-level reading finds zero M07 relationship. All other terms — even those from non-shame home registries — carry at least one verse with a genuine connection to shame, disgrace, or humiliation. This confirms that H2616B and H2617B are uniquely misplaced: their corpus evidences the loyal-love root exclusively, with no shame dimension at any verse.

### 2. Contempt-shame axis: G1848 exoutheneō

The most analytically significant revised verdict. All 11 exoutheneō verses evidence the contempt side of the contempt-shame dynamic — the act of dismissive devaluation that produces shame in the recipient. This term evidences the active mechanism by which shame is inflicted. M06 and M07 are structurally linked through this term: M06 holds the contempt-projection register; M07 holds the shame-reception register. Phase 9 T6 cross-cluster prompts should record this relationship.

### 3. Innocence-shame polarity: H5356A and H5356B

The innocence terms (niq.qa.von, qe.ha.von) evidence the structural opposite of shame. Every verse in their corpus engages the innocence-shame polarity — innocence as the inner condition that protects against, resolves, or is expected to prevent shame. This relationship is analytically rich for M07's understanding of shame's inner-being preconditions and remedies. The polarity is a Phase 5/7 asset.

### 4. Shame-silencing register: G5392 fimoō and G0880 afōnos

Both terms evidence a shame-silencing connection — shame as the force that stops the mouth, and silence as the posture of bearing shame. This parallels M07's own ke.lim.mah corpus (Eze 16:63: "shame stops the mouth"). The silencing-shame connection may form a distinct sub-group or VCG within M07 at Phase 5/7.

### 5. Verbal shaming instruments: G2551 kakologeō and G3059 loidoria

Both reviling/slander terms evidence shame inflicted through speech — verbal attack as an instrument of public disgrace. Their presence in M07 captures the speech-to-shame mechanism. This complements but differs from exoutheneō (contempt as attitude) — here the medium is specifically verbal.

### 6. Post-transfer M07 core after Phase 4

After the two checed transfers, M07 holds 34 terms (31 STAYS + 5 BOUNDARY pending researcher decisions — but all 5 remain in M07 as BOUNDARY, not transferred). The cluster is substantially larger than v1's projected 21-term post-transfer corpus. This will affect Phase 5 distribution gate considerations.
