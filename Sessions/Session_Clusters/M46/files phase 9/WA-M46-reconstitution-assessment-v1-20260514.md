## Proposed reframe of M46

**From:** Abundance, Prosperity and Wealth (a characteristic cluster — contested and dissolved)  
**To:** Wealth, Riches and Material Abundance — the role and impact of material wealth on the inner being

This framing recognises that wealth is not itself an inner-being characteristic, but that it is one of Scripture's primary *domains of testing* for the inner person. The biblical vocabulary of wealth is extensive, theologically weighted, and genuinely distinct from the vocabulary of greed (M28), trust (M19), or wisdom (M15). A reconstituted M46 would hold the vocabulary of *material wealth as a domain* — including the state of being rich or poor, the inner effects of abundance and deprivation, the distortion and liberation that wealth produces, and the contentment or insatiability it exposes.

## Section B — Named cluster candidates

### From M28 Envy, Greed and Lust

The key boundary question: does M28 hold the *inner orientation toward wealth* (greed, envy, lust for money), or does it hold *wealth-adjacent vocabulary* more broadly? If M28 is about the characteristic of greed, then filarguria (love of money) belongs there — the characteristic is the craving, not the wealth. The wealth is M46's domain.

**Recommendation:** Leave filarguria and pleonexia in M28. They name the inner characteristic (craving, love of money), not the domain of wealth. M46 and M28 will naturally intersect — the wealth domain is where greed operates — but the terms themselves name the inner state, not the domain.

| Term | Gloss | Tier | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| filarguria (G5365) | love of money | **STAY IN M28** | Names the inner love/craving for money — a characteristic of the person, not a property of wealth. 1 Tim 6:10. |
| filarguros (G5366) | money-loving | **STAY IN M28** | Adjectival form of above. |
| pleonexia (G4124) | greediness | **STAY IN M28** | Wanting more — the inner orientation of insatiable desire. Operates across domains (not wealth-specific). |
| pleonektēs (G4123) | greedy | **STAY IN M28** | The greedy person. |

### From M05 Love, Compassion and Kindness

| Term | Gloss | Tier | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| autarkeia (G841) | self-sufficiency | **MOVE TO M46** | The noun form of autarkēs (T2 candidate above). 1 Tim 6:6 "godliness with contentment (autarkeia) is great gain"; 2 Cor 9:8 "God is able to make all grace abound to you, so that having all sufficiency (autarkeia) in all things... you may abound in every good work." It belongs in M46's domain (the inner-being response to material wealth/poverty) more naturally than in M15 Wisdom. The wisdom connection is real but secondary. |
