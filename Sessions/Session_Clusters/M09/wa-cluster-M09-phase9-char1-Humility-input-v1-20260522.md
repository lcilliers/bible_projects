## §1 — Characteristic 1 (Humility)

**Short name:** Humility
**Characteristic_id:** 19
**Definition:**

> The inner disposition of voluntary self-lowering before God and others. The settled posture of the heart that does not assert standing, claim merit, or insist on its own way. Volume-split into M09-A (willed self-lowering — voluntary/dispositional) and M09-B (experienced/imposed lowliness — state). Vocabulary: tapeinos / tapeinōsis / tapeinofrosunē / tapeinoō family; ka.na voluntary-humbling verses; tsa.na / tsa.nu.a (walking humbly with God).

**Constituent sub-groups:** 2
**Total verses in scope:** ~49
