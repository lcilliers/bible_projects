## VCG design

### M09-F-VCG-01 — Meekness as calibrated restraint: governed strength and volitional readiness

**Context description:** This VCG carries the meekness and gentleness characteristic — the inner quality of holding force, capability, or will in calibrated, measured expression. The high priest's capacity to deal gently with the erring is grounded in his own shared creaturely vulnerability rather than detachment or superiority — meekness here is not weakness but measured, compassionate restraint that requires both capability and the inner decision to modulate it (Heb5:2). The disciples' urging Peter to come without delay names the opposite pole: immediate, unimpeded volitional responsiveness — the absence of hesitation and reluctance that characterises the will that does not resist or drag (Act9:38). Both verses evidence the inner quality of meekness: not force absent, but force governed. Mat11:29 (vc_id 21101 — Jesus naming himself gentle and lowly in heart) contributes to this VCG as a **secondary multi-faceted member** (primary placement M09-A-VCG-04); its gentleness dimension belongs here as the paradigmatic self-description of calibrated inner disposition.
