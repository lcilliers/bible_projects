## Per-term verdicts

### G1299 diatassō — to direct (mti_id=2866) — STAYS (with cross-register flag)

**Primary register flag:** M23 (Strength, Power and Dominion — authority/directive function)

**Rationale:** The corpus of 12 verses is dominated by authority/command-giving content — apostolic directives, imperial edicts, divine instruction. Under the §6.3.2 verse-level test, however, Luk 17:10 evidences M09-relational content: the meaning records "an inner posture of self-assessment that rejects merit" in the servant who has done all that was directed. Being subject to direction produces — in this verse — a humility disposition of self-regarding unworthiness rather than self-claim. This single verse is sufficient under §6.3.2 to confirm STAYS: diatassō as the instrument that occasions an M09 inner posture. The term's primary register (authority/directive) is M23; the M09 relationship is carried by Luk 17:10 and flagged for Phase 5 sub-group placement and Phase 9 T6.

**Note:** The brief characterised diatassō as having "4 verses, all no inner-being content." The constitution report shows 12 is_relevant verses. All 12 were read. Luk 17:10 — not listed among the brief's pre-noted subset — evidences M09 content and determines the STAYS verdict.

### G3356 metriopatheō — be gentle (mti_id=3988) — STAYS

**Rationale:** Heb 5:2 (the single verse) names gentleness as "an inner capacity for measured, compassionate restraint grounded in shared creaturely vulnerability." This is core M09 meekness vocabulary — the inner quality of calibrated restraint rooted in awareness of one's own weakness. No cross-register flag needed.

---

### G3635 okneō — to delay (mti_id=7359) — STAYS

**Rationale:** Act 9:38 (the single verse) names "an absence of hesitation or reluctance, pointing to immediate, unimpeded responsiveness as the inner-volitional quality expected." Volitional readiness — the absence of resistant hesitation — is M09-relational: the will that does not resist or drag in response to a call. The meaning does not evidence the sloth register that would warrant TRANSFERS; it evidences appropriate volitional responsiveness.

---

### G4587 semnotēs — dignity (mti_id=812) — STAYS (with cross-register flag)

**Primary register flag:** M08 (Pride, Arrogance and Boasting — semnotēs is the structural opposite of boastful pride)

**Rationale:** All three verses evidence semnotēs as grounded moral gravity and seriousness of character — not social honour-rank. In 1Ti 2:2, it is the inner quality characterising a peaceable, godly life; in 1Ti 3:4, it is the character quality from which orderly household governance flows; in Tit 2:7, it is the gravitas that authenticates teaching. This settled, non-self-assertive gravity is M09-aligned: it is the inner quality that precludes ostentation, arrogance, and self-promotion. The M08 flag is advisory — semnotēs functions structurally opposite to pride's self-display — for Phase 9 T6 findings.

### G5011 tapeinos — lowly (mti_id=56) — STAYS

### G5012 tapeinofrosunē — humility (mti_id=57) — STAYS

### H3665 ka.na — be humble (mti_id=4475) — STAYS

**Rationale:** All 18 is_relevant verses evidence M09 content across the voluntary and imposed dimensions of humbling. The dominant pattern is voluntary inner humbling before God in the face of judgment or God's word (Lev 26:41, 1Ki 21:29, 2Ki 22:19, 2Ch 7:14, 2Ch 12:6, 2Ch 30:11, 2Ch 32:26, 2Ch 33:12, 2Ch 34:27; also negatively in 2Ch 33:23, 2Ch 36:12). Note: 14 verses were set aside at Phase 1 (the military-subduing register); the 18 is_relevant verses are the inner-humbling register confirmed by Phase 1 classification. No cross-register issues.

### H5081G na.div — noble: willing (mti_id=3079) — STAYS (with cross-register flag)

**Primary register flag:** M04 (Joy/Gladness — spontaneous generous-delight dimension); M29 (Desire/Will — volitional dimension)

**Rationale:** All 5 verses evidence the willing-heart/willing-spirit inner disposition — the freely-moved heart that gives and serves without compulsion (Exo 35:5, 35:22, 1Ch 28:21, 2Ch 29:31) and the willing spirit as a divine gift sustaining freely-offered devotion after moral failure (Psa 51:12). The willing-heart disposition is M09-relational: it is the inner posture of the will that opens toward God and community rather than self-protects — the structural opposite of the proud, resistant, self-asserting will. Psa 51:12 in particular, pairing the willing spirit with the joy of salvation in a penitential context, locates this in M09's characteristic scope. No verse evidences social noble-rank. Cross-register flags to M04 (the spontaneous-generosity/delight dimension) and M29 (the volitional readiness dimension) are advisory for Phase 5 sub-group placement and Phase 9 T6.

## Cross-term observations for Phase 5

The following patterns are noted as informational input to Phase 5 sub-group formation. These are observations, not prescriptions — sub-groups must emerge from meaning-cluster reading at Phase 5.

1. **Tapein- root family** (G5011, G5012, G5013, G5014): four terms from the same root form a cohesive humility-vocabulary family covering the adjective, noun of quality, verbal, and noun of state. Likely candidates for one or closely linked sub-groups in Phase 5.

2. **Submission/obedience register** (G5218, G5219, G5292, H3349): four terms covering the will's yielding to authority — two Greek noun/verb pairs and one Hebrew term — share the submission-of-will characteristic. Likely sub-group candidate.

3. **Hebrew humility register** (H6800A, H6800B, H1793A, H3665): four Hebrew terms covering humility and contrition. H3665 ka.na dominates in volume (18 verses); the others are thin (1–2 verses each). Sub-group formation should attend to whether ka.na's volume warrants a separate sub-group or whether the Hebrew cluster holds together.

4. **Volitional-willing register** (H5081G na.div, G3635 okneō): both evidence volitional readiness/willing disposition — the freely-moved will. May cluster with submission terms or stand as a distinct characteristic depending on Phase 5 reading.

5. **Gentleness/meekness register** (G3356 metriopatheō, G5011 tapeinos in its gentleness dimension): metriopatheō is a single-verse thin term; its VCG may combine with tapeinos's gentleness dimension within a broader meekness sub-group.

6. **Cross-register terms** (G1299 diatassō, G4587 semnotēs): both are thin in M09 content relative to their primary registers. Phase 5 should consider routing their M09-relational verses to existing sub-groups rather than forming dedicated sub-groups.

7. **Phase 5.5 flag — Luk 3:5 (G5013 tapeinoō):** Single verse of physical-prophetic imagery; set-aside candidate. Carries forward to Phase 5.5; no sub-group action required.
