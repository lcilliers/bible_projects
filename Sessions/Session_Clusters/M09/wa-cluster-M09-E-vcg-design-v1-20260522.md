## VCG design

### M09-E-VCG-01 — The crushed and broken spirit: contrition before God

**Context description:** This VCG carries the full contrition register — the acute, grief-laden inner brokenness of spirit that follows genuine confrontation with one's failure or with the holiness of God. Both verses evidence the same phenomenon: the contrite spirit is crushed inwardly and broken in heart (Psa34:18), and contrition is paired with lowliness and located in the spirit (Isa57:15). In both instances God's direct response is the immediate correlate — his nearness and saving action to the broken-hearted (Psa34:18), his dwelling with and reviving of the contrite and lowly spirit (Isa57:15). The pairing of contrition with lowliness in Isa57:15 places this VCG in the closest proximity to M09-A (willed self-lowering), showing contrition as the acute, crisis-precipitated form of the humility characteristic — emotionally loaded, spirit-located, and specifically responsive to the weight of moral failure rather than a stable dispositional posture.
