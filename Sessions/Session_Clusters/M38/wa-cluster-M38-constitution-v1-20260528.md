## §1. Cluster characteristic statement (the debate target)

M38's term inventory spans three related semantic registers — salvation/rescue/deliverance, propitiation/atonement, and giving/gift in salvation-relevant contexts. The cluster's working characteristic statement (post-Phase A) is:

> The inner-being state of being saved, ransomed, rescued, atoned-for, or receiving as gift the salvation-relevant content of God's redemptive action. The cluster names what happens within the human person at the receiving end of God's saving work — and the inner-being conditions that surround it (faith exercised, will turning, conscience cleansed, hope sustained, gratitude, soul rejoicing).

Whether the three semantic registers (salvation / atonement / gift) constitute one coherent characteristic or should be split into multiple characteristics or partial-transfers is the Phase B question this constitution debate must address.
