## Per-term verdicts

### G0193 akratēs — intemperate (mti_id=1113) — BOUNDARY

**Analytical question:** Does akratēs belong in M08 as a supportive/qualifying sub-group, or should it transfer to a will/self-control cluster (when built)?

**Valid reason cited:** Supportive/qualifying register (§6.3.1 reason 3).

**Rationale:** 2Ti 3:3 places akratēs (lack of self-control) alongside huperēfanos and self-love in a vice catalogue where M08 characteristics are clearly present. The meaning corpus identifies it as "the will's inability to restrain impulses, contributing to the broader collapse of inner discipline" in the pride context. The term qualifies M08's characteristic — self-control failure is the breakdown of the inner restraint through which pride is held in check — without itself carrying pride as a primary inner-being characteristic. The owning registry is self-control (which belongs to a future cluster, likely in the volitional domain).
