## VCG M08-E-VCG-01 — National Proud Might — Strength and Power as Ground for Arrogant Self-Sufficiency

**Description:** Verses where national military might, structural power, and collective strength becomes the inner ground for arrogant self-sufficiency — pride rooted in the capacity to overpower, defend, and project force. The ga.on proud-might vocabulary dominates: Israel's "pride of power" is the inner confidence that sustains their strength, which God threatens to break (Lev 26:19); Egypt's proud might is its inner power and national confidence declared subject to divine humiliation (Eze 30:6; 30:18; 32:12); Israel's proud might becomes total desolation when spent (Eze 33:28); pride belongs to the powerful in Israel — the inner strength and confidence of the strong targeted by God (Eze 7:24); Sodom's pride as the first and root sin paired with material excess (Eze 16:49); Israel's emotional and spiritual pride of power in the sanctuary (Eze 24:21). **Cross-register flag: M23** — the proud-might content is the pride-as-misuse-of-genuine-strength dimension that Phase 3 flagged.

**Anchor:** vc_id=33996 — Eze 30:6 — H1347 ga.on — "Egypt's proud might is its inner power and national confidence; God declares it will come down, showing that national pride rooted in military and political strength is subject to divine humiliation." The anchor is the most direct single-verse portrait of M08-E's central dynamic: strength → inner proud confidence → divine humiliation.

## VCG M08-E-VCG-02 — Wealth, Position, and Self-Secured Elevation — Material Pride

**Description:** Verses where material prosperity, physical fortification, or wealth-secured position becomes the ground for arrogant inner self-sufficiency. Includes: wealth gained through wisdom causing the heart to become proud (Eze 28:5 ga.vah); Babylon's love of pleasures paired with declarations of self-sufficiency feeding a prideful heart (Isa 47:8 a.din — voluptuous ease); haughtiness tied to material prosperity, placing trust and identity in riches (1Ti 6:17 hupsēlofroneō); the proud heart mistaking physical high ground for security (Jer 49:16 ma.rom); Babylon's fortified stronghold as symbol of self-secured elevation (Jer 51:53 ma.rom); lofty dwelling reinforcing the proud heart's delusion of security (Obd 3 ma.rom); and self-protective nest-setting on high born of greed (Hab 2:9 ma.rom). Also the imperious self-will of the heart so corrupted it acts without shame or restraint (Eze 16:30 shal.le.tet — the pathological form of M08-E's interior: unchecked willful self-assertion without any remaining moral constraint). **Cross-register flag: M23** for the structural-power dimension.

**Anchor:** vc_id=9563 — Isa 47:8 — H5719 a.din — "Babylon's love of pleasures is paired with inner declarations of self-sufficiency ('I am, and there is no one besides me'); the voluptuous ease feeds a prideful heart that imagines itself invulnerable to loss." The anchor captures the M08-E dynamic in its sharpest form: material ease feeding the proud heart's claim to sole existence.
