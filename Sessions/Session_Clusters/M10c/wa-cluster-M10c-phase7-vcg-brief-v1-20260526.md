### Within-sub-group polysemy already resolved

Unlike M10b, M10c **has no MANDATORY polysemy splits parked for Phase 7**. Phase 5 sub-group formation already separated the major axes:

- The unclean-spirit register (akathartos ~21V) is its own sub-group **M10c-E** — already separated.
- The moral-inner NT register is its own sub-group **M10c-C** — already separated.
- The corporate/covenantal register is its own sub-group **M10c-D** — already separated.
- Ritual contact vs ritual category were split (§8.6 gate) into **M10c-A** vs **M10c-B**.

**However**, attend to the following within-sub-group sub-registers when designing VCGs (these are NOT mandatory splits — they are signals to test against the meaning evidence):

1. **M10c-A — death-contact sub-register**. A substantial sub-set involves corpse/carcass contact (Lev 11:24–40 carcasses; Num 19 corpse defilement; Lev 21–22 priestly corpse-restrictions; Lev 22:4-8 corpse-and-discharge). Consider a death-contact-specific VCG distinct from bodily-discharge VCGs (menstrual nid.dah, seminal emissions, leprous discharges).
2. **M10c-A — voluntary self-defilement sub-register**. Lev 11:43-44, Lev 18:24-30, Lev 20:25 — willful contact framed as moral choice ("ye shall not defile yourselves"). Consider a distinct VCG within M10c-A for verses that frame the bodily-contact defilement as volitional inner choice rather than mechanical contagion.
3. **M10c-D — sanctuary vs land vs name vs covenant-relational**. The corporate register branches: sanctuary defilement (Eze 5:11, 8:6, 23:38), land defilement (Lev 18:24-28, Num 35:34, Jer 2:7, 16:18, Eze 36:17), name defilement (Eze 20:39, 43:7-8), covenant-relational defilement (Num 5 ordeal-of-jealousy, sexual-betrayal defilement). The verse mass may force 3–5 VCGs along these axes.
4. **M10c-C — defiled conscience vs habitual moral corruption vs body-and-spirit unity vs idolatrous-impurity-of-heart**. The Greek register clusters around several inner-being phenomena: conscience defiled (1Cor 8:7), prior moral-life slavery (Rom 6:19), body-and-spirit unity (2Cor 7:1), idolater = heart impurity (Eph 5:5), inner moral corruption causing outer abomination (Tit 1:15-16, 2Pe 2:10-20).
5. **M10c-E — unclean-spirit register**. Recognition-and-expulsion narratives (Mar 1:23-27, 5:1-15, Luk 4:33-36, Luk 8:29) vs commissioning-authority verses (Mat 10:1, Mar 6:7, Luk 9:1) vs general-affliction-context verses. Consider whether the recognition-and-expulsion narratives form one VCG and the authority-over-unclean-spirits verses form another.

## Process per sub-group (§10.2 + §10.7 staged write-out)

1. **Read** every verse-meaning + keyword set in the sub-group's section of the meanings report. **Every row. No skipping. No sampling.**
2. **Cluster meanings into provisional VCGs.** Use the per-sub-group keyword breakdowns in the analytics report (§6) as a starting point — high-frequency keyword patterns within a sub-group are candidates for VCG groupings. Verify against the meanings.
3. **Name each VCG** with a provisional code (`M10c-{X}-VCG-{seq}`, e.g. `M10c-A-VCG-01`) and a one-paragraph `context_description`.
4. **Designate ONE anchor verse per VCG** — most directly and definitionally evidences the phenomenon. Anchor's vc_id must be in the VCG's member list.
5. **Note dual-membership verses** explicitly (rare; usually within the same sub-group).
6. **Write the per-sub-group design document to disk immediately:**
   `Sessions/Session_Clusters/M10c/wa-cluster-M10c-{subgroup_code}-vcg-design-v1-20260526.md`
   (e.g. `wa-cluster-M10c-M10c-A-vcg-design-v1-20260526.md`)
7. **Verify sum.** Sub-group's VCG member sum must equal the sub-group's verse count from the meanings report. Record a verification line at the end of the per-sub-group document:
   ```
   **Verification**: VCG member sums = N1 + N2 + ... + Nk = TOTAL, matches M10c-{X} input count of TOTAL ✓
   ```
8. Move to next sub-group; repeat.

After all 5 sub-groups are processed, produce:

## Specific signals to attend to per sub-group

### M10c-A — Bodily-contact defilement-state (93V)

Largest sub-group. Primary corpus: ta.me verb (50V), ta.me adj (28V), nid.dah (15V).

Candidate VCG axes:
- **Corpse-contact** (Num 5:2, 6:7, 6:9, 9:6-13, 19:11-22 thorough death-defilement procedure; Lev 21:1-11 priestly corpse-restrictions; Lev 22:4 priestly corpse-discharge; Hag 2:13) — distinctive in being seven-day defilement and requiring red-heifer ash
- **Carcass-of-animal contact** (Lev 11:24-43 — touch, carry, eat carcass) — overlap with M10c-B classification, but here the action of contact + defilement-incurred is the focus rather than the categorical classification
- **Bodily discharge — menstrual** (nid.dah Lev 12:2, 15:19-33; Eze 18:6, 22:10, 36:17 menstrual-impurity-as-image — though Eze 36:17 is corporate-prophetic, watch for routing) — nid.dah heavy concentration
- **Bodily discharge — seminal / leprous / abnormal** (Lev 15:1-32 zav.b genital discharge; Lev 22:4 priestly seminal/discharge restrictions; Deu 23:10)
- **Objects-defiled-by-contact** (Lev 11:32-40 vessels, food, water, oven; Lev 15:4-12 bed, seat, saddle, vessel, pottery) — the defilement-transfer mechanism via material
- **Voluntary self-defilement** (Lev 11:43-44, Lev 18:24-30, Lev 20:25, Eze 22:3-4 — willful contact framed as moral choice)
- **Cleansing-procedure** verses where bodily-contact defilement is the framing for the cleansing instruction (Lev 14:36, 14:46, 16:19; Num 19:7-22; Eze 44:25-26)

Expected: 5–8 VCGs.

### M10c-B — Categorical/classificatory unclean-state (40V)

Smaller, focused. Primary corpus: ta.me adj (21V), ta.me verb (15V), akathartos (4V).

Candidate VCG axes:
- **Leprous-skin priestly verdict** (Lev 13 corpus — the priest sees, declares, isolates; the inner-being content is conscience-formed-by-purity-categories submitting to authoritative diagnosis. Includes Lev 13:11, 13:14-15, 13:22, 13:25, 13:30, 13:36, 13:44-46) — heavy verse mass
- **Categorically-unclean-animal classification** (Lev 11 verses where the category-determination is the focus rather than the contact-act: Lev 5:2, 7:21, 11:8, 20:25, 27:11, 27:27; Deu 14:7-19) — overlap with M10c-A; route based on whether the verse frames the unclean-thing as a category to be discerned, or the act of touching
- **Categorically-unclean-objects / persons** (the unclean as a publicly-marked state: Isa 35:8, Isa 52:1, Isa 52:11, Lam 4:15, Eze 22:26, Eze 44:23, Hag 2:14) — the social-discernment register
- **Public-stigma / isolation** (Lev 13:45-46 "unclean! unclean!" cry, dwell alone; Lam 4:15 "depart! unclean!" — corporate exilic version)
- **NT akathartos categorical use** (Act 10:14, 10:28; Rom 14:14; 1Cor 7:14; 2Cor 6:17; Heb 9:13) — discernment register applied to the new-covenant question of food/persons/marriage/inheritance

Expected: 3–5 VCGs.

### M10c-C — Moral-inner defilement-state (26V)

Greek register primarily (akatharsia 10V, akathartos 5V, molunō 3V, molusmos 1V, miasmos 1V) + Hebrew metaphorical (ta.me adj 6V: Job 14:4, Isa 6:5 twice, Isa 64:6, Pro 30:12, Hag 2:14 inner aspect — read each meaning carefully to confirm sub-group routing).

Candidate VCG axes:
- **Defiled-conscience-as-faculty** (1Cor 8:7 conscience-weak-defiled; Tit 1:15 "their mind and conscience is defiled") — the conscience-itself-as-the-locus
- **Prior moral-life slavery / habitual corruption** (Rom 1:24, Rom 6:19, Eph 4:19, Col 3:5, Gal 5:19, 1Th 4:7 — impurity as the dominion-of-flesh inner condition the believer is called out of)
- **Body-and-spirit unity purification call** (2Cor 7:1 "cleansing ourselves from all filthiness of the flesh and spirit")
- **Idolater = heart-impurity** (Eph 5:5 "no covetous man = idolater hath any inheritance") — heart-allegiance-disorder
- **Inner moral corruption causing outer abomination** (Tit 1:15-16, 2Pe 2:10-20)
- **Hebrew metaphorical inner-defilement** (Job 14:4 "who can bring a clean thing out of an unclean?"; Isa 6:5 "I am a man of unclean lips"; Isa 64:6 "all our righteousnesses are as filthy rags"; Pro 30:12 "generation pure in own eyes, yet not washed from filthiness"; possibly Hag 2:14 if routed here rather than D)

Expected: 3–5 VCGs.

### M10c-D — Corporate/covenantal defilement (83V)

Heavy concentration. Primary corpus: ta.me verb (63V — Eze-heavy + Num/Jer/Hos), ta.me adj (11V), nid.dah (9V — corporate-prophetic uses).

Candidate VCG axes:
- **Sanctuary-defilement** (Eze 5:11, 8:6-17, 23:38-39, 44:7; Lev 15:31; Num 19:13, 19:20 — defilement reaching the holy place)
- **Land-defilement** (Lev 18:24-28, Num 35:33-34, Deu 21:23, Jer 2:7, 16:18, Eze 36:17-18, Hos 5:3, 6:10) — land-as-victim-of-corporate-moral-defilement
- **Divine-name defilement** (Eze 20:39, 43:7-8) — name as object of defilement through corporate idolatry
- **Covenant-relational / sexual-betrayal defilement** (Num 5:13-29 ordeal of jealousy; Gen 34:5/13/27 Dinah; Eze 18:6, 18:11, 18:15, 22:11, 23:7/13/17/27, 33:26) — sexual betrayal as defilement of covenant
- **Nazirite / vow defilement** (Num 6:7, 6:9, 6:12 + Lev 21:1-4 priestly analogue if not in A)
- **Camp / sacred-space defilement** (Num 5:3, Num 19:13, 19:20; Lev 18:24)
- **Corporate-prophetic cleansing-promise** (Eze 36:25, 36:29 cleanse-from-uncleannesses; Eze 36:33; Zec 13:1 fountain opened; Mal — watch for routing)
- **Idol-defilement (Ezekiel idolatry corpus)** (Eze 14:11, 20:7/18/30/31/43, 22:3-4, 23:30, 36:18, 37:23, 43:8 — defilement-through-idolatry) — M10b cross-register concentration here
- **Hosea/Jeremiah harlotry-as-defilement** (Hos 5:3, 6:10; Jer 2:23, 3:1-2, 13:27) — corporate-prophetic harlotry-defilement

Expected: 4–7 VCGs (the Ezekiel-idolatry corpus may force its own VCG by sheer mass).

### M10c-E — Defilement by external spiritual agency (21V)

Smallest sub-group. Exclusively akathartos in NT unclean-spirit register.

Candidate VCG axes:
- **Recognition-and-expulsion narratives** (Mar 1:23-27, 1:34, 3:11, 5:1-15, 7:25-30, 9:25; Luk 4:33-36, 6:18, 8:29, 9:42; Act 5:16, 8:7 — the unclean spirit recognises divine authority; expulsion restores wholeness)
- **Authority-over-unclean-spirits commissioning** (Mat 10:1, Mar 6:7, Luk 9:1 — Jesus commissioning the Twelve with authority over unclean spirits)
- **General-affliction-context / Revelation cosmic** (Rev 16:13, Rev 18:2 — unclean spirits as cosmic-evil agents in eschatological context)

Expected: 1–3 VCGs. The recognition-and-expulsion register is dominant.
