## §2 — Sub-groups (capacity organisers — not the analytical unit)

### M10-I — Injustice as moral failure of right conduct

**core_description:** CHAR-9 (Injustice). The inner state/disposition of doing what is wrong toward persons — unjust conduct, unjust character, the moral faculty bent toward wrongdoing against others and against right order. Injustice devised in the heart before enacted by hands (Psa 58:2), as fixed character trait expressed across all scales (Luk 16:10), as the inner condition the person cannot escape God's moral accounting (Act 24:15; 2Pe 2:9), and as the opposite of righteousness (Deu 32:4; Rom 3:5). M26 cross-register flags travel with all terms; their M10 representation is the sinful-conduct-against-right-order dimension.

**Role under Injustice as moral failure of right conduct:** Single-sub-group representation (sub-group = characteristic 1:1).

**Verses in this sub-group:** 72

**VCGs within this sub-group:**

- `M10-I-VCG-01` (20 verses) — Judicial and commercial injustice: the corruption of right process (M26)
- `M10-I-VCG-02` (33 verses) — Injustice as oppression and harm to persons (M26)
- `M10-I-VCG-03` (19 verses) — Character of the unjust person: adikos, av.val, a.vil, adikēma (M26)
