## §1 — Characteristic 11 (Sin as universal condition)

**Short name:** Sin as universal condition
**Characteristic_id:** 35
**Definition:**

> Sin as a structural condition of the inner person — not primarily an act but a state under which every person stands. Universal human condition, fall-short-of-glory register.

**Constituent sub-groups:** 1
**Total verses in scope:** ~112
