## §1 — Characteristic 1 (Wilful sinning)

**Short name:** Wilful sinning
**Characteristic_id:** 25
**Definition:**

> The will committing a known wrong — sinning as a deliberate relational act directed against God, another person, or the self, where the person knows what they are doing and does it.

**Constituent sub-groups:** 1
**Total verses in scope:** ~98
