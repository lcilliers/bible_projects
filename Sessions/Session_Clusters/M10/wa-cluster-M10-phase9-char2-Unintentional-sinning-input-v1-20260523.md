## §1 — Characteristic 2 (Unintentional sinning)

**Short name:** Unintentional sinning
**Characteristic_id:** 26
**Definition:**

> Sin committed without deliberate intent — real and morally consequential even though the inner person did not consciously defy God's command; conscience engages once the act surfaces to awareness.

**Constituent sub-groups:** 1
**Total verses in scope:** ~18
