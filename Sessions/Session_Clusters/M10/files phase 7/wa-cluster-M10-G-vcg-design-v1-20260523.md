## VCGs

### M10-G-VCG-01 — Unfaithfulness toward God: covenant-betrayal directed upward (58 verses)

**context_description:** The ma.al corpus where unfaithfulness is directed against God — Israel breaking covenantal loyalty with the Lord, sacrilege against holy things, trespass into priestly domain, and the inner disposition of disloyalty toward God expressed through specific acts. The inner-being characteristic is faithlessness as the will's deliberate breach of the vertical covenant bond: "acting faithlessly implies a deliberate breach of covenant loyalty, an inner turning away" (Eze 14:13). Covers Levitical trespass against holy things, Achan's violation, Saul's disobedience, Uzziah's pride-driven trespass, and the Ezra-Nehemiah faithlessness corpus. M13/M31 cross-register throughout. Keyword axis: `breach faith` (7), `covenant violated` (5), `loyalty broken` (5).

### M10-G-VCG-02 — Relational treachery: faithlessness between persons (27 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where unfaithfulness is directed toward persons in intimate or communal bonds — marital betrayal (Num 5:12/27; Mal 2:14/15/16), brothers acting treacherously (Job 6:15; Mal 2:10), the unreliable friend (Pro 25:19), and the treacherous neighbour. The inner-being characteristic is faithlessness as relational breach between persons: the will betraying a human bond it was entrusted to keep. Cross-register M13/M31. ### M10-G-VCG-03 — Treachery as constitutional inner character (16 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where treachery is a settled, defining inner disposition — the character that repeatedly produces betrayal. The inner-being characteristic is faithlessness as an entrenched will-orientation: threefold intensified treachery (Isa 24:16), constitutional unfaithfulness known before birth (Isa 48:8), disposition deepening under pressure (2Ch 28:22), and community pervaded by treachery (Jer 9:2).
