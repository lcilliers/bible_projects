## VCGs

### M10-J-VCG-01 — Sin directed against God: the vertical relational offense (32 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where sinning is explicitly directed against God — the will defying the divine, disobeying the covenant, acting faithlessly against the Lord. Joseph names resisting temptation as "sinning against God" (Gen 39:9). Ezekiel's soul-accountability declarations (Eze 18:4/20). Jeremiah's succession of covenantal disobedience passages. The characteristic is sin as a vertical relational offense: the will's act against God as its primary object. Keyword axis: `defection wilful` (4), `sin committed` (6).

**Anchor:** vc_id=18923 (Gen 39:9)

### M10-J-VCG-02 — Sin against the neighbour: the horizontal relational offense (18 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where sinning is directed against a specific other person. Jonathan frames sinning as acting unjustly against an innocent person (1Sa 19:4/5). NT interpersonal hamartanō: sinning against a brother (Mat 18:15/21; Luk 17:3/4), sinning against a brother's conscience reaching Christ (1Cor 8:12), sin against a neighbour resolved at the altar (1Ki 8:31), false accusation as verbal sin against another (Isa 29:21), despising the neighbour as sinful act (Pro 14:21). Keyword axis: `sin relational` (6), `harm transferred` (2).

### M10-J-VCG-03 — Sin deterred, restrained, or denied (19 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where the inner-being focus is on preventing sinning, restraining the will, or protesting innocence. God's terrifying presence as inner deterrent (Exo 20:20). Job restrained from sinning with his lips under suffering (Job 2:10; 31:30). Guarding the mouth (Psa 39:1). Conscience pondering to avoid anger becoming sin (Psa 4:4). Sin as a trap set by enemies (Neh 6:13). Haste without knowledge as missing the way (Pro 19:2). The mouth leading the self into sin through rash speech (Ecc 5:6). Keyword axis: `will restrained` (2), `sin prevented` (2).

**Anchor:** vc_id=19073 (Exo 20:20)

### M10-J-VCG-04 — Sin requiring ritual remedy: the cultic-atonement register (17 verses)

**context_description:** Verses from cha.ta within M10-J where the sin-act creates a cultic obligation — the Levitical sin-offering system as the prescribed remedy for committed sin. Lev 4 (sin-offering for leaders/community: 19080/53/81/82/83/85), Lev 5 (compensation offerings and restitution: 19092/93/87/88/89/90/91/55/96/94), and Eze 43:23 altar purification. ### M10-J-VCG-05 — NT wilful sinning: hamartanō and paraptōma in M10-J (12 verses)

**context_description:** NT verses carrying the wilful-sinning register. Sexual sin uniquely against one's own body (1Cor 6:18). Sinning as a real possibility to be guarded against (1Jo 2:1). Denying sinfulness as lying to God (1Jo 1:10). Jesus commands "sin no more" (Joh 5:14; 8:11). Joh 9:2/3: sin-causation question challenged. Paul denies any offense (Act 25:8). Sinning punished earns no credit (1Pe 2:20). Angels' sin forfeiting standing (2Pe 2:4). Trespass caught-in requiring gentle restoration (Gal 6:1). Keyword axis: `sin consequential` (2), `will defiant` (2).

**Anchor:** vc_id=37544 (Joh 8:11)
