## VCGs

### M10-P-VCG-01 — Contagious sin: leadership transmitting moral defection (19 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where sin is a moral course set by a leader whose inner commitment to wrongdoing is transmitted to those under authority — the will of followers bent by the corrupting influence of those above them. Idolatrous influence through foreign nations (Exo 23:33; Deu 20:18). Jeroboam's active drawing of the whole nation into sin (1Ki 14:16). Each subsequent king walked in Jeroboam's sin and spread it to Israel (1Ki 14:22; 15:26/30/34; 16:2/13/19/26). Ahab charged with making Israel sin through misused authority (1Ki 21:22); Ahaziah followed the sin-path of father/mother/Jeroboam (1Ki 22:52). Jeroboam drove Israel from following the Lord (2Ki 17:21). Manasseh's idolatry becoming a corrupting force on all Judah (2Ki 21:11/16/17); Josiah's destruction of the Bethel monument to that sin (2Ki 23:15). Idolatrous leadership causing Israel into child sacrifice (Jer 32:35). Keyword axis: `leadership corrupting` (8), `sin transmitted` (3), `sin contagious` (2), `sin propagated` (2), `sin induced` (2).

**Anchor:** vc_id=18993 (1Ki 14:16 — "Jeroboam's sinning was not merely personal; his will actively drew the whole nation into sin")
