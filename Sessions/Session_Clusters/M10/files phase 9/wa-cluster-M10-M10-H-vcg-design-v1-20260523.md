### M10-H-VCG-01 — Perversity of speech: the tongue as vehicle of moral inversion (10 verses)

**context_description:** Verses where perversity expresses itself through verbal output — words bent away from truth, twisted speech, the mouth producing what is morally inverted. ### M10-H-VCG-02 — Perversion of judgment: bribery corrupting discernment (6 verses)

**context_description:** The sa.laph corpus — verses where perversion specifically operates on the inner faculty of discernment and judicial perception. Bribery is the instrument that corrupts the judge's ability to see clearly, twisting the inner cognitive faculty so that justice is bent (Exo 23:8; Deu 16:19). Also: sin perverting or ruining those who follow it (Pro 13:6), folly ruining one's own path (Pro 19:3), God overturning perverted speech (Pro 22:12), God overturning the wicked (Pro 21:12). The inner-being characteristic is perversion of the discernment faculty — the mind's ability to judge rightly corrupted by corruption. Keyword axis: `discernment corrupted` (2 co-occ with `judgment subverted`).

**Anchor:** vc_id=32895 (Exo 23:8)
