**Description:**

> Sincerity is the quality of being genuinely what one appears to be — without hidden motives, without the wearing of a face that conceals a different interior. The Greek vocabulary (eilikrineia) covers the testing of something pure in sunlight, the unmixed quality. Sincerity is the inner correspondence between what one says and what one actually means, between the face one presents and the person one actually is. It is closely related to integrity and honesty, but where integrity emphasises consistency over time, sincerity emphasises the unmixed quality of the present moment.
