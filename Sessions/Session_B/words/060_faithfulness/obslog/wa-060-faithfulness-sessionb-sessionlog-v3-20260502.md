### Governing finding
**Registry label / OWNER term structural gap:** The label "faithfulness" names a dispositional quality; the OWNER term na.tan is an action verb. The registry captures *enacted faithfulness* — the giving that constitutes commitment — not the dispositional quality vocabulary (emunah, emet, hesed, pistis) which resides entirely in XREF, owned by R059, R044, and R103. This gap is not an error; it is analytically productive. The registry holds one necessary pole of biblical faithfulness; the XREF registries hold the other.
