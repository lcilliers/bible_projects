**Description:**

> Love is the most foundational and most theologically dense word in the entire corpus. The Hebrew and Greek vocabulary spans a vast range: the steadfast covenant love of God (hesed), the deep personal affection (ahava), the tender compassion (racham), the Greek agape (self-giving, willed, unconditional love), phileo (warm affection), and eros (passion and longing). God is love: not merely loving but constituted by it. Human love is modelled on and enabled by divine love. Everything else in the moral life is an expression of love rightly ordered.
