# Chapter 3 — Verses

**Registry 068 — Grace** · *Annotated Verse Evidence*

---

This chapter walks the 15 anchor verses for this registry in canonical order. Each anchor is presented with its full verse text, group context, dimension assignment, and the Stage 2a analysis (five cross-registry vision questions, inline observations, and any SD pointers raised at the verse) drawn from `verse_context.analysis_note`.


## G5483 (charizō) — group 5470-001

**Group context:** Term names God's gracious giving and forgiving — the divine act of freely granting, releasing, and bestowing on the basis of grace alone, supremely in the gift of the Son and forgiveness of sins


### Rom 8:32

> Rom 8:32 He who did not spare his own Son but gave him up for us all , how will he not also with him graciously give us all things?


## G5483 (charizō) — group 5470-002

**Group context:** Term names the human act of forgiving — releasing another from debt, offence, or resentment, patterned on and grounded in the divine forgiveness received, as an inner-relational act of grace extended to another


### Eph 4:32

> Eph 4:32 Be kind to one another , tenderhearted , forgiving one another , as God in Christ forgave you .


## G5485 (charis) — group 888-001

**Group context:** Term names grace as the sovereign, unmerited divine disposition toward humanity — the freely given, transforming gift through which God justifies, saves, calls, and sustains, supremely displayed in Christ


### 2Cor 12:9

> 2Cor 12:9 But he said to me , “ My grace is sufficient for you , for my power is made perfect in weakness .” Therefore I will boast all the more gladly of my weaknesses , so that the power of Christ may rest upon me .


### Eph 2:8

> Eph 2:8 For by grace you have been saved through faith . And this is not your own doing; it is the gift of God ,


## G5485 (charis) — group 888-002

**Group context:** Term names grace as the relational sphere and power in which the believer stands and lives — the ongoing inner-relational condition of being under grace, accessed by faith, sustained by God, and expressed in the transformed inner being


### Rom 5:2

> Rom 5:2 Through him we have also obtained access by faith into this grace in which we stand , and we rejoice in hope of the glory of God .


### 1Cor 15:10

> 1Cor 15:10 But by the grace of God I am what I am , and his grace toward me was not in vain . On the contrary , I worked harder than any of them , though it was not I , but the grace of God that is with me .

> Exo 33:17 And the Lord said to Moses , “ This very thing that you have spoken I will do , for you have found favor in my sight , and I know you by name .”
