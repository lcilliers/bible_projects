### Key analytical findings (Stage 2b)

1. God's chesed is his defining relational attribute — superlative grammar throughout (abounding, inexhaustible, better than life)
2. Kindness grounds human image-bearing ontologically — to be kind is to function as image-bearer (Gen 1:26 + Mic 6:8 + Eph 4:32)
3. Chesed is loyal-relational-kindness — the faithfulness dimension distinguishes it from mere warmth; it is composite not simple
4. Working definition: stable inner disposition of loyal goodwill toward another, rooted in the heart, constituted by faithfulness + compassion + absence of self-serving motivation
5. Constitutional heart-location is explicitly named: Pro 3:3 — "write them on the tablet of your heart"
6. Constitutional movement pattern: Spirit-formation → heart-inscription → relational disposition → deliberate acts
7. Three modes of operation: dispositional orientation / deliberate act of will / Spirit's interior production
8. Three structural opposites: pride/self-glorification, severity/cruelty, withdrawal from the other
9. Counterfeit explicitly named: chrēstologia (G5542) — pleasant speech covering self-serving motivation
10. Nine of eleven inner faculties engaged; imagination and creativity genuinely absent (expected gap for a relational characteristic)
11. Volition strongly engaged: kindness is chosen, not merely felt — "love kindness," "put on kindness"
12. Taste as primary perceptive mode: "tasted that the Lord is good (chrēstos)" — experienced before understood
13. Divine kindness is the source and ground of human kindness — Eph 4:32 makes the grounding explicit
14. The causal network: divine kindness → praise + repentance → reproduced human kindness → sustained covenant relationships
15. Lam 3:22 is the primary anchor verse — asserts permanent inexhaustible chesed in maximum desolation
