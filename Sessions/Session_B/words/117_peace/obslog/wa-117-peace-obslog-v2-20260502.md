**OBS-117-147:** Group 2508-004 anchor verses — Isa 9:6 ("his name shall be called... **OBS-117-151:** Group 2510-001 anchor verse — 1Ch 12:17 ("If you have come to me in friendship [sha.lom] to help me, my heart will be joined to you"). The conditional structure acknowledges the possibility of betrayal (the contrasting clause: "if to betray me"). The sha.lom-friend is defined by the alignment of their sha.lom-posture with the heart's intention.

**OBS-117-152:** Group 2511-001 anchor verse — 1Sa 25:6 ("And thus you shall greet him: 'Peace be to you, and peace be to your house, and peace be to all that you have'").
