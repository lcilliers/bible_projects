## Term H7965G — sha.lom (peace) — mti_id=2508 — 148 verses

### Classification

**Groups identified (reading complete before classifying):**

Reading all 148 verses, five inner-being groups emerge:

**Group 2508-001:** Shalom as the inner condition of wholeness, welfare, and peace — the positive experiential state of the whole person at rest, at peace, flourishing. Covers verses where sha.lom names the inner-being condition directly (soul bereft of peace; mind in perfect peace; abundant peace for the meek; those who love the law have great peace; peace and uprightness as character).

**Group 2508-002:** Shalom as relational covenant peace — God's covenantal peace with his people (covenant of peace; God speaks peace to his saints; God gives peace; righteousness and peace together; peace as divine gift/ordination; my covenant of peace shall not be removed). Covers the divine-human relational dimension.

**Group 2508-003:** Shalom as the inner disposition toward peace — the orientation of the will and character toward peace or against it (those who plan peace; those who are for peace; those who speak peace with evil in their hearts; the prophets who cry Peace falsely; the way of peace unknown to the wicked; seeking peace and pursuing it; love of truth and peace; man of peace). This is the moral-character and volition dimension.

**Group 2508-004:** Shalom as messianic/eschatological peace — the peace that is inseparable from the person and reign of the coming king (Prince of Peace; he shall be their peace; upon him was the chastisement that brought us peace; no end to his peace; great peace promised to the children of Zion; peace extended like a river; the fruit of righteousness will be peace). This covers the peace that goes beyond human attainment into divine provision.

**Set aside — no_inner_being:** Verses where sha.lom functions primarily as a social farewell formula ("go in peace," "return in peace," "is it peace?"), political peace between nations/parties, or purely external welfare without inner-being engagement through this term.

**Set aside — wrong_face:** One verse where inner-being content is clearly carried by another term in the verse (noted where applicable).
