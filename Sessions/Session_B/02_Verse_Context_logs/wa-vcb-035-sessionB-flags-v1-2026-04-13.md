**Term:** G0236 allassō (to change) | mti_id 7542
**Flag type:** Evidential status guidance
**Raised at:** VCB-035 classification — 2026-04-13

**Observation:**
allassō functions as a property term across two distinct inner-being characteristics in its 4-verse relevant corpus:

- Group 7542-001 (Rom 1:23; Gal 4:20): volitional relational reorientation — allassō names the mechanism of exchange/change through which the inner self re-orients its object of worship or its affective register
- Group 7542-002 (1Cor 15:51–52): eschatological constitutional transformation — allassō names the transformation of the whole person at resurrection

allassō does not name a single inner-being state or characteristic. It names a *mechanism of change* that operates across different characteristics. This is the same pattern as other property terms in the programme (compare sha.ma/listen, o.zen/ear in VCB-031).

**Implication for Session B:**

- The two groups represent genuinely different characteristics and should be treated as distinct analytical threads
- Session B should determine whether allassō's role in 7542-001 (relational reorientation through volitional exchange) is semantically continuous with apokatallassō (7543) and diallassō (7544) — if so, this is an important cross-term pattern within Registry 130
