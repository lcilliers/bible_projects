## Structural Observations

**Emerging pattern across Registry 43:** The desire-will-choose complex in the Greek and Hebrew terms is revealing a consistent multi-directional inner-being structure:
- **Desire-as-inclination** (a.vah, orexis, himeirō, katastrēniaō) — the inner being inclines toward its object
- **Will-as-faculty** (thelō, thelēma) — the inner being directs itself
- **Seeking-as-pursuit** (zēteō) — the inner being actively moves toward its object
- **Choosing-as-deliberation** (ba.char) — the inner being selects between alternatives

Together these four movements describe one continuous inner action: inclination → direction → pursuit → selection. Session B will have rich data to work with.

**Divine inner will as recurring category:** Across G2307, G2309, H0183, H0977, a consistent fourth group emerges — God's own inner willing, desiring, or choosing. The programme is accumulating data on the divine inner life as well as the human. This may warrant a programme flag for Session D: does the characterisation of the human inner being illuminate or contrast with the divine inner being across the same terms?
