## Classification Decisions

### R072 — groaning

Key groupings:
- G4726/G4959: single-verse terms; G4726 — Spirit's inarticulate intercession (Rom 8:26); G4959 — creation's corporate groaning (Rom 8:22).
- H0584: split into (1) groaning under oppression and desolation; (2) groaning as inner posture of the faithful over sin — Eze 9:4 anchor for group 2: mark on foreheads of those who sigh and groan over abominations.
- H0603: single verse (Mal 2:13) — groaning directed toward God while persisting in covenantal unfaithfulness; God's non-response reveals the inner-being diagnostic.
- H1897: split into (1) inner meditation on God's word (Psa 1:2 and Psa 63:6 anchors); (2) muttering, groaning, and scheming in the dark register (Isa 59:11 anchor). Set aside: literal speech, animal sounds, occult muttering, non-human growling.
- H7580: split into (1) divine roaring commanding inner response — fear, trembling, return (Amo 3:8 anchor); (2) inner groaning-roaring of anguish (Psa 38:8 anchor). Set aside: animal roaring, military roaring similes, cosmic/meteorological.
- H7581: single group; Psa 22:1 anchor — the groaning of dereliction.

### R073 — guilt

Primary filtering categories applied:
- **Cultic-procedural sin offering specifications (H2403H):** ~106 of 110 verses set aside. The sin offering ritual in Leviticus/Numbers/Ezekiel constitutes a large procedural block with minimal direct inner-being engagement. Only 4 verses retained (Lev 10:17, 10:19, 2Ch 29:23–24).
- **Cultic purification procedure verbs (H2398):** ~12 verses set aside where the verb functions as purification instruction rather than inner-being sinning act.
- **H0817 (guilt offering):** 32 of 41 verses set aside as cultic-procedural; 9 retained including Isa 53:10 anchor (the Servant's soul as guilt offering).

Key anchors and rationale:
- G0266 group 2 anchor: Rom 7:17 "sin that dwells within me" — sin as inner-being power distinct from the person's will; most radical inner-being formulation of sin's indwelling.
- H2403B anchor: Jer 17:1 "sin of Judah engraved on the tablet of their heart" — the heart as the site of sin's indelible record; most distinctive inner-being formulation.
- H0816 group 2 anchor: Hos 5:15 "until they acknowledge their guilt and seek my face" — guilt-acknowledgement as the inner turning point toward seeking God.
- H5771H anchors: Jer 2:22 (indelible stain of guilt) + Isa 6:7 (seraphic removal of guilt) — the two poles of the guilt dynamic.
- H2398 group 1 anchor: Psa 51:4 "against you, you only, have I sinned" — most radical personal sin-confession.
