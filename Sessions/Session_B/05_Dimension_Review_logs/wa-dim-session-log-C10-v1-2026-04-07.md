## Vocabulary Observations

1. **Emotional/Affective gap** — No existing dimension label captures inner affective states (anguish, dread, hope, longing, delight). Multiple C10 groups assigned Spiritual/God-ward as the best available fit for groups that are fundamentally about inner emotional experience. Programme-level decision needed.

2. **Eschatological/transformational gap** — The putting on of incorruptibility and the transformation of inner conditions at the end are assigned Theological/Divine-Human as the best available fit. Not ideal. Programme-level decision needed.

3. **Character/Disposition underused by automated classifier** — Phase C assigned this dimension to 17 groups, compared with automated assignments of 3. The classifier's keyword triggers appear to route these into Moral/Conscience, Volitional/Will, or Relational/Social inappropriately. Programme may want to review keyword triggers.

4. **Distinction between Moral/Conscience and Character/Disposition clarified** — Moral/Conscience applies to evaluative/judicial moral dimensions (standing, assessment, failure, law). Character/Disposition applies to stable inner qualities (Spirit-produced or otherwise) that endure as the person's character.
