### Vocabulary observations — C03

The 12-category vocabulary performs well across C03 with two tensions worth noting:

1. **Spiritual/God-ward is doing significant work.** The majority of joy, gladness, and comfort groups land here — not because the vocabulary is over-broad, but because the data genuinely shows that these inner states in Scripture are characteristically God-ward. This is an analytical finding, not a classification problem.

2. **Theological/Divine-Human as a distinct dimension is confirmed.** Groups naming God's inner delight, exultation, and pleasure (distinct from human response to God) consistently warrant this label and cannot be collapsed into Spiritual/God-ward without losing the distinction between divine and human inner states.

3. **The teleological gap.** Group 378-002 and 6701-001 point toward a category the existing vocabulary does not name: the inner life as designed toward an end, or joy/wonder as the *intended* inner state before God. This may require a vocabulary extension or a new dimension label. Flagged for Session D.
