## Phase A — Cluster Assignment Review

**Coherence assessment:**

C08 groups the inner-being vocabulary of virtue formation — the positive character qualities through which the inner person is shaped, governed, and expressed. The binding affinity is the *formative* dimension: these are not primarily states that happen to the person (like C06) or drives that the person generates outward (like C07), but qualities of inner character that emerge through formation, discipline, and relationship with God. Humility, patience, endurance, gentleness, self-control, and devotion all name the *shaped* inner person.

**Internal variety:** The cluster contains distinct sub-families:
- **Governance vocabulary** (self-control, patience, endurance): the inner person managing its own states and sustained under pressure
- **Disposition vocabulary** (humility, gentleness, meekness, devotion): the inner person oriented correctly toward God and others
- **Drive vocabulary** (boldness, courage): the inner person acting from strength in the face of opposition
- **Generosity**: the inner person giving outward — the overflow of inner singleness

These sub-families are coherent within a formation-cluster framework. They describe different aspects of the virtuous inner life.

**Registry 33 (courage):** Contains substantial XREF content — cha.zaq (to strengthen) sub-glosses, yad (hand) sub-glosses, and notably the na.vi (prophet) groups. The prophet groups are analytically distant from courage per se but entered the registry through XREF connections. Their dimension assignments will reflect their actual content (prophetic vocation, false prophecy, inner resistance to God's word) rather than the courage register.

**Registry 48 (diligence):** Zero groups. Verse Context Complete.
**Registry 109 (meekness):** Zero groups. Verse Context Complete.

**Boundary observations:**
- Some humility terms (a.nav, tapeinos, tapeinofrosunē) overlap with gentleness and meekness vocabulary. This is expected — the semantic families share inner-being territory. No reassignment needed; the programme registers them separately.
- Reg 65 (generosity) contains the agathos (good) vocabulary (881-001 to 881-005) — broad goodness vocabulary entering through generosity's XREF connections. Groups cover the full range of agathos: divine goodness, human goodness, commanded goodness, eschatological goodness, and the inner conflict over doing good. This breadth is expected and analytically rich.

**Recommendation:** Confirm C08 as-is.
