### Coherence assessment

C12 is a **moral defilement and inner-corruption** cluster — the domain of desires, impulses, and inner orientations that corrupt the person's covenantal relationship with God and their relational integrity with others. The registries hold together under the theme of inner defilement from without and within:

- **Abomination, defilement, impurity, whoredom** — name the conditions of moral-ritual defilement and the inner orientations that produce them
- **Debauchery, lust, sensuality** — name the inner orientation of disordered desire and self-indulgence
- **Temptation** — names the dynamic of inner vulnerability to solicitation
- **Slander** — names the inner orientation of contempt expressed as destructive speech
- **Malice** — names the deep inner disposition of enmity

The cluster has genuine inner-being affinity — all registries address aspects of the inner person as defiled, disordered, or threatening moral-covenantal integrity. There is a structural parallel with C10 (moral character) and C11 (moral failure): C12 maps the domain of *defilement and disordered inner desire* — the corrupting of the inner person from the side of unclean desire and impulse.

**Structural note on debauchery (Reg 39):** Six of eight debauchery groups are salvation vocabulary (*sōzō*, *sōtēr*, *sōtērion*) — structurally unexpected in this registry but already flagged in pre-existing Session B finding DIM-039-001. This registry is assessed in Phase B with this in mind.
