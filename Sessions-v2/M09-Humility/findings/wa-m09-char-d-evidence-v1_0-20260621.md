## Definition
**The inner submission of the will expressed as heeding/compliance with an authority** — the will that "hears under" (hupó-akouō). It runs in two directions: **God-ward** (the gospel, the faith, Christ) and **human-authority-ward** (parents, masters, husband). The largest band in the cluster.

## Inner-being signature
- **Kind:** action (*hupakouō*) and status (*hupakoē*); **faculty = perception** uniformly — obedience is rooted in *hearing/heeding*, then enacted.
- **Seat:** the **heart** is named as the source (Romans 6:17 "obedient from the heart"; Ephesians 6:5; Colossians 3:22 "sincerity of heart"); also **soul** (1 Peter 1:22 "purified your souls by your obedience").
- **Valence:** **righteous → commanded**; once **forbidden** (Romans 6:12 "obey its passions" — obedience mis-aimed at sin).
- **Object split:** abstract/God-ward (gospel, faith, truth, Christ) vs person/human-authority (parents, masters, Abraham).

## Sub-patterns
1. **Obedience of faith (God-ward, salvific)** — Romans 1:5; 16:26; 10:16; 2 Thessalonians 1:8; Hebrews 5:9; 1 Peter 1:2, 22; Acts 6:7 "obedient to the faith".
2. **From-the-heart obedience (interiorised)** — Romans 6:17; Ephesians 6:5–6; Colossians 3:22 — not eye-service but heart-sincerity.
3. **Household/authority obedience** — Ephesians 6:1; Colossians 3:20 (children); Ephesians 6:5; Colossians 3:22 (servants); 1 Peter 3:6 (Sarah); Genesis 49:10; Proverbs 30:17 (yiqqehah).
4. **The Christological learning of obedience** — Hebrews 5:8 "he learned obedience"; Philippians 2:8 "obedient to death" (the D↔A hinge).
5. **Mis-aimed obedience (forbidden)** — Romans 6:12, 16 — one may "obey" sin; obedience's moral worth depends on its object.

## Cross-links
- **A Self-humbling:** Philippians 2:8 fuses them.
- **E Submission:** adjacent — obedience is the *active compliance*, submission the *ordering-under* (decide merge at interpret).
- **M01 (fear):** obedience repeatedly paired with "fear and trembling" (Ephesians 6:5; Philippians 2:12; 2 Corinthians 7:15) — fobos/tromos co-terms.
- **M31 (faith):** "obedience of faith" binds D to faith; M36 (service): doulos/slave frame.

## Interpret-phase flags
- **Fork D+E (open):** obedience vs submission — keep distinct or merge.
- **God-ward vs human-ward axis:** hold as an internal axis rather than splitting D in two.
- **Romans 6:12/16 (obey sin):** the forbidden pole — confirm it stays within D as the mis-aimed case.
