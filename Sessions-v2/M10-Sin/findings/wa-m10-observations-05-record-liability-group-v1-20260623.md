## Observations (each grounded)

**O1 — A record/liability is the FORENSIC WEIGHT the inner being incurs and bears before God — a judicial/accounting object, distinct from the act and the condition.**
- *Evidence:* #13 "objective moral weight existing before God — **recorded, accumulated, named**"; sin "**written with a pen of iron… engraved**" (Jer 17:1), "her sins **double from the Lord's hand**" (Isa 40:2 — the reckoned account), "**not count** his sin" (Rom 4:8). #17 "the conscience's recognition of **moral liability before God**". The governing metaphors are **debt / account / record / measure** — judicial, not descriptive of an act or a mode.
- *So:* the object describes the inner being by the **answerable weight it carries** — an accountancy/forensic object.

**O2 — It has an OBJECTIVE pole (the record before God) and a SUBJECTIVE pole (felt guilt/conscience).**
- *Evidence:* OBJECTIVE — #13: recorded/written/counted **even if hidden from men** (Jer 17:1; Isa 40:2; Psa 130:3; Num 5:15 "iniquity remembered"). SUBJECTIVE — #17: "the **subjective inner experience of being guilty**"; the conscience operation "**realizes his guilt**… and **confesses**" (Lev 4–6); guilt borne with a "false **heart**" (Hos 10:2); "no **pang of conscience**" (pu.qah, 1 Sa 25:31).
- *So:* the same forensic weight registers **twice** — in God's record (objective) and in the conscience (subjective). Two poles of one liability.

**O3 — It ACCUMULATES / is MEASURED — a quantitative, temporal weight (unlike a mode or a type).**
- *Evidence:* #18 — "the iniquity of the Amorites is **not yet complete**" (Gen 15:16); "gone over my head; like a **heavy burden**" (Psa 38:4); "risen higher than our heads… **mounted up to the heavens**" (Ezr 9:6); "**bound up… kept in store**" (Hos 13:12); "**fill up the measure** of sins" (1 Th 2:16); visited to generations (Exo 34:7).
- *So:* unlike a condition (a mode) or identity (a type), the record/liability has **magnitude** and **accrues over time and lineage** — a quantitative object.

**O4 — a.von is itself a LIABILITY-PROCESS: crime → guilt → punishment.**
- *Evidence:* the a.von tri-fold (one lemma, sub-entry split): **CRIME / act-classification (H5771G)**; **GUILT / liability (H5771H)** — "On me alone be the **guilt**" (1 Sa 25:24); **PUNISHMENT / penalty-borne (H5771I)** — "My **punishment** is greater than I can bear" (Gen 4:13; Eze 4:4–6 "bear their punishment").
- *So:* the object carries an internal **arc** — the wrong is *classified* (crime), *incurs liability* (guilt), and is *discharged in penalty* (punishment). The "punishment" tail is **external** (retribution/judgement) — see §G.

**O5 — It is DISCHARGED by atonement/forgiveness — the record is precisely what atonement settles.**
- *Evidence:* #13 "**blot out** your transgressions… **will not remember** your sins" (Isa 43:25); "**not count** his sin" (Rom 4:8); a.von "**blotted-out / atoned / cast into the sea**" (Psa 51:9; Mic 7:19; Zec 3:4); the guilt-offering (a.sham) discharges guilt (Isa 53:10); Lev 16:30 atonement "clean… from all your sins".
- *So:* the record/liability is **defined by its discharge** — atonement *blots the record*, the offering *covers the guilt*. The account exists to be **settled** (→ SDX-02 atonement: atonement *is* the settling of this account; → #13/#14 interlock).

**O6 — The group SPANS A GRADIENT: external penalty → objective record → subjective guilt — and the inner end (#17 guilt) borders on a CHARACTERISTIC.**
- *Evidence:* at one end, a.von-**punishment** (H5771I) is an **external** penalty borne (Gen 4:13). In the middle, the **objective record** (#13) is *not felt* — it stands before God whether or not the person feels it. At the inner end, **guilt (#17)** is a **felt inner experience / conscience-operation** — it is "**realized**", drives "**seek my face**" (Hos 5:15), couples with shame (Ezr 9:6); pu.qah names the conscience directly.
- *So:* "record/liability" is **not one uniform object** — it runs from the **external pole** (punishment) through an **objective account** (#13) to a **subjective conscience-state** (#17). #17 guilt, being a felt conscience-operation, is **the most inner-being member and arguably a characteristic** (the conscience registering liability) rather than a record.

## Candidate label (held open)
**RECORD / LIABILITY** — the **forensic weight the inner being incurs and bears before God**: a judicial/accounting object (debt · account · record · measure) with an **objective pole** (God's record, even if hidden from men) and a **subjective pole** (felt guilt/conscience); it **accumulates and is measured** (magnitude, over time/lineage); it follows an **arc** (crime → guilt → punishment); and it is **defined by its discharge** (atonement settles it). It **spans from the external pole (punishment) to a near-characteristic inner end (guilt)**.

## §G — Bias-guard
- **⚠ This group spans a wide gradient and may be SEVERAL kinds, not one.** External **punishment** (a.von H5771I) is **external-pole**, not inner-being (it is a penalty *borne*, not a state) → routes to SDX-08, not necessarily in an inner-being study. **Objective record** (#13) is an account before God. **Subjective guilt** (#17) is a felt conscience-state — arguably a *characteristic*. *Do not assume one object-kind.*
- **⚠ #13 "not hidden from divine memory even if hidden from others"** is the divine-record frame — grounded in the written/counted/remembered texts (Jer 17:1; Isa 40:2; Num 5:15), but the universal "always known to God" is a theological generalisation; the **verses are the warrant**, not a blanket claim.
- **⚠ accumulation/measure (#18, O3) is DATA-SHAPE** — "iniquity full / mounting / bound in store" is a **cross-verse process** (accrual over time/a people), not visible per-occurrence; the texts are real but the *accrual* reading needs a verse-narrative pass. Grounded but partial.
- **⚠ the a.von tri-fold is asserted by the SUB-ENTRY split**, while `lemma_meaning="crime"` is uniform across all three and `type=status` is uniform — so the crime/guilt/punishment distinction lives in **gloss/sense**, not in lemma_meaning or type. Real, but do not over-formalise it as three settled objects.
- **⚠ guilt-as-characteristic (O6)** is a hypothesis — #17 reads as a conscience-*operation* (realize→confess), which looks like a faculty in operation; but it is also clearly a *liability* (the being-answerable-for). Held open whether #17 is a record/liability or a characteristic.
