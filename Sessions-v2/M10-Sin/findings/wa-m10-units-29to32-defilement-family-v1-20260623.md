## A. General observations (whole defilement field)

**Mechanical signature (226 occ):** type action 110 / quality 88 / status 28 · faculty **none 226/226** (term-intrinsic; interior reads from co-terms only) · valence **sinful 96 / neutral 84 / forbidden 31 / none 11 / righteous 4** · object_type none 113 / person 65 / thing 24 / spiritual-being 13. Books: Lev 78 · Eze 24 · Num 23 (ritual/corporate OT) + Mar/Mat/Luk (unclean spirits) + NT epistles (moral).

- **⚑ SYNTH — defilement is largely a STATE, not a moral act.** The large `neutral` block (84) is ritual uncleanness that is *unclean but not sinful* (menstruation, corpse-contact, skin-disease, non-kosher animals). Defilement spans a **non-moral ritual-state ↔ a moral-corruption sense**. This is the prime **"other object-kind"** question for the §10 gate: a *state* (and one often **contracted by contact** or **imposed by an external agent**) is neither a chosen characteristic nor a self-incurred status. **Surface, not resolved.**
- **⚠ HYGIENE — pneuma ≠ inner seat here.** Of 16 pneuma co-seatings, essentially all are the **"unclean spirit" (akathartos pneuma) = the external defiling agent**, NOT the human spirit-seat. Do not count as inner-being seating. The *genuine* inner seats are only ~5 — sarx 3 (Rom 6:19; 2Cor 7:1; Gal 5:19), kardia 1 (Rom 1:24), suneidesis 1 (1Cor 8:7) — all in unit #30.
- **⚠ HYGIENE — ne.phesh idiom** (7 co-seatings) reads as person/life, not soul-seating. **⚠ HYGIENE — ta.me is two lemmas** (H2930A verb "to defile/become unclean" / H2931 adj "unclean") — keep distinct.
- **↔ BOND — M12 (Purity, Holiness, Consecration) is the structural opposite of all four units** (the pole-pair, not a same-valence characteristic-view); also M25 (Life), M28 (Envy/Greed/Lust), M23 (Strength). M12 bond is dominant (#29/#31 = M12 29 each).
- **⚑ SYNTH — atonement seam (exploration Thread 1).** Purification / cleansing / "pronounce clean" is part of the **atonement mechanism** alongside chat.tat sin-offering + kip.pu.rim (see units #14 + Thread 1). The removing-side of defilement belongs with the dealing-with-sin apparatus. **Surface for the atonement synthesis.**
- **⚖ BORDER — dual-view / characteristic-home.** Per the architecture-reflection: most sin-elements have a *characteristic-view* cluster elsewhere; defilement's only sibling is its **opposite (M12)**, not a same-valence cluster — so defilement's characteristic-home appears to be **this unit-set itself**. Flag whether that is consistent with the dual-view model.

### Unit #29 — Ritual defilement-state
- **DEF (DB):** the inner-being condition of ritual uncleanness — bearing on sacred participation, communal life, conscience. Acquired two ways (bodily contact with a defiling agent; authoritative verdict / categorical designation). Inner content: awareness of contamination, watchful restraint, distress at worship-exclusion, conscience formed by purity categories, duty not to transmit. Structural opposite: M12.
- **Terms/salient:** ta.me (H2930A/H2931), nid.dah (H5079). Concentration Lev 11–15 · Num 19. Salient: Lev 11–15 (clean/unclean categories, discharges), Num 19 (corpse), nid.dah Lev 15:19ff; Eze 36:17 (likened to menstrual uncleanness).
- **Signature:** the largest defilement unit; overwhelmingly **neutral** valence (the ritual STATE). object_type thing/person.
- **⚖ BORDER:** #29 (personal ritual state) vs #31 (corporate) share ta.me/niddah — distinct by **scope** (person vs land/sanctuary), kept separate; confirm.
- **⚑ SYNTH:** the "authoritative verdict" mechanism (priest *pronounces* clean/unclean) is a distinct route into the state (vs contact) — worth synthesising as a sub-mechanism.

### Unit #30 — Moral-inner defilement-state
- **DEF (DB):** defilement as the inner condition of moral corruption — conscience, will, heart corrupted by desire, habitual sin, idolatry; locus is the inner person, not external contact. Primary NT register + Hebrew metaphor. Structural opposite: M12.
- **Terms/salient:** akatharsia (G0167), akathartos (G0169 moral), molunō/molusmos/miasmos, ta.me (H2931 metaphor). Salient: Rom 1:24; 6:19; Eph 4:19; 5:3; 2Cor 7:1 ("defilement of flesh and spirit"); 1Cor 8:7 (conscience); 2Pe 2:10; Isa 6:5 ("unclean lips"); Isa 64:6; Job 14:4.
- **Signature:** **the only defilement unit with genuine inner seats** — sarx 3, kardia 1, suneidesis 1.
- **↔ BOND:** **#12 (Sin as enslaving power)** — Rom 6:19 "slaves to impurity"; the dominion-of-flesh link. Also M28 (desire/lust drives it).
- **⚑ SYNTH:** moral defilement as the *inner* counterpart of ritual defilement (#29) — same word-family, opposite locus (inner vs external contact). Surface the inner/outer axis.

### Unit #31 — Corporate/covenantal defilement
- **DEF (DB):** defilement extending from persons/community to land, sanctuary, the divine name through moral defection (idolatry defiling sanctuary/land; sexual betrayal; Nazirite/ordeal). Inner dim: the corporate will in defection; memory of defilement as the mechanism of corporate repentance (Eze 20:43). Structural opposite: M12.
- **Terms/salient:** ta.me (H2930A/H2931), nid.dah. Salient: Eze 36:17–18; Jer 2:7; 2Ki 23; Lev 18:24–28 (land "vomits out"); Num 35:34 (do not defile the land); Hag 2:14.
- **Signature:** the corporate/extension register — defilement spreads from person → land/sanctuary/name.
- **⚑ SYNTH — data-shape:** the corporate/spread dimension is cross-verse (a *process* over a community), not visible per-occurrence — needs a verse-narrative pass to evidence the spread. **Surface.**
- **↔ BOND — abomination bridge:** #31 connects strongly to the abomination units (#25/#26): *abominations defile* the sanctuary/land (the M10b↔M10c bridge, ~23 touches). Cross-reference for synthesis.

### Unit #32 — Defilement by external spiritual agency
- **DEF (DB):** the inner-being condition produced by an **unclean spirit** inhabiting/controlling/corrupting a person from without — will overridden, faculties suppressed (Mar 9:25), bodily agency lost (Luk 8:29), communal access severed; spirits recognise divine authority (Mar 3:11); restoration = wholeness (Luk 6:18). Structural opposite: M12 (wholeness restored).
- **Terms/salient:** akathartos (G0169) = "unclean spirit". Salient: Mar 1:23–27; 5:2–13; 9:25; Luk 8:29; Mat 10:1; Act 5:16; 8:7; Rev 16:13; 18:2. Plus recoded **finding #108** (CLOSED/superseded, session_b) belongs here.
- **⚑ SYNTH — distinct object-kind (§10 test case):** defilement here is **imposed by an external agent**, not contracted (#29) or self-incurred (#30). The pneuma is the **agent**, the person the **site**. This is arguably a different *object-kind* altogether (a possession-state) — a prime case for the §10 "is this a status/characteristic/other?" question. **Surface, not resolved.**
- **⚠ HYGIENE:** this is where the pneuma "co-seated" tally lives — agent, not seat (see §A).
