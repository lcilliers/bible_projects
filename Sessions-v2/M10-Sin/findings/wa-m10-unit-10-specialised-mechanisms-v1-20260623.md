## A. General / non-verse observations (copied from the source digest)

**Definition (DB):** sin operating through a specific inner mechanism — desire exploited by enticement (deleazō; M14 cross), identity falsified through hypocrisy (sunupokrinomai; M08 cross), covenantal identity abandoned through apostasy (apostasia; M31 cross).

**From the R1 digest (§1 table, §2 synergy, §3 hygiene):**

**deleazō (3 occ) — entice/seduce/lure, reaching desire and the soul:**
- Lexicon meaning "entice, seduce, lure" (R1 §1). **CORE ×0** — deleazō does NOT touch the sin-core lemmas; its field is the **inner appetites**: *epithumia* (desire) ×2, *psuchē* (soul), *pleonexia* (greed), *aselgeia* (debauchery) (R1 §1 table).
- **Genuine inner-being seat (R1 §3):** "entice unsteady **souls**" (2 Pe 2:14); "entice by passions of the **flesh**" (2 Pe 2:18) — the verse text supports the soul/flesh seating. deleazō is one of the few Ring-1 dynamics whose seat-claim is borne out by the verse.
- The mechanism is **desire exploited from outside** — the lure works *on* an existing appetite (Jam 1:14 "lured and enticed by his own desire"). This is the M14 (temptation/desire) register.

**apostasia (1 occ) — apostasy / turning away:**
- Lexicon meaning "apostasy / turning away" (R1 §1). **No core touch recorded** (R1 §1 table: "none recorded").
- **⚠ HYGIENE — origin claim (R1 §3 "spurious origin / hold"):** Act 21:21 tags `origin=received-from-outside` ("taught… to forsake Moses"). R1 holds this open — it is *arguably* induced-from-outside (the teaching comes from a reported external source), but the tag is flagged rather than confirmed. (surface; reading question, not resolved.)
- The mechanism is **covenantal/identity abandonment** — forsaking Moses/the law (M31 faithfulness-pole register).

**sunupokrinomai (1 occ) — join in hypocrisy:**
- Lexicon meaning "join in hypocrisy" (R1 §1). Field: *hupokrisis* (hypocrisy) — it is intrinsically paired with the hypocrisy noun.
- The mechanism is **identity falsified through hypocrisy** — acting a part, being "led astray by their hypocrisy" (Gal 2:13). This is the M08 (pride/self-presentation/falsehood) register.

**Structural signature (this unit):** mixed type (deleazō/sunupokrinomai = **action**, apostasia = **status**); faculty largely absent on the lemmas themselves; the inner-being signal reaches the **appetites** (deleazō → desire/soul/greed/flesh) but not the heart-seat; experiencer spans self and other; valence=sinful throughout.

**Flags requiring deeper synthesis (surfaced, NOT resolved):**
- **⚖ BORDER — tiny heterogeneous unit:** 3 lemmas / 5 verses, with no shared substrate beyond "a mechanism of sin." This is not one phenomenon; it is three. Synthesis must decide whether "specialised sinful mechanisms" survives as a unit at all, or dissolves into its home clusters.
- **⚑ SYNTH — home calls (each mechanism likely belongs to its flagged cluster):** deleazō (enticement of desire) → **M14**; sunupokrinomai (hypocrisy) → **M08**; apostasia (apostasy/covenant abandonment) → **M31**. Each is a *better fit* in its flagged cluster than under generic sin. Worth synthesising whether M10 keeps a cross-reference or cedes the term entirely.
- **⚑ SYNTH — the enticement→desire→sin chain:** deleazō + epithumia + peirazō (Jam 1:14) sketches a *process* (tempted → lured/enticed → by one's own desire). Worth synthesising deleazō as the *external trigger* on an *internal appetite* — the M14 desire mechanism.

**↔ BOND (cross-cluster):** **M28** (the appetite field deleazō keeps — epithumia/pleonexia/aselgeia all carry M28), **M14** (enticement/temptation — deleazō home; peirazō at Jam 1:14), **M08** (hypocrisy/self-presentation — sunupokrinomai home), **M31** (faithfulness/covenant-abandonment — apostasia home), **M47** (Constitution — the soul/heart/flesh seats deleazō reaches: psuchē, kardia).
