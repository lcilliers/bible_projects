## A. General / non-verse observations (copied from the source digests)

**Definition (DB):** the wilful defiance of known moral or covenantal order — the will that knowingly crosses a boundary or breaches a covenant. Pe.sha / pa.sha / parabasis-family register.

### A1. From the pešaʿ digest (PE — pe.sha H6588 noun 90 · pa.sha H6586 verb 37 = 127 occ / 125 verses)

**Mechanical signature (PE §1):** type=status **90** / action **37**, tracking part-of-speech cleanly — the **noun pe.sha reads `status`** (the standing wrong), the **verb pa.sha reads `action`** (the act of rebelling). Faculty=**none on all 127** (pešaʿ is term-intrinsically faculty-less — a status/act, not an inner faculty; inner-being engagement reads only from co-terms/co-seated). Valence: sinful 122 · neutral 5. Sense: "transgression(s)" dominates (≈90), then the verb "rebelled/revolted/transgressors," with a legal-register tail ("trust" Exo 22:9, "treason" 1Sa 24:11, "trespass" 1Sa 25:28, "offended" Pro 18:19). object_type: none 55 · abstract 35 · person 15 · God 11 · situation 11. Compound web: partner 258 · qualifier 21 · **co-seated 7** (ne.phesh 4, lev 3). Book spread widest: Isa 18 · Psa 16 · Pro 14 · Eze 12 · Amo 11 · Job 10.

**Faces observed (PE §2 — HELD OPEN, not conclusions):**
- **(a) pe.sha as a standing wrong that is carried, borne, removed** — the noun behaves as an object acted upon: **blotted out** (Isa 43:25; 44:22; Psa 51:1), **forgiven/covered** (Psa 32:1,5; Exo 34:7; Num 14:18; Mic 7:18), **confessed** (Psa 32:5; 51:3), **borne by another** (Isa 53:5 "pierced for our transgressions"; 53:8; 53:12), **turned-from** (Eze 18:28,30,31; 33:12), **remembered/not-remembered** (Isa 43:25; Psa 25:7). The strongest single behaviour of the noun. (Touches remedy/forgiveness + divine-record — recorded, not fused.)
- **(b) pa.sha as rebellion against God** (God-ward verb): "children… have **rebelled against me**" (Isa 1:2); Jer 2:8,29; 3:13; Eze 2:3; 20:38; Hos 7:13; 8:1; Isa 66:24. Relational defiance of a covenant Lord; object_type=God.
- **(c) pa.sha as political revolt** — **→ this is unit #8, not #19.** Vassal nations/kings (Moab, Edom/Libnah, the northern tribes). Several of the 5 neutral-valence occ sit here. Cross-referenced; analysed under #8.
- **(d) Bounded interior seat** — the **7 co-seated occ are the whole inner-being signal**:
  - **heart (lev, 3):** "Transgression speaks **deep in his heart** to the wicked" (Psa 36:1); "bring it to **mind/heart**, you transgressors" (Isa 46:8); "conceiving and uttering from the **heart** lying words" (Isa 59:13).
  - **soul/life (ne.phesh, 4):** "you hunt my **life/soul**… though there is no transgression" (1Sa 24:11, legal-protestation); Psa 59:3; "he poured out his **soul** to death and was numbered with the transgressors" (Isa 53:12); "my firstborn for my transgression, the fruit of my body for the sin of my **soul**" (Mic 6:7).
  - The interior is **bounded, not pervasive** (7 of 127), concentrated in Psalms/Isaiah — consistent with the M10b pattern that *status/verdict* terms carry little intrinsic interior.
- **(e) Measured, counted accumulation** — Amos's "**For three transgressions… and for four**" (Amo 1:3,6,9,11,13; 2:1,4,6) treats pešaʿ as a counted, accruing tally against nations (object_type=situation). (Accumulation/record register — recorded.)
- **(f) Boundary/covenant breach** — lemma_meaning "transgression, rebellion"; the legal register (Exo 22:9 "any **breach of trust**" in a property dispute; 1Sa 25:28 "**trespass** of your servant"; Pro 18:19 a brother "**offended**") shows pešaʿ as a **crossing of a boundary or bond** — civil as well as God-ward. This is the unit's named concept.

### A2. From the R2p digest (Greek transgression-status band, 30 occ)

**The *crossing/breaking* of law/covenant (R2p §1 "Transgression-status, Greek"):**
- **paraptōma** "trespass" is **Pauline and tightly paired with grace and forgiveness** — Rom 5 (Adam's trespass); "dead in **trespasses**" (Eph 2:1,5); "not counting their **trespasses**" (2 Cor 5:19). **The remedy sits right beside the term.**
- **parabasis / parabatēs / parabainō** = law-breaking / transgressor / breaking the command (Mat 15:2–3); the law **"brings transgression"** (Rom 4:15) — transgression is *constituted by* the law it crosses.
- **Minimal inner-being content** — a **juridical/relational status** (Col 2:13 "flesh"; 1 Ti 2:14 deception→transgression). Faculty near-absent across the band (R2p §2 audit: faculty 16/207 overall, none on the Hebrew aspect-terms; the inner-being signal is carried by co-occurrence, not the `faculty` field).

### A3. From the R1 digest (sa.rah defection, H5627 — 8 occ)

- sa.rah lexicon meaning "**turning aside / defection**" (R1 §1 table). **CORE ×1**; field includes *le.vav* (heart) and somatic *choli/davva* (sickness/faint).
- **Genuine inner-being seat (R1 §3):** "the whole **heart** faint" while continuing to revolt (Isa 1:5); "conceiving and uttering from the **heart**" (Isa 59:13). sa.rah's defection is **heart + somatic** — the turning-aside reaches the heart and shows in the body.

### A4. Structural signature (this unit, composite)

type splits cleanly by POS — **status** (the standing transgression: pe.sha noun, paraptōma, parabasis) vs **action** (the act: pa.sha verb, parabainō, sa.rah). Faculty intrinsically **absent** on the boundary-breach terms; inner-being engagement reads only from the **7 co-seated** pešaʿ occ + the sa.rah heart-seatings. experiencer spans God-ward (covenant Lord), person-ward, and civil/legal. The unifying concept is **crossing a known boundary / breaching a bond** — God-ward, civil, and juridical faces of one boundary-breach idea.
