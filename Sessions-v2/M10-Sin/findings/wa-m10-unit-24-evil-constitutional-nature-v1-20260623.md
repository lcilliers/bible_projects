### A1. Structural signature — evil as a moral QUALITY, but interior-heavy (B4 audit §2)
- **type: quality 64 / status 22 / action 2** — the Greek evil family names evil predominantly as a **moral quality/property** ("evil" the adjective), with a status tail (the evil one / the evildoer). This **contrasts with the Hebrew families** (*ra.sha* status-verdict; *a.ven* status/action-process). The *adikia* portion is **status** throughout (8/8, the standing condition of unrighteousness).
- **faculty = moral_evaluation 82** (none 5; *faulos* volition+moral 1).
- **⚑ SYNTH — the Greek evil family is interior-heavy: interior 13 / 88 (~15%)** — heart/mind/soul/conscience seatings — **far above** B1 (*ra.sha*) and B2 (abomination), comparable to B3 (Hebrew evil ~16%). co-seated: *kardia* ×7, *suneidesis* ×2, *dianoia*, *phrēn*, *psuchē*, *pneuma* = 13 (B4 audit §4). This interior-density is what makes #24 the **constitutive-nature** unit. **Surface.**

### A2. The "EVIL ONE" — personified Satan/cosmic adversary — UNIQUE to Greek ⚑ SYNTH
- **⚑ SYNTH — distinct object-kind; no Hebrew analogue (§10 test case).** ~11 *ponēros* occurrences are **"the evil one" by referent** (B4 §2; B4 audit §3; Bsum §4): "deliver us from **the evil one**" (Mat 6:13); "the evil one snatches" (13:19); "**sons of** the evil one" (13:38, object_type=spiritual-being); "keep them from the evil one" (Joh 17:15); "darts of the evil one" (Eph 6:16); "guard against the evil one" (2 Th 3:3); "you have overcome the evil one" (1 Jo 2:13–14); "Cain was **of the evil one**" (3:12); "the evil one does not touch him" (5:18); "the whole world lies in the power of the evil one" (5:19). Plus the cosmic "**spiritual forces of evil**" (Eph 6:12, *ponēria*). **No M10/M10b Hebrew term personifies evil as an adversary.** Evil here is not a status of the inner being but an **external personal agent** — arguably a different *object-kind* altogether. **Surface for §10, not resolved.**

### A3. The heart as the SOURCE-STORE / treasury of evil (the constitutive interior) ⚑ SYNTH
- The defining face of #24 — evil **originates within**: "why do you think evil in your **hearts**?" (Mat 9:4); "out of the abundance of the **heart** the mouth speaks… you are evil" (12:34); "the evil person **out of his evil treasure**… the good person out of his good **treasure of his heart**" (Mat 12:35; Luk 6:45); "out of the **heart** come **evil thoughts**, murder, adultery, sexual immorality, theft, false witness, slander" (Mat 15:19; Mar 7:21–23) — "these evil things come from **within**, and they **defile** a person" (Mar 7:23, *koinoō*, an M10c-type defilement link); "an **evil, unbelieving heart**" (Heb 3:12); "**hostile in mind**, doing evil deeds" (Col 1:21, *dianoia*). **8 heart/mind seatings** — the heart is the **source-store** of *ponēros* (B4 §2). This is the data-level anchor of "the constitutive nature from which corrupt choices flow."

### A4. The conscience thread (suneidesis) ↔ M10 core
- Evil/good is **registered at the conscience**: "hearts sprinkled clean from an **evil conscience**" (Heb 10:22, *suneidesis* + *kardia*); "having a **good conscience**… reviled as evildoers" (1 Pe 3:16, *suneidesis*). A direct link to the M10 core (conscience-suppression units) (B4 audit §3). co-seated *suneidesis* ×2.

### A5. Malice — the vice-disposition to PUT AWAY (kakia) ⚑ SYNTH
- *kakia* is the **inner malice** that the ethics texts command be put off: "put away all **malice**, deceit, hypocrisy, envy, slander" (1 Pe 2:1; Col 3:8; Eph 4:31; Jam 1:21 "put away… from the **soul**", *psuchē*; 1 Cor 14:20 "infants in evil", *phrēn*); "repent of this **wickedness**… the intent of your **heart**" (Act 8:22); "turning you from your **wickedness**" (Act 3:26, *ponēria*). The ethical "put off" of *kakia*, recurrently in **vice-catalogues** (Rom 1:29 lists *ponēria*/*kakia*/*adikia* together). Echoes the Hebrew turn-from/remove theme (B4 audit §3).

### A6. adikia — unrighteousness as the antithesis of righteousness/truth (the B5 face) ⚑ SYNTH
- *adikia* is the standing condition of **unrighteousness/wrongdoing** — the **antithesis of righteousness** (*dikaiosunē*, Rom 3:5; 6:13) and of **truth** ("**suppress the truth** by unrighteousness", Rom 1:18; "rejoices not at wrongdoing but rejoices with the truth", 1 Cor 13:6); **sin-associated** ("the members as **instruments for unrighteousness**", Rom 6:13, *hamartia*[CORE]); it draws **wrath** (Rom 1:18; 2:8, *orgē*); it is in the Rom 1:29 vice-list with *ponēria*/*kakia*; and it is **forgivable under the new covenant** ("merciful toward their **iniquities**… remember their sins no more", Heb 8:12) (Bsum §2). All 8 are **type=status** — the standing inner condition, fitting #24's "structural condition of the inner person."

### A7. The good/evil antithesis (agathos polarity)
- *ponēros* is consistently **defined against *agathos*** (good): sun on evil and good (Mat 5:45); evil men give good gifts (7:11; Luk 11:13); good tree/bad fruit (7:17–18); good/evil treasure (12:35); "**abhor what is evil, hold fast to good**" (Rom 12:9); 1 Pe 2:14; 2 Cor 5:10 (B4 §2; B4 audit §3). The polarity parallels *to.e.vah*/*ratson* in the abomination units (#25/#26).

### A8. The spread of poneros (the verdict faces — recorded as diversity)
- *ponēros* also carries the **verdict** faces that overlap the person/speech units (B4 §2): the **evil generation** ("an evil and adulterous generation", Mat 12:39; 16:4; Luk 11:29); **evil deeds/works** (Joh 3:19; 7:7; Luk 3:19; 2 Ti 4:18; 2 Jo 11; 3 Jo 10; Col 1:21); the **evil eye** (stinginess/envy — "if your eye is bad", Mat 6:23; "do you begrudge", Mat 20:15); the **wicked servant** (Mat 18:32; 25:26; Luk 19:22); **evil men purged/delivered-from** (1 Cor 5:13 "purge the evil person"; 2 Th 3:2; 2 Ti 3:13; Act 17:5); the **present evil age / evil day** (Gal 1:4 +*hamartia*; Eph 5:16; 6:13). These verdict-faces are recorded; their per-occurrence home (vs the person-unit #23 / the evildoer face) is a §10 call.

### A9. Touch points — what the Greek evil family reaches (B4 audit §4; B5 §2)
- **Sin-core:** *hamartia*/*hamartanō*[CORE], *suneidesis*[CORE] ×2; *adikia* sits on *hamartia* (Rom 6:13).
- **Within M10b / wickedness field:** *blasfēmia*[R2] ×6 (the heart-evils vice-catalogues, Mat 15:19; Mar 7:22; 1 Ti 6:4); *adikos*/*adikēma*[R2]; *ponēria*↔*kakia*↔*adikia* mutually (Rom 1:29).
- **M10c defilement:** *koinoō* (Mar 7:23 "evil things… defile a person") — the evil-from-within ↔ defilement link.
- **External:** the constant *agathos* (good), *dikaiosunē* (righteousness), *alētheia* (truth), *orgē* (wrath).

### A10. Field hygiene (surfaced, not resolved)

- ⚠ **HYGIENE — kakia "trouble" sense** at Mat 6:34 ("sufficient for the day is its own *trouble*") is the non-moral sense; recorded, not the malice-vice (B4 audit §6).
- ⚠ **HYGIENE — rely on co-seated, not `location`, for seating** (the M10b standing caveat, Bsum §5).
