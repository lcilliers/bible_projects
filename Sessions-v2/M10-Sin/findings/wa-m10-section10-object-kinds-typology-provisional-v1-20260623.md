- **Answers the researcher's gate question:** "what is a *characteristic* vs a *status*, and are there other objects that are neither, or hybrids?"

## 0. The headline answer
The binary "characteristic vs status" is **insufficient**. The M10 evidence shows the inner being is described by **(at least) eight object-kinds**, plus **hybrids/gradients**. Two are the obvious pair (characteristic, status/condition); **six more are neither** (record, identity, expression, mechanism, remedy, agency). An object can sit on a **gradient** between kinds, and a unit can be **mixed** (part one kind, part another).

## 1. The eight object-kinds — definition · discriminating test · data-signal · M10 anchor

> **How to use:** for any unit/term, run the discriminating test; confirm against the data-signal. Where two fit, it is a **hybrid/gradient** (§2). All grounded in the observation groups (G1–G8).

### A. CHARACTERISTIC — *a faculty/disposition in operation*
- **Definition:** the inner being **actively doing or being-bent** — a faculty (heart/mind/will/conscience) engaged in an operation.
- **Test:** *Is the inner being acting / being-bent here (a verb-life), and is a faculty actually seated?*
- **Data-signal:** **high interior seatedness** (the strongest discriminator) + type=action/disposition + lifecycle verbs (conceive→devise→bring-forth).
- **M10 anchor (G7):** #27 iniquity-scheming (the model — ~16% interior, the generative arc Psa 7:14); #21 perversion; #20 faithlessness.

### B. CONDITION / STATE — *a standing mode the inner being is in or under*
- **Definition:** a **standing situation/mode**, not an act — universal / constitutional / bondage / contracted.
- **Test:** *Is this a standing state the person is in/under, rather than something they do?*
- **Data-signal:** type=status + "under / in" framing; **can be non-moral** (valence neutral — the cleanest proof it is not "sin-as-wrong").
- **M10 anchor (G4):** #11 universal-condition, #12 enslaving-power, #24 constitutional-nature, #29–31 defilement-states.

### C. RECORD / LIABILITY — *the forensic weight borne before God*
- **Definition:** an **accountable weight** (debt / account / measure) the inner being incurs and bears.
- **Test:** *Is this an answerable weight before God (recorded, counted, owed), rather than a state or an act?*
- **Data-signal:** debt/account/written/blot-out/measure language; **objective↔subjective poles**; **accumulation**.
- **M10 anchor (G5):** #13 divine-record, #17 guilt, #18 accumulated-iniquity; the a.von crime/guilt/punishment tri-fold.

### D. IDENTITY — *a verdict-classification of the whole person*
- **Definition:** **what kind of person** the inner being is at its moral core — a verdict become a standing type.
- **Test:** *Does this name a kind of person (a type), rather than a state, weight, or act?*
- **Data-signal:** type=status/quality + person-label + **defined-by-contrast** (pole-pair) + faculty=moral_evaluation (a verdict); interior thin (declares, doesn't open).
- **M10 anchor (G1):** #16 the sinner, #23 the wicked.

### E. EXPRESSION / OUTFLOW — *the inner state externalised*
- **Definition:** an **output through an organ** (mouth, act) that **reveals** the inner state; a connective hub to other kinds.
- **Test:** *Is this an output that manifests/exposes an inner source, rather than the inner state itself?*
- **Data-signal:** an organ (speech/act); **faculty empty on the lemma** but **co-seated heart** (the source); a direction/target.
- **M10 anchor (G2):** #9 sinful speech, #28 evil-through-speech (#8 political revolt is the per-occurrence borderline).

### F. MECHANISM / ROUTE — *a route/trigger by which sin enters or operates*
- **Definition:** a **how** — an external trigger acting on an internal appetite/faculty.
- **Test:** *Is this a route/process (a trigger→effect), rather than the thing it triggers?*
- **Data-signal:** **CORE ×0** (doesn't touch the sin-core); points to a **source cluster**; a trigger→appetite chain.
- **M10 anchor (G3):** #10 specialised mechanisms (enticement/hypocrisy/apostasy).

### G. REMEDY / ATONEMENT — *the undoing of a sin-object*
- **Definition:** the **counter-object** — the covering/blotting/cleansing/bearing-away that discharges a sin-object; bilateral (divine mechanism + human movement).
- **Test:** *Does this undo/remove a sin-object, rather than be one?*
- **Data-signal:** **neutral/commanded valence**; cover/blot/cleanse/bear/ransom language; **cross-cluster** (M11/M38/M12).
- **M10 anchor (G6):** #14 forgiveness/atonement + the atonement seam.

### H. EXTERNAL AGENCY — *an agent acting on the inner being from without*
- **Definition:** an **external personal agent** that invades/controls; the person is the **site/victim, not the source**.
- **Test:** *Is the inner being the OBJECT of an external will here, rather than the subject?*
- **Data-signal:** experiencer=other/external; a personal-agent referent; present only by its **effects** on the inner being.
- **M10 anchor (G8):** #24 the "evil one", #32 the unclean spirit.

## 2. Hybrids, gradients, and the "neither" cases (grounded)
- **CONDITION ↔ AGENCY gradient:** sin-as-impersonal-power (#12, within) → personified-sin → personal agent (#24/#32, without). One continuum; the kind shifts along it.
- **RECORD gradient:** external-penalty (a.von punishment, external-pole) → objective-record (#13) → subjective-guilt (#17, which borders on a CHARACTERISTIC — the conscience in operation).
- **EXPRESSION as connective:** always points to a characteristic (its source) **plus** a condition/identity/record (its result) — possibly a *relation* more than a standalone kind.
- **REMEDY as inverse:** the counter of each sin-object (record→blotted, guilt→covered, defilement→cleansed, condition→freed); likely **not an M10 object at all** but a cross-cluster mechanism.
- **Partial-characteristics:** #21 (part consequence/R3 decay), #20 (relational, pole in M13/M31) — characteristic + another kind.

## 3. The structural hypotheses (to test programme-wide — NOT settled)
- **H1 — the CHARACTERISTIC is the generative centre.** The faculty-in-operation **produces** the expression, **incurs** the record, **hardens** into the identity, **settles** into the condition. If true, the typology centres on the characteristic, with the other kinds as its **modes/registers/derivatives**. (Anchored on #27; generalise with care.)
- **H2 — bi-level resolution.** A sin-object is resolved two ways by kind: the **RECORD is forgiven** (atonement, G), the **CHARACTERISTIC is transformed/renewed** (the NT resolution — Spirit, new heart; lives in M11/M38/M45). Remedy-mode varies by object-kind (atonement vs deliverance for AGENCY).
- **H3 — pole-pairs.** Many objects are one side of a paired opposite (sin↔righteousness M26, defilement↔purity M12, faithlessness↔faith M13/M31) — the inner being described by **paired opposites**.
- **H4 — the dual-view.** Each object has an *operation-view* (in the host cluster) and may have a *characteristic-view* cluster of its own (M10b↔M27) — the multi-dimensional model.

## 4. How this serves the M11+ method (the lens in use)
Per the researcher's forward direction, for each cluster CC will, **by unit**:
1. **Distil** the unit (its lexical data + Session B findings + Session D pointers/flags, presented per unit).
2. **Classify** each unit's object-kind via §1 (the test + data-signal) — **statuses (and ambiguous characteristics) go through this object-kinds work**.
3. **Run characteristics through the tier questions** (the catalogue) — the established characteristic analysis.
4. **Synthesise** units → groups → cluster, using the hybrids/gradients (§2) and hypotheses (§3) to relate them.
> This typology is the **classifier** for step 2 — the reason to have it *before* M11. It will be **refined** as each cluster tests it (especially the non-sin clusters, where CHARACTERISTIC should dominate — the inverse of M10).

## §G — Bias-guard / status

The first **characteristic-heavy cluster** (e.g. a virtue cluster) will be the real test of A and of H1. *Expect the typology to evolve.*
