### Synergy — how the terms operate together (the compound web within the verse)
All four are **co-seated in the heart / inward-parts**: the verse runs entirely in one seat. *lev* (the heart) is the **patient** — *washed* (*kabas*) — while *machashavah* (thoughts), named as *a'ven* (wickedness) and characterised as *ra'ah* (evil/harm), are what **lodge** (*lun*) there. The verse's **faculty engagement is built from the co-terms**, not from *a'ven* alone: *lev* contributes cognition/perception/volition/conscience (the seat's whole range), *machashavah* cognition, *ra'ah* affect+cognition+moral, *a'ven* affect+moral — together a heart engaged in thinking, willing, judging, and feeling. *matay* ("how long") qualifies the **duration** of the lodging.

### Inner-being reality evidenced (neutral)
The heart is a **seat that harbours lodging, dwelling evil cognition** (*lun* — it stays overnight) and is **capable of being laundered clean** of it (*kabas*), with that cleansing tied to **being saved**. So here the inner being operates as a seat whose contents (thoughts) can be evil and *persistent*, and which can be washed — purity/cleansing language applied to the **heart itself**, not the body. (Relation to the bodily-defilement material noted; not interpreted.)

### Flags (extract-tag cautions — held open, not inherited)
- *valence = sinful* applied to **all four including *lev* "heart"** — the seat is not the wickedness; do not read "the heart is sinful."
- *a'ven* `object` text = "within" is a mechanical hint (an adverb, not a true object); *object_type* "abstract" is the authoritative read.
- Four clusters meet here (M47/M27/M14/M10b) — evidence is the **joint** reading; the cluster split is not doing analytical work.
