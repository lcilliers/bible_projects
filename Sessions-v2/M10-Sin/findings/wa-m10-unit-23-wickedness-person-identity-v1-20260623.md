### A1. Structural signature — the cleanest field-uniformity in M10 (B1 audit §2)
- **type = status: 258 / 258.** Every occurrence is a *status*, not an action or quality-event.
- **faculty = moral_evaluation: 258 / 258.** Uniform and **term-intrinsic** — the wicked-family names a **moral verdict on a person/thing**, not a faculty-seated inner state. This is the cleanest field-uniformity seen in any M10 unit so far, and anchors what the wicked-family *is* at the data level: **a classification, not a movement.**
- Within the M10b whole (588 occ), the predominance holds: type=status 492/588 (84%) (Bsum §3). *type=status · faculty=moral_evaluation* is the data-level identity of this unit.

### A2. The FOUR/FIVE FACES of ra'sha (held open — facets, NOT a developmental line) ⚑ SYNTH
The completed read shows *ra'sha* is **not one thing** (B1 audit §5; SP4 digest §5; recovery §5). Five strands coexist in the evidence; **no single reading is adopted** — this diversity is itself the finding to carry forward.

1. **Forensic verdict** — the guilty party in court; "condemning the guilty / vindicating the righteous" (Deu 25:1–2; 1 Ki 8:32; 2 Ch 6:23); "the man in the wrong" (Exo 2:13); Pharaoh's "I and my people are in the wrong" (Exo 9:27). (SP1.)
2. **Characterised interior** — heart-denial of accountability, soul-desire boasted, godless thought, concealed evil, pride; mouth/heart/appetite/counsel of the wicked (SP2 Psalms; SP3 Proverbs). **Bounded** to specific texts (see A4).
3. **The fixed two-destinies antithesis** — *tsaddiq* (righteous) vs *ra'sha*, two ways, two fates; **now properly dominant** after the recovery restored most of Psalm 37 and Pro 24/28/29 (SP3; recovery §2a).
4. **Reversible condition — turn and live** — the wicked summoned to turn from sin and live; addressable, warnable, reversible (Ezekiel 18/33; Isa 55:7). **14 of 44** prophetic occurrences carry the turn/live/pardon field (SP4 §2). Stands in tension with face 3.
5. **Theodicy / wisdom-crisis** (candidate 5th face, surfaced by the recovery) — the wicked who **prospers** and seems to escape the order: "envious… of the prosperity of the wicked" (Psa 73:3,12); Ecclesiastes' empirical inversion — affirmed in principle (3:17; 8:13) yet observed to fail (7:15; 8:14; 9:2) (recovery §2b).

- ⚑ SYNTH — **the gradient does not survive the family.** The B1→B2→B3 "verdict → detestableness → interior" impression is broken by B4 (Greek evil spans the whole range and adds personification). The five faces are better held as **co-present facets of the evil/wickedness field, not a developmental sequence** (Bsum §4, §6). Surfaced for §10, not resolved.

### A3. The defining axis — righteous vs wicked (tsaddiq) ↔ M26 BOND
- Nearly every Wisdom/Histories verse pairs *ra'sha* with *tsaddiq / tsadeq / tsedaqah* (Gen 18:23,25; Exo 23:7; Deu 25:1; 1 Ki 8:32; 2 Ch 6:23; 2 Sa 4:11; Job 9:22; the governing proverb-shape of Pro 10–15; Psa 1, Psa 37; Mal 3:18). *ra'sha* is **defined by what it is *not*** more than by co-occurrence with the sin-core (SP1 §2; B1 audit §4).
- ↔ **BOND M26 (Righteousness — tsaddiq antithesis, the dominant bond, 81 shared verses).** The antonym sits in the **righteousness field, external to M10**; the wicked-family is defined as much by this contrast as by its sin-core touches. *(Per the unit brief: dominant cross-cluster bond.)*

### A4. The wicked's interior is BOUNDED (~5 genuine heart seats) — not pervasive (B1 audit §3; recovery §3)
- 22 co-seatings across B1: *nephesh* 14, heart-family (*lev* 5, *levav* 2, *libbah* 1) 8. The *nephesh* set is **mostly "life/person"** (the wicked threatening or losing life; the psalmist's own soul) — **not** the wicked's seat.
- **Genuine wicked-heart seatings (~5):** the heart that denies accountability (Psa 10:13); evil in the hearts behind peaceful speech (Psa 28:3); the heart "of little worth" (Pro 10:20); the heart that "pours out evil" (Pro 15:28); the "proud heart [that is] sin" (Pro 21:4).
- The recovery (+69) added **almost none** to the interior (*nephesh* ×1 only, ="life") — **confirming** the interior is bounded to those texts, a real distribution, not a gap (recovery §3).
- Other interior threads (SP2/SP3, recorded as diversity): soul's desire boasted (Psa 10:3, *ta'avah*); "all his thoughts are 'there is no God'" (Psa 10:4) — **practical atheism**; transgression's inner voice "deep in his heart… no fear of God before his eyes" (Psa 36:1); proud/arrogant face (Psa 10:2,4; Pro 21:29); the soul/appetite that "desires evil" / "thwarted craving" (Pro 10:3; 21:10; 13:25; 12:10); deceitful counsels (Pro 12:5, *machashavah*/*tachbulah*/*mirmah*); congenital — "estranged from the womb… go astray from birth" (Psa 58:3); cognitive deficiency — "does not understand knowledge" (Pro 29:7; cf. Dan 12:10).

### A5. God's posture toward the wicked (SP2 §3; SP3 §4; SP4)
- **Tested:** "you test the minds and hearts" (Psa 7:9, *kilyah*+*libbah*) — God examines the wicked's interior.
- **Hated:** "his soul hates the wicked" (Psa 11:5, God's *nephesh*).
- **Far / cursed:** "the Lord is far from the wicked" (Pro 15:29); "curse on the wicked's house" (Pro 3:33); divine **abomination** of the wicked's sacrifice/way/justifying (Pro 15:8,9; 17:15; 21:27) — *ra'sha* touches *to'evah* ×4 (within-M10b, hold for synergy).
- **Judgment falls on this person-type:** chaff/perish/Sheol/snared/broken-arms/"be no more" (Psa 1:4,6; 9:16,17; 37 passim); cut off from the land, name rots, lamp put out, house overthrown (Pro 2:22; 10:7,30; 13:9; 12:7; 14:11); storm/wrath on the wicked's head, crushed head, trodden to ashes (Jer 23:19; 30:23; Hab 3:13; Mal 4:3).

### A6. Conduct & fate motifs (continuing, recorded) (SP2 §4; SP3 §4; SP4 §3)
- **Conduct:** violence, bow/arrow against the upright-in-heart, prowling, plotting and gnashing, pursuing the poor, borrowing without repaying; "mouth conceals violence / pours out evil / words lie in wait for blood / what is perverse / devours iniquity."
- **Counter-currents:** **incorrigibility** — "if favor is shown to the wicked, he does not learn righteousness" (Isa 26:10), a tension with the turn-theme; **restlessness** — "the wicked are like the tossing sea… no peace for the wicked" (Isa 57:20–21; 48:22); **eschatological** — "the wicked shall act wickedly… none shall understand" (Dan 12:10); the **Servant** "made his grave with the wicked, though he had done no violence" (Isa 53:9).
- **Core-binding heaviest in the Prophets** (SP4 §1): *a'von* ×7, *cha'ta* ×3, *chat'tat* ×3, *pe'sha* ×1 — there *ra'sha* is paired directly with the sin-core ("turn from his sin/iniquity"), unlike Wisdom's *tsaddiq*-antonym pairing.

### A7. The minor lemmas (B1 audit §5)
- ***re'sha* (H7562, abstract "wickedness")**: a **commodity/possession** ("treasures of wickedness", "wicked scales", Mic 6:10–11); a **bondage** to be loosed ("loose the bonds of wickedness", Isa 58:6); a **confessed wrong** before God ("we acknowledge our wickedness… for we have sinned", Jer 14:20, with *a'von*+*cha'ta*); something **sown and reaped** (Hos 10:13); **self-implicating** ("your wickedness concerns a man like yourself", Job 35:8).
- ***mirsha'at* (H4849)**: wickedness attributed to a person — "that **wicked woman**" (2 Ch 24:7).

### A9. Field hygiene (surfaced, not resolved)

The reliable heart-based interior concentrates in the **evil/abomination** terms (*a'ven*, *to'evah*, *ro'a*), **not** in *ra'sha* itself.

- ⚠ **HYGIENE — *che.vel* "pain" homonym** at Psa 119:61 (here = "cords" of the wicked, not pain) — note (recovery §4).
- ⚠ **HYGIENE — loose seat readings:** Eze 13:22 location=heart ("disheartened the righteous… encouraged the wicked") is a loose seat reading; *nephesh* in the turn-or-die clauses (Eze 3:19; 18:27; 33:9) is borderline seat vs "life"; location=flesh (Jer 25:31; Eze 21:4) = "all flesh" (humanity-wide), not a seat (SP4 §4).
