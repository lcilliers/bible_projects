## A. General / non-verse observations (copied from the source digests)

**Definition (DB):** Sin committed through the mouth — blasphemy and slander originating in inner moral corruption, directed against God or persons. Cross-register flag: **M06**.

**From the R2p blasphemy sub-ring (Ring 2 close, §1 — 22 occ):** blasphemy presents in **two senses**:
- **Against God / the Spirit** — "the blasphemy against the Spirit will not be forgiven" (Mat 12:31); the Jews charge Jesus: "for blasphemy… you, being a man, make yourself God" (Joh 10:33); the beast "uttering haughty and blasphemous words… blasphemies against God" (Rev 13:1–6).
- **Slander against people** — in the vice lists: "out of the heart come… slander" (Mar 7:22); "let all… slander be put away from you" (Eph 4:31); "put… slander out of your mouth" (Col 3:8).

**Heart-seated and decisive:** "out of the **heart** come evil thoughts… **slander**" (Mat 15:19, *kardia* co-seated) — blasphemy/slander proceeds **from the heart** (this is the inner-being seating that makes it M10 inner-corruption, not mere external speech).

**Paired with hamartia:** "every **sin** and blasphemy will be forgiven" (Mat 12:31) — blasphemy is set directly beside the sin-core lemma.

**blasfēmos = the blasphemer (G0989):** the person-term — "though formerly I was a **blasphemer**… in unbelief" (1 Ti 1:13), tying the act back to the unbelieving inner state.

**Structural signature (this subset, from the R2p audit §2):** the Greek blasphemy/slander terms carry **faculty=moral_evaluation** where faculty is populated (part of the 16/207 faculty-tagged Ring 2 occurrences — the NT *adik-*/moral terms). For the rest, **faculty is empty but the heart is engaged via co-occurrence** (Mat 15:19 *kardia*) — the recurring Ring 2 pattern: the inner-being signal is carried by the seat co-term, not the lemma's own faculty field.

**Field hygiene (from the R2p end-of-ring audit §2):**
- **⚠ HYGIENE — faculty sparse:** faculty populated on only 16/207 Ring 2 occ; do not infer "no inner faculty" from an empty faculty field — read the co-seated heart (surface; not resolved).
- **⚠ HYGIENE — location/heart-seating is the cleanest signal:** Mat 15:19 (slander from the heart) is among the 8 genuine HEART-seatings in Ring 2 — the cleanest inner-being evidence; weight it.

**Flags requiring deeper synthesis (surfaced, NOT resolved):**
- **⚖ BORDER — unit boundary (blasphemy noun vs verb):** this unit (#9) holds the **blasfēmia/blasfēmos NOUN**; unit #28 (Evil expressed through speech) holds the **blasfēmeō VERB**. One speech-phenomenon is **split across two units** by part-of-speech — synthesis must decide whether to consolidate. ↔ see #28.
- **⚑ SYNTH — two-sense axis (God-ward vs person-ward):** the same lemma covers blasphemy *against God/the Spirit* and slander *against people*. Whether these are one inner-corruption operating on two objects, or two distinct units, is a synthesis call (the object differs; the heart-origin is shared).
- **⚑ SYNTH — the unforgivable seam:** Mat 12:31 sets blasphemy-against-the-Spirit apart as the one sin "not forgiven" — sits against the whole M10 forgiveness register (#14); worth synthesising the limit-case.
- **⚑ SYNTH — heart-origin of speech-sin:** Mat 15:19 grounds slander in the heart (alongside evil thoughts, murder, etc.) — the cross-faculty link from the speech-organ to the inner seat; the M06 (mouth/speech) cross-register flag and M15 (mind) are engaged here.

**↔ BOND (cross-cluster):** **M06** (the cross-register flag — sin through the mouth/speech organ), **M11** (Repentance — the forgiveness/unforgivable seam), **M15** (Wisdom/mind — heart-origin of evil thoughts), **M28**, **M42** (Speech).
