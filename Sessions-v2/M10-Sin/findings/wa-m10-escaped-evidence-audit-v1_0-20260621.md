I documented only `focus_cluster=true` terms (the 61 focus strongs). But the extract tags co-terms with their **own** cluster, and two of them are M10 sub-clusters I never surfaced:

**M10c — defilement / impurity (~25 occ):** *ta.me* "unclean/defile" ×19 · *nid.dah* "impurity" ×6.

These are squarely in the sin field, and two cut across word-families I *did* document: ***blasfēmeō*** is the verb of my focus *blasfēmia*, and ***adikia*** is the noun behind my focus *adikos* — so the focus/sub-cluster boundary splits live word-families. By filtering to `focus_cluster=true` I excluded the cluster's own wickedness and defilement vocabulary. **~93 occurrences of M10-tagged evidence went unexamined.**

*This is a scope decision for you, not mine to make:* are M10b (wickedness/evil/abomination) and M10c (defilement/impurity) in scope for the inner-being analysis? They were in the extract all along.

## 3. ESCAPED FINDING 2 — the **`co-seated`** compound role is the real inner-being-seating index, and I under-used it

The `compound` field carries a **role** on each co-term: *partner* (7,275), *qualifier* (597), and **`co-seated` (245)**. The meta is explicit: the `faculty` field is **term-intrinsic only**, and co-occurring-faculty assignment is *"a known not-yet-implemented extension."* So the inner-being seating does **not** live in `faculty` (16/1,462) or even mainly in `location` — it lives in the **`co-seated` role**, which flags every sin-term that co-occurs with a constitutional seat:

| Co-seated faculty | Occurrences |
|---|--:|
| *ne.phesh* (soul/life) | 104 |
| *lev* + *le.vav* (heart) | 81 |
| *sarx* + *ba.sar* + *she.er* (flesh) | 25 |
| *pneuma* (spirit) | 11 |
| *psuchē* (soul) | 8 |
| *kardia* (heart) | 7 |
| *nous* (mind) | 7 |
| *suneidesis* (conscience) | 2 |
| **Total** | **245** |

So there are **245 sin-term-to-faculty co-seatings** in M10 — roughly **15× the `location` field's coverage** and far richer than what I built the ring digests on. Even discounting *nephesh* (much of it the person/life idiom we flagged), the genuine-faculty co-seatings — heart 88, flesh 25, spirit 11, soul 8, mind 7, conscience 2 ≈ **140** — are the actual map of where sin touches the inner being. I used `compound` for *touch points* but never extracted `co-seated` as the **seating index**. This is the single most important inner-being signal in the cluster, and the check caught that I had been reading around it.
