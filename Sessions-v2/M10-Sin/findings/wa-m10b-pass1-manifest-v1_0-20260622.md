## 3. Bias register — what NOT to inherit from Pass 1
1. **Wrong unit.** Pass 1 is term/family-keyed; the evidence unit is the **verse**. (Archive in §2 re-keys to verse to correct this for preservation.)
2. **Architecture framing.** "Core / rings R1–R4 / satellites", "the wicked family", "facets of the sin field" are **imposed categories**. In the archive, the ring labels survive only inside `co_terms_and_seating` as **provenance**, not as a neutral classification.
3. **Prejudged the open question.** Pass 1 read terms as **expressions of a sin-status**. The status-vs-characteristic differentiation is **undetermined and to be debated** — it must not be assumed in the evidence.
4. **Selective / blotting.** Foregrounding the sin-relation left each phenomenon's own inner-being behaviour (seat, rise, dynamics, what acts on it, what it produces, change/removal, co-existence) under-captured.
5. **Extract-tag bias to flag, not absorb** (surfaced in Pass 1, carried forward as standing cautions):
   - `valence = "sinful"` is over-applied — e.g. tagged on *lev* "heart" (Jer 4:14) and on ritual contact-uncleanness (Lev 5:2). The seat is not the wickedness; ritual uncleanness is not moral sin. Hold open.
   - `location` polluted by *nephesh* = "soul/person/life" idiom (esp. *ra'sha* cultic corpus). Use **co-seated** (compound role) for genuine seating, not `location`.
   - Single-term truncation incident (*ra'sha* H7563) — **resolved** via +69 recovery; recorded so it is not re-litigated.
