## Observations (each grounded)

**O1 — A remedy/atonement is the UNDOING of sin — a counter-object, categorically distinct from a sin-object.**
- *Evidence:* the chat.tat **sin-offering** (H2403H, 102+4) carries **neutral/commanded** valence, NOT sinful (the cultic remedy named by the very word for the offense); kip.pu.rim "atonement" is cultic; Lev 16:30 "atonement… to **cleanse** you… **clean from all your sins**"; Isa 43:25 "**blot out**… will not remember". The §A BORDER puts the question directly: "is the sin-offering a face of sin at all, or of its undoing?"
- *So:* the object is the **dealing-with / removal** of sin (cover, blot-out, take-away, cleanse, bear-for), a **counter-object** — the inverse of sin, not a face of it.

**O2 — It is BILATERAL — a DIVINE/objective mechanism + a HUMAN/subjective movement toward it.**
- *Evidence:* DEF #14 "the inner movement toward God in search of forgiveness + the divine response." DIVINE side: ka.phar "**cover**", the sin-offering, "propitiation" (Rom 3:25), "Christ **died for our sins**" (1 Cor 15:3), "takes away the sin of the world" (Joh 1:29), "**made him to be sin**" (2 Cor 5:21). HUMAN side: seeking, "**confess**" (Mat 3:6; 1 Jo 1:9), turning; "**afflict your souls**" on the Day of Atonement (nephesh, Lev 23:27).
- *So:* the remedy object has **two sides** — a divine mechanism and a human movement meeting it. The **inner-being part is the human movement** (seek / confess / turn / afflict-the-soul); the divine mechanism is cultic/Christological.

**O3 — It is the INVERSE of the record/condition objects — defined by what it discharges.**
- *Evidence:* atonement **blots the RECORD** (#13; "blot out… not remember" Isa 43:25; "not count" Rom 4:8 — #13/#14 are "obverse/reverse of the same accounting"); the offering **covers the GUILT** (a.sham guilt-offering, Isa 53:10); cleansing **removes the DEFILEMENT** (#29–32; M12 purity, Lev 16:30 record→cleanse); forgiveness **lifts the CONDITION**.
- *So:* the remedy is the **inverse-operation** for each sin-object (record→blotted, guilt→covered, defilement→cleansed, condition→freed). It is defined **relationally by what it undoes** — confirming, from the remedy side, Group 4-O6 and Group 5-O5.

**O4 — It is a CROSS-CLUSTER mechanism — not housed in one unit.**
- *Evidence:* bonds **M11 (Repentance/Forgiveness) DOMINANT — 83 shared verses**; **M38 (Salvation) — 51**; **M12 (Purity)** — the cleanse/wash remedy. The apparatus spans the sin-offering (M10) + cleansing (M10c/M12) + forgiveness/repentance (M11) + salvation/borne-for (M38).
- *So:* atonement is a **distributed, cross-cluster mechanism** — which is exactly *why it is "not encapsulated in a unit"* (SDX-02). Its home is shared, not M10-proper.

**O5 — It works through a FAMILY of removal-metaphors — covering is central.**
- *Evidence:* **cover** (ka.phar / kip.pu.rim — the masc-plural noun is the provision, the verb ka.phar the act); **blot-out** (Isa 43:25); **take-away** (Joh 1:29); **cleanse / wash** (Lev 16:30; Act 22:16); **bear-for / die-for** (Isa 53; 1 Cor 15:3); **ransom** ("for your lives", Exo 30:16); **not-count** (Rom 4:8).
- *So:* the remedy is a **family of inverse-operations** (cover / blot / take-away / cleanse / bear / ransom / not-count), each matched to a sin-object's mode.

## Candidate label (held open)
**REMEDY / ATONEMENT** — the **undoing of sin**: the mechanism (and the human movement toward it) by which the sin-record / condition / guilt / defilement is **covered, blotted-out, taken-away, cleansed, borne-for, or ransomed**. A **counter-object** (the inverse of the sin-objects), **bilateral** (divine mechanism + human movement), **defined by what it discharges**, and **cross-cluster** (M10/M10c + M11/M38/M12) — not housed in one unit.

## §G — Bias-guard
- **⚠ Is the remedy an M10 object at all?** The evidence says it is the **undoing** of sin (neutral/commanded valence; the §A BORDER), and it is **cross-cluster** (M11/M38/M12). So "atonement" likely **does not belong to M10's object-set** — it is the counter-mechanism whose home is distributed (→ SDX-02; and a home-call, SDX-06: #14's "forgiveness" face is largely **M11**'s territory; the cultic apparatus may need a sacrifice/cult home).
- **⚠ Separate the divine mechanism from the human movement.** The **divine/Christological side** (propitiation, "made him to be sin", Christ died-for) is about **God's action**, not the person's interior — largely **outside an inner-being study**. The **inner-being part is the human movement** (seeking, confessing, turning, afflicting-the-soul, Lev 23:27). Do not import the cultic/Christological mechanism into the inner-being object-set; keep the two sides distinct.
- **⚠ kip.pu.rim is "largely objective/external" (blood, atonement-money, ram)** — only **one** inner-being thread (Lev 23:27 "afflict your souls"). Don't over-read the cultic provision as an inner-being state.
- **⚠ object/object_type 0/8 on kip.pu.rim** is an extract gap (the "what atonement is for" is in the verse text) — grounded from text, not the fields.
