## 2. Structural signature
- **Type:** **quality 64**, status 22, action 2 — the Greek evil family names evil predominantly as a **moral quality/property** ("evil" the adjective), with a status tail (the evil one / the evildoer). This contrasts with the Hebrew families (*ra'sha* status-verdict; *a'ven* status/action-process).
- **Faculty:** moral_evaluation 82 (none 5; *faulos* volition+moral 1).
- **Interior: 13 / 88 (~15%)** — heart/mind/soul/conscience. Comparable to B3 (~16%) and far above B1/B2. The Greek evil family is interior-heavy.

## 3. The faces (held open)
- **The personified "evil one" (Satan) — UNIQUE to the Greek family.** ~11 *poneros* by referent (Mat 6:13; 13:38 spiritual-being; Joh 17:15; Eph 6:16; 1Jo 2:13–14; 5:18–19), plus the cosmic "spiritual forces of **evil**" (Eph 6:12, *poneria*). No Hebrew M10/M10b term personifies evil as an adversary.
- **The heart as the source of evil (strong interior).** "out of the **heart** come **evil thoughts**…" (Mat 15:19); "the evil person out of his evil **treasure**… the good treasure of his **heart**" (12:35; Luk 6:45); "evil in your **hearts**" (Mat 9:4); "an evil, unbelieving **heart**" (Heb 3:12). And evil **defiles**: "these evil things come from within and **defile** a person" (Mar 7:23, *koinoō* — an M10c-type link). co-seated *kardia* ×7.
- **The conscience thread (*suneidesis*, core term).** "hearts sprinkled clean from an **evil conscience**" (Heb 10:22); "having a **good conscience**… reviled as evildoers" (1Pe 3:16). Evil/good is registered at the conscience — a direct link to the M10 core.
- **Malice as the vice-disposition to PUT AWAY.** "put away all **malice**, deceit, hypocrisy, envy, slander" (1Pe 2:1; Col 3:8; Eph 4:31; Jam 1:21 "from the **soul**", 1Cor 14:20 "infants in evil" *phrēn*). The ethical "put off" of *kakia*, in vice-catalogues; "repent of this **wickedness**… the intent of your **heart**" (Act 8:22); "turning you from your **wickedness**" (Act 3:26, *poniera*). Echoes B3's turn-from/remove.
- **The good/evil antithesis (*agathos*).** Pervasive polarity (Mat 5:45; 7:11,17–18; 12:35; Rom 12:9; 1Pe 2:14; 2Cor 5:10) — evil consistently defined against good (cf. *to'evah*/*ratson*).
- **The evildoer / evil generation / evil deeds / evil eye / wicked servant / present evil age** — the spread of *poneros* (see lemma digest).

## 5. The gradient — now complicated (held open)
B1→B2→B3 suggested *verdict → relational detestableness → interior process*. **B4 does not sit on that line:** the Greek evil family spans the *whole* range at once — verdict (evildoer, evil generation), strong interior (evil heart/treasure/conscience), good/evil polarity, defilement link — **and adds personification (the evil one) that no Hebrew term carries.** So the gradient is better held as *facets co-present in the Greek vocabulary*, not a developmental line. Recorded as a correction-in-progress to the B3 note; no conclusion.
