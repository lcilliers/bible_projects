## 2. What SP3 confirms / adds
- **The conceive→birth metaphor, a third corpus.** "they **conceive** mischief and give **birth** to iniquity" (Isa 59:4) — with Psa 7:14 and Job 15:35, this is *a'ven*'s signature across Psalms, Wisdom, and Prophets.
- **The heart devises/harbours it (strongest interior verses).** "the fool's **heart** is busy with iniquity" (Isa 32:6, *lev*+*nephesh*); "**wash your heart** from evil… how long shall your **wicked thoughts** lodge within you?" (Jer 4:14, *lev*+inward-parts, *machashavah*); "men who **devise** iniquity" (Eze 11:2, *chashav*); "those who **devise** wickedness… on their beds" (Mic 2:1). The heart is the seat of *a'ven*'s devising — and the seat to be **washed** of it.
- **Volitional / turn (remedy).** "let the **unrighteous** man [forsake] his **thoughts**… **return** to the Lord… he will abundantly **pardon**" (Isa 55:7, *shuv*/*racham*/*salach*); "take away… the speaking of **wickedness**" (Isa 58:9, *sur*).
- **The idol / vanity sense (minority, now attested).** "their soul delights in their [idol-offering]" (Isa 66:3, sense=idol, *nephesh*+*chaphets*+*shiqquts*); "the household gods utter **nonsense**" (Zec 10:2, sense=nonsense) — *a'ven* as idolatrous emptiness/false utterance.
- **The prophet made to see it (theodicy).** "why do you make me **see iniquity**?" (Hab 1:3).
- **Iniquitous decrees/works:** "woe to those who **decree iniquitous decrees**" (Isa 10:1); "works of **iniquity**… thoughts of iniquity" (Isa 59:6–7).
