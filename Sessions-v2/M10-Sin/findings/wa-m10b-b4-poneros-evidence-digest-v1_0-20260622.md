## 2. The faces of poneros (the broadest M10b term — all held open)
- **"The evil one" (Satan, a spiritual being) — NEW to M10b.** ~11 by referent: "deliver us from **the evil one**" (Mat 6:13); "the evil one snatches" (13:19); "sons of the evil one" (13:38, object_type=spiritual-being); "keep them from the evil one" (Joh 17:15); "darts of the evil one" (Eph 6:16); "guard against the evil one" (2Th 3:3); "you have overcome the evil one" (1Jo 2:13–14); "Cain was of the evil one" (3:12); "the evil one does not touch him" (5:18); "the whole world lies in the power of the evil one" (5:19). Evil is **personified** as the adversary — no M10/M10b Hebrew term did this.
- **The evil heart / evil from the heart (strong interior).** "why do you think evil in your **hearts**?" (Mat 9:4); "out of the abundance of the **heart** the mouth speaks… you are evil" (12:34); "the evil person out of his evil **treasure**… out of the good treasure of his **heart**" (12:35; Luk 6:45); "out of the **heart** come **evil thoughts**, murder, adultery…" (Mat 15:19; Mar 7:21) — and "these evil things come from **within**, and they **defile** a person" (Mar 7:23, *koinoō* — an M10c-type defilement link); "an **evil, unbelieving heart**" (Heb 3:12); "hearts sprinkled clean from an **evil conscience**" (Heb 10:22, *suneidesis* + *kardia*); "hostile in **mind**, doing evil deeds" (Col 1:21, *dianoia*). **8 heart/mind/conscience seatings** (*kardia* ×7, *dianoia*, *suneidesis*) — the heart is the source-store of *poneros*.
- **The good/evil antithesis (*agathos* polarity).** Pervasive: sun on evil and good (Mat 5:45); evil men give good gifts (7:11; Luk 11:13); good tree/bad fruit (7:17–18); good/evil treasure (12:35); "abhor what is evil, hold fast to good" (Rom 12:9). *poneros* is consistently defined against *agathos* (cf. *to'evah*/*ratson*).
- **The evil generation:** "an evil and adulterous generation" (Mat 12:39; 16:4; Luk 11:29).
- **Evil deeds / works:** Joh 3:19 (deeds were evil); 7:7; Luk 3:19; 2 Ti 4:18; 2 Jo 11; 3 Jo 10; Col 1:21.
- **The evil eye (stinginess/envy):** "if your eye is **bad**" (Mat 6:23); "do you **begrudge**" (lit. is your eye evil, Mat 20:15).
- **The wicked servant** (parabolic verdict): Mat 18:32; 25:26; Luk 19:22.
- **Evil men purged/delivered-from:** "purge the evil person" (1 Cor 5:13); delivered from "wicked and evil men" (2 Th 3:2); 2 Ti 3:13; Act 17:5.
- **The present evil age / evil day:** Gal 1:4 (+*hamartia*); Eph 5:16; 6:13.

## 3. Touch & seating
- co-seated: *kardia* ×7, *suneidesis* (Heb 10:22), *dianoia* (Col 1:21), *pneuma* (Mat 12:45 "spirits more evil"). 8 interior seatings.
- Touch into M10: *blasfēmia*[R2] ×3 (the heart-evils catalogues, Mat 15:19; Mar 7:22; 1 Ti 6:4), *hamartia*/*hamartanō*/*suneidesis*[CORE], *adikos*/*adikēma*[R2], *ponēria*[M10b]. Plus the constant external *agathos*.

## 4. Held open
*poneros* gathers into one Greek term what Hebrew split: the **verdict** (evil men/servant/generation, like *ra'sha*), the **interior** (evil heart/thoughts/treasure, like *a'ven*), the **good/evil polarity** (like *to'evah*/*ratson*), the **defilement** link (Mar 7:23, like the abomination↔M10c bridge), and the **conscience** (Heb 10:22) — plus the wholly new **personified "evil one."** No reading adopted.
