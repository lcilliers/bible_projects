## Observations (each grounded)

**O1 — The object is an OUTPUT through an organ, not a faculty in operation.**
- *Evidence:* #28 — blasfēmeō `type=action` 11/11, "a deed of the mouth, not a standing status" (A1); faculty `moral_evaluation` term-intrinsic, "minimal interior… reads from co-terms, not term-seating" (A1/A9). #9 — faculty empty on the lemma; inner signal only via co-seated heart (A §sig). #8 — `type=action`, faculty=none, experiencer=other (A).
- *So:* the unit is the **externalised act** (mouth: speech; action/relation: revolt), not the inner faculty itself.

**O2 — For SPEECH, the output is DIAGNOSTIC — it reveals the inner state (a window on the will/heart).**
- *Evidence:* #9 — "out of the **heart** come evil thoughts… **slander**" (Mat 15:19, kardia). #28 — the distinctive inner content is "**the hardened will that reads judgment/the-good through inverted moral categories**" (A4); defiance-under-judgment: "they **cursed** God… and **did not repent**" (Rev 16:9,11,21) (A2).
- *So:* speech-sin describes the inner being by **exposing** it — the mouth manifests the will/heart and its hardened, inverting posture. The object's value is **diagnostic**.

**O3 — INVERSION is the speech-evil's signature — it calls good evil and evil good.**
- *Evidence:* #28 A4 — "deceptive misrepresentation that **inverts moral reality**" — the good "slandered" as evil (Rom 14:16); the innocent labelled kakopoios ("if this man were not **doing evil**…", Joh 18:30 — the wicked label the innocent); blaspheming "the glorious ones" (Jude 8).
- *So:* the speech-output is not merely expressive but **morally inverting** — a distinctive content of this object, not present in the other groups so far.

**O4 — The expression object is a HUB — it branches into the other object-kinds.**
- *Evidence:* → **CONDITION/defilement:** "these dreamers **defile** the flesh… and **blaspheme**" (Jude 8, blasfēmeō + miainō [M10c], A5). → **IDENTITY:** blasfēmos "the blasphemer" (1 Ti 1:13), kakopoios "the evildoer" person-label (1 Pe 2:12 — A7, overlaps #23/#24). → **RECORD/forgiveness:** blasphemy-against-the-Spirit = unforgivable (Mat 12:31, #9); repentance refused (Rev 16, metanoeō). 
- *So:* expression is **connective** — the inner state, externalised, then *defiles* (condition), *labels* (identity), and *is/ isn't forgiven* (record). It links characteristic → condition / identity / record.

**O5 — The output carries a DIRECTION / TARGET (an outward vector).**
- *Evidence:* #9/#28 — God-ward (blasphemy against the name, Rev 13:6), person-ward (slander, Rom 3:8), against-the-good (Rom 14:16), against-glories (Jude 8). #8 — against an authority/ruling hand (yad partner; 1 Ki 12:19 "against the house of David").
- *So:* the expression is always **aimed at an object** — it is relational/directional, not self-contained.

**O6 — #8 POLITICAL REVOLT is the weak member — the evidence does NOT sustain it as a heart-expression.**
- *Evidence:* #8 — faculty=none; **experiencer=other** (reported *of* nations/kings, not a first-person inner state); **no inner-being seating** in the subset; valence inconsistent (neutral on 2 Ki 1:1; 3:5; 3:7). Only **2 Ch 21:10** ("because he had forsaken the Lord") gives any inner/covenant content; the other 8 are bare political fact.
- *So:* unlike speech, #8 does **not** reveal a person's interior — it records a **political-historical act of nations**. It is an **external/relational act**, not an outflow of the heart.

## Candidate label (held open)
**EXPRESSION / OUTFLOW** — the inner state externalised through an organ, describing the inner being **diagnostically** (a window on the will/heart), and acting as a **connective hub** to condition/identity/record. Holds **strongly for speech (#9/#28)**; **doubtfully for political revolt (#8)** — see §G.

## §G — Bias-guard (where I over-reached / where the definition out-runs the evidence)
- **⚠ I over-grouped #8.** My plan filed political revolt with the speech units under "expression/outflow." **The evidence does not support #8 as a heart-expression** (faculty=none, experiencer=other, no seating, mostly bare political fact). **Correction:** #8 is better read as an **EXTERNAL / RELATIONAL ACT** — and it is open whether it is an *inner-being* object at all, or a political-historical one (an external-pole question, cf. the rule that punishment/judgement is the external pole). *This is the over-grouping the discipline was meant to catch — recorded, not buried.*
- **⚠ "the mouth reveals the heart" is strong but not universal.** It rests on the heart-linked texts (Mat 15:19; the "abundance of the heart" frame). **Many blasphemy occurrences are bare vice-list items** (Eph 4:31; Col 3:8) with no heart-link in the verse — do not generalise the diagnostic reading to all 22+ occ; it is the *pattern of the heart-linked subset*.
- **⚠ The hub-branches (O4) are real but thin** — defiles=Jude 8 (one text), unforgivable=Mat 12:31 (one text). Recorded as **seams**, not pervasive structures.
- **The inversion reading (O3)** is well-grounded (Joh 18:30; Rom 14:16; Jude 8) but is specifically the *speech* signature — do not export it to #8.
