**Injustice / unrighteousness (77).** The social-judicial *doing of wrong*, set against righteousness (*mishpat, tsedeq, tsaddiq, yashar* recur). Done in court (Lev 19:15,35), in speech ("injustice on my tongue", Job 6:30; "lips speak no falsehood", Job 27:4), in the hands (Psa 7:3). **Heart-seated:** "in your **hearts** you devise wrongs" (Psa 58:2); "the inward mind and **heart**… are deep" (Psa 64:6); the fool's corrupt **heart** (Psa 53:1). Ezekiel's refrain: the righteous who "turns from righteousness and does injustice" dies (Eze 3:20; 18:24–26; 33:13–18) — injustice as apostasy, touching *shuv*, *cha.ta*, *ma'al*. NT *adikos* (11) all carry faculty=moral_evaluation, contrasted with *dikaios*. *al.vah*'s empty gloss is **resolved**: "the unjust" (Hos 10:9, touching *cha.ta*).

**Transgression-status, Greek (30).** The *crossing/breaking* of law/covenant. *paraptōma* "trespass" is Pauline and tightly paired with **grace and forgiveness** (Rom 5 Adam's trespass; "dead in trespasses", Eph 2:1,5; "not counting their trespasses", 2 Cor 5:19) — the remedy sits right beside it. *parabasis/parabatēs/parabainō* = law-breaking, transgressor, breaking the command (Mat 15:2–3; the law "brings transgression", Rom 4:15). Minimal inner-being content — a juridical/relational status (Col 2:13 "flesh"; 1 Ti 2:14 deception→transgression).

**Perversity (18).** *tah.pu.khah* dominates and is strongly **inner**: "a **perverted heart** devises evil" (Pro 6:14, *lev* co-seated); "your **heart** utters perverse things" (Pro 23:33); plus perverted *speech* (Pro 2:12; 8:13; 10:31–32). *ho.phekh* "turn upside down" touches *yetser* (intention) (Isa 29:16). *te.vel* = sexual "perversion/violation of order" (Lev 18:23; 20:12). *sur* "degenerate/turn away" (Jer 2:21; 17:13).

**Blasphemy (22).** Two senses: blasphemy *against God/the Spirit* (Mat 12:31; Joh 10:33; the beast, Rev 13:1–6) and *slander against people* (vice lists: Mar 7:22; Eph 4:31; Col 3:8). **Heart-seated and decisive:** "out of the **heart** come evil thoughts… **slander**" (Mat 15:19, *kardia* co-seated) — blasphemy/slander proceeds *from the heart*. Paired with *hamartia* ("every sin and blasphemy", Mat 12:31). *blasfēmos* = the blasphemer (1 Ti 1:13 "I was a blasphemer… in unbelief").
