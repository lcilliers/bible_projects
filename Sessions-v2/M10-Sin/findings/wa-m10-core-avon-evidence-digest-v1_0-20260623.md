## 2. Diversity observed (faces — HELD OPEN, not conclusions)

**(a) a.von as CRIME — the act / standing wrong (H5771G, the dominant sub-entry; sense "iniquity/iniquities" + "crime").** The wrong itself, often unbounded and accruing: "the **iniquity** of the Amorites is not yet complete" (Gen 15:16); "an **iniquity** to be punished by the judges" (Job 31:11,28); "for any **crime** … any wrong" (Deu 19:15, sense="crime"); "the **iniquities** of his youth" (Job 13:26); "brought forth in **iniquity** … in sin did my mother conceive me" (Psa 51:5). Object_type for this face is overwhelmingly abstract or none.

**(b) a.von as GUILT — the incurred liability (H5771H; sense "guilt", 28 occ).** The standing being-answerable-for, frequently in a forensic / self-protestation register: "God has found out the **guilt** of your servants" (Gen 44:16); "What is my **guilt**? … my sin" (1Sa 20:1); "if there is **guilt** in me, kill me" (1Sa 20:8; cf. 2Sa 14:32); "On me alone … be the **guilt**" (1Sa 25:24; 2Sa 14:9, taken upon the speaker); "God exacts of you less than your **guilt** deserves" (Job 11:6); "Do not cover their **guilt**" (Neh 4:5). H5771G also carries "guilt" senses in the keeping-oneself-clear idiom: "I kept myself from **guilt**" (2Sa 22:24 = Psa 18:23). (Liability register — recorded.)

**(c) a.von as PUNISHMENT — the penalty borne (H5771I; sense "punishment", 16 occ).** The one word swings to the consequence: "My **punishment** is greater than I can bear" (Gen 4:13); "swept away in the **punishment** of the city" (Gen 19:15); "**punishment** will overtake us" (2Ki 7:9); "the **punishment** of the sword" (Job 19:29); "**punishment** upon **punishment**" (Psa 69:27); the Ezekiel sign-act "place the **punishment** of the house of Israel upon it … bear their **punishment**" (Eze 4:4,5,6). This sense edges toward consequence/external-pole (noted §4). Some neutral-valence sits here (1Sa 28:10).

**Tri-fold note:** the same lemma a.von, split only by sub-entry letter, names the act (G), the liability it creates (H), and the penalty it incurs (I). This three-in-one is the group's distinguishing feature — recorded as diversity, not resolved.
