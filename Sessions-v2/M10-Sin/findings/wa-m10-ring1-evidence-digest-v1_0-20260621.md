## 2. Synergy — how Ring 1 touches the core and the inner being

**Touch with the core (the "live with sin" evidence):** the two largest dynamics are differently bound. *ma'al* (faithlessness) is tightly fused to the core — it co-occurs with *cha.ta*, *chat.tat*, *a.von* 17 times; in the Levitical laws it is literally "commits a breach of faith **and sins**" (Lev 5:15; 6:2; Num 5:6). *a.vah* (distortion) likewise pairs with *cha.ta* (5×) in the confession formula "we have **sinned** and acted **perversely**" (1 Ki 8:47; 2 Ch 6:37). By contrast *ba.gad* (treachery) touches the core only twice; its field is the righteous/wicked and covenant vocabulary (*ra.sha*, *tsad.diq*, *be.rit*, *shamar*) — treachery is documented against a *fidelity* axis more than against the sin-core.

**Touch with inner-being terms (recorded, to weigh later):** *a.vah* → *lev/levav* (heart) and *shuv* (turning/repenting); *sa.rah* → heart, and somatic *choli/davva* (sickness/faint); *deleazō* → *epithumia* (desire), *psuchē* (soul), *pleonexia* (greed); *pu.qah* → heart + conscience; *sa.laph* → the justice field (*mishpat*, *tsadiq*). These are the points where the dynamics actually reach an inner faculty.
