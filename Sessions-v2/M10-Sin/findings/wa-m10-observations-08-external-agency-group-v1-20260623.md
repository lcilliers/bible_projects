## Observations (each grounded)

**O1 — An external-agency object is an EXTERNAL PERSONAL AGENT acting ON the inner being from without — the person is the SITE/victim, not the source.**
- *Evidence:* #24 "**the evil one**" — a personal agent: "deliver us from **the evil one**" (Mat 6:13); "**the evil one snatches**" (Mat 13:19); "**darts of the evil one**" (Eph 6:16); "the whole world lies in the **power of the evil one**" (1 Jo 5:19); "**sons of** the evil one" (Mat 13:38, object_type=spiritual-being). #32 the unclean spirit "**inhabiting, controlling, corrupting** a person **from without**… the mechanism is **external** — an unclean spiritual force **enters and dominates**."
- *So:* the object is **not the person's own interior** — it is an *other* agent; the inner being is the **object** of its action.

**O2 — It PRODUCES inner-being effects — overriding the will, suppressing faculties, imposing a state.**
- *Evidence:* #32 — "**will overridden**, faculties suppressed (speech/hearing, Mar 9:25), **bodily agency lost** (Luk 8:29), communal access severed" — and the result is a **defilement-state** in the person. #24 — the evil one **snatches the word** sown in the heart (Mat 13:19), tempts, assails with "darts".
- *So:* external-agency is relevant to an inner-being study **only through its EFFECTS** — it imposes a condition / captivity / suppressed-faculty state on the inner being (links to #12 enslaving-power and the defilement-states #29–31).

**O3 — It is structurally UNIQUE — the only object-kind where the inner being is the OBJECT of an external will, not the subject.**
- *Evidence:* every other kind describes the person's **own** inner being — its operations (characteristic), states (condition), weights (record), types (identity), outputs (expression), routes (mechanism), undoings (remedy). External-agency describes **another agent's action on** the person.
- *So:* it is, strictly, **not a description of the inner being** — it is a description of **what acts upon it**. (This is what made the recognition read put it outside the characteristics from the start.)

**O4 — It lies on a GRADIENT with the enslaving-power condition (#12) — impersonal-power → personified-sin → personal-agent.**
- *Evidence:* #12 sin "**reigns / dwells / enslaves**", "the **power** of sin" (Rom 5–8; 1 Cor 15:56) — a mastering force *within*; Group 4-O4 already flagged condition↔agency. The "evil one" (#24) and the unclean spirit (#32) are the explicit **personal** agents *without*.
- *So:* there is a gradient — **sin-as-impersonal-power (#12, within) → personified-sin → the evil one / unclean spirit (#24/#32, without)**. External-agency is the **personal end** of that continuum. (Held open whether one continuum or distinct objects.)

**O5 — Its remedy is EXPULSION / DELIVERANCE — not atonement.**
- *Evidence:* #32 — "**expulsion restores inner freedom**"; "**restoration is treated as wholeness** (Luk 6:18)"; the spirits "**resist expulsion** (Act 8:7)" yet "**recognise superior divine authority** (Mar 3:11)".
- *So:* unlike the sin-objects (discharged by **atonement**, Group 6), the agency object is removed by a **power-confrontation / deliverance** restoring wholeness — a **distinct remedy-mode** (deliverance vs atonement).

## Candidate label (held open)
**EXTERNAL AGENCY** — an **external personal agent** (the evil one / the unclean spirit) that **acts on, invades, or controls** the inner being from without; the person is the **site/victim, not the source**. Structurally unique (the inner being is the *object*, not the subject); present in the study **only by its inner-being effects** (will overridden, faculties suppressed, a state/captivity imposed); on a **gradient** with the enslaving-power condition (#12); removed by **expulsion/deliverance**, not atonement.

## §G — Bias-guard
- **⚠ This is, strictly, NOT an inner-being object — it is an external agent.** It belongs in an inner-being study **only by its effects**, as a **cross-reference to the spiritual-being interface**, not as a description of the inner being itself. *Flag: external-agency may sit outside the inner-being object-set proper* (→ SDX-07; cf. the external-pole question SDX-08).
- **⚠ The "evil one" is UNIQUE to the Greek/NT (~11 occ); no Hebrew M10 analogue.** The personal-cosmic-agent reading is **NT-specific** — do not generalise it to the Hebrew sin material.
- **⚠ #32 is a small, bounded corpus** (the unclean-spirit subset of akathartos, the Gospel exorcism narratives + Acts/Rev). The agency claims rest on those narratives — grounded but narrow.
- **⚠ Do not collapse #12 into external-agency (O4).** Sin-as-power (#12) is the **person's own captivity to an internal-but-agent-like force**; external-agency is an **invading personal agent**. Related on a gradient, but distinct — keep them apart.
