## 1. The principle (your direction)

Sin is **not** treated as an inner-being characteristic. Most of these lemmas *name the status or category* of the wrong — they describe the **status of the inner being** (sinful / guilty / in transgression), not an operation of it. A minority describe a **dynamic operating in or through the inner being** (a disposition, a turning, an enticement). The two groups are investigated differently:

- **Group B (Sin & related sin terms)** → studied as a **status system** — how the inner being comes to be in this status, bears it, and is released from it (incl. the cultic remedy).
- **Group A (Inner-being-relevant)** → studied as **operations/dispositions** — how each works in or through the inner being (these are the candidates "to be investigated", per your note).
