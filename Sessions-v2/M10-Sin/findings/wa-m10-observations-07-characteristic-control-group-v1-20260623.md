## Observations (each grounded)

**O1 — A characteristic is a FACULTY / DISPOSITION IN OPERATION — the inner being actively *doing* or *being-bent*, not a standing weight/type/state.**
- *Evidence:* #27 "an **active inner process** — devised, conceived, schemed… born into deeds" (type **action 18**/status 65); #21 "the inner **state or disposition** of moral distortion… **inverting** the moral order from within"; #20 "the inner **disposition** of deliberate unfaithfulness… the will that **breaks** covenant loyalty."
- *So:* unlike a condition (a standing mode), a record (a weight), or an identity (a type), a characteristic is the **faculty in motion** — conceiving, twisting, betraying.

**O2 — It is the MOST SEATED — the highest genuine interior density (the cleanest discriminator).**
- *Evidence:* #27 — **interior 13/83 (~16%)**, the highest of any M10b batch (vs ra'sha-identity ~2%, abomination ~6%); heart/mind seats throughout ("evil in their **hearts**" Psa 28:3; "his **heart gathers** iniquity" Psa 41:6; "a **heart that devises** wicked plans" Pro 6:18). #21 — perverted **heart** (Pro 6:14), depraved **mind** (1 Ti 6:5).
- *So:* the characteristics carry **far more genuine inner-being seating** than the status/identity/record kinds (whose interior was *bounded ~5 seats*). **Seatedness is the data-level signature of a characteristic** — the faculty is actually engaged.

**O3 — It has a LIFECYCLE / operation — it *does something*, over time.**
- *Evidence:* #27 the **generative arc** — evil "**conceived** → **pregnant** → gives **birth**" across three corpora (Psa 7:14; Job 15:35; Isa 59:4); "**devised → cherished → chosen → worked out → uttered**". #21 the twisting/inverting motion; #20 the act of betraying/breaking-faith.
- *So:* a characteristic has an **operation** (a verb-life), unlike the static kinds (status/record/identity *stand*; a characteristic *runs*).

**O4 — It is the GENERATIVE inner SOURCE from which the other object-kinds derive (a structural hypothesis).**
- *Evidence:* #27 — a'ven is "**devised in the heart… born into deeds**"; the heart a "**treasury**" the act draws on (#24, Mat 12:35). The inner process **produces** the outward act (→ EXPRESSION), **incurs** the weight (→ RECORD), **hardens** into the type (→ IDENTITY), **settles** into the mode (→ CONDITION).
- *So (hypothesis):* the **characteristic is the generative centre**; status / record / identity / expression are **how it is described or registered around it**. This would reorganise the whole object-kind picture with the characteristic as the operative core and the others as its modes/derivatives. (Held open — see §G.)

**O5 — The control contrasts (what makes it NOT the other kinds):**
| vs | Characteristic (#27/#21/#20) | The other kind |
|---|---|---|
| CONDITION (#11/#24) | the faculty **active** (conceiving) | a standing **mode** (being-under) |

- *So:* the characteristic is the **operative inner core**; the seven others describe the inner being's **states, weights, types, outputs, routes, and invaders** *around* that core.

**O6 — The NT RESOLUTION operates at the CHARACTERISTIC level — by TRANSFORMATION, not (only) forgiveness (scholarship-informed; held open).**
- *Evidence (from the Logos weave):* the NT resolution is "the Spirit **transforming life from within**… a **new creature**"; the new-covenant "**Law a motive force within personality**" (Jer 31/Ez 36); death-with-Christ to sin (White). This targets the inner **disposition/faculty** — i.e. the characteristic level.
- *So (the missing piece for Group 7):* there are **two resolution-modes** — the **record** is discharged (atonement → forgiven; Group 6), but the **characteristic** is **transformed/renewed** (made new). The trio maps to its renewal: faithlessness #20 → **faith** (M31); perversion #21 → **renewal of the mind** (M45/M15); scheming-evil #27 → the **new heart** (M45). **This is *why* Group 7 was incomplete without the resolution context** — a characteristic's full description includes its *transformability*, not just its sin-face. The record gets *forgiven*; the characteristic gets *changed*.
