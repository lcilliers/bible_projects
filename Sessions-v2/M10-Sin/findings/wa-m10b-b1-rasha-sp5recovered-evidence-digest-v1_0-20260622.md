## 2. Why the recovery mattered — it rebalances, it doesn't just extend
Had synthesis run on the truncated 179, three things would have been wrong:

**(a) "Face 3" (the fixed two-destinies antithesis) was under-weighted — now corrected.** The recovery restores the heart of the antithesis material: most of **Psalm 37** (the two-ways psalm — "the children of the wicked shall be cut off"; "the future of the wicked shall be cut off"; 37:28,32,34,35,38,40) and the **Proverbs 24/28/29** antithesis ("the wicked flee when no one pursues, but the righteous are bold as a lion", 28:1; "when the wicked rule, the people groan", 29:2; "when the wicked increase, transgression increases", 29:16). The *tsaddiq/ra'sha* axis is far more dominant than the 179 showed.

**(b) A dimension that was almost entirely absent now appears — theodicy / the wisdom-crisis.** The recovered verses carry the *problem* of the wicked, not just their verdict:
- **Prosperity of the wicked:** "I was **envious** of the arrogant when I saw the **prosperity of the wicked**… always at ease, they increase in riches" (Psa 73:3,12).
- **Ecclesiastes' empirical inversion** (all 6 occurrences are new): the order is affirmed in principle — "God will judge the righteous and the wicked" (3:17); "it will not be well with the wicked… because he does not fear God" (8:13) — yet observed to fail — "a righteous man perishes… a wicked man prolongs his life" (7:15); "the same event happens to the righteous and the wicked" (8:14; 9:2). This *wisdom-crisis* face is a candidate addition to the four, recorded for later.

**(c) New nuances:**
- **Congenital wickedness:** "the wicked are **estranged from the womb**; they go astray **from birth**, speaking lies" (Psa 58:3) — wickedness as an innate condition, not only an enacted one.
- **Cognitive deficiency:** "a wicked man does **not understand** knowledge" (Pro 29:7, *bin*/*da'at*/*yada*) — echoing Dan 12:10.

## 3. What the recovery did NOT add — the wicked's interior
co-seated in the recovered set: *nephesh* ×1 only (Psa 97:10 ="life"; 104:35 is the psalmist's own soul). The recovered material is dominated by **fate, antithesis, and theodicy**, not the wicked's inner faculties. This **confirms** that "face 2" (the characterised interior — heart-denial, soul-desire, pride, concealed evil) is concentrated in the already-read material (Psa 10, 28, 36; Pro mouth/heart/appetite), not spread evenly. A useful boundary, recorded.
