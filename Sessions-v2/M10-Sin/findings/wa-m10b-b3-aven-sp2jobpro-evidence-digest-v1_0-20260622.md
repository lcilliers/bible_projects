## 1. Coverage & sense
*a'ven* in Job (12) + Proverbs (7) + 1 Sa (1) = **20/69**, all read. Senses: iniquity 9, wicked 5, evildoers 4, evil 1, wrong 1 — moral-evil throughout; faculty=[affect, moral_evaluation] uniform (continues).

## 2. The generative metaphor — confirmed and strongest here
SP1's "conceive→birth" image (Psa 7:14) recurs robustly:
- "those who **plow** iniquity and **sow** trouble **reap** the same" (Job 4:8, *a'mal*, *charash*).
- "they **conceive** trouble and give **birth** to evil, and their **womb** prepares deceit" (Job 15:35, *a'mal*, *mir'mah*).
*a'ven* is consistently **gestated and brought forth** — an evil that is generated, not merely a static verdict. Across SP1–SP2 this is *a'ven*'s most distinctive signature.

## 3. The heart devises it (interior, continues)
"a **heart that devises** wicked plans" (Pro 6:18, *lev* co-seated, *machashavah*, *charash*) — part of the seven abominations of Pro 6. The interior-devising of *a'ven* holds.

## 4. NEW — a volitional dimension (turn from / choose)
*a'ven* is something the will engages:
- "if iniquity is in your **hand**, **put it far away**" (Job 11:14, *av'lah*).
- "[God] commands that they **return from** iniquity" (Job 36:10).
- "do not **turn to** iniquity, for **this you have chosen** rather than affliction" (Job 36:21, *o'ni* affliction, *bachar* choose) — iniquity vs affliction as an object of choice.
So *a'ven* can be **chosen, turned to, turned from, put away** — a volitional/agential dimension beyond the verdict.

## 5. Judged deeds, and an idolatry equivalence
- The "**workers of iniquity**" meet calamity/disaster (Job 31:3, *av'val*); God "sees" and "stores up" iniquity (11:11; 21:19); "the mouth of the wicked **devours iniquity**" (Pro 19:28, *ra'sha*); evildoers meet destruction/terror at justice (Pro 10:29; 21:15).
- **1 Sa 15:23** — "presumption is as **iniquity** and idolatry" (*a'ven* with *teraphim*; *chat'tat*, *meri* rebellion): Samuel equates rebellion/presumption with iniquity and idolatry.
- Denial: the adulteress "I have done no **wrong**" (Pro 30:20).

## 6. Touch & seating
- co-seated *lev* (Pro 6:18). Touch: *chat'tat*[CORE], *av'lah*/*av'val*[R2], *re'sha*/*ra'sha*[M10b] — light, as in SP1; *a'ven* travels mostly with external *a'mal*/*mir'mah*/*she'qer*.

## 7. Held open
*a'ven* now shows a coherent profile distinct from the verdict-families: an **affect-bearing evil that is conceived/devised in the heart, chosen or turned from, and worked out** as deeds/speech. Whether Isaiah/Prophets (SP3) — where "Beth-aven" and the idol/vanity sense may appear — sustains or complicates this is next. No reading adopted.
