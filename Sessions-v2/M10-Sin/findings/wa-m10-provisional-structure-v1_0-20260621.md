## 2. The headline: M10 is not shaped like an affect cluster

Three measures from the full aggregate, all pointing the same way:

| Measure | Result | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| `type` | status **933** · action **443** · quality **84** | The cluster is built of *conditions/states* and *acts*, with a thin *character* band — not affective states. |
| `faculty` | **absent on 1,022 / 1,462 (70%)**; present only as `moral_evaluation` 247 and `volition` 191 | Most M10 terms do not, at term level, engage an inner-being faculty. Iniquity, transgression, guilt, treachery, injustice, perversion, corruption, blasphemy are all faculty-absent. |
| `valence` | sinful 1,288 · neutral 98 · commanded 44 · forbidden 13 · righteous 3 | A clean minority band is *not* sinful — it sits on the cultic-remedy terms (sin offering, guilt offering, atonement). |

**Consequence.** Your steer is borne out by the data: sin does not naturally decompose into inner-being *characteristics* the way Fear or Joy did. It decomposes into **moral-legal statuses and acts**, organised by **semantic word-family**, with a relational profile (against God / against persons) and a distinct **remedy** band. The closest fit to the evidence is a *combination* model — a matrix of word-family × ontological kind × valence — which I read as your "series of statuses or qa combination." This is raised as a fork in §5, not assumed.

## 4. Two cross-cutting axes the detail phase will need

Beyond the family grouping, two axes run *through* the families and may matter more than the families themselves for the programme's governing question (the inner being):

- **Ontological kind (the `type` axis):** ACT (the doing — 443), STATE/CONDITION (the standing one is in — 933), CHARACTER (the person so marked — 84). The same root often supplies all three (e.g. *ḥāṭāʾ*: to sin / sin / sinner).
- **Valence axis:** WRONG (sinful, 1,288) vs PRESCRIBED-REMEDY (neutral/commanded/righteous, ~145). The remedy band is functionally the inverse of the cluster and may warrant separation.

A third, weaker axis is **relational direction** (object_type God 117 / person 117 / abstract 344): sin *against God* vs *against persons* vs *as abstract condition* — present but a minority signal at term level.
