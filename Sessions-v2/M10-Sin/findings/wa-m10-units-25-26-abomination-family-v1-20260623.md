### A1. Structural signature — a verdict of detestableness, not an inner movement (B2 audit §2; Bsum §3)
- **type = status 139 / 140** — the one exception (Hos 9:10 "**became** detestable") is *quality*, a transformation (see A6). The family is a **verdict of detestableness**, not an inner faculty-act.
- **faculty = moral_evaluation** is the term-intrinsic field (present on 108/140; the *shiqquts*/*bdelugma* idol-records often carry no faculty — they name **objects**, not evaluations; recorded, not an error — B2 audit §7).
- **⚑ SYNTH — the verdict is fixed by what GOD detests (object_type=God dominant).** object_type = **God 21** + the pervasive "to the Lord / in my sight" frame; the rest are abstract practices (25), idols/things (15), spiritual-beings (3 — the foreign gods *are* "the abomination of" their nations), situations (7), persons (7). Abomination is fixed not by an internal faculty but by **the Lord's reaction to it** (Law §3): hatred ("every abominable thing the Lord **hates**", Deu 12:31, *sa.ne*; Jer 44:4), jealousy/anger (Deu 32:16, *qana*/*ka.as*), the required posture "you shall utterly **detest and abhor** it" (Deu 7:26, *ta.av*). So abomination is a **relational status** — what is detestable *to* God and to be detested *by* his people. **Surface as the defining structural axis.**

### A2. The FACES of to.e.vah (held open — corpus-borne facets, NOT a developmental line) ⚑ SYNTH
Across 108 occurrences *to.e.vah* is one verdict of detestableness worn in distinct ways by corpus (B2 audit §6; Bsum §4; SP4 §3) — **no single reading adopted**:
1. **Law** — a cultic/conduct verdict on **acts and objects**, **no interior**: sexual transgression (Lev 18:22; 20:13; Deu 24:4), idolatry/images (Deu 7:25–26; 27:15), forbidden cult (divination, child sacrifice — Deu 18:9–12; blemished sacrifice Deu 17:1; prostitute's fee Deu 23:18), dishonesty (false weights, Deu 25:16, *a.vel*), disordering of kinds (cross-dressing, Deu 22:5), the nations' land-defiling practices (Lev 18:26–30).
2. **Wisdom (the heart-engaged face)** — abomination **enters the interior** and runs on a **polarity** (see A3): crooked/arrogant heart, wicked thoughts, the deceiver's "seven abominations in his heart" (Pro 26:25).
3. **Ezekiel** — the prophetic **catalogue/lawsuit** ("make known to Jerusalem her abominations", 16:2; 22:2; 20:4; the temple tour of escalating "greater abominations", 8:6,9,13,15,17): idolatry, harlotry, bloodshed, child sacrifice, adultery/incest, oppression/usury; the **heart goes after** it (see A4); the **death-line** of the two ways (Eze 18:12–13,24 — "commits abomination… he shall surely die"); to be **turned from/removed** (14:6 *shuv*; 11:18 *sur*); triggers **self-loathing/shame/grief** (see A5).
4. **Histories/Prophets** — the **idol itself** as abomination ("an abomination is he who chooses [the idol]", Isa 41:24; 44:19); the kings' **pagan imitation** ("did according to the abominations of the nations", 1 Ki 14:24; 2 Ki 16:3; 21:2,11; 2 Ch 28:3; 33:2; 36:8 — centrally child sacrifice; reform = removal, 2 Ch 34:33 *sur*); **shamelessness** (A5); **faithlessness/treachery** (Mal 2:11, *ba.gad* [R1]); hypocritical cult rejected (Isa 1:13, *a.ven*).
- **Constant across all faces:** relational to **God's revulsion**, and the **defilement (M10c) bridge** (A7). ⚑ SYNTH — the B1→B2→B3 *verdict → detestableness → interior* gradient does **not** survive B4 (Bsum §4); the faces are **co-present facets**, not a developmental sequence.

### A3. The abomination / delight POLARITY (the Wisdom face) ⚑ SYNTH — feeds #25
- Four times *to.e.vah* is set against *ratson* (delight / acceptance / favour): "A false balance is an abomination… a just weight is his **delight**" (Pro 11:1); crooked heart vs blameless = abomination vs delight (11:20); lying lips vs faithful = abomination vs delight (12:22); the wicked's sacrifice vs the upright's prayer (15:8). Abomination is the **negative pole of God's regard** — the structural opposite of what he accepts (SP2 §3).
- **Reciprocity / relative valence** — what one detests tracks one's moral orientation: "to turn away from evil is an abomination **to fools**" (Pro 13:19); "the scoffer is an abomination **to mankind**" (24:9); the mutual revulsion of Pro 29:27 ("an unjust man is an abomination **to the righteous**… one whose way is straight is an abomination **to the wicked**"); Wisdom's own posture "wickedness is an abomination **to my lips**" (8:7). (SP2 §4.)

### A4. The interior — abomination engages the heart (real but corpus-bounded) ⚑ SYNTH — feeds #25/#26
- **The heart is the abomination** (Wisdom): "those of **crooked heart**" (Pro 11:20, *lev* co-seated); "everyone who is **arrogant in heart**" (16:5, *lev*); "seven abominations in his **heart**" (26:25, location=heart); "the **thoughts of the wicked**" (15:26, *machashavah*); the Pro 6:16–19 "seven" list includes "a **heart that devises** wicked plans" (*nephesh*="himself").
- **The heart goes after them** (Ezekiel — the **idolatrous** interior, feeds #26): "broken over their **whoring heart** that has departed from me… all their abominations" (6:9, location=heart); "those whose **heart goes after** their detestable things and their abominations" (11:21, location=heart); "uncircumcised in **heart** and **flesh**… profaning my temple" (44:7, *ba.sar* co-seated); "the **eyes** feast on" them (20:7).
- **The soul delights in them** (idolatrous, feeds #26): "their **soul** delights in their abominations" (Isa 66:3, *nephesh* + *chaphets*).
- **The idol-maker's deadened mind** (idolatrous, feeds #26): falls down before an abomination with no "knowledge or discernment" — "none considers in his **heart**" (Isa 44:19, *lev* co-seated, *te.vunah*/*da.at*).
- **Values inversion:** "what is exalted among men is an **abomination** in the sight of God… God knows your **hearts**" (Luk 16:15, *kardia*).
- **~9 heart engagements total**, concentrated in Wisdom + Ezekiel + Luk 16:15 (SP4 §4; B2 audit §4). co-seated tally (B2 audit §7): *lev* 3, *nephesh* 3, *kardia* 1, *ba.sar* 1 = 8.

### A5. The shame ↔ shamelessness axis ⚑ SYNTH — the conscience face (feeds #25)
- **Self-loathing in the penitent:** upon remembering, "you will **loathe yourselves** for your iniquities and your abominations" (Eze 36:31; 6:9); "they shall bear their **shame** and the abominations they committed" (44:13, *kelimmah*); the faithful remnant "**sigh and groan** over all the abominations" (9:4, *a.nach*).
- **Shamelessness in the hardened (the inverse):** "Were they **ashamed** when they committed abomination? No… they did not know how to **blush**" (Jer 6:15; 8:12, *bosh*/*kalam*) — the abominators' conscience is seared. **The same act produces shame in the penitent and none in the hardened** (SP4 §2; B2 audit §4) — the conscience-violated face of #25.

### A6. "Becoming what you love" — the one quality-typed occurrence ⚑ SYNTH — feeds #26
- **Hos 9:10:** "they **became detestable like the thing they loved**" — the sole *quality*-typed (not status) occurrence; **idolatry assimilates the worshipper to the detestable object** (B2 audit §4). A distinct mechanism — the idolater takes on the character of the idol; surface for the #26 synthesis.

### A7. ↔ BOND — the M10c defilement bridge (THE dominant structural finding) ↔ BOND
- ↔ **BOND — STRONG M10c-defilement bridge (the single strongest cross-satellite link in M10b — ~23 touches; B2 audit §3; Bsum §3).** The abomination-family is interwoven with **defilement** more tightly than with anything else: *ta.me* ×15, *nid.dah* ×3, *tum.ah* ×1, plus the purity contrasts *ta.her*/*ta.hor* ×3 and *akathartos* ×1. **Abominations DEFILE the sanctuary, the land, the self** — Eze 5:11 ("you have defiled my sanctuary with all your detestable things", *shiqquts*+*ta.me*); 20:7; 37:23; Jer 7:30; 32:34; 2 Ki 23:13; 2 Ch 36:14; Ezr 9:11; Mal 2:11 (Judah "profaned the sanctuary"). *to.e.vah* and *shiqquts* travel together ("their detestable things and their abominations") and both defile. Plus NT: *poneros* defiles (Mar 7:23, *koinoō*), *blasfēmeō* defiles (Jude 8, *miainō*). **This bridges directly to the defilement units #29 (ritual) / #31 (corporate/covenantal) — abominations are the moral content that the defilement units register as contamination of sanctuary/land.** Hold for synergy.

### A8. The abomination of desolation + Babylon (the eschatological face)
- The eschatological **desecration of the holy place**: *shiqquts* (Dan 9:27; 11:31; 12:11) → *bdelugma* (Mat 24:15; Mar 13:14) — "the abomination of desolation"; and Babylon's abominations (Rev 17:4–5; 21:27). *bdelugma* is the NT carrier of the OT *to.e.vah*/*shiqquts* verdict (B2 audit §5).

### A9. Touch points — what the abomination-family reaches (B2 audit §5; SP touch sections)
- **Sin-core (15 touches):** *cha.ta*/*chat.tat*/*a.von*/*pe.sha* — "so you **sin** against the Lord" (Deu 20:18; Deu 24:4); abomination is sin.
- **Within the wickedness field:** *ra.sha*[#23] ×6 — the wicked's sacrifice/way/thoughts are the abomination (a strong *to.e.vah*↔*ra.sha* bridge; Pro 15:8,9; 17:15; 21:27); *a.ven*/*a.vel*[injustice, R2] ×4.
- **Rings/reform:** *sur* (remove/put away) ×6 (Jer 4:1; 2 Ch 15:8; 2 Ki 23:24; Eze 11:18); *shuv* (turn/repent, Eze 14:6); *ma.al*/*ba.gad*[R1] ×5; *mash.chit*[R3] ×2.
- **Desire:** "you shall not **covet** the silver or gold [of the idol]… lest you be **ensnared**" (Deu 7:25, *chamad*) — the abominable object draws desire (Law §4); ↔ M28.
