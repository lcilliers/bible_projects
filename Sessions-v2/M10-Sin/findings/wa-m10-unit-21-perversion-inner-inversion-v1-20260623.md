## A. General / non-verse observations (copied from the source digests)

**Definition (DB):** the inner state/disposition of moral distortion — twisting what is right, corrupting what was good, inverting the moral order from within. Sexual-perversion and moral-corruption registers. Cross-flags **M03, M07, M23/M35**.

### A1. From the R1 digest (the Hebrew bend/twist dynamics)

- **a.vah (16 occ)** — lexicon "**to bend, twist, distort**" (R1 §1 table). **CORE ×6** (cha.ta ×5) — pairs with cha.ta in the confession formula "we have **sinned** and acted **perversely**" (1 Ki 8:47; 2 Ch 6:37, R1 §2). Inner-being touches: *lev/levav* (heart) ×3, *shuv* (turn/repent) ×4.
  - **Genuine inner-being seat (R1 §3):** "if they turn their **heart**… we have sinned and acted perversely" (1 Ki 8:47; 2 Ch 6:37); "a man of perverse **heart** / twisted mind" (Pro 12:8); "take it to **heart**" (2 Sa 19:19). a.vah's distortion is **heart-seated** where the verse marks it. *(Note: the corpus splits a.vah into H5753A "to twist" and H5753B "to pervert/commit iniquity"; R1 records the lemma as one — both sub-entries belong here.)*
- **sa.laph (6 occ)** — lexicon "**to twist, pervert, overturn**" (R1 §1 table). CORE ×1 (chat.tat); field = the **justice axis** (*mish.pat* justice, *tsad.diq* righteous) (R1 §1, §2).
  - **Genuine inner-being seat (R1 §3):** "his **heart** rages against the LORD" (Pro 19:3). sa.laph's perverting reaches the heart and the **justice it overturns** (it "subverts/overthrows" justice, the cause of the righteous).

### A2. From the R2p digest (the perversity band, 18 occ — R2p §1 "Perversity")

- **tah.pu.khah dominates and is strongly INNER:** "a **perverted heart** devises evil" (**Pro 6:14**, *lev* co-seated); "your **heart** utters perverse things" (**Pro 23:33**); plus perverted **speech** (Pro 2:12; 8:13; 10:31–32). **This is the cleanest heart-seated perversion evidence in the unit.**
- **ho.phekh** "turn upside down" touches *yetser* (intention) — "shall the thing made say… you have no understanding?" the potter/clay **inversion** (Isa 29:16). The moral order *turned over*.
- **te.vel** = sexual "**perversion / violation of order**" (Lev 18:23; 20:12) — a *physical/sexual* inversion of created order (the sexual-perversion register named in the DB definition).
- **sur** "degenerate / turn away" (Jer 2:21; 17:13) — turning from the planted "true vine" to a "degenerate" one.
- **R2p audit (§2):** of the 207-occ ring, location 19/207 with **8 genuine HEART-seatings**, of which Pro 6:14 and Pro 23:33 are this unit's — "the cleanest inner-being evidence in Ring 2." faculty near-absent on the Hebrew aspect-terms (the signal carried by co-occurrence, not the `faculty` field).

### A4. Structural signature (this unit, composite)

The unit is a **two-language inversion family**: a Hebrew **bend/twist/overturn** group (status + action; heart-seated where marked — a.vah, sa.laph, tah.pu.khah, ho.phekh) and a Greek **corrupt/deprave** group operating on the **mind/desire/self** (ftheirō, fthora, diaftheirō). The shared idea is **moral order inverted from within** — what is right twisted, what was good corrupted. faculty is intrinsically thin on the lemmas; the inner-being signal is carried by the **heart** (tah.pu.khah, a.vah, sa.laph) and the **mind/desire/flesh/old-self** (the Greek group). Type splits status (perversity-state) vs action (the act of perverting/corrupting).
