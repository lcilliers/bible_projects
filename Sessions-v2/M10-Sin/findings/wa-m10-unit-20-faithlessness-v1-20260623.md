## A. General / non-verse observations (copied from the source digests)

**Definition (DB):** The inner disposition of deliberate unfaithfulness — breaks covenantal loyalty, betrays a trust, acts treacherously against God or persons. Cross-register flag: **M13 + M31**.

**From the R1 digest (§1 scope, §2 synergy, §3 hygiene) — ma'al (62 occ) + ba.gad (39 occ):**

**ma'al fuses to the sin-core (the tightest Ring 1 bond):**
- ma'al co-occurs with the core (*cha.ta*, *chat.tat*, *a.von*) **17 times** — cha.ta ×8, chat.tat ×5, a.von ×4 (R1 §1 table). It is the most core-bound qualifier in Ring 1.
- In the Levitical breach-of-faith laws it is literally **"commits a breach of faith AND sins"** — Lev 5:15; 6:2; Num 5:6 (R1 §2). Faithlessness and sin are named in the same breath.
- Heart-seated: "his **heart** was lifted up… he was unfaithful" (2 Ch 26:16, R1 §3 genuine seat).

**ba.gad sits on the fidelity / righteousness axis (not the sin-core):**
- ba.gad touches the core only **twice**; its field is the **righteous/wicked and covenant vocabulary** — *ra'sha* (wicked), *tsad'diq* (righteous), *be'rit* (covenant), *shamar* (keep) (R1 §1 table + §2). Treachery is documented **against a fidelity axis** more than against the sin-core.
- **stem evidence** — ba.gad's treachery is read through its stem behaviour and the righteous/wicked field it keeps, not via core co-occurrence.

**Structural signature:** these are **action**-typed dynamics (the act of betraying); faculty is largely absent on the lemma (the inner-being signal carried via the heart co-term where present); experiencer spans God-ward and person-ward betrayal; object_type = person/situation (the party betrayed).

**Field hygiene (from R1 §3, carry-forward list):**
- **⚠ HYGIENE — nephesh-as-person artefact:** ma'al "soul" ×6 is the **nephesh legal idiom** ("if **anyone** (nephesh) commits a breach of faith", Lev 5:15; 6:2; Num 5:6) — NOT an inner-being seat. Do not read these as soul-seatings (surface; flagged, no DB change proposed).
- **⚠ HYGIENE — borderline nephesh=appetite:** ba.gad "soul" — "the **desire** (nephesh) of the treacherous is for violence" (Pro 13:2); "his **greed** (nephesh) is as wide as Sheol" (Hab 2:5) — nephesh = appetite/craving; read on synthesis.
- **⚠ HYGIENE — spurious origin:** ba.gad Pro 2:22 and ma'al 2 Ch 26:18 tag origin=received-from-outside with no external-source clause (surface; flagged).

**Flags requiring deeper synthesis (surfaced, NOT resolved):**
- **⚖ BORDER — home call (the faithfulness pole):** the **faithfulness POLE lives in M13 / M31**; this unit is the **faithlessness mirror**. Whether faithlessness is best homed here (M10 sin) or as the negative aspect of the M13/M31 faithfulness characteristic is a **home call** for synthesis.
- **⚑ SYNTH — two bond profiles, one unit:** ma'al is core-fused (sin) while ba.gad is fidelity-axis (covenant) — two distinct co-occurrence signatures gathered into one "faithlessness" unit. Synthesis should test whether these are one disposition or two (treachery-against-trust vs breach-of-sacred-faith).
- **⚑ SYNTH — covenant-breaking as the inner act:** the Levitical "breach of faith AND sins" pairing positions faithlessness as the *inner* defection that *generates* the sin-act — worth synthesising the disposition→act link.

**↔ BOND (cross-cluster):** **M13** + **M31** (the faithfulness pole this mirrors — home call), **M26** (righteousness/wickedness — the ra'sha/tsad'diq/be'rit axis ba.gad keeps), **M41** (Remembrance — covenant remembered/forsaken), **M30**, **M23**.
