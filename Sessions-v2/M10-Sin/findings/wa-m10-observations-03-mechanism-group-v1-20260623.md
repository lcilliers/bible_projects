## Observations (each grounded)

**O1 — A "mechanism" is a ROUTE / HOW, not a state or a faculty.**
- *Evidence:* the DB definition — "sin operating **through a specific inner mechanism**"; the three lemmas are each a *how*: deleazō = how desire is exploited (entice/lure), apostasia = how covenant-identity is abandoned (turning-away), sunupokrinomai = how identity is falsified (joining in hypocrisy). type is mixed (action/status) — the unifier is **process**, not a shared state.
- *So:* the object is a **HOW** (a route/process by which sin enters or operates), not a WHAT (condition) or a faculty.

**O2 — The clearest member (deleazō) is an EXTERNAL trigger on an INTERNAL appetite — the enticement→desire→sin chain.**
- *Evidence:* Jam 1:14 "each person is tempted when he is **lured and enticed by his own desire**" (deleazō + epithumia + peirazō); 2 Pe 2:14 "**entice** unsteady souls… hearts trained in greed" (deleazō + pleonexia + psuchē, co-seated soul); 2 Pe 2:18 "**entice by passions of the flesh**" (deleazō + epithumia + aselgeia). deleazō touches **CORE ×0** — its field is the **appetites** (epithumia/pleonexia/aselgeia, all M28) and the **seat** (soul/flesh, M47), never the sin-core.
- *So:* this mechanism = a **lure working *on* an existing appetite**, issuing in sin — a process-chain (external trigger → internal desire → sin). It describes *how sin gets in* through the inner being's appetites.

**O3 — The mechanism object POINTS OUTWARD to a home cluster — it is connective/borrowed.**
- *Evidence:* deleazō's whole field is **M28 (desire) / M14 (temptation)**; sunupokrinomai is intrinsically the hypocrisy noun (hupokrisis, **M14/M08**); apostasia is covenant-abandonment (**M31**). All touch CORE ×0 (or none recorded). The DB definition itself cross-flags each (M14 / M08 / M31).
- *So:* these are **barely "M10"** — they belong to the desire/temptation/hypocrisy/faithlessness clusters and appear under M10 only because each is *a mechanism of sin*. Like expression (Group 2), the object is **connective** — but it points to the **source/trigger** clusters, not (as expression did) to the output.

**O4 — The unit is NOT one object — three unrelated routes, no shared substrate.**
- *Evidence:* 3 lemmas / 5 verses / CORE ×0 / no shared terms; enticement-of-desire, covenant-abandonment, and hypocrisy are three different routes.
- *So:* "specialised sinful mechanisms" is a **grab-bag**, not a coherent object. ⚖ BORDER — it most likely **dissolves into its home clusters** (M14/M08/M31), with M10 keeping at most a cross-reference.

## Candidate label (held open)
**MECHANISM / ROUTE** — a *how*: a process/route by which sin **enters or operates** through the inner being, typically an **external trigger acting on an internal faculty/appetite** (deleazō→desire). It is **connective**, pointing to a **source cluster** (desire/temptation/hypocrisy). In M10 it is thin, borrowed, and heterogeneous.

## §G — Bias-guard (thin evidence)
- **⚠ This is 5 verses.** Any "object-kind" claim is **thinly grounded** — deleazō (3 occ) carries almost all of it; apostasia and sunupokrinomai are **1 occ each**. Do not promote "mechanism" to a major object-kind on this alone.
- **⚠ The enticement→desire→sin chain (O2) is real but lives outside M10's core** — Jam 1:14 + 2 Peter are M28/M14/M35 texts. So "mechanism" may **not be an M10 object at all** — it is M10's *window* onto the desire→sin pathway, whose home is elsewhere.
- **⚠ apostasia's `origin=received-from-outside` (Act 21:21)** is flagged-not-confirmed in R1 — do not lean on it as evidence of an external-induction mechanism.
