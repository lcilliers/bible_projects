## 1. Identity
Fear named by its **bodily realisation** — the body shaken, the visible crossing of the inner state (T1.1.1). The *ragaz*/*charad* family: H7264 *ra.gaz*, H2729 *cha.rad*, H7460 *ra.ad*, H6426 *pa.lats* "to shudder" + Greek **G5156 *tromos*, G1790 *entromos*** (T1.1.2). An **act** (the body shaking — action 83), with a strong **causative Hiphil** (25) alongside Qal and Hithpael — operates both as something done and something *made* to happen (T1.2.1/T1.4.1). Its name is **constitutional (bodily)** (T1.1.3). A **constitutional compound** — couples an inner state with bodily expression (T1.2.2). It is the characteristic whose mode **varies by constitutional level** — as deep as the spirit, through soul/heart, out to the body and cosmos (T1.4.2).

## 2. God-relation, image, typology
**Agent** (17) — God **makes** creation and peoples tremble: Psa 99:1, "The LORD reigns; let the peoples tremble!" — imaging God's **reigning lordship** (T0.1.1). Never God's experiencer (T0.1.2); a creaturely analogue (T0.3.2). It instantiates the image in its **contrite, submissive** form — the spirit that trembles at God's word (Isa 66:2) and before the reigning Lord (Psa 99:1) (T0.3.1); its right (contrite/commanded) form is the rightly-ordered presence (T0.3.3). Typology: **theophanic** (Sinai, Exo 19:18), **eschatological** (day of the LORD near), **christological** (NT *tromos*) candidates (T0.4.1).

## 3. Created purpose / design-or-fall
Neutral 77, **with a created-design signal** in the commanded/righteous forms (commanded 7 + righteous 13) — the contrite spirit trembling at God's word (Isa 66:2) and trembling commanded before the reigning Lord (Psa 99:1): a bodily form that, **rightly directed, equips reverent submission** (T0.2.1). Reads as **both** — its commanded/righteous strand is created design (T0.2.2). Immediate response: **the shaking itself, reaching the body** — Hab 3:16, "my body trembles; my lips quiver… my legs tremble" — consistent (T1.5.1–1.5.2). Sustained, it can produce the **trembling heart** as a chronic affliction (Deu 28:65, "a trembling heart") (T1.6.1–1.6.2); the immediate shaking differs from the sustained chronic state (T1.6.3).

## 4. Constitution — the cluster's key witness
**The movement witness of the whole cluster** (T2.10.1): it crosses the **entire constitutional range** — **spirit** (Isa 66:2, the deepest and most positive case in the cluster), through **soul/heart** (heart 7 / soul 2), **outward to the body** (Job 4:14, "made all my bones shake"; Hab 3:16), and even the **cosmos** (Exo 19:18 Sinai; Psa 99:1, "let the earth quake!"). Movement is **inner-to-outer** — the inner state made visible — making the body the **visible crossing of the spirit-soul-body boundary**, permeable outward (T2.7.2; T2.10.2). It is not *primarily* spirit-level (heart-dominant), but it is the one fear that reaches the spirit (T2.1.2/2.1.3). Body-link is most developed here — bones, lips, legs — and **expressive** (the inner state moved outward) (T2.6.1–2.6.2). **Silent on mind** (T2.4).

## 5. Faculties
Constitutively **affective**, single-faculty (T3.4). Lighter cognitive/memory co-terms (T3.2/T3.3). **Silent** on volition, agency, moral evaluation, creativity, conscience at the faculty level (T3.6–3.10). Its distinctiveness is constitutional/somatic, not faculty-based.

## 6. Relational interfaces
God object 13 — **commanded worship-trembling**: Psa 2:11, "Serve the LORD with fear, and rejoice with trembling"; Php 2:12, "work out your own salvation with fear and trembling" (T4.2.1). Required posture: **contrite, humble submission** (Isa 66:2) (T4.2.2). Persons as the trembling-inducing party (person 9) (T4.3.1). God's agent-mode makes peoples/creation tremble (Psa 99:1) (T4.1.1). **Silent** on the spiritual-beings interface (T4.6.4).

## 7. Over time, suffering, eschatology
A genuine **sequence toward restoration**: Hos 11:10–11, "when he roars, his children shall come trembling… and I will return them to their homes" — trembling **leads to** homecoming; the trembling is the **threshold** of restoration, not the end-state (*inferential*; T5.2.1–5.2.2). Present in Job (6 refs) (T5.4). **Eschatological — the day of the LORD**: Joe 2:1, "Let all the inhabitants of the land tremble, for the day of the LORD is coming" (T5.6.1).

## 8. Within the cluster
Opposite: **rest / quiet / security** (*ra.ga*, *na.chat*, *sha.a.nan*, *be.tach*, *nu.ach*) (T1.3.1); excludes rest (T1.3.2). Distinguished from **c4 (terror)** by kind (act/bodily expression vs nominal state) and from **c6 (horror)** by quality (shaking vs recoil/bristling) (T6.5.1). **Co-occurs most broadly** within the terror-complex — with fear (c2), dread (c3), terror (c4), horror (c6): Psa 55:5, "Fear and trembling come upon me, and horror overwhelms me"; Job 4:14 (T6.1.1). **Produced by fear/dread** (c2/c3) as the inner affect realised in the body, and intensifying into **horror** (c6) (T6.2.1/T6.3.1–6.3.2); it is the **bodily constituent** of the fear-experience (T6.3.3). Cross-cluster partners: **rest / righteousness** (held → Session D).

## 9. Interpretive lens
Genre: **prophecy-dominant (47)** + narrative 34 — trembling in theophany/prophetic settings (Sinai, day of the LORD) (T7.2.2). Logical structure: the **worship paradox** — "Serve the LORD with fear, *and* rejoice with trembling" (Psa 2:11), fear and joy held together (T7.2.3). Anchor **Isa 66:2** uniquely locates trembling in the **contrite spirit** — the cluster's deepest, most positive constitutional case (T7.2.6). Science lens: **psychophysiology / somatic research** (Cannon; James-Lange; Damasio; Porges) — illuminating the cluster's **unusually high somatic density**, of which c5 is the chief witness (T7.3.1–7.3.2).

## 10. Inferential flags & silences
*Inferential:* trembling-as-threshold-of-restoration (Hos 11). *Silences:* mind; volition/agency/moral faculties. The somatic/constitutional reach is the load-bearing finding.

## 11. Essence
Trembling is the cluster's **constitutional witness** — fear made body. It crosses the whole spirit-soul-body range outward into shaking bones and quivering lips, and even into the quaking earth; yet its deepest and most positive form is the trembling of a **contrite spirit** at God's word, held paradoxically together with rejoicing.
