## 1. Identity
The overwhelming, often inflicted pole of fear — acute, **soul-deep** (T1.1.1). Terror core: H0367 *e.mah*, H4032 *ma.gor*, H2851 *chit.tit*, H1091 *bal.la.hah* (14 terms) + Greek G1630 *ekfobos*, G5398 *foberos*, G5400 *fobetron* (T1.1.2). A **state** — 90% status, **entirely nominal** (47 nouns + adjectival), operating purely as a condition (T1.2.1/T1.4.1). Its name carries a **constitutional (soul-deep)** implication (T1.1.3). Appears **simple** (purely affective) at first encounter (T1.2.2).

## 2. God-relation, image, typology
**Agent-dominant** (8 of ~11 divine) — God **spreads terror**: Exo 15:16, "because of the greatness of your arm, they are still as a stone" — imaging God's **overwhelming might in deliverance/judgement** (T0.1.1). Never God's experiencer (T0.1.2); creaturely, an exclusively creaturely analogue (T0.3.2). Expresses **creaturely finitude/vulnerability** under threat (T0.3.1); presence reveals the creature under threat (neutral) (T0.3.3). Typology: **eschatological** (Luk 21:11; Heb 10:27) and **theophanic/christological** (Heb 12:21 Sinai; Mar 9:6 transfiguration) candidates in NT verse_text; direction is divine-instance-establishes-the-pattern (T0.4.1–0.4.2).

## 3. Created purpose / design-or-fall
**Neutral 50 of 53** — no equipping purpose; an overwhelming condition (T0.2.1). **Fallen-condition response** (T0.2.2). Immediate response: **paralysis / petrification** — Exo 15:16, "still as a stone" — consistent (freezing) (T1.5.1–1.5.2). As a status state, its sustained form is the **continuance of the condition** (T1.6.1).

## 4. Constitution
The **first characteristic where terror reaches the soul as much as the heart** — soul 3 ≥ heart 3 — the soul-deep register (Psa 31:13, "terror on every side!"); this reveals it as the **innermost-personal** fear (T2.2.1–2.2.2). **Silent on spirit and mind** (T2.1; T2.4). **No specific body-part link** — it reaches the soul, not the body (T2.6.3/T2.7.3). Origin: **God-sent** (agent) on enemies; **falls upon / seizes** peoples — downward-onto, no internal level-crossing (T2.9.3/T2.10.1).

## 5. Faculties
Constitutively **affective**, affect-dominant (T3.4). One perception blend (Luk 21:11, "fearful sights" — terror at perceived eschatological signs) (T3.1). **Acute and pre-reflective** — minimal cognitive co-operation (T3.2.3). **Impairs/overrides volition** — it **freezes agency** ("still as a stone"), the limit case where fear suspends the will (*inferential*, strongly supported by the paralysis response) (T3.6/T3.7). Morally **neutral** — moral-evaluation faculty not engaged (T3.8).

## 6. Relational interfaces
God object 2 (T4.2.1). At the human interface, terror is **inspired in another by threatening power** — Pro 20:2, "The terror of a king is like the growling of a lion" (T4.3.1–4.3.2); received from the powerful (addressed 6) (T4.4.1). God's agent-mode is **responsive/judicial** against opposition (Exo 15:16) (T4.1.2). **Silent** on the spiritual-beings interface (T4.6.4).

## 7. Over time, suffering, eschatology
Silent on transformation/sanctification (T5.1/T5.5). **Concentrated in the suffering literature** — 13 Job/Lam refs; terror as the inner register of calamity (T5.4). **Eschatological terror** genuinely evidenced (Luk 21:11; Heb 10:27) (T5.6.1).

## 8. Within the cluster
Opposite: **peace** (*sha.lom*) (T1.3.1); excludes peace (T1.3.2). Distinguished from **c3 (dread)** by form + intensity (terror = nominal, overwhelming, soul-deep, ↔peace; dread = verb-capable state, ↔security), and from **c5 (trembling)** by kind (terror = nominal state; trembling = act/bodily expression) (T6.5.1). **Terror is the intensified pole of dread** — constitutively related by degree (T6.3.3); the *chatat* root bridges terror (c4) and dismay (c7) (T6.4.2). Word-paired with dread (Deu 11:25). Cross-cluster: the **love-casts-out-fear** territory and the peace foil (held → Session D).

## 9. Interpretive lens
Genre: **prophecy 22 + wisdom (Job) 14** — terror in prophetic judgement and Joban affliction (T7.2.2). Anchor **Exo 15:16** uniquely shows terror **freezing agency** ("still as a stone") as God's deliverance-instrument (T7.2.6). Science lens: **affective neuroscience** (the freeze response) and **psychophysiology** (the collapse/freeze register) — and a flagged open question (Porges' polyvagal): whether the terror-paralysis carries a **dissociative/shutdown** sense distinct from arousal (worth testing in the verse evidence; T7.3.4).

## 10. Inferential flags & silences
*Inferential:* volition/agency-freezing reading. *Open question:* freeze/shutdown vs arousal register (→ T7.2/Session B). *Silences:* spirit, mind, body-link, moral faculties; transformation/sanctification.

## 11. Essence
Terror is the **overwhelming, soul-deep, inflicted** pole — the nominal state God spreads on his enemies and the petrifying response that leaves the creature "still as a stone." It reaches the soul (not the body), freezes agency, and its foil is peace.
