## Summary
Trembling is the **somatic, outward-moving act** of fear — the body shaking. Its distinctive mark, unique in the cluster so far, is that it **crosses the whole constitutional range**: it begins as deep as the **spirit** (the contrite who "tremble at my word," Isa 66:2), runs through soul and heart, and moves **outward** to the body (bones, lips — Hab 3:16) and even the **cosmos** (Sinai, Exo 19:18). It is frequently **caused by God** (strong causative Hiphil; divine agent), can be **commanded** as the right bodily response to the reigning Lord (Psa 99:1), and stands against **rest/peace/security**.

## T0 — Divine Image and Created Design
God never its experiencer (0); divine role **agent** (16) — God *makes* tremble. Mostly neutral, with a small created-design signal in the commanded form. Its commendable form is the **contrite spirit trembling at God's word** (Isa 66:2). **T0.4 (inferential):** theophanic (Sinai; the Lord roaring), eschatological (day of the Lord near), christological (NT *tromos*, verse-check pending); covenantal none. → T7.2.

**Name** — *ragaz* / *charad* + Greek *tromos*. **Kind** — an **act** (action 73%). **Boundary** — **rest / peace / security**. **Modes** — verbal with a strong **causative** (Hiphil 25). **Immediate response** — the shaking; cosmic tremor. **Sustained** — the trembling heart (Deu 28:65). **Conditions** — caused/befalling, yet commandable.

## Reflection (significance and affected perspectives)
- **Trembling is the cluster's key witness to the spirit-soul-body question.** Where the other characteristics sit (sparsely) in the heart or soul, trembling alone is evidenced **from the spirit outward to the body and the cosmos**. It is the lexical picture of fear *crossing constitutional levels* — the inner state made visible. For the programme's governing question this is the most structurally informative characteristic so far.
- **Its deepest form is positive:** the contrite spirit that trembles at God's word (Isa 66:2) — a sought, commended state — sitting alongside the neutral creaturely shaking and the divinely-caused trembling of nations. The same bodily act spans contrite reverence and terror-stricken collapse, separated (as elsewhere) by object and valence.
- **Shared antonym, distinct register.** Trembling's opposite (rest/peace/security) overlaps dread's and terror's — the three "state/expression" fears share a settledness foil, where reverent and ordinary fear (C1/C2) had relational/volitional foils. A structural observation to test at T6.
