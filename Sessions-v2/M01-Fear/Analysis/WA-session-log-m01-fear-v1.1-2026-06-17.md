**Programme-relevant patterns (lexicon-grounded, inferential where noted):**
- **A consistent T0 finding:** God is never the experiencer of fear anywhere in M01 (0); fear is a creaturely characteristic, attributed to God only as object/agent/giver/addressee.
- **Three structural families** emerge by `type`: dispositional/trait (C1, C11), episodic act (C2, C5, C7, C8, C10), and abiding state (C3, C4, C6, C9). 
- **The discriminator is object + valence, not lemma** (confirmed repeatedly): the same vocabulary is commended toward God and forbidden toward circumstances.
- **Each characteristic has a distinct foil** — reverence↔withholding, ordinary fear↔courage, dread↔security, terror↔peace, trembling↔rest, anxiety↔comfort, cowardice↔power/love/self-discipline. The "state/expression" fears (C3–C6) share a settledness foil; the others have relational/volitional foils.
- **Constitutional reach:** only **trembling (C5)** spans spirit→soul→body→cosmos; **anxiety (C9)** is the most interior (heart/inner-parts) and the only **cognition-engaging**, divine-absent fear; **terror (C4)** is the most soul-deep.
- **Moral poles:** reverent fear (created design) and cowardice (culpable vice) bracket the cluster.
