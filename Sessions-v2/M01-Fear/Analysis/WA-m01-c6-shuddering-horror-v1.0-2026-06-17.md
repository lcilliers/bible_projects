## Summary
Shuddering / horror is the **appalled recoil** — the inner being seized and overwhelmed by what it confronts. A small, OT-only, mostly **neutral state** (status 13/17), located in heart/soul/flesh, whose verses speak of horror that **seizes** and **overwhelms** ("horror overwhelms me," Psa 55:5; "horror has appalled me," Isa 21:4; "horror seizes them," Job 18:20). It is the recoil pole of the terror family.

## Reflection
Horror is the most *visceral recoil* term — the inner being overwhelmed and the flesh bristling. It overlaps trembling (somatic) and dismay (collapse) and is hard to separate cleanly; its distinctive note is **revulsion/recoil** (bristling, being appalled) rather than mere fright. Its small size (17) and OT-only range limit broader claims.
