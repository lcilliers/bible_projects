## T0 — Divine Image and Created Design
**T0.1.** God never the experiencer (0); divine role **agent 16** (giver 1) — God *makes* tremble. **T0.2.** Mostly neutral, but the commanded form ("let the peoples tremble") gives a small created-design signal — trembling as the fitting creaturely response to the reigning Lord. **T0.3.** Creaturely; its commendable form is the contrite spirit that trembles at God's word (Isa 66:2). **T0.4 (inferential):** theophanic (Sinai; the Lord roaring), eschatological (the day of the Lord near), christological (NT *tromos*, verse-check pending); covenantal none. → T7.2.

## T1 — Definition
**T1.1 Name.** *ragaz* (H7264, 40), *charad* (H2729, 39), +Greek *tromos*/*entromos*. **T1.2 Kind.** An **act** (action 73%) — the body's shaking. **T1.3 Boundary.** **Rest / peace / security** (compound). **T1.4 Modes.** Verbal with a strong **causative** (Hiphil 25) — trembling is something done *and* something inflicted. **T1.5 Immediate Response.** the shaking itself; cosmic tremor. **T1.6 Sustained Effect.** the trembling heart as an ongoing condition (Deu 28:65). **T1.7 Conditions.** caused/befalling, but commandable as right response.

## T2 — Constitutional Location and Boundaries (rich — the distinctive tier)
The only characteristic so far to populate the full range:
- **T2.1 Spirit** — Isa 66:2 ("contrite in spirit and trembles at my word"). The cluster's first spirit-location.
- **T2.2 Soul / T2.3 Heart** — Deu 28:65 (trembling heart), Eze 32:10.
- **T2.6/T2.7 Body** — bones, lips, body (Job 4:14; Hab 3:16): the soul/spirit **expresses through the body**.
- **T2.10 Constitutional Movement** — the evidence shows fear **moving outward**: spirit → soul → body → cosmos (Sinai). Trembling is the visible crossing of the spirit-soul-body boundary.
