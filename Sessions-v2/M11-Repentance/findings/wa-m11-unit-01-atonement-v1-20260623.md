## A. General observations

**Definition (DB):** the priestly/sacrificial **MECHANISM** by which sin/defilement are covered, removed, or borne — the inner-being state of mediation that effects sin-release.

### §10 object-kind classification — **REMEDY (mechanism-side)** — GROUNDED

Provisional kind from the kickoff: **REMEDY** (the counter-object of M10's G6). The per-verse signature **confirms it** (not merely asserts it):

- **Computed mechanical signature (81 focus occurrences of H3722A):**
  - **type = action 81/81** (100%) — kip.per is *always* an act performed, never a standing state or a faculty-disposition. This is the REMEDY discriminator at work: it *does something to* a sin-object (covers/purges/reconciles), it is not itself an object the inner being is in.
  - **faculty = none 81/81** — term-intrinsically faculty-less (like the M10 sin-status lemmas); the inner being is the *site/beneficiary*, not the seat. REMEDY is not a CHARACTERISTIC (no faculty-in-operation).
  - **valence = commanded 41 · righteous 23 · neutral 16 · (none 1) — and CRUCIALLY zero `sinful`/`forbidden`.** This is the §10 REMEDY data-signal exactly: "neutral / commanded valence." Atonement is the cultic-prescribed, righteous, or merely-narrated act — never itself a wrong. It is the *undoing* of a sin-object, so it cannot carry sin's valence.
  - **object_type = person 53 · abstract 10 · thing 3 · situation 2 · God 2 · (none 11)** — the dominant object is a **person** (the one atoned-*for*); abstract objects are the sins/uncleannesses covered. The remedy operates *on behalf of* a person against a sin-weight.
  - **co-seated inner-being seat = ne.phesh ("soul: life") ×5** (the soul addressed/ransomed by atonement — e.g. the life-for-life ransom logic). The only constitutional seat that recurs; confirms atonement's specific operation is **on the nephesh** (cf. Session B finding #1609 below).
