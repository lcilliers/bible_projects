## A. General observations

**Definition (DB):** the sovereign divine **ACT** of releasing sin's guilt — the inner-being state of being pardoned **and** the divine disposition of pardoning.

### §10 object-kind classification — **REMEDY (act of pardon) + STATE (being-pardoned)** — bilateral — GROUNDED

The kickoff classed this **bilateral** (divine act ↔ human state). The per-verse signature **confirms the bilateral split lexically**: the verb (act) vs the adjective/noun (disposition + standing).

- **Computed mechanical signature (49 focus occurrences of H5545/H5546/H5547):**
  - **type = action 45 · status 3 · quality 1.** The dominant face is **action 45/49 (92%)** = the REMEDY act of pardon (sa.lach, the divine releasing verse). The **status 3 + quality 1** = the STATE/disposition face (se.li.chah "forgiveness *is with* God" Psa 130:4; sal.lach the standing divine adjective Psa 86:5). The vocabulary itself encodes both modes — exactly the §10 REMEDY-act + STATE bilateral.
  - **faculty = none 49/49** — faculty-less on the lemma (consistent with REMEDY; the act has a divine *subject* but seats no human faculty). Not a CHARACTERISTIC.
  - **valence = righteous 35 · neutral 9 · (none 5) — zero sinful/forbidden.** The §10 REMEDY data-signal (neutral/righteous, never wrong): pardon is a righteous divine act. The neutral subset = the bare-narration / abstract-noun uses.
  - **object_type = abstract 15 · person 11 · God 1 · (none 22).** Abstract objects = the sin/iniquity released; person = the one pardoned. The high `none` reflects intransitive/declarative pardon-statements and the standing-noun uses (forgiveness as a possession, no object).
  - **co-seated inner-being seat = ne.phesh ("soul: myself") ×3, le.vav ("heart") ×1** — the soul/heart of the one pardoned (the human STATE side); thin but present, confirming the recipient-state face.

- **Test (§10 G + B):** *"Does this undo/remove a sin-object?"* — **Yes (the act, REMEDY).** *"Is this a standing mode the inner being is in?"* — **Yes for the noun/adjective (STATE: being-pardoned / the divine disposition of pardoning).** Confirmed **REMEDY (act) + STATE/CONDITION (being-pardoned)**, bilateral, with the disposition-adjective bordering on a divine quality.

- **Divine-exclusivity (a definitional signature):** the salach root is **divine-subject only** in the OT (see #1986, #354 below) — this is *why* the unit is "**Divine** forgiveness" and structurally distinct from the broader (human-capable) Release unit (#3, afiēmi). The faculty=none + righteous-valence signature is the lexical face of that exclusivity.
