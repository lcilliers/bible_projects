## A. General observations

**Definition (DB):** the act and state of **releasing** — from sin's record, debt, obligation, bondage, possession, or relationship. **Broader** than divine forgiveness (#2): it includes non-remedy senses (leaving, permitting).

### §10 object-kind classification — **REMEDY operating on the RECORD (#13) + CONDITION (release from bondage)** — multi-kind — GROUNDED

The kickoff classed this **REMEDY-on-the-RECORD + CONDITION**, with a multi-sense afiēmi caveat. The per-verse signature **confirms the multi-kind, broad-valence character** that distinguishes this unit from the clean REMEDY of #1/#2.

- **Computed mechanical signature (108 focus occurrences):**
  - **type = action 91 · status 17.** Action-dominant (the releasing *act*), with a substantial **status 17** tail = the *resultant condition* (afesis "the state of having been let go" — the §10 CONDITION face) + standing-deprivation (apobolē). The act↔state duality the noun afesis names is visible in the data.
  - **faculty = none 108/108** — faculty-less on the lemma (REMEDY/CONDITION, not CHARACTERISTIC).
  - **valence = righteous 53 · neutral 18 · commanded 14 · sinful 8 · forbidden 3 · (none 12).** **CRUCIALLY, this unit DOES carry `sinful` (8) and `forbidden` (3)** — unlike the pure-remedy Units #1/#2 (which had zero). This is the lexical fingerprint of the **non-remedy afiēmi senses**: G0863G "leave/forsake" (forsaking can be sinful — abandoning the first love, Rev 2:4) and G0863I "permit" (permitting/forbidding what ought not). So the unit is **broader than remedy**: REMEDY (forgive-sense) + non-remedy release (leave/permit). The object-kind is **not uniform across the lemma**.
  - **object_type = abstract 20 · person 17 · thing 9 · spiritual-being 2 · (none 60).** The high `none` reflects intransitive "let it be / leave / permit" uses; abstract = sins/debts released (REMEDY); thing/person = goods/relations left (leave-sense).
  - **co-seated inner-being seat = pneuma ("spirit/breath: breath") ×5, kardia ("heart") ×1, tharseō ("take heart") ×1** — the spirit/heart present where release touches the inner being (e.g. "yielded up his spirit" with afiēmi G0863G; the heart-level forgive command). Confirms the inner-being seating is **co-seated, term-external** (the lemma itself is faculty-less).

- **Tests (§10):**
  - **REMEDY-on-RECORD (G + C-record):** **exaleifō G1813 "blot out"** (4×) is the explicit **inverse of M10 #13's "written/engraved" record** — the sin-record *blotted out*. Passes the REMEDY test acting specifically on the RECORD object-kind. ↔ BOND below.
  - **CONDITION (B):** **afesis G0859** as the *state* of release (from bondage/debt) — passes the CONDITION test ("a standing mode the inner being is in/under" — here, the released-from-bondage state).
  - **Non-remedy / multi-sense:** G0863G (leave) and G0863I (permit) are **release acts that are NOT remedy** — they do not undo a sin-object; they release a bond or grant/withhold access. These are the `sinful`/`forbidden`-tagged occurrences. → the unit is genuinely **multi-kind** and per-occurrence.

- **Distinct from #2 (Divine forgiveness):** where salach is divine-only and pure-remedy, afiēmi is **divine + human + non-moral**, and broader in object (record/debt/bondage/possession/relationship/access). #2 is the narrow divine core; #3 is the broad Greek release field around it.

- **↔ BOND — exaleifō "blot out" ↔ M10 #13 record (pole-pair, H3 confirmed):** **G1813 exaleifō "blot out" is the explicit inverse of M10 #13 "written/engraved" (the divine RECORD)** — sin recorded ↔ sin blotted-out, the same accounting read from both ends. This is the concrete §10 H3 (pole-pairs) confirmation the kickoff predicted. Surfaced for synthesis (the record↔release pole-pair).
- **⚑ SYNTH — afesis as the resultant CONDITION of the act:** the act (afiēmi/exaleifō) produces the standing afesis-condition (#367/#440). Whether afesis is a separate object-kind (CONDITION) or the standing-form of the REMEDY act is a §10 question (parallels the #2 act↔state border).
- **⚑ SYNTH — apobolē (G0580) the dark face:** casting-away/deprivation (Rom 11:15) is release *as rejection* — borders on M05 (Anger/rejection) and the reconciliation outcome (#5). High theological density (#149); worth synthesising whether "release" spans both pardon-release and rejection-release.
- **⚑ SYNTH — release ↔ calling (R019):** the leave/forsake sense (Mar 10:29) ties release to vocation (#461) — the same act releases sin *and* releases family/property in response to the call. Cross-cluster synthesis target.
- **⚠ HYGIENE — high `object_type=none` (60/108):** intransitive "let/leave/permit" uses legitimately have no object; not a data gap. Trust `object_type` over raw `object`.
