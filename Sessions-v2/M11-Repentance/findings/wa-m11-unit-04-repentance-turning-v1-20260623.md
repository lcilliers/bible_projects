## A. General observations

### A.1 Grounded §10 object-kind classification

**Provisional §10 kind (from the kickoff lens): CHARACTERISTIC** — the human *turning*, a faculty in operation; **PLUS** a divine-relenting counterpart (na.cham of God). The per-verse evidence **grounds this**:

- **type = action — 42/42 (100%).** Every focus occurrence is an action (verb/participle morphology). This is the §10 CHARACTERISTIC data-signal (type=action/disposition + verb-life) at full strength — the cleanest action-typing in the cluster. ✔ confirms "a faculty in operation."
- **faculty IS seated, and splits by term** — affect=34 (all na.cham), memory=7 (shuv "return/recall"), volition=7 (shuv as the volitional turn), none=1 (ud). Unlike the M10 sin-statuses (faculty-empty), this unit **carries faculty on the lemma** — the §10 strongest discriminator for CHARACTERISTIC. The faculty is **not uniform**: na.cham reads as *affect* (sorrow/regret/relenting — the contrite/relenting movement), shuv as *memory/volition* (the deliberate turn-back). This is a per-term faculty split inside one characteristic, exactly the "faculty must be per-term not per-cluster" caution.
- **interior seatedness — heart=9, soul=1** (32 none). Where it IS seated it seats in the **heart** (co-seated with lev/levav 7×, ne.phesh 1×) — the constitutional centre of the turning. Moderate interior signal (heart-seated ~24% of occurrences), consistent with CHARACTERISTIC (interior seatedness is the strongest discriminator; here present but not dominant because much of na.cham is divine and not heart-seated).

**★ The human-turning vs divine-relenting split (grounded, by experiencer + divine role):**
- **experiencer:** other=20 · self=13 · other(addressed)=4 · none=5. The *self/addressed* band (~17) is the **human turning** (the contrite person, the called "return!"); the *other* band overlaps the **divine relenting** (reported of God).
- **divine_involvement: agent=24** · none=17 · object=1. The large **agent=24** is na.cham *of God* — God is the one relenting/turning from judgment (Gen 6:6–7; Exo 32:12,14; the prophetic relentings). This is the divine-relenting counterpart, mechanically visible.
- **valence:** neutral=21 · none=10 · righteous=8 · commanded=2 · sinful=1. The **righteous=8 + commanded=2** band is the human turning framed as the right/commanded act ("turn and live"); the **neutral=21** band is largely the divine relenting (a narrated divine act, not a moral verdict on a human). The lone **sinful=1** is a turning-back into evil (a shuv used for relapse) — the per-occurrence reading the lemma alone would miss.

**Conclusion (grounded):** the §10 CHARACTERISTIC classification holds — action-typed, faculty-seated, heart-centred — **but it is a bilateral characteristic**: a *human* contrite turning (affect/volition, heart-seated, righteous/commanded) and a *divine* relenting (na.cham of God, agent, neutral). The two are the same lexical movement read from the two experiencers. This is the cluster's genuine inner-being characteristic → tiers.
