## A. General observations

### A.1 Grounded §10 object-kind classification

**Provisional §10 kind (from the kickoff lens): STATE / OUTCOME (relational restored-state)** — and **⚑ a CANDIDATE NEW lens-kind** (see flags). The per-verse evidence **grounds this**, and also exposes the act↔outcome duality the DB definition names:

- **type = action — 8/8 (100%).** The focus lemmas are all verbs ("reconcile / be reconciled"). So on the *lemma* this is an **act** (the act that effects restoration), not a standing state. The **outcome/STATE** is carried by the co-term **katallagē (G2643) "reconciliation"** (a *noun*, type=status — 2Cor 5:18,19) — the result-state of the act. This is the §10 EXPRESSION-pattern in mirror: an *act* (verb) that *produces* a *state* (the noun katallagē). The unit is genuinely **act + outcome**, as the DB definition says — confirmed by the verb-focus / status-partner split.
- **faculty = NONE — 8/8.** Unlike #4 (repentance, faculty-seated), reconciliation carries **no inner faculty on the lemma**. It is a **relational act between parties**, not a faculty in operation — it is *done to / between* the inner being, not *by* a single faculty. This is the §10 signal that pushes it AWAY from CHARACTERISTIC and toward a **relational STATE/OUTCOME**: no seat, no faculty-life, just the restored relation. (location=flesh appears once — Col 1:22 "in his body of flesh" — the *site* of the reconciling act, not an inner seat.)
- **valence = righteous 5 · commanded 2 · none 1.** **No `sinful`, no `neutral`.** This is the cleanest **positive-valence** signature in the cluster — every occurrence is either the righteous accomplished restoration (Rom 5:10; 2Cor 5:18,19; Eph 2:16; Col 1:20,22) or the *commanded* call to enter it ("be reconciled to God," 2Cor 5:20; "be reconciled to her husband," 1Cor 7:11). M10 (Sin) had **no positive states at all** — this all-positive valence is precisely what the sin-cluster could not surface (see ⚑ flag).
- **object_type / divine role — God 2 · person 3 · none 3; divine: object 3, agent 2, none 3.** The object of reconciliation is **God** (Rom 5:10; 2Cor 5:20 — "reconciled *to God*") or a **person** (1Cor 7:11 "to her husband"; Col 1:22 "us"). The two-axis range — **vertical** (to God) and **horizontal** (to a spouse) — is in the 8 verses. Where God is the *agent* (2Cor 5:18,19 "God … reconciling") the act is divine-initiated; where God is the *object* (Rom 5:10; 2Cor 5:20) the human is brought back *to* him.

**Conclusion (grounded):** #5 is best read as a **relational STATE/OUTCOME effected by an act** — verb-focus (the reconciling act) + status-partner katallagē (the reconciled-state), faculty-empty (a relation, not a seat), uniformly **positive** valence, ranging vertical↔horizontal. It answers the **sin-alienation** of M10 (the relational pole) — see ↔ BOND.

### A.5 Flags placed

- **⚑ SYNTH / CANDIDATE NEW LENS-KIND — "positive relational STATE/OUTCOME of restoration."** Reconciliation is a **positive relational state/outcome** — the inner being *brought back into right relationship*. The provisional §10 typology (built on M10, a sin-cluster) has **no positive states** (its STATE/CONDITION kind B is sin-states; its REMEDY kind G is the *undoing*, not a positive resultant relation). Reconciliation is **neither** a sin-state nor a mere remedy-act — it is the **positive relational terminus** the remedy produces. **Flag this as a §10 typology-evolution candidate** (a new kind, or a positive sub-pole of kind B CONDITION/STATE). Grounded by: all-positive valence (5 righteous / 2 commanded / 0 sinful / 0 neutral) + faculty-empty + the status-noun outcome (katallagē) + the vertical↔horizontal object range. This is exactly the §G bias-guard prediction ("the first cluster with positive states will evolve the typology") coming true.
- **⚖ BORDER — act vs outcome straddles M11↔M05.** The reconciling **act** (verb katallassō/apokatallassō) is owned by **M11**; the reconciliation **outcome-noun** (katallagē G2643) and "make peace" (eirēnopoieō G1517) are tagged **M05 (Peace)**. So the unit's act-half and outcome-half sit in different clusters — a coverage seam (parallel to the metanoeō split on #4 and the atonement migration on #1). The synthesis must reach into M05 to hold the outcome-state. Flag, not resolved.
- **↔ BOND — reconciliation answers M10 sin-alienation (the relational pole).** The reconciliation outcome is the **relational answer to the sin-alienation of M10**: 2Cor 5:19 makes it explicit — "reconciling the world to himself, **not counting their trespasses**" — reconciliation IS the **non-reckoning of the sin-record** (the inverse of M10 #13 "the divine record"). Bond to M10 (the record/alienation pole) and to M05 (Peace, the outcome), M38 (Salvation, Rom 5:10 sōzō), M22 (Holiness, Col 1:22 hagios). Grounded by the partner web (paraptōma/logizomai/sōzō/eirēnopoieō/hagios).
- **↔ BOND — vertical ↔ horizontal.** The same lemma reconciles *to God* (Rom 5:10; 2Cor 5:20) and *to a spouse* (1Cor 7:11) — the vertical/horizontal duality the forgiveness findings (#367 F-003, #375) name for the resolution field, here in the reconciliation register too.
