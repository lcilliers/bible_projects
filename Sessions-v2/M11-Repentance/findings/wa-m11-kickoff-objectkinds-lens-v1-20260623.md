## 3. The striking first finding — M11 is the INVERSE/COMPLEMENT of M10
Where M10 was dominated by **sin-objects** (status · record · identity), M11 is dominated by **REMEDY** (the undoing) + **one CHARACTERISTIC** (repentance, the human turning) + a **relational STATE** (reconciliation). This is the resolution side of the same field. Concretely, the lens shows:
- **SDX-02 answered (provisionally):** "where does atonement live?" → **M11 #1 (Atonement, kip.per)** is its home; M10 kept only the OT cultic remnant. The migration (SDX-09) lands here.
- **H2 (bi-level resolution) confirmed in structure:** the **human side = repentance #4** (shuv — the characteristic), the **divine side = atonement/forgiveness #1/#2** (the remedy). The two resolution-modes M10 G7 predicted are M11's own #4 vs #1/#2.
- **H3 (pole-pairs) made concrete:** **M11 #3 "blot out" (exaleifō) is the explicit inverse of M10 #13 "written/engraved" (the record)** — sin recorded ↔ sin blotted-out, the same accounting read from both ends.
- **A lens refinement:** the §10 bias-guard predicted CHARACTERISTIC would dominate *virtue* clusters; but a **resolution cluster** (M11) is **REMEDY-heavy**, with repentance the lone characteristic. So: *resolution clusters are remedy-dominated; the simple-virtue test of CHARACTERISTIC awaits a different cluster.*
