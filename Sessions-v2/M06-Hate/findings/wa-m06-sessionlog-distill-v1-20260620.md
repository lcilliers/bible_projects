## 3. The debate / thinking trail
- **Method.** The cluster fans out per verse (every term in a verse is listed); M06's own terms were isolated by the `focus_cluster` flag (431 occurrences, 34 terms). All aggregation was mechanical from the JSON — no transcription, so the evidence appendices are faithful by construction.
- **Hypothesis first, then test.** The v1_0 six-way split was treated as a hypothesis. Each term's membership was tested against its own faculty/type/seat/valence profile. Where a term's evidence pulled against its candidate home, it was **flagged, not silently moved** — preserving the researcher's authority over placement.
- **Self-correction during the pass.** Three corrections emerged from the evidence rather than from prior labels:
  1. The v1_0 map had **omitted `ba.ash`** (×8). Its faculty is `moral_evaluation`, so it was placed in C-B (contempt) and the gap closed on evidence.
  2. The v1_0 label **"loathing = visceral repugnance (interior)"** overstated the interior content: C-C is faculty-absent on all 29 occurrences; the only interior signal is 3 flesh-seated cases. The synthesis was rewritten to match.
  3. The **inner/outer boundary is not a clean by-characteristic split** — it runs *through* C-B and C-D, each of which has an interior-faculty pole and a faculty-absent expression pole. This reframed how IC3 must be answered.
- **Tension surfaced.** C-E (enmity) shows the weakest interior engagement of the six — faculty-absent, unseated, neutral — raising a genuine GR-PROG-007 filter question about whether it is an inner-being *characteristic* or a relational-interface *layer*. Left open for the researcher.

## 4. Conclusions (evidence-grounded)
- M06 decomposes into six lexically distinguishable characteristics, but **the boundaries are not all equally solid**: C-A and C-D are strongly attested; C-B is two-register; C-C is thin and possibly a sub-mode; C-E is borderline as a standalone characteristic.
- **Bivalence (commanded hatred of evil) is characteristic-specific**, living only on `sa.ne`/`ta.av`, which leans toward modelling it as a valence-axis rather than a seventh characteristic.
- The cluster's **relational-to-God vector differs by characteristic**: God is predominantly the *object* of contempt and taunt, and as often the *agent* as the object of aversion.
