## Method note for execution
- Grouping basis = lemma + per-occurrence `sense` + faculty/type (+ seat/cause/object_type where present). The `sense` field (per-occurrence ESV word) is the key discriminator for homonymous/multi-sense lemmas.
- Verse-level assignment: a verse is assigned to the characteristic(s) its focus term(s) engage. Multi-characteristic verses and non-fitting verses are flagged, not forced.
- Verse text consulted to confirm boundary/ambiguous assignments (GR-PROG-001 verse-leads).
