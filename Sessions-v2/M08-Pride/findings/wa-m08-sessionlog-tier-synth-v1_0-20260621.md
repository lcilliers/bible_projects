## 2. Method debate and decisions (the thinking trail)

**The governing risk, named up front.** The tier-answering phase is the exact phase that failed in M07 — 126 × N answers produced fast by **scripted field-aggregation**, collapsing judgement-questions to a false "Silent". The working decision held throughout: every question answered by **reading the characteristic's verses with judgement**, never by aggregating coded fields (dos-and-donts §A2/§5; GR-PROG-001). Silence was reserved for genuine evidence-absence and **classified by cause** — *structural* (silent by the characteristic's nature / deferred to synthesis), *data-silence* (the verses are genuinely silent), *recoverable* (would yield to a read — to be eliminated). No recoverable silences were left standing.

**Exemplar-first.** Per the "lock the template on one worked exemplar" discipline (§4), Characteristic A's full 126-question catalogue was produced first and confirmed with the researcher before the remaining six were mass-produced — avoiding a six-fold redo had the format needed changing. The researcher confirmed the per-tier Q-by-Q form, the classified-silence convention, and the inferential labels.

**Per-characteristic re-judgement, not copying.** Each characteristic's silences were re-judged from its own evidence. This was vindicated by how sharply the profiles diverged: A is structurally silent on the relational gift-interfaces; **D answers them fully**; C1 is narrowly will-centred; C2 is thin in the agent's interior but rich in the human-interface and sufferer-vantage; E is narrowly cognitive; F is thinnest of all.

**Honesty about thin bases (§A3/§A4).** E (~89/126) and F (~88/126) carry the lowest coverage. The decision was to record their many faculty-silences as **genuine data-silence**, not to infer content to inflate coverage. F's 2-occurrence direct base in particular was documented as a real limitation, with its significance located structurally (the cluster's terminus) rather than asserted beyond the evidence.

**Science confinement.** T7.3 answers were drawn from the M08 science extract and **attributed**; the science was kept out of the verse-evidence tiers, and the science-surfaced shame-under-grandiosity question (M07↔M08) was explicitly flagged as **absent from the verse evidence** and carried to Session D rather than asserted as a finding (GR-PROG-009).

## 3. Findings of substance (cluster-level)

The synthesis (wa-m08-cluster-synthesis-v1_0-20260621.md) establishes:

- **One reality in seven modes, seated in the heart.** B (settled pride) is the core; A is its active form (act↔state, roots g-'-h/r-w-m); C1/C2 its outward discharge toward God and toward people (root z-d, one disposition/two directions); D its voiced form; E its cognitive inflation; F its terminus.
- **The organising principle is theological** (T0): every characteristic is defined by its relation to the exaltedness/majesty/glory that belongs to God. Pride seizes it; **F occupies it** ("I am, and there is no one besides me", Isaiah 47:8); **D's right pole returns it** ("Let the one who boasts, boast in the Lord", 1 Corinthians 1:31).
- **A single legitimate pole, located entirely in D** — the only created-design, eschatologically-hopeful, constructively-relational, suffering-perfected form in the cluster. The cluster's most distinctive structural feature.
- **The relational-interface silence is the diagnostic** (T4.1/T4.3/T4.4 structural across A, B, C1, E, F; inverted only by D).
- **Faculty-signature:** will + agency + relational damage + moral inversion; affect isolated in D; cognition in E; memory engaged by *failure* (forgetting God) in A and B; creativity uniformly silent.
- **One developmental arc** (prosperity → pride → brought-low) with **self-humbling** the only off-ramp — except D's right pole, perfected through weakness.
