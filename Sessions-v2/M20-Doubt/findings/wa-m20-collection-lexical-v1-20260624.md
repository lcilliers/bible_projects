## 6. The Trust в†” Doubt pole-relation (tested)

The prompt asks whether M20 sits on a pole with M19 Trust. **The evidence supports a Trustв†”Doubt axis, but it runs through M31 (Faith) lexically, and the axis is unit-specific, not cluster-uniform:**

- **U5 Doubt/wavering** is the *direct* inverse of faith: distazЕЌ is rebuked as "little faith" (Mat 14:31, oligopistos M31); dipsuchos is healed by single-hearted drawing-near (Jam 4:8). **Doubt = faith divided/wavering.** Strong, explicit, lexical pole.

- **U2 Despair** is the inverse of *hope/reliance*: 2Cor 1:8вЂ“10 (despaired в†’ rely on God who raises the dead). Pole partner: **M31 reliance/Trust**.
- **U3 Disheartenment** is the inverse of *strength/encouragement* (M23 cha.zaq, M05 parakaleЕЌ), not primarily of trust.
- **U4 Perplexity** is the inverse of *understanding* (M15 bin), and is **off the trust axis** (valence-neutral; not unbelief).

**Verdict:** the Trustв†”Doubt pole is REAL and lexically grounded for U1/U2/U5 (anxiety/despair/doubt = the failure-modes of trusting God for the future), but **M20 is not a single pole-mirror of M19/M31** вЂ” it is a *family of trust-failures plus two trust-neutral cognitive/affective conditions* (U4 perplexity, and the neutral edges of every unit). Pole-paired assembly: M20 should be finalised alongside **M31 (Faith) / M19 (Trust)** and **M18 (Peace)** вЂ” but U4 (Perplexity) should be assembled with **M15 (cognition)**, not the trust axis. `role_in_cluster=pole-anchor` is the right tag for the righteous-care and honest-doubt occurrences (cited, not positive members).
