## 1. What this session did

Took the M04 lexical extract (1,236 focus-term occurrences / 1,103 verses / 60 distinct terms) from raw decomposition through to a cluster-wide tier synthesis, in six batches:

1. **Distillation** — distilled seven candidate inner-being characteristics from the lexical.
2. **Deep dives** — one md per characteristic, exhaustive aspect-by-aspect evidence with citations.
3. **Synergy** — added compound/co-term analysis ("why these terms are together, from this char's perspective").
4. **Naming** — replaced cluster codes with names; confirmed satellite membership from the cluster definition.
5. **Tier application** — applied all 126 T0–T7 questions to each characteristic, one md each, silences stated.
6. **Cluster synthesis** — folded the seven per-char tier analyses into one by-tier cluster-wide synthesis.

## 2. The debate / thinking trail (key turns)

- **Boundary question raised (batch 1–2):** the cluster name "Joy, Gladness and Delight" covers the affect-core (rejoicing, delight, pleasantness) but the term set also includes wonder, gratitude, cheer and soothing — characteristics with *different faculties*. I flagged this as a possible "should these be in another cluster?" issue, plus three independent data/relevance items (tov over-breadth; sha'a lemma mismatch; nichoach = God's satisfaction not a human state; pleasantness = object-property).
- **Researcher resolution (batch 3):** the shared-verse / full-span architecture means a verse is presented to every cluster whose terms it contains, each analysed from its own focus. This **resolved the co-term/relocation dimension** — reach into other clusters is by design. I distinguished this cleanly from the *residual* term-level items it does **not** resolve (tov relevance, sha'a error, nichoach/pleasantness relevance), and added a compound/synergy section to every deep dive.
- **Cluster overview (batch 4):** the M04 cluster definition explicitly lists the wonder/thankfulness/cheer/soothing terms, **closing the membership question** — the satellites are M04 by definition; only nichoach's human-relevance (a separate GR-PROG-007 test) remains. Cluster names applied throughout.
- **Tier application (batch 5):** mapped every one of the 126 questions to the lexical fields (faculty→T3, location→T2, divine_involvement→T0/T4, valence→T0.2, compound→T6, type→T1.2, etc.) and the science extract (T7.3). Stated **Silence** honestly for the question families the lexical cannot address (typology, eschatology, sustained-effect, literary/logical structure, creativity/conscience).
- **Cluster synthesis (batch 6):** folded by tier, with the core-triad / satellite-ring structure and the heart/soul seating as the organising findings.

## 3. Conclusions reached

- **Structure:** M04 = a **core triad** (A Rejoicing → B Delight → C Pleasantness: state → directed state → object-quality) plus a **satellite ring** of four faculty-distinct characteristics (D Wonder, E Thankfulness, F Cheer, G Soothing).
- **Constitution:** joy is **heart-seated**, delight **soul-seated**, C straddles; none engage spirit/mind/conscience.
- **Morality:** the affect-core is **object-determined** — joy/delight are good or sinful by what they rest on.
- **Divine dimension:** strong and shared — God bears joy/delight/pleasantness/wonder (image-bearing); is addressee of thanks and the pleasing aroma.
- **Faculty range:** affect + cognition core, with volition (B), moral-evaluation (D), perception/memory (E/F); **creativity and conscience absent across all seven**.
- **Expression & tension:** joy externalises as praise/song (Praise, Speech) and stands in lexically-evidenced tension with grief/suffering (sorrow-into-joy).

## 4. Decisions made (by researcher)

- Proceed char-by-char rather than pre-resolve boundary cases (batch 2).
- Shared-verse architecture governs co-term reach; analyse synergy from this char's focus (batch 3).
- Use cluster names, not codes, in prose (batch 4).
- Apply the full 126-question catalogue to every char; state silence where no answer; science extract for T7.3 (batch 5).
- Fold into one by-tier cluster synthesis (batch 6).

## 5. Open items / next steps

- **tov relevance pass (C):** under GR-PROG-007, many of the 306 *tov* occurrences (evaluative "good") may not engage a human inner-being characteristic; a relevance pass would right-size characteristic C.
- **nichoach human-relevance test (G):** does *nichoach* (God's acceptance, no faculty tag) engage a *human* inner-being characteristic, or is it an interface datum? Membership is settled; relevance is not.
- **sha'a (H8173B) data check:** gloss "to delight" vs lemma "to be smeared over, be blinded" — verify (6 occ).
