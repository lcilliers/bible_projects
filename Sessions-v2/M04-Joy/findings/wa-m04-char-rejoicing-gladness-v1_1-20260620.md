## 1. Overview

The affective core of the cluster: joy as an inner state and as the act of rejoicing. The largest characteristic by far (545 occurrences across 446 verses), carried by Hebrew *samach/simchah* and *gil*, *sus/sasson*, *masos*, and Greek *chairo/chara* and *agalliao*. Faculty is almost purely **affect** (544/545).
