## 4. What the lexical surfaces — facets of the characteristic

*Observation grounded in §3; interpretation labelled where it goes beyond the field values.*

1. **It is God's satisfaction, not a human inner state.** Divine involvement is *object* (15) and *addressee* (13); object_type *God* (8). The 'how' field is dominated by *burn/offer* (sacrificial verbs). The aroma is pleasing **to the LORD**.

2. **It carries no inner-faculty tag.** Faculty is empty across all 43 — consistent with this being a cultic predicate ('pleasing aroma') rather than a felt inner state of a person.

3. **Its valence is the cult's righteousness.** Valence *righteous* (34) — the pleasing aroma is the accepted, right offering; the few *sinful* (3) cases are aromas offered to idols.

## 5. Boundary and data-quality notes

- **BOUNDARY / relevance — doubtful as a human inner-being characteristic.** *nichoach* describes God's satisfaction at sacrifice. Its relevance to the *human* inner being is doubtful and should be tested verse-by-verse under GR-PROG-007. May be an interface datum (God's pleasure) rather than an M04 human characteristic.
