## 1. The cluster at a glance

M04 holds **seven characteristics** across **1,236 focus-term occurrences in 1,103 verses**. Three affect-core characteristics carry roughly nine-tenths of the volume; four smaller satellites each engage a *different* faculty, extending the cluster beyond pure affect.

| Char | Characteristic | Occ | Verses | Dominant faculty | Grammatical kind | Constitutional seat | Valence character |
|---|---|--:|--:|---|---|---|---|
| A | Rejoicing & gladness | 545 | 446 | affect (pure) | act + status | heart (64) > soul (18) | righteous-led; real sinful/forbidden register |
| B | Delight (pleasure-in) | 189 | 186 | affect + volition + cognition | act + status (willed) | soul (13) > heart (7) | neutral/righteous; notable sinful |
| C | Pleasantness (quality) | 339 | 335 | affect + cognition | quality | heart (16) ≈ soul (13) | neutral/righteous; minor sinful |
| D | Wonder & marvel | 97 | 95 | affect + cognition + moral-eval | act | barely seated | neutral/righteous |
| E | Thankfulness | 16 | 16 | perception + memory | status | barely seated | righteous/commanded |
| F | Cheer & courage | 7 | 7 | perception | act | barely seated | neutral/commanded |
| G | Soothing aroma | 43 | 43 | none tagged | status | not seated | righteous (cultic) |

The cluster therefore has a **core triad** and a **satellite ring**:

- **Core triad** — **A Rejoicing** (gladness as state and act), **B Delight** (that gladness made *directional and willed* — pleasure *in* an object), and **C Pleasantness** (the object-pole: the *quality* that A and B reach toward). Together these are the affective heart of the cluster.
- **Satellite ring** — **D Wonder** (a cognitive amazement at what exceeds the frame), **E Thankfulness** (a perceptual/memorial recognition of benefit), **F Cheer/courage** (a perceptual morale toward the future), and **G Soothing** (a cultic predicate — God's acceptance, not a human state). Each satellite reaches into a different faculty and a different neighbouring cluster.

The M04 cluster definition (wa-cluster-overview-20260610.md) explicitly lists the wonder, thankfulness, cheer and soothing terms, so all seven are M04 by definition; the satellites' distinct faculties describe the cluster's *internal range*, not a mis-assignment.

## 2. Synthesis by tier

### T0 — Divine Image and Created Design

The cluster carries a **strong, patterned divine dimension**. God appears as *agent* of the affect in A, B, C and D (he rejoices — Deu 28:63; he delights; he pronounces the created good; he does the wonders), as *giver* of joy and pleasantness (A, C), as *object* of joy and wonder (A, D), and as *addressee* of thanks and the pleasing aroma (E, G). The split is telling: for the affect-core and wonder (A/B/C/D) God is shown **bearing** the characteristic, so these read as **shared between God and the person** — a genuine image-bearing — whereas gratitude and soothing (E/G) are **creaturely movements directed to God**, not states God bears.

On **created design vs the fallen condition**, the cluster's most important T0 finding is the **valence asymmetry across the ring**: the affect-core A and B carry a real *sinful/forbidden* register (A: 44 sinful+forbidden; B: 19) — joy and delight wrongly placed (gloating over a fall, illicit pleasure) — while the satellites D/E/F/G are righteous or neutral. Positive affect, in this cluster, is **not self-justifying**: its standing as created good or fallen distortion turns on its *object*. **Eschatology and typology are silent** across all seven in the lexical (the term *makarismos*, 'blessedness', is present in A but carries no eschatological marker here); these belong to an evidence layer the lexical does not supply.

### T1 — Definition

Definitionally the cluster spans a **gradient from a single pure affect to a non-faculty predicate**. A is *simple* (affect only); B, C and D are *compound* (affect joined to volition, cognition, or moral-evaluation); E is perceptual/memorial; G engages no inner faculty at all. By **grammatical kind** the cluster ranges across the full set — act (A, B, D, F), status/condition (A, B, E, G), and quality (C) — so M04 describes joy both as *something done*, *something borne*, and (in C) *a quality of what pleases*.

The **internal boundaries** are now precise: A→B is the addition of an object and a will (gladness becomes delight-*in*); B→C is the shift from a borne state to an object-property (delighting vs pleasant); A→D is the shift from affect to cognitive affect (gladness vs being-exceeded). The **external** structural opposite of the core is **Grief (M03)**. A **commanded mode** is prominent in A (joy enjoined, 69) and E (thanks enjoined, 6) — a definitional peculiarity: this cluster's affects can be *summoned*, not only *reacted*.

### T2 — Constitutional Location and Boundaries

This tier yields the cluster's **clearest constitutional structure: a heart/soul division of labour**.

| Char | heart | soul | flesh | inward-parts |
|---|--:|--:|--:|--:|
| A Rejoicing & gladness | 64 | 18 | 6 | 2 |
| B Delight (pleasure-in) | 7 | 13 | 1 | 2 |
| C Pleasantness (quality) | 16 | 13 | 3 | 0 |
| D Wonder & marvel | 2 | 3 | 1 | 0 |
| E Thankfulness | 1 | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| F Cheer & courage | 0 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
| G Soothing aroma | 1 | 0 | 0 | 0 |

**Rejoicing (A) is heart-seated** (heart 64 over soul 18); **Delight (B) is soul-seated** (soul 13 over heart 7); **Pleasantness (C) straddles** the two (heart 16 ≈ soul 13). The satellites are barely located at all. **No characteristic ever engages spirit, mind, or conscience.** This is a substantive cluster finding: M04's affects live in the heart and soul, not in the spirit or the mind, and the gladness/delight pair are seated *differently* from one another — a constitutional distinction, not noise.

On **body direction**, only A shows a clear link, and it runs **outward** (soul→body): joy breaks into the shout (*renanah*) and the leap (*salad*). On **origin**, the cluster is mostly unmarked (generated within), with God marked as *giver* in A and C; no characteristic shows movement up the spirit→soul→body axis.

Read across, the cluster occupies **affect** (A/B/C/D) and **cognition** (B/C/D) as its main territory, with **volition** appearing only in B (the choosing of *eudokeo*), **moral-evaluation** only in D (the 'surpassing' of *pele*), and **perception/memory** carrying E and F. Two faculties are **absent across all seven — creativity and conscience.** That double silence is a genuine cluster-wide statement: M04 is an affective-evaluative cluster that does **not** touch the sin-witness of conscience or the originating power of creativity. Agency is engaged only derivatively (through the action terms); relational capacity is engaged wherever the affect is directed at persons or God (A, B, C).

### T4 — Relational Interfaces

The cluster is **overwhelmingly a God-and-person affair, and strongly bidirectional on the divine axis.** God moves toward the person as *giver* and *agent* of joy, delight and pleasantness (A, B, C); the person moves toward God as the *object* of rejoicing and delight and the *addressee* of thanks and the pleasing aroma (A, B, E, G). The **human-to-human** interface is thin but real — A rejoices *with* and *over* another, B delights *in* a person — and the **spiritual-beings** interface is minimal (a handful of occurrences in A, B, D; no adversarial weaponisation or angelic mediation is evidenced). The relational scope of A notably reaches even to enemies (joy expressed *over* a defeated foe — Relational, M44).

### T5 — Formative and Developmental Dimension

This is the **thinnest tier in the lexical**, and the synthesis must say so plainly. The lexical records no sustained-effect, sanctification-arc, or eschatological field, so most T5 questions return silence across all seven. The **one substantive datum** is **A's co-occurrence with Grief (M03, 57 links) and Weakness (M24, 22)** — the lexical footprint of the **sorrow-into-joy movement** and of **joy in the presence of suffering** (e.g. Ezr 3:12, weeping and shouting in one verse). The science review reinforces this as the cluster's distinctive against the immanent model (joy that co-exists with grief, joy in suffering). The formative *depth* of M04 — how joy forms a person over time — lies beyond what the lexical alone can establish and is a candidate for verse-level work.

### T6 — Structural Relationships with Other Characteristics

This tier carries the **interrelationships** most fully — both within M04 and outward.

**Within the cluster**, the nearest-neighbour map is: A↔B (gladness vs directed delight — a difference of *direction and will*); B↔C (state vs object-quality — a difference of *constitutional pole*); A↔D (affect vs cognitive affect — a difference of *faculty*); E and G stand apart by faculty (perception/memory; none). The core triad coheres as **state (A) → directed state (B) → object-quality (C)**.

Three cluster-wide patterns emerge. First, **Joy externalises** — A's heaviest non-self links are Praise (M22) and Speech (M42): joy becomes song and shout. Third, **the satellites each have their own home neighbourhood** — Wonder with Wisdom and Remembrance, Gratitude with Prayer and Praise, Soothing with Repentance, Salvation and Purity. Under the shared-verse architecture these reaches are read here from joy's perspective and re-presented when those clusters are processed; the reach is by design, not leakage.

### T7 — Evidential and Methodological Foundation

**Lexically** the cluster is **trilingual**: a Hebrew core throughout, Greek carrying A/B/D and *all* of E, Aramaic traces in A and D. The Testamental pattern is itself informative — **gratitude (E) is Greek-only** (an NT-register development, *eucharistia*), while **soothing (G) is Hebrew-only** (the OT sacrificial cult). The act/status/quality spread (T1.2) gives the vocabulary its range from the *act* of rejoicing to the *condition* of joy to the *quality* of the pleasant.

**Scientifically** (T7.3), the cluster maps comprehensively onto **positive psychology and affective neuroscience** — joy to Fredrickson's broaden-and-build, delight to Berridge's wanting/liking, wonder to awe/elevation research, gratitude to Emmons & McCullough, pleasantness to eudaimonic-flourishing models, cheer to self-efficacy/optimism. The science **illuminates** the expansive, social, savouring character of biblical joy; it **diverges** at three theological distinctives the lexical insists on — *commanded* joy, joy that *co-exists with grief and suffering*, and joy *grounded in a certain future* — none of which an immanent, circumstance-reactive model accommodates; and it **surfaces a gap**: hedonic adaptation predicts joy fades to baseline, which throws the cluster's tie to *renewed encounter, remembrance and praise* into relief as an anti-adaptation mechanism worth verse investigation. For **G (Soothing)** the science *divergence is the finding*: the wellbeing frame does not apply to a human subject, because *nichoach* describes God's acceptance — reinforcing the open relevance question.

## 3. Cluster-wide integration — the picture that emerges

Folded together, **M04 is an affective–evaluative cluster whose centre of gravity is gladness-and-delight, seated in the heart and the soul, whose rightness is determined by its object — God and his acts pre-eminently — and which externalises into praise and song.** Around that core sit four faculty-distinct satellites that extend the cluster into cognition (wonder), perception and memory (gratitude, cheer), and the sacrificial cult (soothing), each reaching by design into a neighbouring cluster.

Five findings hold across the whole cluster:

1. **A constitutional division of labour** — joy seats in the heart, delight in the soul; neither touches spirit, mind, or conscience.
2. **Object-determined morality** — the affect-core is morally bivalent; joy and delight are good or sinful according to what they rest on, not in themselves.
3. **A strong, shared divine dimension** — God bears joy, delight, pleasantness and wonder (image-bearing), and is the chief object and addressee of the cluster's God-ward movements.
4. **Outward expression and living tension** — joy issues in praise and song and stands in lexically-evidenced tension with grief and suffering (the sorrow-into-joy movement).
5. **A bounded faculty range** — the cluster occupies affect, cognition, and (at its edges) volition, moral-evaluation, perception and memory, but is wholly silent on creativity and conscience.

**What the lexical cannot yet establish**, and the synthesis flags honestly: the cluster's **formative depth** (T5), its **eschatological trajectory** (T0.2.3/T5.6), and its **typology** (T0.4) all return silence — these require a verse-reading layer beyond the lexical decomposition. And two term-level items remain open and unaffected by this synthesis: the **tov relevance pass** (C) and the **nichoach human-relevance test** (G).
