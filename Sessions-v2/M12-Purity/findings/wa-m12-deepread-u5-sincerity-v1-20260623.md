### Verdict on the hypothesis — **MIXED: it is NOT a standalone CHARACTERISTIC — it reads as a QUALIFIER/MODIFIER of other dispositions (and partly an attribute of a thing); the M13 overlap is REAL; conscience/heart-seatedness is NOT lemma-borne (faculty = none 9/9) — only co-seated by partner**

**(a) Characteristic vs state — NEITHER cleanly: U5 behaves as a QUALIFIER/MODIFIER, not a standalone faculty-disposition.** ⚖ This is the deep-read's main catch. Three lines of evidence:

1. **faculty = none 9/9** — every U5 lemma is **faculty-empty**. Unlike a true characteristic (which carries a faculty-in-operation, e.g. tamim's conscience/heart), sincerity carries **no faculty on the lemma**. It only becomes "seated" because its partner-noun is (suneidesis, kardia, dianoia, psuchē). So it is not itself a faculty in operation.
2. **anupokritos (6 of 9) modifies ANOTHER disposition, never stands alone.** It is always *"X, genuine"*: "let **love** be genuine" (Rom 12:9), "**faith** unfeigned / sincere" (1Ti 1:5; 2Ti 1:5), "sincere **brotherly love**" (1Pe 1:22), "wisdom … **sincere**" (Jam 3:17, in the virtue-list from-above), "**genuine love**" (2Cor 6:6). The inner object is the love / faith / wisdom; *genuine* is the **predicate of its authenticity** (the un-hypocrisy of it). In §10 terms this is an **EXPRESSION/quality modifier** of a characteristic — closer to *"the authenticity-register of M05 love, M13 faith, M15 wisdom"* than to a standalone M12 disposition.
3. **eilikrines (2) is the one mind-seated case but is still modifier-like**, and **adolos (1) modifies a THING.** "your **sincere mind** (dianoia)" (2Pe 3:1 — mind co-seated; the strongest disposition-edge) and "be **pure and blameless** for the day of Christ" (Phil 1:10, partners aproskopos "blameless"). adolos qualifies "the **pure spiritual milk**" (1Pe 2:2) — an attribute of a **thing** (the milk/word), **not an inner-being object at all**. ⚠

→ **U5 is not a clean characteristic; it is the authenticity/genuineness QUALIFIER applied to other inner dispositions (love/faith/wisdom/mind).** It is real M12 vocabulary (the un-mixed, un-feigned register of purity applied to the heart's dispositions), but as a **modifier-quality**, not a faculty-in-operation unit. Whether it should survive as its own unit, fold into M12's inner-purity unit (U2) as its "authenticity" facet, or be read as a cross-cutting qualifier of M05/M13/M15, is the structural question — *noted only.*

**(b) The M13 (truth/integrity) overlap — CONFIRMED, in-verse.** ↔ The sincerity register sits at the M12/M13 seam explicitly. 1Pe 1:22: souls "purified by **obedience to the truth** (alētheia, M13)" → "**sincere** (anupokritos) brotherly love" → "love … from a **pure heart**" — sincerity is generated *by truth* and *toward* purity, the M12↔M13 bridge in one verse. The "without hypocrisy" core of anupokritos is the un-falseness pole of M13 (truth vs hypocrisy/deception). eilikrines (judged-by-sunlight, "unmixed") is the **integrity/wholeness** sense shared with M13's tom/integrity and M12's purity — sincerity = un-adulterated truth-in-the-inner-person. The draft's "overlaps M13" holds; the overlap is **constitutive** (sincerity is largely the M13 truth-virtue read as inner un-mixedness), which strengthens the case that U5 is a qualifier at a cluster seam rather than an independent M12 disposition.

The genuine inner-being seats are all **co-seated via partners**, never lemma-borne: suneidesis "conscience" + kardia "heart" (1Ti 1:5 "**pure heart and a good conscience** and a sincere faith"); kardia + psuchē (1Pe 1:22 "purified your **souls** … from a **pure heart**"); dianoia "mind" (2Pe 3:1). These confirm the draft's "conscience/heart/mind-seated" observation **at the partner level** — sincerity attaches to the whole inner-person (conscience/heart/mind/soul), which is exactly the behaviour of a qualifier, not a single-faculty disposition.
