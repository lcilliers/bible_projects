## §OBJECT-TYPE — the reading that flows from the evidence

**PU-2 is a moral-purity STATE with an inner-cleansing EXPRESSION inside it — and the per-verse OBJECT carries the ritual→inner distinction.**

### The STATE (katharos / hagnos adj + purity nouns) — *moral purity as a condition*
- **valence-variation (righteous 23 / commanded 7 / neutral 4) is the state-signal**; **righteous-dominant** (heavier moral register than PU-1's neutral-dominant ritual state).
- object near-empty (11%) is **nature-correct** for "a *pure* heart / *purity*".
- co-seated with **kardia / suneidesis M47 (heart/conscience) ×9** — the **seat** of the purity is the inner faculty, carried in co-seating (per-verse, legitimate), not in a faculty tag.
- → **STATE / CONDITION** — moral/inner purity. Its negative pole is the defilement register (NP-1) and hamartia (M10, co-seated).

### The ACT (katharizō / hagnizō) — *inner cleansing as an EXPRESSION*
- object **varies (13 distinct)** + experiencer = a directed act; but **how = 0%** — the manner is the **cleansing agent/mechanism** (blood Heb 9:14,22; **faith** Act 15:9 "cleansed their hearts **by faith**"; the **word/washing** Eph 5:26), bound in the verse, not an inner disposition.
- → an **EXPRESSION read through its binding** (expression-lens §1): the act *produces* the purity-STATE; its content is the bound agent (blood/faith/word) and the inner object it targets (heart/soul/conscience). **NOT a characteristic** (no inner faculty operating *as* the act — the agent is external: blood, faith, the word) and **NOT a state** (object varies, it acts). The **cleansing-EXPRESSION that produces the purity-STATE**: *cleanse the heart → pure heart*.

### The ritual→inner distinction (PU-1 vs PU-2) — does the per-verse OBJECT carry it? **YES — decisively.**
- The split is **in the object field, verse by verse**, exactly as required (not faculty-imposed):
  - **Ritual/bodily objects:** lepers, leprosy, Naaman, **cup** (Mat 23:25 — "you clean the *outside* of the cup"), blood, temple, washing.
  - **Inner/moral objects:** **hearts** (Act 15:9, Jam 4:8), **souls** (1Pe 1:22), **conscience** (Heb 9:14), hands+hearts (Jam 4:8 "cleanse your *hands*… purify your *hearts*").
- The NT itself thematises the shift **within single verses**: Mat 23:25-26 (clean the *outside* of the cup [ritual] → first clean the *inside* [inner]); Jam 4:8 (cleanse *hands* → purify *hearts*); Mar 7:19 (food does not defile — co-seated **kardia**). The OT-ritual→NT-inner movement named in the unit is **grounded in the per-verse object**, confirming the PU-1 (ritual things) / PU-2 (hearts/conscience) split holds verse-by-verse.
- The STATE adjectives carry it in **co-seating**: katharos/hagnos co-seat **kardia/suneidesis M47 ×9** ("pure in *heart*", "*good conscience*") — the inner seat, per-verse.

**Summary:** PU-2 = the **moral-purity STATE** (pure heart/conscience; righteous-dominant valence; seat = kardia/suneidesis M47) + the **inner-cleansing EXPRESSION** (katharizō/hagnizō, produces the state, manner = bound agent blood/faith/word). The ritual→inner distinction vs PU-1 is **carried by the per-verse object** (cup/leper/blood/temple → heart/soul/conscience), not imposed. No standalone M12 *characteristic* — purity is a condition, inner-cleansing is the act that effects it.
