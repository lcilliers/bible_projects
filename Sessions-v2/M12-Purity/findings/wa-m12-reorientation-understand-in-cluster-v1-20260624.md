## §3. The corrected method (governing — applies to every cluster)

1. **In-cluster first.** Every owned term is understood in M12's own context before any cross-cluster note. The cross-cluster relation is the *last* observation, not the first.
2. **Frequency ≠ noise.** A high-frequency or generic-gloss term (na.tan 1,069×) is a candidate *centre* of a characteristic, to be read — not exported. (Read the heart/soul-seated occurrences, not the gloss.)
3. **No exporting.** A term's relation to another cluster (or its appearance there under the dual-view) does **not** remove it from M12. The defilement pole, the forming verbs, the giving verb are M12 content.
4. **Hypotheses, not conclusions, from structure.** The "privative-virtue" pattern (§2) and the giving/forming readings are **hypotheses to test against the verses**, not gloss/etymology conclusions. Ground them or drop them.
5. **Drop only the genuinely absent.** (tsur H6696C / mikh.lah etc. — confirm presence in the extract before any structural claim; absence is a data fact, not an export.)
