### Verdict on the hypothesis — **SPLITS (into a forensic STATE + a character CHARACTERISTIC), conscience-seatedness HOLDS as a tag but is term-intrinsic, guilt-inverse CONFIRMED for the naqi axis**

**(a) The unit SPLITS — naqi (forensic STATE) vs tamim (character CHARACTERISTIC).** ⚖ This is the deep-read's main catch; the draft's "one forensic-positive STATE" is too coarse.

- **naqi / naqah = the FORENSIC-POSITIVE axis (STATE + the verb that produces it).** The naqi corpus (54 verses dedup) is dominated by the **"innocent blood / the innocent / free-from-liability"** register: the `how`-verbs are **shed (blood) ×10, purge ×4, kill ×2, liable** — judicial-bloodguilt language, not faculty-life. Anchors: "the owner of the ox shall **not be liable**" (Exo 21:28); "do not kill the **innocent** and righteous, for I will not acquit the wicked" (Exo 23:7); "lest **innocent blood** be shed … the **guilt** of bloodshed be upon you" (Deu 19:10); "purge the **guilt of innocent blood**" (Deu 19:13). The verb na.qah is the forensic **operation** ("be free / be clear / acquit": Gen 24:8 "free from this oath"; Num 5:19; Exo 20:7 "will not hold guiltless"). This is **§10 kind = STATE (forensic-positive) / RECORD-inverse** — a verdict-classification of liability-status, not a faculty in operation. It is the clean **inverse of M10 #17 guilt / #13 record**.
- **tamim = the CHARACTER axis (the blameless WALK / integrity of heart).** The tamim corpus (34 verses) reads as a **faculty-in-operation, lived register**: its `how`-verbs are **walked / ponder / minister / acted**, and it is **heart-located** (heart ×3 + inward-parts; le.vav/lev co-seated). Anchors: "Noah … **blameless** in his generation. Noah **walked** with God" (Gen 6:9); "**walk** before me and **be blameless**" (Gen 17:1); "He who **walks blamelessly** and does what is right and **speaks truth in his heart**" (Psa 15:2, le.vav co-seated); "I will **walk with integrity of heart** within my house" (Psa 101:2, tom "integrity" + le.vav co-seated). The sense-set itself carries the character register — **integrity ×4, uprightly, truth** — and tamim partners overwhelmingly with **M13 (truth/faithfulness: e.met, a.man, tom)** and **M26 (righteousness: tsad.diq, tse.deq)**, not with bloodguilt. This is **§10 kind = CHARACTERISTIC** (a disposition/walk that is being-bent and faculty-seated). → **tamim is NOT primarily a STATE; the draft's STATE classification holds only for the naqi axis.**

**(b) Conscience-seatedness HOLDS as a tag (132/135 = 97.8%) but is TERM-INTRINSIC, not verse-evidenced per occurrence.** ⚠ The `faculty=conscience` tag rides on the lemma uniformly — it is present even on the bare forensic "innocent blood" verses (Deu 19:10/13) and on the civil "not liable" (Exo 21:28), where no conscience is actually in operation in the verse. So: the **conscience signature is real at the lexical level** (this family IS the conscience's positive verdict-vocabulary, the counterpart to M10's guilt-conscience), but it should be read as *"the faculty this vocabulary belongs to"* and **not** over-read as *"the conscience is actively seated in each verse."* The genuine per-verse interior seatings concentrate on the **tamim** axis (le.vav/lev in Psa 15:2; 101:2 — the heart-walk), exactly where the character-register lives — corroborating the split. (Mirrors the M10 a.von hygiene flag: term-intrinsic faculty ≠ per-verse seat; and the ne.phesh×9 / le.vav co-seatings here are mostly the "person/life" idiom, e.g. the legal "if any **soul** … is innocent," not soul-as-interior.)

**(c) tamim "integrity / blameless walk" reads as a CHARACTERISTIC, not a STATE** — see (a). The decisive signals: heart-location, walk/ponder/minister verbs, the integrity/uprightness sense-band, and the M13/M26 partner-web. tamim is the **lived disposition of blamelessness** (a faculty-in-operation), whereas naqi is the **standing verdict of innocence** (a forensic state). The draft folded both under one "forensic-positive STATE" head; the evidence requires **two kinds**.
