## §OBJECT-TYPE — the reading that FLOWS FROM the evidence

**Question (from the preliminary split):** does IN read as ONE unit or TWO — IN-1 naqi = forensic STATE / liability-inverse, vs IN-2 tamim = candidate CHARACTERISTIC (the blameless walk / integrity)? Decided below from **sense distribution + the `how` predicate (what the person DOES) + co-seating** — NOT from the faculty tag.

### Verdict: TWO units. IN-1 (naqi) = STATE (forensic-positive); IN-2 (tamim) = CHARACTERISTIC (the blameless walk). The split holds verse-by-verse on three independent evidence axes.

**Axis 1 — SENSE.** The sense field itself bifurcates and does not blur:
- **naqi/naqah:** *innocent ×55 · guiltless ×12 · free ×9 · liable ×2 · free-of-obligation ×2 · acquit · clean · leave/empty* — a **non-liability / not-charged** band. Every value is a verdict on standing, not a description of conduct.
- **tamim:** *blameless ×19 · perfect ×6 · **integrity ×4** · uprightly · blamelessly · truth · sincerity* — a **wholeness-of-character** band. "integrity / uprightly / sincerity / truth" name a lived quality, not a liability-status.

**Axis 2 — the `how` predicate (what the person DOES).** This is the field the LRT confirmed is populated (~70%), and it is **decisive**:
- **naqi `how`:** *shed ×10 · be/is ×10 · purge ×4 · said ×4 · eaten ×2 · kill ×2 · set ×2 · liable ×2 · sin ×2* — the predicates are **judicial operations on liability** (innocent blood *shed*; guilt *purged*; not held *liable*; will not *acquit*). The person is not DOING anything; the verbs act *on* the innocent's standing. This is the signature of a **STATE / verdict**, not a faculty-in-operation. The bare "be/is" (Gen 44:10, Jos 2:19-20, Num 32:22) is pure standing.
- **tamim `how`:** ***walked*** (Gen 6:9) · **ponder** (Psa 101:2, +integrity-of-heart) · **acted** in good faith (Judg 9:16,19) · **minister** (Psa 101:6) · **remain** (Pro 2:21) · **delivered** ("whoever walks in integrity", Pro 28:18) · **served** (Jos 24:14) · **be** (Gen 17:1, commanded) · **made** (2Sa 22:33, God makes my way blameless). The predicates are **things the person DOES / a way they walk** — a lived operation. tamim is *how one conducts the whole life* (walk / act / minister / ponder the blameless way). This is the signature of a **CHARACTERISTIC-in-operation**, not a standing verdict. → **tamim's "integrity/walk" is a characteristic-in-operation (how = walked/acted/kept), NOT a bare state.** The decisive answer to the prompt's question.

**Axis 3 — CO-SEATING (in-verse, in-cluster first).**
- **naqi** co-seats with the **liability web**: a.von "guilt" (M10) ×4, cha.ta "to sin" (M10) ×4, ra.sha "wicked" (M10), tsad.diq/tsa.deq/mish.pat "righteous/justify/justice" (M26) ×9. The explicit pole-pairs sit in single verses: *"do not kill the **innocent** … I will not **acquit** the **wicked**"* (Exo 23:7); *"innocent blood … the **guilt** of bloodshed"* (Deu 19:10); *"On me be the **guilt** … let the king be **guiltless**"* (2Sa 14:9). **naqi is the positive verdict where M10 guilt/record is the negative liability — the RECORD/STATE inverse, on the forensic axis.**
- **tamim** co-seats with the **character web**: e.met/a.man "truth/faithful" (M13) ×6, ya.shar "upright" (M13) ×3, tom "integrity" (M13), cha.sad/cha.sid "kind/pious" (M05) ×6, tsad.diq "righteous" (M26) ×3, **le.vav/lev "heart" (M47) ×2 (Psa 101:2 — the one genuine per-verse heart-seat: "walk with integrity of heart")**. *"He who **walks blamelessly** and does what is right and **speaks truth in his heart**"* (Psa 15:2); *"I was **blameless** … and kept myself from **guilt**"* (2Sa 22:24, the character-side meeting a.von). **tamim is the lived disposition partnered with truth, loyalty and the heart — a different web from naqi's bloodguilt/acquittal web.**

**Why not one unit / why the faculty tag does NOT decide it.** A single-unit reading would have to lean on the shared `faculty=conscience` constant (132/135) — exactly the move the discipline forbids, and the tag is demonstrably term-intrinsic (present on civil "not liable for an ox", Exo 21:28, where no conscience operates). The **per-verse-varying** evidence — sense band, `how` predicate, and co-seat web — splits cleanly and **three-fold**, with no axis dissenting. The split is therefore grounded in what varies per verse, not in the constant.
