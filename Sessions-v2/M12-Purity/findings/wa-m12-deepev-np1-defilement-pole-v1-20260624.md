## §OBJECT-TYPE — flowing from the evidence (not from grammar)

**NP-1 is a CONDITION / STATE (the negative pole of M12's purity axis), carrying TWO registers the evidence keeps distinct: a ritual/bodily uncleanness and a moral/inner defilement.** This is M12's own content — the dark side of clean·clear·whole·set-apart.

What the evidence shows, in M12's own terms (in-cluster first):

1. **It is a STATE, not a faculty or act.** type=status 34/34, faculty none 32/34. "Uncleanness" is a *condition the inner being is IN* (or the body is in), never something a faculty does. The valence-variation (the classic state-signal) is fully present.
2. **The valence field carries the moral↔ritual split openly.** **neutral 8 + forbidden 4 = the ritual/bodily register** (discharge, menstruation, corpse-contact, Nazirite food-boundary — Lev 15:3 "flesh", 2Sa 11:4 self-purifying); **sinful 21 = the moral register** (the "spirit of uncleanness" Zec 13:2; defiled mind+conscience Tit 1:15; uncleanness = transgressions Eze 39:24). The brief's question — *is it moral sin or a ritual state?* — is answered: **BOTH, and the corpus keeps them in evidential proportion (~62% moral-sinful, ~35% ritual).** The OT priestly mass is ritual-state; the prophets (Ezekiel) and NT moralise it (uncleanness = the inner condition needing a new heart / a cleansed conscience).
3. **As an inner-being object it is "the staining of the inner person."** Where it is located, the locus is **soul / mind / conscience / flesh** (6 explicit) — Tit 1:15 is the clearest inner-being case ("their **minds and consciences** are defiled," contra "to the pure all things are pure"); Lev 22:3 / Num 19:13 seat it in the **soul**; Heb 12:15 a "root of bitterness" defiles. Defilement here = **the inner being rendered unfit to approach the holy** — the inverse of consecration (note the heavy partnering with **qa.dash "consecrate"** and the cleansing verbs **ta.her / ta.hor**: defilement is precisely what M12's purity-acts remove).
4. **It is the constitutive CONTRAST that defines the cluster's positive pole.** M12's clean/whole/set-apart is only meaningful against this — the same axis, opposite end (Eze 36:25 holds both in one verse: "you shall be **clean** from all your **uncleannesses**"). It belongs **in** M12, not exported.

**Object-type reading:** **CONDITION / STATE — the negative pole of the M12 purity axis**, a two-register state (ritual-bodily uncleanness · moral-inner defilement of soul/mind/conscience). It is inner-being **content in its own right** (the staining-condition the purity-faculty acts against), not a faculty and not an act.
