## §2. The shape of M12 (emerged, not imposed)

M12 is the **clean / whole / set-apart inner being**, and the evidence sorts it into:
- **ONE clear CHARACTERISTIC:** **blamelessness / integrity-of-walk** (tamim) — the faculty-in-operation (a person *walking* in integrity), the only M12 unit whose per-verse `how` shows the person acting from an inner disposition.
- **A family of positive STATES:** ritual cleanness, inner/moral purity, holiness (holy-state), innocence/non-liability, incorruptibility — the inner being described by what it positively *is*.
- **Their producing EXPRESSIONS:** cleansing (ritual + inner), consecrating, giving — acts that produce/manifest the states; studied via their binding (the cleansing agent, the consecrating warrant, the giving manner).
- **The negative pole** (defilement) — M12's own contrast STATE.
- **A QUALIFIER** (genuineness) and **peripheral/mis-scoped** terms (anointing, soundness).

This is the §10-grid failure *avoided*: no object-kind was assigned from a lemma-constant; the one characteristic earned it on the `how` predicate; the states earned it on valence-variation; the expressions were identified as acts whose inner content is the binding.
