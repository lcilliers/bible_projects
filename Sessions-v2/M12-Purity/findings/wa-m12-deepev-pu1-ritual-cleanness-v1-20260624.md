## §OBJECT-TYPE — the reading that flows from the evidence

**PU-1 is a STATE with an EXPRESSION act inside it — split by grammar, confirmed by the per-verse fields.**

### The STATE (ta.hor adj / to.har noun) — *cleanness as a condition*
- **valence-variation is the state-signal** (LRT framework §2 / spec §2): valence takes 4 values (neutral/righteous/commanded/sinful) — a condition with a moral register, exactly the profile the LRT clears as a state.
- object near-empty (16%) is **nature-correct for a predicate-adjective**: cleanness is *predicated of* a subject (person/priest/clothes), it does not act.
- → **STATE / CONDITION.** "Clean/pure" is the positive pole of the cleanness condition; its negative pole is **ta.me M10 (×19 co-seating)** — the unclean state. This is the cleanness register of M12.

### The ACT (ta.her verb) — *cleansing as an EXPRESSION (a ritual procedure)*
- object **varies (35 distinct)** + experiencer (other/self/addressed) = a directed act on a varying target — the act-profile.
- BUT the manner (`how`) is **absent/non-inner** (LRT-B): cleansing carries no willing/grudging disposition. Its "how" is the **bound atonement procedure** (kip.per/ka.phar ×20 combined). This is an **EXPRESSION read through its binding** (per the expression-lens): the act manifests/produces the *state* (cleanness), with the manner in the co-seated rite, not in an inner faculty.
- It is **NOT a characteristic** (no inner faculty-in-operation: it is a ceremonial procedure, manner non-inner) and **NOT a state** (object varies, it acts). It is the **cleansing-EXPRESSION that produces the cleanness-STATE**: *cleanse → clean*.

### The ritual→inner distinction (PU-1 vs PU-2) — does the OBJECT carry it?
- **Yes, and it places PU-1 firmly on the RITUAL side.** The per-verse **objects are ritual/bodily things**: thumb, clothes, discharge, disease, blood, priest, head, gardens — never a heart/conscience/soul. The experiencer is the body/person undergoing a rite. This is the **ritual pole** of the cleanness axis, grounded in the object field, **not** imposed from faculty. (PU-2 will show the contrast: hearts/souls/conscience.)

**Summary:** PU-1 = the **cleanness STATE** (positive pole, contrast = ta.me M10) + the **ritual cleansing EXPRESSION** (ta.her, produces the state, manner = bound atonement rite). Both ritual/bodily by object. No standalone M12 characteristic here — cleanness is a condition, and cleansing is the act that effects it.
