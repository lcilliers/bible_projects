## §OBJECT-TYPE — flowing from the evidence (not from grammar)

**The evidence settles GN-1 as a QUALIFIER / MODIFIER (the "expression-lens" authenticity-register), NOT a standalone characteristic and NOT a state.**

What the per-verse evidence shows, in its own terms:

1. **No own faculty, no own object.** faculty = none 9/9 and own-object 33% (and those are elliptical/thing). A characteristic is a *faculty-in-operation directed at an object*; GN-1 has neither on the lemma. This is the decisive evidential fact, and it is the opposite of (e.g.) the innocence/blamelessness family (how 71%, faculty-borne).
2. **It always predicates the authenticity of ANOTHER inner thing.** anupokritos (6/9) = "*love / faith / brotherly-love / wisdom*, genuine"; eilikrines (2) = "*mind* sincere," "pure [for the day]"; the un-feigned-ness is the **truth-value of the partner virtue**, not an inner object in itself.
3. **The "object" the LRT flagged is the partner.** The gap is not the lexical missing operational content — it is the correct signature of a word that *modifies* rather than *acts*. So the object-type call rests on a field the LRT confirmed is *genuinely* sparse-by-nature.
4. **Within M12's own composition** (re-orientation §2), GN-1 is the head of the **privative "un-vice" virtues** (an-upokritos "without hypocrisy", a-dolos "without guile", ei-likrines "sun-judged / unmixed"): the inner being **free-of** a named defect. As a *family* this is a real M12 face (genuineness = unmixed purity of the inner person); but the **unit-as-graded** behaves as a qualifier — it qualifies the dispositions M12 shares with M05/M13/M15. The right M12 reading: GN-1 is **the authenticity / un-mixed-ness REGISTER of the inner being** — best carried as a facet of the inner-purity unit (PU-2) and/or a cross-cutting qualifier, **not** a free-standing characteristic with its own tier.

**Object-type reading:** **EXPRESSION/QUALIFIER** (the authenticity-register applied to a characteristic) — one near-edge (eilikrines 2Pe 3:1 "sincere mind", the only mind-seated, near-standalone case) and one out-of-scope (adolos 1Pe 2:2, a thing-attribute).
